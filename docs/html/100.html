For the TV show, see aiThe 100.
100, spoken as one hundred or a hundred, is a natural number following 99 and preceding 101. 100 can be divided by 1, 2, 4, 5, 10, 20, 25, 50, and 100, and can be square rooted, equaling 10. 100 is a full percentage (which is Latin for per hundred). The prefix for 100 is hecto-.
