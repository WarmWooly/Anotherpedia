10 (Balatro)

10 is a rank of playing cards in Balatro that is worth 10 Chips when played without Modifiers or Jokers. 10s are considered to be a numbered card. The Abandoned Deck removes all face cards, so 10 is more likely to be drawn. The Erratic Deck randomly selects ranks, so 10 may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks. The Idol gives x2 Mult when a card of a randomly selected rank and suit is played, including 10s of any suit.
