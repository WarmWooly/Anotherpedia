11.22.63

{{i11.22.63}} is a 2016 television miniseries based on Stephan King's 2011 novel 11/22/63, in which a time traveler attempts to stop the assassination of John F. Kennedy. The series was created by Bridget Carpenter, with James Franco playing the lead role as the time traveler. The series has a total of 8 episode.
