{{tFor the host of the web series {{aiThe Power of Two}}, see Two ({{aiThe Power of Two}}).}}&sp{{b2}} (spelled {{btwo}}) is a even (math), prime number number between 1 and 3. It is the square root of 4 and can only be evenly divided by 1 and 2. 2 is the 2nd prime number. Dividing a number by 2 will give you half the number, and multiplication it by 2 will double the number. 2 is the fourth (ordering) number in the Fibonacci sequence.
