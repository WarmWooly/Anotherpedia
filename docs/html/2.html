2

For the host of the web series {{aiThe Power of Two, see Two (aiThe Power of Two).}}&sp2 (spelled two) is a even, prime number between 1 and 3. It is the square root of 4 and can only be evenly divided by 1 and 2. 2 is the 2nd prime number. Dividing a number by 2 will give you half the number, and multiplying it by 2 will double the number. 2 is the fourth number in the Fibonacci sequence.
