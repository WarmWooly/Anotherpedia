2.1.4 {{iBetter Than Wolves}} Speedrun Competition

<><>The {{b2.1.4 {{iBetter Than Wolves}} Speedrun Competition}} was a [[speedrun competition]] hosted by [[Cahd]] for the {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|mod (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Better Than Wolves]]}} for the version [[2.1.4|Better Than Wolves CE 2.1.4]] that was announced on May 20, 2025, and started on June 1, 2025. The competition ended on July 1, 2025, with the top 3 prizes going to JustBeCool ($60), [[Franzy|franzy14]] ($45), and [[DraViGen]] ($25). Additionally, JustBeCool and Franzy got $75 for each having 12 top three times.<>All players are required to follow the rules for [[{{iBetter Than Wolves}} speedrunning]] and post their runs onto the {{iBetter Than Wolves}} [[speedrun.com]] page.<> Collaberating with other players or cheating will deduct points or cause a player to be disqualified.&pScoring is based on movement on the leaderboard and posted scores to the speedrun.com page:<>&spPlayers who were on the leaderboard prior will automatically be awarded 1 point per submitted run in each category. Submitting a series of record takes (i.e. getting 3rd, then 1st) will only award the highest value {{iunless}} another player on the board has moved up. Runs completed before the competition will not be awarded "Take nth place" points, and new runs must be submitted during the competition to award points. Cahd, who runs the event, does not participate and will not be counted for scoring. During the event, there were a total of 12 categories for {{iBetter Than Wolves}} speedrunning that players can run for, with each having an RSG (randomized [[seed|seed (Minecraft)]]) and a SSG (preset seed) version:<>&spBased on the player who has the most points by the end of the competition, the prizes were $15 [[USD|United States Dollar]] for 3rd most points, $35 for 2nd, and $50 for 1st. After enough donations, the prizes changed to $25 for 3rd, $45 for 2nd, and $60 for 1st. Additionally, two $75 prizes were awarded for holding the most top three times on the leaderboard.<>The following are the final placements (usernames from speedrun.com):<><>On July 1st, following the prize distributions and conclusion of the initial event, a [[bonus round]] was added alongside a new speedrun category, Tradeless%. The goal is to kill the [[Ender Dragon|Ender Dragon (Better Than Wolves)]] with a mod to add craftable [[Eyes of Ender|Eye of Ender (Better Than Wolves)]] in a random seed the fastest, with a $100 prize after a currently unset time frame to the fastest time. <>, there haven't been any submitted Tradeless% runs.
