3D Block game (JoonBoHono)

3D Block game is a Desmos video game made within Desmos created by JoonBoHono where the player places blocks on a 5x5x5 cube grid to match five given sculptures as fast as possible. JoonBoHono made a YouTube video about his game on June 12, 2025. The player can place blocks from the faces of other blocks, delete blocks, rotate their view, and show what shape they are trying to build. The player has to build a square, a cactus, a cat, a person in front of a heart, and three Japanese writing system.
