3D printing

3D printing or additive manufacturing is a process to manufacture a 3D object utilizing a 3D model or a computer-aided design. Various material can be used in 3D printing, often in the form of plastic or a composite material including such. The device used to 3D print is known as a 3D printer, with different specifications based on the size, precision, and unique operations that the 3D printer can perform. 3D printing involves the material to be added or set in some way, which is distinct from subtractive manufacturing where the material is reduced into shape.
