6 (Balatro)

6 is a rank of playing cards in Balatro that is worth 6 Chips when played without Modifiers or Jokers. 6s are considered to be a numbered card. The Abandoned Deck removes all face cards, so 6 is more likely to be drawn. The Erratic Deck randomly selects ranks, so 6 may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks.
