A splode (also written asplode or a-splode) is an Engrish comedic explosion, typically to the head. The term, especially within the context of "your head a splode", originates from the Homestar Runner series Strong Bad Email episode "video games", where Strong Bad uses the term upon defeating the theoretical player. A splosions can happen for many reasons, including cosuming specific foods and drinks, watching YouTube Poops, or dividing by zero. A splosions tend to be sudden and abrupt, meaning that anyone (even you) could a splode at any moment.
