Abbreviation

<><>An {{babbreviation}} is any shortening of a term to condense the content. Some examples include [[atomic symbols|atomic symbol]] ([[Hydrogen]] --> H), [[personal titles|personal title]] ([[Doctor]] --> Dr.), [[street names|street name]] ([[Street]] --> St.), [[Latin]] words ([[et cetera]] --> etc.), and more. [[Acronyms|acronym]] are a form of abbreviation.
