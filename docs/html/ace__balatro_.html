Ace (Balatro)

<><>{{bAce}} is the highest [[rank|ranks (Balatro)]] of [[playing cards|playing cards (Balatro)]] in {{i[[Balatro]]}} that is worth 11 [[Chips|Chips (Balatro)]] when played without [[Modifiers|Modifiers (Balatro)]] or [[Jokers|Jokers (Balatro)]]. Aces are neither [[numbered cards|Numbered Card (Balatro)]] nor [[face cards|face cards (Balatro)]]. The [[Abandoned Deck|Abandoned Deck (Balatro)]] removes all face cards, so Ace is more likely to be drawn. The [[Erratic Deck|Erratic Deck (Balatro)]] randomly selects ranks, so Aces may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks.
