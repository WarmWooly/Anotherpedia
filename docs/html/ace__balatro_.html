Ace (Balatro)

Ace is the highest rank of playing cards in Balatro that is worth 11 Chips when played without Modifiers or Jokers. Aces are neither numbered cards nor face cards. The Abandoned Deck removes all face cards, so Ace is more likely to be drawn. The Erratic Deck randomly selects ranks, so Aces may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks. The Idol gives x2 Mult when a card of a randomly selected rank and suit is played, including Aces of any suit.
