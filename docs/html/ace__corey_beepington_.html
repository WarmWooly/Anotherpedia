Ace (Corey Beepington)

<><>{{bAce}} is a [[sentient|living object]] [[Panasonic PV-M1357W]] [[character]] created by [[Corey Beepington]], introduced on [[Twitter]] and [[YouTube]] on February 23 and 24, 2025.<><> Corey's videos about Ace are known as "Aceposting."<>Ace is an off-white [[Panasonic]] [[CRT|Cathode-ray tube]] [[TV|Television]] with a blue face comprised of simple lines on its screen. He can also play media, such as [[video games|video game]], [[shows|TV show]], and [[movies|movie]] from external sources (such as a [[VHS tape]] or a [[GameCube]]) or an internal cache of various media clips to express itself. He normally cannot speak, relying on facial expressions and media clips to get his thoughts across.<>Ace can replay clips from media it has seen without having the corresponding VHS tape or external device normally needed to store the media. He can also feel objects or people touching him, hear sounds around him, view his surroundings, contact local [[emergency services|emergency service]], and perform a [[data wipe]] on himself. Ace can display his face, basic [[line art]], external sources, media clips, or text on his screen. He can see the contents of his screen, and if the contents are from VHS, he can interact with the contents and eject the VHS at will. Ace is afraid of [[violence|violence in media]], such as the early scenes of {{i[[Bambi]]}} or the game {{i[[Doom|Doom (1993 video game)]]}}.<><> Ace can talk from a VHS Corey made, but he feels that line art is a part of who he is.<> Ace is friends with Corey and lives with him, but he grows concerned when Corey is [[anxious|anxiety]], [[upset]], or [[sick]].
