Adhesive remover

Adhesive remover is a substance used to cleaning adhesive, such as glue, sticker, or adhesive tape. They dissolve or bond softening chemical bond in the adhesive, making it easier to remove the adhesive from surfaces. Different types of adhesive removers may address specific surfaces (metal, tile, wood, etc.) or specific adhesives. Popular adhesive remover brand include Goo Gone, Goof Off, Max Strip, and 3M.
