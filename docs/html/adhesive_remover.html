Adhesive remover

Adhesive remover is a substance used to remove adhesives, such as glue, stickers, or adhesive tape. They dissolve or soften bonds in the adhesive, making it easier to remove the adhesive from surfaces. Different types of adhesive removers may address specific surfaces (metal, tiles, wood, etc.) or specific adhesives. Popular adhesive remover brands include Goo Gone, Goof Off, Max Strip, and 3M.
