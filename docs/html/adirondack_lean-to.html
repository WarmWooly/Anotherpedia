Adirondack lean-to

<><>An {{bAdirondack lean-to}} (or {{bAdirondack}}) is a [[building]] with 3 [[walls|wall]], a [[roof]], and a [[floor]] almost exclusively comprised of [[wood]]. They are used on [[campsites|campsite]] to be used as [[temporary|temporary shelter]] [[shelter]] and was [[popularized]] by its use in the [[Adirondack Mountains]] in [[New York]].
