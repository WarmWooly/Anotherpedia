Administrative Branch (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Administrative Branch (AB) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who can run tests, vote on facility upgrades, arrest escaped inmates or intruders and run the facility. The AB gets an authorize tool (SCP: Devolved Revamp), which they can use only on Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp) to run tests on them with the SCPs. The AB also gets handcuffs (SCP: Devolved Revamp), which they can use on any player besides other ABs to arrest them for breaking facility or role rules. The AB get a Desert Eagle XIX (SCP: Devolved Revamp) as well which can be used for self defense from hostile intruders or rouge Class D. AB members should focus on running the facility making sure things go smooth, use the control panel to change the alarm lights, and make sure all other facility staff are doing their jobs. The AB has 5 levels, with each giving a new title. At level 3, their keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp) access also increases.<> ABs spawn in a room labeled "Administrative Branch" in Sector 1 of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The room requires a Level 3 Keycard to enter. The room has many file cabinets in it as well as a monitor on top of one, a plant in the corner of the room and two tables, one couch, and one chair. A huge stack of papers can be seen on one of the tables and lots of empty and full coffee mugs on the other, there is also a water dispenser machine at the front of the room, alongside the AB uniform. A clock and alarm code sign can be seen on the walls of the room, and two LED panel light up the room. At the back wall of the room, a viewing fence can be seen as well as a door titled "Meeting Room," and on the other side sits a big table with eight chairs and a computer at the head of the table with the AB voting options. A mini TV sits on the left and right sides of the walls, and a sign that says "We secure. We contain. We protecc." with the SCP Foundation logo on the wall. There is also a storage container in the corner of the room with a special Administrative uniform that players can wear if they own the Administrative Department or a higher level class gamepass the SCP: Devolved Classic, and two LED panels light up the room. There is also a TV at the front of the normal AB room with the following information:For Administrative Branch:

- lead and enforce the role of staff
