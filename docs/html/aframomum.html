Aframomum

{{bAframomum}} is a genus of Zingiberaceae. It can be found across Africa and islands such as Madagascar and Seychelles. Many species of Aframomum grow tall and create flowers. The fruit dagric grows from Aframomum angustifolium. Seeds from Aframomum melegueta, or the Grains of paradise, are often used as a spice in African dishes.
