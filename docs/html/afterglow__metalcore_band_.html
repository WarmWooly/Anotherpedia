Afterglow (metalcore band)

Afterglow is a English-language metalcore band from Mexico City, releasing music since 2019. The members of the band are Albano Arce on vocals, Erik Monsonís Azpíroz on guitar, Aruh on drums, and Mariano Fuentes on bass <>. They have used GenAI for visuals connected to their songs.
