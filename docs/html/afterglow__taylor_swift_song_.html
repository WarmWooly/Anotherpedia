Afterglow (Taylor Swift song)

"Afterglow" is a song for Taylor Swift's music album Lover (album) released on August 23, 2019. It was music production by Louis Bell, Frank Dukes, and Taylor Swift, music mixing by John Hanes (sound engineer) and Serban Ghenea, and music composition by Adam Feeney and Taylor Swift.
