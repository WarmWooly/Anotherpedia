For the character, see Afterglow the Ampharos.
Afterglow Ampharos (also called Afterglow or Glow) is a Canadians YouTuber, Twitch streamer, and digital artist. He was born during November. As a self-described "Pokémon (fictional species) furry (anthropomorphic animal)", he roleplaying as an Ampharos Pokésona while live stream. Notably, he switches his VTuber avatar to be glow when it becomes dark in-game. While his original content focused on Undertale, he has since branched into various indie game. His YouTube channel mostly consists of Video on demand and stream highlight.
