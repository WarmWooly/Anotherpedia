Agility Glove (Laser Fight)

The Agility Glove is a "punching" gun in Laser Fight. While it has terrible range, the main perk of the Agility Glove is the increased movement speed from 20 studs/sec to 25 studs/sec. Killing someone with the Agility Glove will award the badge Best Actor Acceptance Speech.
