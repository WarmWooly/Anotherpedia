AI Overviews

AI Overviews are a feature of the search engine Google Search powered by Google's AI chatbot Gemini that creates a summary of information based on a search as found in sites that would normally appear at the top of the search. AI Overviews are present in most general searches and open-ended queries, and there is no way to hide/disable AI Overviews from appearing in searches.
