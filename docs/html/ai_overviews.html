AI Overviews

<><>{{bAI Overviews}} are a feature of the [[search engine]] [[Google Search]] powered by [[Google's|Google]] [[AI chatbot|Artificial intelligence chatbot]] [[Gemini|Gemini (chatbot)]] that creates a [[summary]] of [[information]] based on a [[search|web search]] as found in [[sites|website]] that would normally appear at the top of the search. AI Overviews are present in most general searches and open-ended queries, and there is no way to hide/disable AI Overviews from appearing in searches.
