AI Overviews reading searches as prompts

AI Overviews reading searches as prompts is a phenomenon within Google Search's AI Overviews for which they take a search (browser) as a prompt (artificial intelligence) instead, causing the response to not match up with the expected output. Instead, it would respond like a typical AI chatbot. This especially became frequent in May 2026, where single-word searches (such as "cancel", "disregard", and "stop") would cause the AI Overview to respond back instead of bringing up content related to those terms. It would cause the response to be one to two sentences long, leaving a large block of whitespace where additional content would normally go. This type of failure would contribute to arguments against AI in search engines as it directly invalidates many common searches that users would make while also hiding human-made content through its unused whitespace.
