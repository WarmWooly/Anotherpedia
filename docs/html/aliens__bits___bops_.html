Aliens (Bits & Bops)

The aliens (also called marchers or globe trotters) are a set of three aliens in Bits & Bops, appearing within the minigames/mixtapes Globe Trotters, Fire Mixtape, and Final Mixtape. They are seen marching along and jumping between planets. The aliens have green, oval-shaped bodies with large eyes and two pairs of legs. The legs are extremely thin, connecting to round feet. While the bottom legs are used for walking, the top legs appear like antennae, and vice versa. The aliens swap between the pairs of legs when jumping between planets. During the Fire Mixtape, the aliens are bright yellow with black eyes and cyan pupils.
