Aliensrock vs. Samet the Turk

Aliensrock vs. Samet the Turk was a 5D Chess with Multiverse Time-Travel game, in which Samet the Turk beat Aliensrock after he forfeit (chess) when Samet played a Jurassic Rook on turn 24. Samet played as white (chess), and Aliensrock played as black (chess). Aliensrock did not know he was playing against Samet because he was stream snipe. The following is done in 5DPGN.
