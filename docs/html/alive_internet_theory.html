Alive Internet theory

For other terms relating to alive internet theory, see alive internet theory (disambiguation).
The alive Internet theory is slang counteracting the term "dead Internet theory" for content made online that is considered to be human uniqueness, for which it can't be made by a Internet bot or generative artificial intelligence. It tends to be used on social media as a form of compliment or commentary on the uniqueness of the content another person made.
