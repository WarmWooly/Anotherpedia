All I Want for Christmas Is You

"All I Want for Christmas Is You" is a Christmas song by Walter Afanasieff and Mariah Carey in 1994 for the album Merry Christmas, with Mariah Carey as the singer. It has since become widely popular, especially during the holiday season in the United States.
