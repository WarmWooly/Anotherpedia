{{iAmigurumi}}

<><>{{b{{iAmigurumi}}}} is the [[Japanese]] art of creating small [[stuffed toys|stuffed toy]] through [[crocheting|crochet]] or [[knitting]]. {{iAmigurumi}} tend to be smaller in size and focus on hiding [[stuffing|polyester fiberfill]] and avoiding visible [[seams|seam]]. Works made through {{iamigurumi}} include [[people|doll]], [[animals|animal]], [[characters|character]], and [[objects|object]]. Within crocheting, pieces are typically made in [[rounds|round (crochet)]], with multiple pieces [[sewn|sewing (crochet)]] together to form a completed {{iamigurumi}}.
