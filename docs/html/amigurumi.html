Amigurumi

{{b{{iAmigurumi}}}} is the Japanese art of creating small stuffed toy through crochet or knitting. {{iAmigurumi}} tend to be smaller in size and focus on hiding polyester fiberfill and avoiding visible seam. Within crocheting, pieces are typically made in round (crochet), with multiple pieces sewing (crochet) together to form a completed {{iamigurumi}}.
