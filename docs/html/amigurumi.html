Amigurumi

{{iAmigurumi}} is the Japanese art of creating small stuffed toy through crochet or knitting. Amigurumi tend to be smaller in size and focus on hiding polyester fiberfill and avoiding visible seam. Works made through amigurumi include doll, animal, character, and object. Within crocheting, pieces are typically made in round (crochet), with multiple pieces sewing (crochet) together to form a completed amigurumi.
