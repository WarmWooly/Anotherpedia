Animation

Animation is the illusion of movement caused by quickly changing Image, usually Drawing or Photo of Object. There are a wide variety of animation types. Here are a few:

Traditional Animation - Animation created by drawn images.
Computer Generated Imagry - Known by the acronym CGI, it is animation made by displaying Model at different positions.
Stop Motion Animation - Animation created by photos of physical objects. Claymation, puppet animation, and cutout animation are some common forms of stop motion.
Rotoscoping - Animation created by drawing over live-action footage.
