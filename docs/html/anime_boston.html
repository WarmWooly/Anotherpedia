Anime Boston

Anime Boston is an annual anime convention held in the Spring (season) in Boston, Massachusetts, USA under the New England Anime Society. The first Anime Boston was hosted in Anime Boston 2003, which is still running <>, with the latest one being Anime Boston 2025. Anime Bostons 2020 and 2021 were cancelled due to the Coronavirus pandemic, reopening in Anime Boston 2022. Participants are often wearing cosplay of character from anime, video game, and other media. Photoshoot, show preview, gameshow, convention panel, fan work (especially anime music video), craft along, and fan interview of those in the anime industry are held during the convention. Other locations, such as a dealer's room, artist's gallery, signature booth, and areas to play tabletop game and video games, are also present at Anime Boston.
