Anno is an original character of a flow worm (fictional species) from Bits & Bops created by Warm_Wooly on March 20, 2026 and uploaded to Anotherpedia the following day. Anno is a play on Wikipedia's Wikipede while representing how popular the page on flow worms had been on Anotherpedia at the time of creation. He is currently used on Anotherpedia's original character, character reference sheet, two-frame animation, and spinning object meme pages as examples. For more images, see Gallery of Anno (Anotherpedia).
Anno matches the appearance of flow worms in all but color, which is based on Anotherpedia's logo. It's a coincidence that he matches Anotherpedia's colors and that he likes Anotherpedia. I don't know whether he can glow or not. I haven't asked him. He just likes reading the various page (Anotherpedia) on Anotherpedia. He's probably reading this right now! He doesn't like to how to make/edit pages, but he swears on his antennae that he will eventually.

Anno is always moving, at least a bit, every 0.3 seconds. He can't help it; it's just how he's animation. He hasn't played Bits & Bops ironically, but he "likes the design of the characters" or something. I don't know if it's a meta commentary on original characters or if he's just messing with me. Anno is glad that flow worms (the species) are more popular than flow worm (the fishing lure), but he's worried that an angler will accidentally confuse him for the lure.

Anno hasn't met Wikipede because Anno doesn't have a passport to travel to the United Kingdom. He's never been mistaken for an ant, but rather as a cat and a donkey, surprisingly. He's afraid to go to Canada (I don't have the faintest clue).
