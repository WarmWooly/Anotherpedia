Another Pedia (article)

"Another Pedia" is an article on jennifermarohasy.com, written by Jennifer Marohasy on April 11, 2007, which can be <>. The article covers Jennifer's experience reading an article titled "Wikipedia references a source of anxiety" in The Australian. She first gives a short synopsis of the article, covering the ban in a college in Vermont of Wikipedia as a source and Jimmy Wales' (the founder of Wikipedia) negative outlook on citing encyclopedias. She then mentions that she was given a note by David Tribe about Citizendium, then links to the article "Citizendium aims to be better Wikipedia".
