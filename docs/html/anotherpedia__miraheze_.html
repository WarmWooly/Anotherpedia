Anotherpedia (Miraheze)

Anotherpedia, translated from アナザーぺディア, also called The United Republic of Anotherpedia, is a Japanese satirical online encyclopedia and a fictional nation with the express purpose of freeing people from Wikipedia, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of another and encyclopedia. Anotherpedia is hosted on Miraheze and is made to contain articles related to parallel worlds. Anotherpedia was made in the spring of 2022. It has a sister project, AnotherDictionary.

Anotherpedia is in a pact known as the "Pact of Hiyokograd Extreme-War" with Uncyclopedia. Anotherpedia's goal is to kill Wikipedia.
