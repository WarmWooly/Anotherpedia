Anotherpedia (Miraheze)

<><><>{{bAnotherpedia}}, [[translated|translation]] from {{bアナザーぺディア}}, also called {{bThe United Republic of Anotherpedia}}, is a [[Japanese]] [[satirical]] [[online encyclopedia]] and a [[fictional|fiction]] [[nation]] with the express purpose of freeing people from [[Wikipedia]], which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of {{i[[another]]}} and {{i[[encyclopedia]]}}. Anotherpedia is hosted on [[Miraheze]] and is made to contain articles related to [[parallel worlds|parallel world]]. Anotherpedia was made in the [[spring|spring (season)]] of 2022. It has a [[sister project]], [[AnotherDictionary]].&pAnotherpedia is in a [[pact]] known as the "Pact of Hiyokograd Extreme-War" with [[Uncyclopedia]]. Anotherpedia's [[goal]] is to kill Wikipedia.
