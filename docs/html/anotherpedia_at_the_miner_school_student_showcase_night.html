Hello, and welcome to Anotherpedia, an open and goofy online encyclopedia! If you're here, you're most likely attending the Miner School Student Showcase Night (or reading this page early). This website was created by Warm_Wooly on August 28th, 2023, as a passion project for 2.5 year. Unfortunately, I am not able to present Anotherpedia as I have a midterm to attend to (very sad). I won't be able to fully see the other projects myself, so if there are college student making the cure for George Washington's skin cancer, then vote for them. Otherwise, I'd be glad to get votes (or, better yet, join the <>)!

Curios on how this project works? Look at the "Internals" section of the Anotherpedia page. This website is dedicated to covering as many random topics, including &randomPage! This website has over 2,100 pages and is growing all the time! It's fully built from the ground up, mobile-first, and does not use generative AI for page content. All ideas for the website are from me, the Anotherpedia community, and from early Wikipedia.

Feel free to search random topics (search bar/magnifying glass button), see a random page ("Random Page"/dice button), click on links, or just leave the website (I won't mind). If you're interested in this website, that's great! Want to contribute? Even better! Don't care for it? That's alright too. Vote for cool, fun projects that aren't "I made an AI do this thing", like indie games or other silly projects. Not every hobby needs to change the world, so use this as an example to get started on your own passion project.

Heads up for anyone trying to contact me during or immediately after the event: I'm first completing a midterm, then heading to Anime Boston and won't be able to immediately respond to how to make/edit pages, but I'd be stoked to see new friendly faces interested in this project!
