Anotherpedia.txt

Anotherpedia.txt was a sister website to Anotherpedia created on February 29th, 2024, made for text-sharing website, specifically Page (Anotherpedia) on Anotherpedia. It had a built-in button when How to make/edit pages to open https://anotherpedia-txt.glitch.me/ and created a hyperlink storing text through GitHub. It also automatically took pages saved to the site and redirected to Anotherpedia with the edited content. Due to the Shutdown of Glitch of Glitch (IDE) on June 8, 2025, Anotherpedia.txt is no longer accessible.&pThe logo followed the design of Anothepedia's three cylinder, now with the colors pink, gray, and yellow, representing the eraser, ferrule, and body of a pencil.
