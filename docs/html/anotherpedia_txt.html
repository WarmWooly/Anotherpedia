Anotherpedia.txt

<><>{{bAnotherpedia.txt}} was a [[sister website]] to [[Anotherpedia]] created on February 29th, 2024, made for [[text sharing|text-sharing website]], specifically [[pages|Page (Anotherpedia)]] on Anotherpedia. It had a built-in button when [[editing|How to make/edit pages]] to open https://anotherpedia-txt.glitch.me/ and created a [[link|hyperlink]] storing text through [[GitHub]]. It also automatically took pages saved to the site and redirected to Anotherpedia with the edited content. Due to the [[shutdown|Shutdown of Glitch]] of [[Glitch|Glitch (IDE)]] on June 8, 2025, Anotherpedia.txt is no longer accessible.&p<>The [[logo]] followed the design of Anothepedia's three [[cylinders|cylinder]], now with the colors pink, gray, and yellow, representing the [[eraser]], [[ferrule]], and body of a [[pencil]].
