Ante meridiem

<><>{{bAnte meridiem}} is a [[Latin]] term, [[abbreviated|abbreviation]] as {{bAM}} or {{ba.m.}}, that means "before [[midday]]". It is used when representing the [[time]] of a [[day]] before noon and after [[midnight]] on a [[12-hour clock]], such as 8:30 AM.
