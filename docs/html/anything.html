Anything

Anything is an indefinite pronoun containing any and thing. Anything is used to describe one or multiple objects, regardless of what it is. This can be narrowed down using adjectives, such as "anything sticky" or "anything green." Anything can act as a plural for something. Grab anything you need in the room.

Do you want anything to eat?

Is there anything interesting in this museum?
