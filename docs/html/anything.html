Anything

<><>{{bAnything}} is an [[indefinite pronoun]] containing [[any]] and [[thing]]. Anything is used to describe [[one]] or [[multiple]] [[objects|object]], regardless of what it is. This can be narrowed down using [[adjectives|adjective]], such as "anything sticky" or "anything green." Anything can act as a [[plural]] for [[something]].<><>
