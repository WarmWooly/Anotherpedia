Apple Jam (Boiled)

<>Apple jam is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Apple jam can be obtained by boiling an apple (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 experience (Minecraft). Apple jam is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Apple Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled) - 1 apple jam and 1 bread (Minecraft) (shapeless). Apple jam can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
