Apple Jam (Boiled)

Apple jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Apple jam can be obtained by boiling an apple in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Apple jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Apple Jam-Filled Bread - 1 apple jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Apple jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
