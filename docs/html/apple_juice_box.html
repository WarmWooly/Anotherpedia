Apple juice box

An apple juice box is a juice box that contains apple juice. They are typically filled with between 4 to 8 fl oz of juice and come with a plastic straw used to break an aluminum foil seal to drink the juice. They are catered towards children from preschool to middle school as a light drink. Apple juice stored in boxes made of paper with a thin layer of polyethylene (plastic). Some of the largest brands that sell apple juice boxes are Mott's, Apple & Eve, Juicy Juice, Honest Kids, and SunCup.
