Appletun

Appletun is an apple pie-like grass type (pokémon) and dragon type (pokémon) Pokémon (fictional species) from the Generation VIII (Pokémon). Appletun has a large, green body which transitions to tan towards the top. Its underbelly is also tan, with a darkened heart-like patch. It has pairs of three-toed front and hind legs, a tail, and a long neck to its head. Appletun's head it relatively flat, with an apple hat and a pair of flappy eyes that are shaped like ears. On its back, a raised hashtag-shaped pattern lies atop, with a pair of leaf-like wings. The outside of the wings is red, with green/yellow on the inside.

Appletun's official size is 1 foot (unit) and 4 inch, while weighing 28.7 pound. shiny (pokémon) Appletuns have a green hat instead of red, and the outside/inside colors of the wings are swapped.

Appletun is likely a portmanteau of apple and ton (altered spelling). Appletun produces a sweet, sticky nectar on its body, which attracts Bug type (pokémon) Pokémon its feeds on. It also eats sweet apples. Lechonk is known to try and feed on Appletun. An Appletun has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Appletun is the 842nd Pokémon in the Pokédex (Pokémon) and has the Pokédex color green.

Appletun evolution (pokémon) from an Applin eating a Sweet Apple (pokémon). Appletun has a Gigantamax form, the same as Flapple's. It has a large red apple base with a spiky tan bottom and an orange, syrupy top. A large tail wraps from behind, with Appletun's neck extending upwards with its eyes raised through an apple hat. Its shiny form has a green apple instead of red.
