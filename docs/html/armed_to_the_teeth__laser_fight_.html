Armed To The Teeth (Laser Fight)

Armed To The Teeth is a mode (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game) where players can equip up to 9 gun (Laser Fight), up from the normal 3. Guns will also have an increased gun spawning (Laser Fight). Players will spawn in with the Basic Gun (Laser Fight). When trying to equip more than 9 guns, the player will be awarded the badge (Roblox) 2nd Amendment Rights (Laser Fight).
