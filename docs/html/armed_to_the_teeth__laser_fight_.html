Armed To The Teeth (Laser Fight)

Armed To The Teeth is a mode in Laser Fight where players can equip up to 9 guns, up from the normal 3. Guns will also have an increased spawn rate. Players will spawn in with the Basic Gun. When trying to equip more than 9 guns, the player will be awarded the badge 2nd Amendment Rights.
