Armor Vest (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The armor vest is an equippable item within SCP: Devolved Revamp located within the Armory (SCP: Devolved Revamp). It can be Site Investments (SCP: Devolved Revamp) by the Administrative Branch (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and on equipping, it gives a player (Roblox) an additional 25 max HP while fully restoring their health. It can't be equipped if the player has over 100 health or if its not funded.
