Armory (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Armory is a room in the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp that is used to store weapons and armor for combative Facility staff. The room is located in Sector 2 of the Facility, labeled "Armory". The room requires a Level 2 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp) to enter. It is also the location of the armor vest (SCP: Devolved Revamp).

The room is filled with storage crates, all containing various Guns (SCP: Devolved Revamp) and one containing a special Intelligence Agency uniform that can be worn if the Player (Roblox) had previously owned the Intelligence Agency gamepass from SCP: Devolved Classic. In the room, a vent can be seen on the ground, and on the ceiling, the room is lit up by two LED panel and one wall light above the door. On the walls of the room, there are three boards containing Site Security (SCP: Devolved Revamp) weapons, which are the following: M9 Baretta (SCP: Devolved Revamp), FN P90 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), Kriss Vector (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and HK MP5KSD (SCP: Devolved Revamp).
