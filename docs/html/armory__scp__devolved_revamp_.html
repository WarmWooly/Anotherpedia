Armory (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Armory is a room in the Facility in SCP: Devolved Revamp that is used to store weapons and armor for combative Facility staff. The room is located in Sector 2 of the Facility, labeled "Armory". The room requires a Level 2 Keycard to enter. It is also the location of the armor vests.

The room is filled with storage crates, all containing various guns and one containing a special Intelligence Agency uniform that can be worn if the player had previously owned the Intelligence Agency gamepass from SCP: Devolved Classic. In the room, a vent can be seen on the ground, and on the ceiling, the room is lit up by two LED panels and one wall light above the door. On the walls of the room, there are three boards containing Security weapons, which are the following: M9 Baretta, FN P90, Kriss Vector, and HK MP5KSD.
