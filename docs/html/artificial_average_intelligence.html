Artificial Average Intelligence

"Artificial Average Intelligence" is a song created by Nate Ziller for his YouTube video "AI Knowledge (video)" on April 21, 2026, where AI (Nate Ziller) (based on generative AI) sings about his creation. It shows how Artificial intelligence researcher repeatedly try to get some form of new knowledge that is intelligent, with AI giving back average responses. In the end, the researchers focus on the profitability of AI instead of the quality of its outputs. [AI]
