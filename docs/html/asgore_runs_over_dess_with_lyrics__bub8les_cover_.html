Asgore Runs Over Dess With Lyrics (Bub8les cover)

"Asgore Runs Over Dess With Lyrics" is a cover version of KennyTheLyr1c1st's song by the same name by Bub8les, which was uploaded to YouTube on July 4, 2025. The lyrics and audio mixing were by KennyTheLyr1c1st, with Bub8les covering the vocals of Asgore and Ragamuffin performing Dess' scream. The music used is "ASGORE - trap remix w/ mythic apex" by Zeb and Mythic, which is a trap remix of "ASGORE" by Toby Fox.
