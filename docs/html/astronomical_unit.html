Astronomical unit

<><>An {{bastronomical unit}}, [[abbreviated]] {{bAU}}, is a [[measurement]] of [[distance]] that is roughly the average distance [[Earth]] is from the [[Sun]], or 149,597,870,700 [[meters|meter]] (92,955,807.27 [[miles|mile]]) as defined by the [[IAU|International Astronomical Union]]. It is often used for measuring distances within the [[Solar System]].
