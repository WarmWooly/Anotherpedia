Attack of the Roombas

{{iAttack of the Roombas}}, or {{iAotR}}, is a simple shoot 'em up by Warm_Wooly made in GameMaker, first being available on October 2021 on itch.io.<> The game follows the player, covered in mud, fending off various Roombas using rubber balls. The goal is to last as long as possible against the waves of Roombas. The player spawns in an empty room, with Roombas slowly spawning around the room. The player must shoot at the Roombas to "clog" them, with each Roomba having a health bar. If the player comes into contact with a Roomba, they instantly lose.
