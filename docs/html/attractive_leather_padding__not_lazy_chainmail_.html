Attractive Leather Padding ({{iNot Lazy Chainmail}})

<><>||{{bStats}}||{{b[[Rarity|Rarity (Minecraft)]]}}|Common||{{b[[Stack size|Stack size (Minecraft)]]}}|64||{{b[[Fire/lava&spresistance|Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)]]}}|Noinfo>>{{bAttractive leather padding}} is an [[item|item (Minecraft)]] in the {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|mod (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Not Lazy Chainmail]]}} as an upgrade to the [[leather padding|leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. Attractive leather padding is used to upgrade [[chainmail armor|chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] or [[shields|shield (Minecraft)]] in a [[leathersmithing table|leathersmithing table (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], giving +0.5 Attraction. Attraction allows the player to pick up items further while [[sneaking|sneaking (Minecraft)]].<><>Attractive leather padding can be melded in a [[Melder|Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] using 1 [[leather padding|leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] and 1 [[telemagnetic alloy|telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail)]].<>Expert-level [[leatherworkers|leatherworker (Minecraft)]] have a 50% chance to trade 1 [[leather padding|leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] and 6 [[emeralds|emerald (Minecraft)]] for 1 attractive leather padding.<><>In a [[leathersmithing table|leathersmithing table (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], attractive leather padding can be applied to [[chainmail armor|chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] or [[shields|shield (Minecraft)]]. Each attractive leather padding adds +0.5 Attraction, with a full set of armor with 12 attractive leather paddings giving +6 Attraction. Shields will only add Attraction when held in the [[main hand|main hand (Minecraft)]] or [[offhand|offhand (Minecraft)]].<>While [[sneaking|sneaking (Minecraft)]] with Attraction, items within a cube radius of the player will be teleported to the player. The minimum distance for item attraction is 2.5 blocks. To calculate the range, do 2 + {{ix}}, where {{ix}} is the total Attractive amount on a player. The maximum radius of attraction, without a shield, is 8 blocks. With a shield, it is 9.5 blocks.<>{{gal<>}}
