Aurora Firework Amulet (Bits & Blocks)

The aurora firework amulet is a necklace curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that heals and applies buffs upon taking damage from a firework rocket, while causing mobs hit by firework attacks to recieve additional debuffs. The aurora firework amulet can be crafted with 1 firework amulet and 1 Ocean Mixtape (shapeless). When the aurora firework amulet is worn in a necklace slot, taking damage from a firework will apply Instant Health for a tick and Resistance II for 10 seconds. Mobs hit by fireworks launched by the player (either placed directly or from a crossbow) will also receive Slowness II for 10 seconds and Blindness for 5 seconds. If the attacked mob is a player wearing an aurora firework amulet, the effects do not apply.
