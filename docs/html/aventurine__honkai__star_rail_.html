Aventurine (Honkai: Star Rail)

Aventurine is a character released on April 17th, 2024, in the gacha game Honkai: Star Rail, sharing the same name as the quartzite aventurine. Aventurine has a rarity of ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐, is under the path of Preservation, and has a combat type of Imaginary. Aventurine can be obtained via Guilded Imprisonment. Aventurine, real name of Kakavasha, is an Avgin from Sigonia-IV. He has a mother, a father, and a sister. Aventurine is a part of the Interastral Peace Corporation under the Strategic Investment Department and is a member of the Ten Stonehearts. Aventurine is considered a "risk-taker," wagering with his own life. He is almost always smiling, making it difficult to read his emotions. He used to work with Topaz in the SID before Topaz got a demotion. When Aventurine got a project from their boss Diamond involving Penacony, he attempts to get Topaz to be the project manager.
