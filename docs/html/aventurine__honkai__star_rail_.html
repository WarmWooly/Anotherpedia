Aventurine (Honkai: Star Rail)

Aventurine is a character released on April 17th, 2024, in the gacha game Honkai: Star Rail, sharing the same name as the quartzite aventurine. Aventurine has a rarity (Honkai: Star Rail) of ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐, is under the path (Honkai: Star Rail) of Preservation (Honkai: Star Rail), and has a type (Honkai: Star Rail) of Imaginary (Honkai: Star Rail). Aventurine can be obtained via Guilded Imprisonment (Honkai: Star Rail). Aventurine, real name of Kakavasha, is an Avgin (Honkai: Star Rail) from Sigonia-IV (Honkai: Star Rail). He has a mother, a father, and a sister. Aventurine is a part of the Interastral Peace Corporation (Honkai: Star Rail) under the Strategic Investment Department (Honkai: Star Rail) and is a member of the Ten Stonehearts (Honkai: Star Rail). Aventurine is considered a "risk-taker," wagering with his own life. He is almost always smile, making it difficult to read his emotion. He used to work with Topaz (Honkai: Star Rail) in the SID before Topaz got a demotion. When Aventurine got a project from their boss Diamond (Honkai: Star Rail) involving Penacony (Honkai: Star Rail), he attempts to get Topaz to be the project manager.
