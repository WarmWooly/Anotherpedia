B-Bot and the Fly Girls (Bits & Blocks)

B-Bot and the Fly Girls is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. B-Bot and the Fly Girls can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 gold ingot, and 1 redstone comparator (shapeless). B-Bot and the Fly Girls is used in the following crafting recipes:
Dancing Antenna - 1 B-Bot and the Fly Girls and 1 redstone torch (shapeless).
Sky Mixtape - 1 B-Bot and the Fly Girls, 1 mixtape case, 1 Flow Worms, 1 Meet & Tweet, and 1 Steady Bears (shapeless).
