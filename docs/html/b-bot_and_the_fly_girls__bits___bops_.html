B-Bot and the Fly Girls (Bits & Bops)

B-Bot and the Fly Girls is the fifth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is B-Bot dancing alongside the Fly Girls. The minigame has B-Bot dancing with the Fly Girls, with the player following lead-up signals to determine the next move. The player must hit Button 1 four times after the Fly Girls clap three times. After two record scratches, the player must press Button 2 to start spinning, releasing it two beats later. During the level, the poses that B-Bot and the Fly Girls change. The lighting also changes, with multiple times where the characters are only silhouettes.

If the player is on time, a white sparkle will appear at B-Bot's hand or foot. When starting a spin on time, a thick outline will appear on the ground. If they are slightly off, no effects will appear. Being really off will cause B-Bot to sweat with a yellow screen. Being completely off will cause B-Bot to be completely discombobulated with a red screen. If there is no input, B-Bot will sweat with a disappointing noise playing.
