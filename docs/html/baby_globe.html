Baby Globe

Baby Globe is a Mascots for Wikipedia for Wikipedia during its Wikipedia's 25th anniversary, primarily appearing in an experimental feature on Wikipedia from February 23 to April 7, 2026. Baby Globe was also used for Wikipedia's 25th Birthday Reading Challenge. It takes on the appearance of Logos of Wikipedia personification. The original idea for Baby Globe and character design was made by the Wikimedian BaduFerreira. Baby Globe's design would be refined by Sharon Park, Axel Kinnear, and Bex McNally for the Wikimedia Foundation, with its name coming from the Wikipedia community. It would also be turned into a stuffed toy on Makeship until July 17, 2026. For more images, see Gallery of Baby Globe.
Baby Globe is based on the logo for Wikipedia, being an incomplete white sphere made out of jigsaw puzzle with various glyph. Unlike the logo, multiple squares are blank, with most of the pieces being square. It has pairs of blue arms and legs with rounded hands and feet, respectively. In its front is a mouth and a pair of large eyes. Inside Baby Globe is shown to be purely black. Baby Globe does not have a specified age or gender.
