Baby YouTube thumbnail

A Baby YouTube thumbnail is a meme format depicting a baby posing as a YouTuber based on a thumbnail on YouTube. The format is particularly popular on Tumblr and Twitter. The images feature some topic that relates to babies (like baby food or toys) in a similar style to YouTube thumbnails. It often involves clickbait and may be based on a specific YouTuber. The title of the video and channel may also be included to add to the veneer of a baby posting to YouTube.
