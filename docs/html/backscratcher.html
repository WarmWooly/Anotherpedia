Backscratcher

<><>A {{bbackscratcher}} (also written as {{bback scratcher}}) is a [[tool]] used to reach [[itches|itch]] on the [[body|human body]], especially along the [[back|human back]], that would be inaccessible by hands. A backscratcher tends to be a pole or rod with an end that has several contact points to scratch an itch. Backscratchers are made of various [[materials|material]], including [[wood]], [[metal]], and [[plastic]].
