Ball Roller (Laser Fight)

The Ball Roller is a rolling orb-launching gun in Laser Fight. The balls that the Ball Roller shoots are relatively fast and large, punishing those who stay on flat ground. However, the tradeoff is the inability to hit targets above or significantly below the user.
