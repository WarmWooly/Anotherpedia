Bar chart

<><>A {{bbar chart}}, {{bbar graph}}, or {{bcolumn chart}} is a [[chart]] that displays [[data]] in the form of [[bars|bar]], with the [[size]] of the bar showing the proportional [[value]] of what they represent. Bar charts can be [[horizontal]] or [[vertical]], with one [[axis]] that displays what is being quantified, and the other used to show the values represented by the bars. Some bar charts may be clustered, where they have multiple bars under different categories per element, which are generally [[color-coded|color code]]. They may also be stacked, where multiple sources contribute to a whole bar and are colored depending on the source.
