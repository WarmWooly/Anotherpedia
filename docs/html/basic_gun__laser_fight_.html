Basic Gun (Laser Fight)

<>The Basic Gun is the starting gun in Laser Fight. With a mediocre fire rate and no special effect, the Basic Gun is the most rudimentary gun to use and is often swapped out with other, more advanced options. The Rapid-Fire Gun is a direct upgrade to the Basic Gun. When spawning in the modes Normal, Gun Rain, Infinite Stamina, Trigger Happy, or Armed To The Teeth, the player spawns in with a Basic Gun. Players start with the Basic Gun in the mode Arsenal and can obtain the Basic Gun in all modes.
