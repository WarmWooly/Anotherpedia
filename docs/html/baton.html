Baton

For other uses for the term baton, see baton (disambiguation).&spA baton, also known as a nightstick, is a short blunt melee weapon which is made of wood, metal, rubber, or plastic, used by law enforcement, prison officers, security guards, and others for self-defense and for correction. Batons can block attacks, jab, bludgeon, and strike attackers, as well as be used to break windows.
