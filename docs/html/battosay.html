Battosay

Battosay is a French former YouTuber (<>) who played a variety of video game, prominently the Minecraft total conversion mod Better Than Wolves. Battosay's content also included Battlefield 3 and Call of Duty in short bursts. All publicly available videos on his channel are from 2011 to 2012, with no videos since then. Battosay is currently a Discord moderator for the Better Than Wolves Discord (application) server and the most active contributor on the Better Than Wolves Wiki <>.
