Bedrock Shard (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Bedrock shards are an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for creating iron composite and pseduorock. When loose bedrock is mined with a diamond pickaxe or better, it will drop 1 bedrock shard. Fortune does not affect shard drops. Loose bedrock can be found below y-level -54, replacing bedrock. Loose bedrock can be obtained when a Crusher crushes pseudorock (makes 3 shards). When temporal sand is sieved in an iron composite sieve mesh, there is a 1% chance to drop a bedrock shard. Separation can increase the chance/drops of bedrock shards from sieving. Bedrock shards are used in the following melding recipes:
Iron Composite Ingot - 1 bedrock shard, 1 iron ingot, and 1 chromatic dust
Pseudorock - 3 bedrock shards
