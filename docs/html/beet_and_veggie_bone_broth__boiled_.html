Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled)

Beet and veggie bone broth is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Beet and veggie bone broth can be crafted with 2 boiled beetroots, 1 boiled carrot/potato, and 1 bone broth (shapeless). Beet and veggie bone broth can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. A bone-filled bowl is returned to the player after eating for reuse.
