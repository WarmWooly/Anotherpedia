Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled)

<>Beet and veggie bone broth is a food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Beet and veggie bone broth can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 boiled beetroot (Boiled), 1 boiled carrot (Boiled)/boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless). Beet and veggie bone broth can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. A bone-filled bowl (Boiled) is returned to the player after eating for reuse.
