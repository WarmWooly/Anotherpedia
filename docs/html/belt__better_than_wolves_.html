Belt (Better Than Wolves)

<resistance|No||Buoyancy|Floatsinfo>>The belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Better Than Wolves used in crafting the saw and the bellows. Straps can be crafted with 4 straps (place in a circle). Belts are used in the following crafting recipes:
Bellows - 1 belt, 2 gears, 3 tanned leather/cut tanned leather, and 3 wood sidings (place the leather in the middle row, the sidings on the top row, the gears in the bottom corners, and the belt in the middle bottom slot).
Saw - 1 belt, 3 gears, 3 iron ingots, and 2 wood planks/sidings (place the iron in the top row, the belt in the middle slot, the gears to the left/right/bottom of the belt, and the wood in the bottom corners). In a stoked cauldron, 2 belts can be processed into 1 glue.
