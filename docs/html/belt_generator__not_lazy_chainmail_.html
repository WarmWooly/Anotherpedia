Belt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The belt generator is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to turn industrial belt power into Forge Energy (FE). FE is not used in any blocks/items in Not Lazy Chainmail, but rather made for cross-compatibility with mods that do use FE or other power systems. The belt generator can be crafted with 2 empty spools, 1 crude engine, and 6 cobblestone (place the engine in the center, the spools to the left and right, and the cobblestone in the remaining rows). The belt generator requires an industrial belt to run, with better belts increasing the power generated. A toggle can allow up to 64 FE/t to be dispersed to any adjacent blocks.
