Belt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The belt generator is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to turn industrial belt power into Forge Energy (FE). FE is not used in any blocks/items in Not Lazy Chainmail, but rather made for cross-compatibility with mods that do use FE or other power systems. The belt generator can be crafted with 6 cobblestone and 2 iron ingots (fill the top and bottom rows with cobblestone, put iron in the left and right slots).
