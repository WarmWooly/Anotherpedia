Belt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The belt generator is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to turn industrial belts (Not Lazy Chainmail) power into Forge Energy (Minecraft modding) (FE). FE is not used in any blocks/items in Not Lazy Chainmail, but rather made for cross-compatibility with mods that do use FE or other power systems. The belt generator can be crafting (Minecraft) with 6 cobblestone (Minecraft) and 2 iron ingot (Minecraft) (fill the top and bottom rows with cobblestone, put iron in the left and right slots).
