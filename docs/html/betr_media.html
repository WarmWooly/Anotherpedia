Betr Media

Not to be confused with the digital marketing company Betrmedia Inc..
Betr Media is a media company and creator network under Betr Holdings, Inc. that specializes in podcasts, lifestyle media, and sports. Some of the most notable partnerships with Betr Media include Jake Paul for BS with Jake Paul, Haliey Welch for Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch, and Bo Nickal's YouTube channel.
