Bismillah Song

For the YouTube channel, see Bismillah Song (YouTube channel).&spThe Bismillah Song, officially titled "(Official) MV BISMILLAH (2013 Edition - ENGLISH)", is a song produced by PixelGraf Artists and Famille Musulmane (which translates to Muslim Family) on October 30, 2013. It is comprised of Arabic, French and English lyrics, where it goes over the importance of prayer to Allah by starting with bismillah.
