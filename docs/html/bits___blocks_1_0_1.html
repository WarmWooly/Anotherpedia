Bits & Blocks 1.0.1

{{iBits & Blocks 1.0.1}} is a day one patch to the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks released on July 23, 2026, which fixed bugs and an incorrect texture. This update comes the day following its release. These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;The Beach Ball, Mangrove Apple, and Party Ball will stop healing themselves when their health is 0 and drop themselves on death.|Fixed the Beach Ball, Mangrove Apple, and Party Ball not despawning and dropping themselves on cramming/suffocating.|Updated the texture for the Beach Ball entity to match the item.|The Tooth Necklace, Parading Legs, and Exotic Parading Legs no longer trigger when interacting with any blocks.|The Tooth Necklace and Ripe Tooth Necklace no longer double saturation.;;
