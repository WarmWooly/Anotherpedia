Black Market ({{iLaser Fight}})

<><>{{bBlack Market}} is a [[kill streak|kill streak (Laser Fight)]] in {{i[[Laser Fight|Laser Fight (Roblox game)]]}} that gives [[exp|experience (Laser Fight)]] for your [[gun|gun (Laser Fight)]]. The gun to be sold must be held. Most guns have their sell value calculated as {{code[[spawn time|gun spawning (Laser Fight)]] * (5/6)}}, rounded to the nearest interval of 5. The [[Basic Gun|Basic Gun (Laser Fight)]] has a sell value of 25 exp. Higher levels give more exp per gun. This streak only awards 1/5th the exp in the [[Gun Rain|Gun Rain (Laser Fight)]] [[mode|mode (Laser Fight)]]. This streak is disabled in the [[Starting Weapons|Starting Weapons (Laser Fight)]], [[Arsenal|Arsenal (Laser Fight)]], and [[One Gun Run|One Gun Run (Laser Fight)]] modes.<>
