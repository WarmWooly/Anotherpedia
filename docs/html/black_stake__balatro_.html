Black Stake (Balatro)

Black Stake is the fourth available Stakes (Balatro) in Balatro, where: ;;Jokers (Balatro) have a 30% chance of having an Eternal Sticker (Balatro) (can't be sold or destroyed)|Blind (Balatro) have faster scaling per Ante (Balatro) (requiring more chips to win)|Small Blind (Balatro) will not award any money;; The White Stake (Balatro), Red Stake (Balatro), and Green Stake (Balatro) must be defeated before unlocking the Black Stake. Upon defeating a Deck with the Black Stake, the Blue Stake (Balatro) will become unlocked, alongside the Anaglyph Deck (Balatro).
