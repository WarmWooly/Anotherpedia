Blaze Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Blaze bread is food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. blaze flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce blaze bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft). Blaze bread can be used in a breadmaking station (Bread Reborn: Redefined) to make a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined), adding its stats to the platter. Blaze bread can be eaten as food (Minecraft). As it has effects, blaze bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
