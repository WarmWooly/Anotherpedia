Blaze Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Blaze flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Blaze flour can be baked into blaze bread. Blaze flour can be obtained by milling blaze rods on a millstone. Blaze flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce blaze bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience.
