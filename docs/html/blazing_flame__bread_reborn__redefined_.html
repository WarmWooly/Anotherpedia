Blazing Flame (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Blazing flame is an effect in Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined that allows the player to launch fireballs (default keybind: R). The source of blazing flame is from eating blaze bread or a bread platter containing such. Each time a fireball is launched, 5 seconds are removed from the duration of the effect.
