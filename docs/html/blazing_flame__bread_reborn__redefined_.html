Blazing Flame (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Blazing flame is an effect (Minecraft) in Minecraft modding (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined that allows the player to launch small fireball (Minecraft) (default keybind: codeR). The source of blazing flame is from eating blaze bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) or a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined) containing such. Each time a fireball is launched, 5 seconds are removed from the duration of the effect.
