Blazing Trotting Antennae (Bits & Blocks)

Blazing trotting antennae is a head curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that applies buffs when going between dimensions. Blazing trotting antennae can be crafted with 1 trotting antennae, 1 Fire Mixtape, and 1 blaze rod (shapeless). When the blazing trotting antennae are worn in a head slot, going between dimensions will apply Slow Falling for 30 seconds, Regeneration II for 1 minute, Speed II for 2 minutes, and Fire Resistance for 3 minutes.
