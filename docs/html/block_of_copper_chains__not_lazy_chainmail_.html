Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bblock of copper chains}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). Blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spCopper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 block of copper chains (shapeless).&spCopper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).&spCopper Flail (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 block of copper chains, 1 copper tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 stick (Minecraft) (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).&spWaxed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless). Right-clicking a block of copper chains with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a block of copper chains will become an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). {{gal}}
