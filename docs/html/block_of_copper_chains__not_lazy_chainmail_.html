Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The block of copper chains is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). Blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 4 blocks of copper chains, 1 (bright spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail)) spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 (waxed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)) copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 piston (Minecraft), and 1 lever (Minecraft) (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 block of copper chains (shapeless).
Copper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).
Copper Flail (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 block of copper chains, 1 copper tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 stick (Minecraft) (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).
Waxed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless). Right-clicking a block of copper chains with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a block of copper chains will become an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). gal
