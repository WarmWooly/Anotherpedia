Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains or a waxed block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into a block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. Blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spCopper Chain x4 - 1 block of copper chains (shapeless).&spCopper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).&spCopper Flail - 1 block of copper chains, 1 copper tool chain, and 2 sticks (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).&spWaxed Block of Copper Chains - 1 block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (shapeless). Right-clicking a block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a block of copper chains will become an exposed block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. gal
