Block of Gold Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The block of gold chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of gold chains can be crafted with 4 gold chains (place in a 2x2 square). Blocks of gold chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spGold Chain x4 - 1 block of gold chains (shapeless).&spGold Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of gold chains (place side-by-side).&spGold Flail - 1 block of gold chains, 1 gold tool chain, and 2 sticks (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).&spHopper Dropper - 3 blocks of gold chains, 1 sort dropper, and 1 hopper (place the dropper in the middle, hopper on top, and the chains on the other three sides).
