Block of Iron Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The block of iron chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of iron chains can be crafted with 4 chains (place in a 2x2 square). Blocks of iron chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain x4 - 1 block of iron chains (shapeless).
Chain Launcher - 4 blocks of iron chains, 1 (bright) spool block, 1 chain, 1 piston, and 1 lever (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Iron Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of iron chains (place side-by-side).
Iron Flail - 1 block of iron chains, 1 iron tool chain, and 2 sticks (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).
Recycler - 3 blocks of iron chains, 1 lava bucket, and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with blocks of chains, put the lava in the center, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Seive Dropper - 4 blocks of iron chains and 1 sort dropper (surround the sort dropper with the blocks). Blocks of iron chains are used in the following melding recipes:
Iron Chain Cover - 1 block of iron chains and 1 leather padding
