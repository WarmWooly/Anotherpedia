Block of Iron Composite (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The block of iron composite is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail comprised of iron composite ingots. A block of iron composite can be crafted with 9 iron composite ingots (fill the entire grid). A block of iron composite can be used to craft 9 iron composite ingots. Blocks of iron composite are used in the following melding recipes:
Ascension Rune - 1 block of iron composite, 1 pseudorock, and 1 nether star Blocks of iron composite can be used for the base of a beacon like other valuable resource blocks. gal
