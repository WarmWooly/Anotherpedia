Block of Iron Composite Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The block of iron composite chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of iron composite chains can be crafted with 4 iron composite chains (place in a 2x2 square). Blocks of iron composite chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Expanded Barrel Collector - 1 block of iron composite chains and 2 barrel collectors (place the barrels to the left and right of the chains).
Iron Composite Chain x4 - 1 block of iron composite chains (shapeless).
Iron Composite Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of iron composite chains (place side-by-side).
Iron Composite Flail - 1 block of iron composite chains, 1 iron composite tool chain, and 2 obsidian (place the obsidian in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).
Iron Composite Pliers - 1 block of iron composite chains, 2 iron composite ingots, and 2 obsidian (place the block in the middle, then the iron composite above and to the right, and the obisidan to the bottom and the left).
