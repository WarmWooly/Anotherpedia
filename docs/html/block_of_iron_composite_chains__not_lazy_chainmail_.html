Block of Iron Composite Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bblock of iron composite chains}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The block of iron composite chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 iron composite chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square).
