Block of Netherite Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The block of netherite chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of netherite chains can be crafted with 4 netherite chains (place in a 2x2 square). Blocks of netherite chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Netherite Chain x4 - 1 block of netherite chains (shapeless).
Netherite Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of netherite chains (place side-by-side).
Netherite Flail - 1 block of netherite chains, 1 netherite tool chain, and 2 obsidian (place the obsidian in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot). gal
