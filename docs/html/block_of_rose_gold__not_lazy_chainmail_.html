Block of Rose Gold (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The block of rose gold is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail comprised of rose gold ingots. A block of rose gold can be crafted with 9 rose gold ingots (fill the entire grid). A block of rose gold can be used to craft 9 rose gold ingots. Blocks of rose gold can be used for the base of a beacon like other valuable resource blocks.
