Block of Rose Gold Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The block of rose gold chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The block of rose gold chains can be crafted with 4 rose gold chains (place in a 2x2 square). Blocks of rose gold chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Rose Gold Chain x4 - 1 block of rose gold chains (shapeless).
Rose Gold Chain Carpet x3 - 2 blocks of rose gold chains (place side-by-side).
Rose Gold Flail - 1 block of rose gold chains, 1 rose gold tool chain, and 2 sticks (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).
Rose Gold Pliers - 1 block of rose gold chains, 2 rose gold ingots, and 2 sticks (place the block in the middle, then the rose gold above and to the right, and the sticks to the bottom and the left). Blocks of rose gold chains are used in the following melding recipes:
Vivacious Leather Padding - 1 block of rose gold chains and 2 healthy leather paddings
