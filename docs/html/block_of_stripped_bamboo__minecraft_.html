Block of Stripped Bamboo (Minecraft)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The block of stripped bamboo is a block in Minecraft when a block of bamboo is stripped with an axe. When an axe is used on a block of bamboo, it will be stripped into a block of stripped bamboo. Blocks of stripped bamboo are used in the following recipes:
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 6 blocks of stripped bamboo and 2 chains (place the chains in the top corners, then fill the middle and bottom rows with the bamboo).
Bamboo Planks x2 - 1 block of stripped bamboo (place anywhere). In a furnace, blast furnace, or smoker, 1 block of stripped bamboo can fuel for 15 seconds (1.5 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (64 blocks of stripped bamboo, 960 seconds, 96 items). Note blocks placed on a block of stripped bamboo plays bass sounds.
