Blue Stake (Balatro)

Not to be confused with Blue Seal (aiBalatro).
Blue Stake is the fifth available Stake in Balatro, where: ;;-1 Discard|Jokers have a 30% chance of having an Eternal Sticker (can't be sold or destroyed)|Blinds have faster scaling per Ante (requiring more chips to win)|Small Blind will not award any money;; The White, Red, Green, and Black Stake must be defeated before unlocking the Blue Stake. Upon defeating a Deck with the Blue Stake, the Purple Stake will become unlocked, alongside the Plasma Deck.
