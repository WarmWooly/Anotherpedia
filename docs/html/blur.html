Blur

<>{{tFor other terms relating to blur or blurring, see [[blur (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>{{bBlur}} is a term used to describe when the [[visibility]] of an [[object]] is reduced by becoming less [[distinct]], such as [[motion|motion blur]] or reducing the [[contrast]] between [[features|feature]]. Blurring can also be used to describe the process of making something less distinct. If a [[situation]] is [[uncertain]], its outcome may be considered blurry.
