Body of water

<><>A {{bbody of water}}, {{bwaterbody}}, or {{bwater body}}, is an [[area]] of [[water]] contained on a [[planet's|planet]] [[surface]], such as a [[pond]], [[lake]], [[sea]], or [[ocean]]. Bodies of water are most often used to describe water on [[Earth]]. Bodies of water may be used to also describe [[flowing water]], including [[rivers|river]], [[streams|stream]], and [[creeks|creek]]. Roughly 71% of Earth's surface is covered in bodies of water.
