Body of water

A body of water, waterbody, or water body, is an area of water contained on a planet surface, such as a pond, lake, sea, or ocean. Bodies of water are most often used to describe water on Earth. Bodies of water may be used to also describe flowing water, including river, stream, and creek. Roughly 71% of Earth's surface is covered in bodies of water.
