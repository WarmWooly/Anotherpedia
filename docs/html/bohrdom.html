Bohrdom

{{iBohrdom}} is an atom-based fighting game released by Cole Allen on December 20, 2018, on Steam (service). The name is a portmanteau of Niels Bohr and boredom. It became notable after the 2026 White House Correspondents' dinner shooting, in which Cole Allen is the lead suspect of. It would be pulled from Steam shortly after following bipartisan politically-motivated review bombing.
