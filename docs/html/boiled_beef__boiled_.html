Boiled Beef (Boiled)

Boiled beef is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled beef can be obtained by boiling raw beef in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled beef is used in the following crafting recipes:
Beef and Mash - 1 boiled beef and 1 boiled potato (shapeless). Boiled beef can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
