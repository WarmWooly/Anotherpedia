Boiled Beetroot (Boiled)

<>A boiled beetroot is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). The boiled beetroot can be obtained by boiling a beetroot (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). Boiled beetroots are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled) - 2 boiled beetroots, 1 boiled carrot (Boiled)/boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless). Boiled beetroots can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
