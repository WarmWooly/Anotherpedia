Boiled Beetroot (Boiled)

A boiled beetroot is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. The boiled beetroot can be obtained by boiling a beetroot in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Boiled beetroots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth - 2 boiled beetroots, 1 boiled carrot/potato, and 1 bone broth (shapeless). Boiled beetroots can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
