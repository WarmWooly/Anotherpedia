Boiled Brown Mushroom (Boiled)

The boiled brown mushroom is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A boiled brown mushroom can be obtained by boiling a brown mushroom in a boiler. It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Boiled brown mushrooms are used in the following crafting recipes:
Mushroom Stew - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 (boiled) red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 (boiled) carrot, 1 baked/boiled potato, 1 cooked/boiled rabbit, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled potato, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Boiled brown mushrooms can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled brown mushrooms only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
