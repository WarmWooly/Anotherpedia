Boiled Brown Mushroom (Boiled)

<>The boiled brown mushroom is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). A boiled brown mushroom can be obtained by boiling a brown mushroom (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). Boiled brown mushrooms are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Mushroom Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 (boiled red mushroom (Boiled)) red mushroom (Minecraft), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 (boiled carrot (Boiled)) carrot (Minecraft), 1 baked potato (Minecraft)/boiled potato (Boiled), 1 cooked rabbit (Minecraft)/boiled rabbit (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley (Boiled) - 1 boiled brown mushroom, 1 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Boiled brown mushrooms can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled brown mushrooms only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
