Boiled Carrot (Boiled)

The boiled carrot is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A boiled carrot can be obtained by boiling a carrot in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled carrots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth - 1 boiled carrot, 2 boiled beetroots, and 1 bone broth (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie - 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled rabbit, and 1 bone broth (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew - 1 boiled carrot, 1 baked/boiled potato, 1 cooked/boiled rabbit, 1 brown/red/boiled brown/boiled red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley - 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled brown/red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Boiled carrots can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
