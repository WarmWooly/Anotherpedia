Boiled Carrot (Boiled)

<>The boiled carrot is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). A boiled carrot can be obtained by boiling a carrot (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 experience (Minecraft). Boiled carrots are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled) - 1 boiled carrot, 2 boiled beetroot (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie (Boiled) - 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled potato (Boiled), 1 boiled rabbit (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled carrot, 1 baked potato (Minecraft)/boiled potato (Boiled), 1 cooked rabbit (Minecraft)/boiled rabbit (Boiled), 1 brown mushroom (Minecraft)/red mushroom (Minecraft)/boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)/boiled red mushroom (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley (Boiled) - 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled potato (Boiled), 1 boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)/boiled red mushroom (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Boiled carrots can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
