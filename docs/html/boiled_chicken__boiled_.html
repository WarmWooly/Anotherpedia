Boiled Chicken (Boiled)

Boiled chicken is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled chicken can be obtained by boiling raw chicken in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled chicken is used in the following crafting recipes:
Chicken and Mash - 1 boiled chicken and 1 boiled potato (shapeless). Boiled chicken can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
