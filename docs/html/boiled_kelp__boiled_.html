Boiled Kelp (Boiled)

Boiled kelp is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled kelp can be obtained by boiling kelp in a boiler. It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.1 exp. Boiled kelp is used in the following crafting recipes:
Kelp-Wrapped Cod - 1 boiled kelp and 1 boiled cod (shapeless).
Kelp-Wrapped Salmon - 1 boiled kelp and 1 boiled salmon (shapeless). Boiled kelp can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled kelp only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
