Boiled Kelp (Boiled)

<>Boiled kelp is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Boiled kelp can be obtained by boiling kelp (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.1 experience (Minecraft). Boiled kelp is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Kelp-Wrapped Cod (Boiled) - 1 boiled kelp and 1 boiled cod (Boiled) (shapeless).
Kelp-Wrapped Salmon (Boiled) - 1 boiled kelp and 1 boiled salmon (Boiled) (shapeless). Boiled kelp can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled kelp only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
