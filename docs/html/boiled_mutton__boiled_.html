Boiled Mutton (Boiled)

<>Boiled mutton is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Boiled mutton can be obtained by boiling raw mutton (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 experience (Minecraft). Boiled mutton is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Mutton and Mash (Boiled) - 1 boiled mutton and 1 boiled potato (Boiled) (shapeless). Boiled mutton can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
