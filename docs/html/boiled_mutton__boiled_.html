Boiled Mutton (Boiled)

Boiled mutton is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled mutton can be obtained by boiling raw mutton in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled mutton is used in the following crafting recipes:
Mutton and Mash - 1 boiled mutton and 1 boiled potato (shapeless). Boiled mutton can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
