Boiled Pork (Boiled)

Boiled pork is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled pork can be obtained by boiling a raw porkchop in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled pork is used in the following crafting recipes:
Pork and Mash - 1 boiled pork and 1 boiled potato (shapeless). Boiled pork can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
