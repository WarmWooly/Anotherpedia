Boiled Potato (Boiled)

<>The boiled potato is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). A boiled potato can be obtained by boiling a potato (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 experience (Minecraft). Boiled potatoes are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Beef and Mash (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled beef (Boiled) (shapeless).
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato, 2 boiled beetroot (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless).
Chicken and Mash (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled chicken (Boiled) (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato, 2 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled rabbit (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless).
Mutton and Mash (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled mutton (Boiled) (shapeless).
Pork and Mash (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled pork (Boiled) (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled potato, 1 (boiled carrot (Boiled)) carrot (Minecraft), 1 cooked rabbit (Minecraft)/boiled rabbit (Boiled), 1 brown mushroom (Minecraft)/red mushroom (Minecraft)/boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)/boiled red mushroom (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley (Boiled) - 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)/boiled red mushroom (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Boiled potatoes can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
