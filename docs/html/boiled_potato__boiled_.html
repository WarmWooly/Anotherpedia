Boiled Potato (Boiled)

The boiled potato is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A boiled potato can be obtained by boiling a potato in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled potatoes are used in the following crafting recipes:
Beef and Mash - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled beef (shapeless).
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth - 1 boiled potato, 2 boiled beetroots, and 1 bone broth (shapeless).
Chicken and Mash - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled chicken (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie - 1 boiled potato, 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled rabbit, and 1 bone broth (shapeless).
Mutton and Mash - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled mutton (shapeless).
Pork and Mash - 1 boiled potato and 1 boiled pork (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew - 1 boiled potato, 1 (boiled) carrot, 1 cooked/boiled rabbit, 1 brown/red/boiled brown/boiled red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley - 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled brown/red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Boiled potatoes can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
