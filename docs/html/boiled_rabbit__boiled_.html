Boiled Rabbit (Boiled)

<>Boiled rabbit is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Boiled rabbit can be obtained by boiling raw rabbit (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 experience (Minecraft). Boiled rabbit is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Gamekeeper's Pie (Boiled) - 1 boiled rabbit, 2 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 bone broth (Boiled) (shapeless). Boiled rabbit can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
