Boiled Rabbit (Boiled)

Boiled rabbit is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled rabbit can be obtained by boiling raw rabbit in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled rabbit is used in the following crafting recipes:
Gamekeeper's Pie - 1 boiled rabbit, 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled potato, and 1 bone broth (shapeless). Boiled rabbit can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
