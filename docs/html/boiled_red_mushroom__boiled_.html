Boiled Red Mushroom (Boiled)

The boiled red mushroom is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A boiled red mushroom can be obtained by boiling a red mushroom in a boiler. It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Boiled red mushrooms are used in the following crafting recipes:
Mushroom Stew - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 (boiled) brown mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 (boiled) carrot, 1 baked/boiled potato, 1 cooked/boiled rabbit, and 1 bowl (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled potato, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Boiled red mushrooms can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled red mushrooms only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
