Boiled Red Mushroom (Boiled)

<>The boiled red mushroom is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). A boiled red mushroom can be obtained by boiling a red mushroom (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). Boiled red mushrooms are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Mushroom Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 (boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)) brown mushroom (Minecraft), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Rabbit Stew (Minecraft) - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 (boiled carrot (Boiled)) carrot (Minecraft), 1 baked potato (Minecraft)/boiled potato (Boiled), 1 cooked rabbit (Minecraft)/boiled rabbit (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless).
Vegetable Medley (Boiled) - 1 boiled red mushroom, 1 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Boiled red mushrooms can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. Boiled red mushrooms only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
