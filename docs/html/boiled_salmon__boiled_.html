Boiled Salmon (Boiled)

Boiled salmon is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Boiled salmon can be obtained by boiling a raw salmon in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.35 exp. Boiled salmon is used in the following crafting recipes:
Kelp-Wrapped Salmon - 1 boiled salmon and 1 boiled kelp (shapeless). Boiled salmon can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
