Boiler Cap (Boiled)

The boiler cap is a block in the Minecraft mod Boiled that is automatically created when a block of copper is placed atop a boiler. When a block of copper is placed on a boiler, it automatically extends into a boiler cap. When broken, it will drop a block of copper. A boiler cap increases the maximum heat capacity of a boiler from 6,000 to 12,000. The boiler cap will deal damage like a boiler for mobs standing on it when the boiler has 1,000 or more heat. The boiler cap can also be interacted with to open the boiler, but it will not accept items from hoppers. Heating blocks can also be placed adjacent to the boiler cap to passively heat the boiler.
