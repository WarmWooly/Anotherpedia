Bone-Filled Bowl (Boiled)

The bone-filled bowl is a dish item in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Sneaking while using the bone-filled bowl separates the item. The bone-filled bowl can be crafted with 1 bone and 1 bowl (shapeless). A bone-filled bowl can be boiled into bone broth in a boiler, taking 2 seconds at 2,000 heat and awarding 0.1 exp.
