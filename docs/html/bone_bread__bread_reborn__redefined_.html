Bone Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Bone bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Bone flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce bone bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience. Bone bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Bone bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, bone bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
