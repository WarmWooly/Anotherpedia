Bone Broth (Boiled)

<>Bone broth is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Bone broth can be obtained by boiling a bone-filled bowl (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 2 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.1 experience (Minecraft). Bone broth is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth (Boiled) - 1 bone broth, 1 boiled carrot (Boiled)/boiled potato (Boiled), and 2 boiled beetroot (Boiled) (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie (Boiled) - 1 bone broth, 2 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled potato (Boiled), and 1 boiled rabbit (Boiled) (shapeless). Bone broth can be drank as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. A bone-filled bowl (Boiled) is returned to the player after drinking for reuse.
