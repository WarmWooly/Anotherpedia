Bone Broth (Boiled)

Bone broth is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Bone broth can be obtained by boiling a bone-filled bowl in a boiler. It takes 2 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.1 exp. Bone broth is used in the following crafting recipes:
Beet and Veggie Bone Broth - 1 bone broth, 1 boiled carrot/potato, and 2 boiled beetroots (shapeless).
Gamekeeper's Pie - 1 bone broth, 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled potato, and 1 boiled rabbit (shapeless). Bone broth can be drank as food to restore hunger and saturation. A bone-filled bowl is returned to the player after drinking for reuse.
