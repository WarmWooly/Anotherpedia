Bone Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Bone flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Bone flour can be baked into bone bread. Bone flour can be obtained by milling bones or bone blocks on a millstone. Each bone block makes 3 bone flour. Bone flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce bone bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience.
