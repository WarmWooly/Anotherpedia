Bread Platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

The bread platter is combinational food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. It takes on the stats of the breads used to make it. In a breadmaking station, a bowl and up to 4 breads (including normal bread) can be combined into a bread platter. The bread platter will directly add up the hunger values of the breads and take an average of the saturation amount. The duration of effects will also be added together. Bread platters can be eaten as food. As it may have effects, bread platters can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
