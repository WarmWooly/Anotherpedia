Breadmaking Station (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>The breadmaking station is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined used to make bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined). The breadmaking station can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 bowl (Minecraft), 1 stick (Minecraft), and 2 stripped log (Minecraft) (place the logs on the bottom, with the bowl and stick above). Upon obtaining, the advancement (Minecraft) Bread Squared (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will be awarded. The breadmaking station can be used to add up to 4 breads (Bread Reborn: Redefined) (including normal bread (Minecraft)) in a bowl (Minecraft) to make a bread platter (Minecraft), adding the hunger (Minecraft), saturation (Minecraft), and effect (Minecraft) of the breads together. A single platter can be produced with "Serve", and up to 16 with "Batch". A bowl and at least 1 bread item must be used to make a new platter. In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 breadmaking station can fuel for 40 second (4 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 breadmaking stations, 2560 seconds, 256 items).
