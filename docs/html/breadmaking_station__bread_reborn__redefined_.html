Breadmaking Station (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

The breadmaking station is a block in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined used to make bread platters. The breadmaking station can be crafted with 1 bowl, 1 stick, and 2 stripped logs (place the logs on the bottom, with the bowl and stick above). Upon obtaining, the advancement Bread Squared will be awarded. The breadmaking station can be used to add up to 4 breads (including normal bread) in a bowl to make a bread platter, adding the hunger, saturation, and effects of the breads together. A single platter can be produced with "Serve", and up to 16 with "Batch". A bowl and at least 1 bread item must be used to make a new platter.
