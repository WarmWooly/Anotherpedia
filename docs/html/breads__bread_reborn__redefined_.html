The flours (Bread Reborn: Redefined) (besides the normal flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), which makes bread (Minecraft)) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce the breads. Any bread can be used in a breadmaking station (Bread Reborn: Redefined) to make a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined), adding its stats to the platter. The breads can be eaten as food (Minecraft). As they have effects, they can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. The crimson bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) and warped bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds. When gunpowder bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) is eaten, the advancement (Minecraft) My main goal... (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will be awarded.
