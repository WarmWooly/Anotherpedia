Breads (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

The breads are a type of item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. The breads are used as foods, with twelve different types of bread. The breads also apply various effects. The flours (besides the normal flour, which makes bread) can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce the breads. Any bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. The breads can be eaten as food. As they have effects, they can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. The crimson and warped breads only take half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds. When gunpoweder bread is eaten, the advancement My main goal... will be awarded.
