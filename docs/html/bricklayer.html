Bricklayer

For the film, see aiThe Bricklayer (film).&spA bricklayer, also known as a brickmason or blockmason, is an occupation in which the bricklayer makes structures out of bricks. These often include walls, paths, and fireplaces. They serve a similar role to masons, but mainly work with clay bricks, concrete bricks, and mortar.
