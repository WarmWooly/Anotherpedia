Bricklayer

<>{{tFor the [[film]], see [[{{aiThe Bricklayer}} (film)]].}}&sp<>A {{bbricklayer}}, also known as a {{bbrickmason}} or {{bblockmason}}, is an [[occupation]] in which the bricklayer makes [[structures|structure]] out of [[bricks|brick]]. These often include [[walls|brick wall]], [[paths|brick path]], and [[fireplaces|brick fireplace]]. They serve a similar role to [[masons|mason]], but mainly work with [[clay bricks|clay brick]], [[concrete bricks|concrete brick]], and [[mortar]].
