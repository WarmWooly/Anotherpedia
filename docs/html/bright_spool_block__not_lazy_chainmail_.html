Bright Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The bright spool block is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as decoration. The bright spool block can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 glowstone dust (Minecraft) (place the glowstone dust in the center and surround it with the spool blocks). The bright spool block can be haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail) from a spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). Bright spool blocks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 bright spool block, 1 piston (Minecraft), 1 chain (Minecraft), 4 block of iron chains (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 lever (Minecraft) (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Item Aggregator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 bright spool block, 2 telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 6 iron composite nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) (iron composite nuggets on the top and bottom rows, alloy in the left and right sides, and the block in the middle). A bright spool block can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce a spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail), taking 1 second. Bright spool blocks can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into 1 bright empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail). In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 bright spool block can fuel for 20 second (2 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 bright spool blocks, 1280 seconds, 128 items).
