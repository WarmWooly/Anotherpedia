Bright Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The bright spool block is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as decoration. The bright spool block can be crafted with 4 spool blocks and 1 glowstone dust (place the glowstone dust in the center and surround it with the spool blocks). The bright spool block can be haunted from a spool block. Bright spool blocks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Launcher - 1 bright spool block, 1 piston, 1 chain, 4 blocks of iron chains, and 1 lever (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Item Aggregator - 1 bright spool block, 2 telemagnetic alloy, and 6 iron composite nuggets (iron composite nuggets on the top and bottom rows, alloy in the left and right sides, and the block in the middle). A bright spool block can be smoked in a smoker to produce a spool block, taking 1 second. Bright spool blocks can be crushed into 1 bright empty spool. In a furnace, blast furnace, or smoker, 1 bright spool block can fuel for 20 seconds (2 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (64 bright spool blocks, 1280 seconds, 128 items).
