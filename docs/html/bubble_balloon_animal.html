Bubble balloon animal

The bubble balloon animal is a sentient bubble creature similar to a balloon dog created by Goji for their segment in The Nonchalant Elevator Collab. It appears 4:29 into the video and fully exits at 4:44. It is translucent blue with a large body, four legs, a tail, and a neck to its head. Its face consists of circular eyes and a nose with a mouth underneath and a pair of ears above. In the video, the bubble balloon animal gets its backside detached by the elevator, where afterwards the animal attaches it upside-down (tail facing the ground). It then struggles to move before it is pushed upwards by a light breeze.
