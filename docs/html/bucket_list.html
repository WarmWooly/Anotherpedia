Bucket list

For the list of goods and/or services a person wants, see wish list.
For other terms relating to bucket list, see bucket list (disambiguation).
A bucket list or wish list is a list of goal that a person wants to achieve before their death, including partaking in specific event, meeting celebrety, and following through with personal growth. The term is based on the phrase "kick the bucket", which was coinage in 1999 by Justin Zackham and popularized in 2007 with the release of The Bucket List.
