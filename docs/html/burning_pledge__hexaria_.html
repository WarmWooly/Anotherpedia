Burning Pledge (Hexaria)

Burning Pledge is an card rarity (Hexaria) card in the Roblox game Hexaria which gives the caster Bit (Hexaria) in exchange for a permanent source of damage (Hexaria). On release, Burning Pledge, abbreviated as BP or bp, became highly sought after due to the difficulty of grinding (Hexaria) Waelyn Cindra and the time it would take per battle (Hexaria), skyrocketing the cost as it was integrated into strategies relying on gaining large sums of Bits from BP. BP became a symbol of wealth for a short period of time after release until further updates caused other cards to take the spotlight. Name: Burning Pledge
Target: Self
Quickplay (Hexaria): No
Bit cost: 0
Effects: Gives 3 bits and the effect Eternal Burn (Hexaria). The Eternal Burn deals 2 damage with an additional modifier of 1% of the caster's maximum Health (Hexaria) (ex. 300 HP --> +3 damage), with a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 8. The total damage the Eternal Burn can do is between 4 and 10 damage per turn (Hexaria).
