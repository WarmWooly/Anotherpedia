Calcifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The calcifier is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce calcite (Minecraft). Calcifiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail), 2 bone block (Minecraft), and 2 diamond (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the diamonds above and below, and the bone blocks to the left and right). Calcifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create calcite (Minecraft). Calcifiers take 8x as long to generate one calcite compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
