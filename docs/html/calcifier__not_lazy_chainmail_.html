Calcifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The calcifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce calcite. Calcifiers can be crafted using 1 cobblefier, 2 bone blocks, and 2 diamonds (place the cobblefier in the center, the diamonds above and below, and the bone blocks to the left and right). Calcifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create calcite. Calcifiers take 8x as long to generate one calcite compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
