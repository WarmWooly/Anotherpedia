Canary (Bits & Bops)

The canary is a yellow canary in Bits & Bops, appearing within the minigames/mixtapes Meet & Tweet, Sky Mixtape, and Final Mixtape. The canary also appears as the icon for the third save file, in the cuckoo clock, and as a stuffed toy souvenir. They are seen talking with the wren. The canary is a round, yellow bird with small wings. They have orange, three-toed feet, a long tail feather, and an orange beak with dark blue dot eyes. The canary sports three lobes on its head, similar to a hairstyle. They are kept indoors as a pet. They dislike exercise, and enjoy ice cream, potato chips, singing, and talking with the wren.

Within the promotional material for Bits & Bops, the canary appears multiple times, both as serious assets and in memes.
