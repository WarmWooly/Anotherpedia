Canary Feather (Bits & Blocks)

The canary feather is a necklace curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that applies a buff on eating food. The canary feather can be crafted with 1 Meet & Tweet and 1 yellow dye (shapeless). When the canary feather is worn in a necklace slot, eating food will also apply Regeneration for 10 seconds. The canary feather is used in the following crafting recipes:
Wren Feather - 1 canary feather, 1 Sky Mixtape, and 1 blue dye (shapeless).
