Cannon

<>{{tNot to be confused with [[canon]].}}&sp<>A {{bcannon}} is a massive [[firearm]] that is used to launch large [[projectiles|projectile]] often with the aid of [[explosive|explosion]] [[materials|gunpowder]]. Many cannons are mounted to [[wheels|wheel]], [[carts|cart]], or other [[vehicles|vehicle]] due to their weight. A significant portion of cannons are made of [[metal]] to withstand the forces to launch projectiles such as [[cannonballs|cannonball]].
