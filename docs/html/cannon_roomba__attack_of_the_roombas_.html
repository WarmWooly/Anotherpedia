Cannon Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

<Kills|60 killsinfo>>The Cannon Roomba is an enemy in Attack of the Roombas that can shoot rubber balls at the player while chasing them down. If the player gets hit by a rubber ball from a Cannon Roomba, they instantly lose. The Cannon Roomba shoots every 9.33 seconds.
