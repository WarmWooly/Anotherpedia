Cannonball (diving)

<><>The {{bcannonball}} is a style of [[diving]] where a [[diver]] presses their legs up against their chest and holds them with their arms to become as [[spherical|sphere]] as possible, making a larger [[splash]]. It is easy to perform by nearly anyone entering a body of water, regardless of skill, making it a staple move at [[pools|pool]]. It has become a popular dive in the sport [[splashdiving]].
