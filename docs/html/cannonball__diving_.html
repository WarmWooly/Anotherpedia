Cannonball (diving)

The cannonball is a style of diving where a diver presses their legs up against their chest and holds them with their arms to become as sphere as possible, making a larger splash. It is easy to perform by nearly anyone entering a body of water, regardless of skill, making it a staple move at pool. It has become a popular dive in the sport splashdiving.
