Canon, fanon, your design

{{bCanon, fanon, your design}} was a fan art art trend in early 2023, started on Twitter, consisting of three panels labeled {{icanon}}, {{ifanon}}, and {{iyour design}}. The purpose was to put artwork of the canon (fiction) depiction of a character, then a fanon (fiction) based on the fandom's interpretation, then a fan redesign of the character.
