Canon, fanon, your design

Canon, fanon, your design was a fan art art trend in early 2023, started on Twitter, consisting of three panels labeled canon, fanon, and your design. The purpose was to put artwork of the canon (fiction) depiction of a character, then a fanon (fiction) based on the fandom's interpretation, then a fan redesign of the character.
