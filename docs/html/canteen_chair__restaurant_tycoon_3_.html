Canteen Chair (Restaurant Tycoon 3)

<>The canteen chair is a chair in the Roblox game Restaurant Tycoon 3. When building, a directional arrow will appear to show the direction the chair is facing. They can also be given for free from expansions. It is a part of the canteen set, which includes the small table and the large table.
