Canteen Chair (Restaurant Tycoon 3)

<>The canteen chair is a chair (Restaurant Tycoon 3) in the Roblox game Restaurant Tycoon 3. When building (Restaurant Tycoon 3), a directional arrow will appear to show the direction the chair is facing. They can also be given for free from expansion (Restaurant Tycoon 3). It is a part of the canteen set, which includes the small canteen table (Restaurant Tycoon 3) and the large canteen table (Restaurant Tycoon 3).
