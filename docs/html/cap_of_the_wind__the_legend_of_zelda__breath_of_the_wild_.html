<>The Cap of the Wind is an amiibo-exclusive items in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild head armor (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild) in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild that can be obtained through the amiibo Toon Link (Super Smash Bros. amiibo) and Toon Link (The Wind Waker amiibo). The amiibos can be scanned in using the amiibo Rune (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild), with a chance of getting the Cap of the Wind each day.

The design is based on the garb worn by Toon Link in The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker. The Cap is a part of the Hero of Winds set (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild) alongside the Tunic of the Wind (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild) and Trousers of the Wind (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild).
