Cap of the Wind (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild)

For its appearance in {{aiTears of the Kingdom, see Cap of the Wind (aiThe Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom).}}
The Cap of the Wind is an amiibo-exclusive head Armor in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild that can be obtained through the amiibos Super Smash Bros. Toon Link and The Wind Waker Toon Link. The amiibos can be scanned in using the amiibo Rune, with a chance of getting the Cap of the Wind each day.

The design is based on the garb worn by Toon Link in The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker. The Cap is a part of the Hero of Winds set alongside the Tunic and Trousers. The item has the following flavor text:According to legend, this cap was treasured by a hero who traveled the Great Sea. It flaps quite serenely in a soft sea breeze.
