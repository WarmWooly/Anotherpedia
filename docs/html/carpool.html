Carpool

<>{{tFor larger carpools in [[vans|van]] or similar vehicles, see [[vanpool]].}}&sp{{tFor other terms related to carpool, see [[carpool (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>A {{bcarpool}} is the [[sharing]] of a [[car]] among multiple [[people]], especially with [[friends|friend]] or [[family|family member]], when [[driving]] between locations (especially if the [[passengers|passenger]] share the start or end location). Carpooling's main benefits include reducing the cost of [[fuel|car fuel]] and [[tolls|toll road]] compared to multiple people driving, and in some cases, being allowed to drive along specialized [[carpool lanes|high-occupancy vehicle lane]] that incentivize carpooling. Other benefits include decreased [[traffic]], reduced [[emissions|greenhouse gas emissions]], and reduced [[parking demand]].<>
