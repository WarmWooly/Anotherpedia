Carpool

{{tFor larger carpools in van or similar vehicles, see vanpool.}}&sp{{tFor other terms related to carpool, see carpool (disambiguation).}}&spA {{bcarpool}} is the sharing of a car among multiple people, especially with friend or family member, when driving between locations (especially if the passenger share the start or end location). Carpooling's main benefits include reducing the cost of car fuel and toll road compared to multiple people driving, and in some cases, being allowed to drive along specialized high-occupancy vehicle lane that incentivize carpooling. Other benefits include decreased traffic, reduced greenhouse gas emissions, and reduced parking demand.<>
