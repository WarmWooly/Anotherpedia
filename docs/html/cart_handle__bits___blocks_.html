Cart Handle (Bits & Blocks)

The cart handle is a head curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that allows players to self-power minecarts and to get a buff in a minecart. The cart handle can be crafted with 1 Molecano and 1 iron ingot (shapeless). When the cart handle is worn in a head slot, minecarts will keep their momentum even without powered rails. Unpowered powered rails will still cause the minecart to slow down. Riding in a minecart will also apply Fire Resistance. The cart handle is used in the following crafting recipes:
Icy Cart Handle - 1 cart handle and 1 Ocean Mixtape (shapeless).
