Cathay Dragon

{{bCathay Dragon}}, also known as {{bHong Kong Dragon Airlines Limited}}, and formerly known as {{bDragonair}}, was an airline based in Hong Kong Founding by Chao Kuang Piu on May 24, 1985. It ceased operations on October 21, 2020, due to 2020 Hong Kong protests and the COVID-19 pandemic.
