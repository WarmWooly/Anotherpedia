Celsius

For the drink company, see Celsius Holdings, Inc. and their brand CELSIUS (drink).
Celsius is a temperature measurement system created by Anders Celsius. It used to be based on the boiling and freezing points of water (0 °C and 100 °C respectively), which was flipped to the more well-known system of 100 °C for boiling and 0 °C for freezing, but it is now based on the Kelvin scale.
