Celsius Holdings, Inc.

For the brand, see CELSIUS (drink).
CELSIUS is a company that sells fitness drink. CELSIUS is publicly trading stock in NASDAQ as CELH. It was founding in April 2004. The current CEO, <>, is John Fieldly, who was promoted from CFO in April 2018. Eric Hanson is the Chief operating officer and Jarrod Langhans is the CFO.

The member of Board of Directors are John Fieldly, Hal Kravitz, Cheryl S. Miller, Caroline Levy, Damon Desantis, Joyce Russell, Nick Castaldo, Hans Melotte, and Irsael Kontorovsky.
