Centerbeam train car

<><><>A {{bcenterbeam train car}}, also called a {{bcenterbeam railcar}}, is a [[train car]] used to [[transport|transportation]] [[bundled goods]], often in [[rectangular]] [[packaging]]. Most centerbeam train cars are made to carry up to 200,000 [[lbs|pound]] of [[materials|material]], which are often [[building materials|building material]] such as [[wood]]. It is called a centerbeam because there are beams in the center of the car that are used to reinforce the [[center of gravity]] and provide the ability to [[secure|securing packaging]] materials on both sides of the car.
