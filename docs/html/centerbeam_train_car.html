Centerbeam train car

A {{bcenterbeam train car}}, also called a {{bcenterbeam railcar}}, is a train car used to transportation bundled goods, often in rectangular packaging. Most centerbeam train cars are made to carry up to 200,000 pound of material, which are often building material such as wood. It is called a centerbeam because there are beams in the center of the car that are used to reinforce the center of gravity and provide the ability to securing packaging materials on both sides of the car.
