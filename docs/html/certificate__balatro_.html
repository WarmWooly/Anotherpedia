Certificate (Balatro)

Certificate is a Joker in Balatro that creates a playing card with a Seal at the start of a Blind. This playing card will have a random Suit and Rank, but it will not have any Enhancements or Editions.&pThis ability to generate a Seal can be quite powerful, allowing for an improved money, Tarot, and Planet generation with the Gold, Purple, and Blue Seals respectively. The Red Seal's retriggering effect can also be useful for Jokers that activate on specific cards and for Steel and Gold Cards.
