Chain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The chain generator is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to turn chain links into Forge Energy (Minecraft modding) (FE). FE is not used in any blocks/items in Not Lazy Chainmail, but rather made for cross-compatibility with mods that do use FE or other power systems. The chain generator can be crafting (Minecraft) with 5 cobblestone (Minecraft), 2 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The chain generator requires an industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) to run, with better belts increasing the speed the chain generator can convert chains to FE.
