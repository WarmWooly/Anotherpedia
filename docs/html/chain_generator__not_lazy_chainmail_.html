Chain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Power storage|200000 FEinfo>>The chain generator is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to turn chain links into Forge Energy (FE). FE is not used in any blocks/items in Not Lazy Chainmail, but rather made for cross-compatibility with mods that do use FE or other power systems. The chain generator can be crafted with 5 cobblestone, 2 rose gold ingots, 1 recycler, and 1 crude engine (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The chain generator requires an industrial belt to run, with better belts increasing the speed the chain generator can convert chains to FE.
