Chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Chain links are a type of item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Chain links are used in maille crafting and come in the materials of iron, gold, copper, netherite, rose gold, and iron composite. Chain links can be snipped using pliers from a coil or an unfinished project of the respective material type. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes a chain (makes 8 chain links). Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator.
