Chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Chain links are a type of item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail. Chain links are used in Chainmail Project (Not Lazy Chainmail) and come in the materials of iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail), gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail), copper chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail), netherite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail), rose gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail), and iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail). Chain links can be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) using pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) from a coils (Not Lazy Chainmail) or an unfinished projects (Not Lazy Chainmail) of the respective material type. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Chain links can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes a chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 8 chain links).
