Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Chain On is an advancement in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that is earned when the player applies honeycomb directly onto a Not Lazy Chainmail block made of copper. It is the child to the advancement Craft Like a Billionaire and the parent to the advancement Oxide On.
