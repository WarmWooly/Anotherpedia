Chainmail armor is comprised of [[chain links|chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] with a [[chainmail project|chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. Unlike normal armors, chainmail armor can have [[leather paddings|leather paddings (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] applied to them to increase their stats and apply unique buffs. There are six types of chainmail armor: [[iron|chainmail armor (Minecraft)]],<> [[gold|gold chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[copper|copper chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[netherite|netherite chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[rose gold|rose gold chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and [[iron composite|iron composite chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail)]].<>The following table shows the variable stats between items (helmet, chestplate, leggings, and boots), with the number in bold being for a whole set (besides enchantability, which is constant for every piece).<><>{{tFor detailed information regarding how to get normal (iron) chainmail armor, see [[chainmail armor ({{aiMinecraft}})]].}}<>All chainmail armor can be obtained through [[maille crafting|Chainmail Project (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], with the item produced based on the material of [[chain link|chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] used. Helmets require 320 chain links, chestplates require 512, leggings require 448, and boots require 256.<>The [[iron chainmail helemt|chainmail helemt (Minecraft)]], [[iron chainmail chestplate|chainmail chestplate (Minecraft)]], [[gold chainmail helmet|gold chainmail helmet (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and [[gold chainmail chestplate|gold chainmail chestplate (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] each have a 7.69% chance to appear in a [[suspicious sand|suspicious sand (Minecraft)]] within a [[chainmail ruins|chainmail ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail)]].<><>{{tMain page: [[Armor ({{aiMinecraft}})]]}}&spChainmail armor can be equipped to the four armor slots: head, chest, legs, and feet. Only one of each may be applied at a time. Chainmail armor can be manually placed in the slot or automatically swapped by using the armor in the [[main hand|main hand (Minecraft)]]/[[offhand|offhand (Minecraft)]]. While the [[player|player (Minecraft)]] is wearing at least one gold or rose gold chainmail armor piece, [[piglins|piglin (Minecraft)]] will be neutral instead of hostile. This effect does not apply to [[piglin brutes|piglin brute (Minecraft)]].<>In a [[leathersmithing table|leathersmithing table (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], up to 3 [[leather paddings|leather paddings (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] can be applied to chainmail armor. Leather paddings will change the stats on the piece, increasing various stats dependent on the applied padding. Leather paddings are lost when chainmail armor breaks or is [[crushed|crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]<>Chainmail armor can be [[enchanted|enchanting (Minecraft)]] with [[Unbreaking|Unbreaking (Minecraft)]], [[Mending|Mending (Minecraft)]], [[Curse of Binding|Curse of Binding (Minecraft)]], [[Curse of Vanishing|Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft)]], [[Protection|Protection (Minecraft)]], [[Blast Protection|Blast Protection (Minecraft)]], [[Fire Protection|Fire Protection (Minecraft)]], [[Projectile Protection|Projectile Protection (Minecraft)]], [[Thorns|Thorns (Minecraft)]], [[Aqua Affinity|Aqua Affinity (Minecraft)]], [[Respiration|Respiration (Minecraft)]], [[Swift Sneak|Swift Sneak (Minecraft)]], [[Feather Falling|Feather Falling (Minecraft)]], [[Frost Walker|Frost Walker (Minecraft)]], [[Depth Strider|Depth Strider (Minecraft)]], and [[Soul Speed|Soul Speed (Minecraft)]] using an [[enchanted book|enchanted book (Minecraft)]] on an [[anvil|anvil (Minecraft)]].<><> Unbreaking, Protection, Blast Protection, Fire Protection, Projectile Protection, Thorns, Aqua Affinity, Respiration, Feather Falling, Frost Walker, and Depth Strider may also be obtained in an [[enchanting table|enchanting table (Minecraft)]].<>Chainmail armor can be repaired through [[combining|combining (Minecraft)]] by [[crafting|crafting (Minecraft)]], using a [[grindstone|grindstone (Minecraft)]], or using an [[anvil|anvil (Minecraft)]]. Combining two pieces of the same chainmail armor will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some [[experience|experience (Minecraft)]] returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the two chainmail pieces, costing experience.<>In an [[anvil|anvil (Minecraft)]], chainmail armor can be repaired using its corresponding ingot ([[iron|iron ingot (Minecraft)]], [[gold|gold ingot (Minecraft)]], [[copper|copper ingot (Minecraft)]], [[netherite|netherite ingot (Minecraft)]], [[rose gold|rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], or [[iron composite|iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]), costing [[experience|experience (Minecraft)]] and restoring 25% of the peice's durability, rounded down.<>The [[rune|runes (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] [[Chain Repair Rune|Chain Repair Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] can be applied to chainmail armor, costing 3 [[levels|experience (Minecraft)]] with the max [[Chain Repair|Chain Repair (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] level at IV. <>In a [[smithing table|smithing table (Minecraft)]], [[armor trims|armor trim (Minecraft)]] can be applied to chainmail armor with a material ([[amethyst|amethyst shard (Minecraft)]], [[chromatic|chromatic mass (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[copper|copper ingot (Minecraft)]], [[diamond|diamond (Minecraft)]], [[emerald|emerald (Minecraft)]], [[gold|gold ingot (Minecraft)]], [[iron|iron ingot (Minecraft)]], [[iron composite|iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[lapis lazuli|lapis lazuli (Minecraft)]], [[nether quartz|nether quartz (Minecraft)]], [[netherite|netherite ingot (Minecraft)]], [[redstone dust|redstone dust (Minecraft)]], [[rose gold|rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and [[telemagnetic alloy|telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]) to add a [[trim|trimming (Minecraft)]] to the armor. Trims are purely cosmetic, with their coloration based on the material used.<>Chainmail armor can be [[crushed|crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] into an [[unfinished project|unfinished projects (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] of its corresponding material, which can be [[snipped|snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] to recover all the [[chain links|chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] used to make it.<>Iron chainmail armor can be [[smelted|smelting (Minecraft)]] in a [[furnace|furnace (Minecraft)]] or a [[blast furnace|blast furnace (Minecraft)]] to produce an [[iron nugget|iron nugget (Minecraft)]], taking 10 and 5 seconds respectively.
