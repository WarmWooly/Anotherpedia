Chainmail Ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The chainmail ruins are a small structure in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that can spawn in Desert biomes in the Overworld with various items related to Not Lazy Chainmail in suspicious sand. Chainmail ruins have a minimum distance of 15 chunks apart, with the average set to 22 chunks. Chainmail ruins are 8 blocks long and 6 blocks wide, while being 4 blocks tall. Chainmail ruins are set 1 block into the ground, flush with the surrounding terrain.
