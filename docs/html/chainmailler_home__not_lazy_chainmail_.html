Chainmailler Home (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The chainmailler home is a structure in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that can spawn in Mangrove Swamp biomes in the Overworld with various items related to Not Lazy Chainmail in a double chest and a winding jig, alongside a chainmailler villager. Chainmailler homes have a minimum distance of 20 chunks apart, with the average set to 30 chunks. Chainmailler homes are 10 blocks long and 7 blocks wide, while being 8 blocks tall. Chainmailler homes are set 1 block into the ground, flush with the surrounding terrain.
