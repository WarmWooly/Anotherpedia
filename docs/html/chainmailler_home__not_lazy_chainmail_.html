Chainmailler Home (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The {{bchainmailler home}} is a structure (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} modding (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} that can spawn in Mangrove Swamp (Minecraft) biome (Minecraft) in the Overworld (Minecraft) with various item (Minecraft) related to {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} in a double chest (Minecraft) and a winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail), alongside a chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) villager (Minecraft). Chainmailler homes have a minimum distance of 20 chunk (Minecraft) apart, with the average set to 30 chunks. Chainmailler homes are 10 blocks long and 7 blocks wide, while being 8 blocks tall. Chainmailler homes are set 1 block into the ground, flush with the surrounding terrain.
