Chainmailler homes have a minimum distance of 20 chunk (Minecraft) apart, with the average set to 30 chunks. Chainmailler homes are 10 blocks long and 7 blocks wide, while being 8 blocks tall. Chainmailler homes are set 1 block into the ground, flush with the surrounding terrain.
