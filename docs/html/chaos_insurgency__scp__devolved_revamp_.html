Chaos Insurgency (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Chaos Insurgency (CI) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who cause chaos in the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The CI gets a AK-74 (SCP: Devolved Revamp) at rank one, which they can use on all other non-CI player (Roblox). CI members should focus on breaching SCPs (SCP: Devolved Revamp), breaking out Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and causing chaos around the facility by eliminating staff and combative members. The CI has 5 levels, with each giving a new title and a new weapon. At level 4, their keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp) access also increases. They additionally have a special sliding (SCP: Devolved Revamp) ability.<> CIs spawn in a campsite outside the facility of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The camp requires a level 3 keycard to enter the side gate or two players with a level 2 keycard to enter. If the facility gate is broken, players can enter the camp without a keycard. The camp is on a dirt ground with wooden fence posts surrounding it. There is a campfire and logs, around which the player can sit around the fire, and there are also multiple wooden crates surrounding the camp. There are three mini tents, one with a storage container inside that contains a special uniform for players who owned the Chaos Commander (SCP: Devolved Classic) gamepass (Roblox) in SCP: Devolved Classic. The big tent that CI spawns in contains a CI uniform and a TV that says the following:For Chaos Insurgency:
- you are rebels against the foundation
- free SCPs to gain the upper hand
- fight against enemy combatants
- recruit escaped inmates
- cause chaos >:)
