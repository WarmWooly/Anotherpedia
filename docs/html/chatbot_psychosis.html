Chatbot psychosis

<>Chatbot psychosis or AI psychosis is psychosis that is developed by or worsened through interactions with chatbot or other generative artificial intelligence. Chatbots can induce psychosis by generating content that does not align with reality, which can be seen with hallucination (artificial intelligence), incomplete or flawed data set used for training (artificial intelligence), or deliberate misinformation. Chatbots may be sycophancy, constantly agreeing with the user even when in the wrong. They may also create new information or 'inference' something incorrectly. symptom of chatbot psychosis include paranoia, anxiety, depression, delusion, social isolation, and in extreme cases, AI-driven suicide.
