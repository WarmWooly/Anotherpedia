Chatbot psychosis

<>Chatbot psychosis or AI psychosis is psychosis that is developed by or worsened through interactions with chatbots or other generative AI. Chatbots can induce psychosis by generating content that does not align with reality, which can be seen with hallucinations, incomplete or flawed data sets used for training, or deliberate misinformation. Chatbots may be sycophantic, constantly agreeing with the user even when in the wrong. They may also create new information or 'infer' something incorrectly. Symptoms of chatbot psychosis include paranoia, anxiety, depression, delusion, isolation, and in extreme cases, suicide.
