Chemical symbol

A chemical symbol or element symbol is an abbreviation consisting of one or two letters to represent an element. The first letter is capitalized, while the second (if present) is lowercase. Each of the 118 elements has a unique chemical symbol. Chemical symbols are often seen in chemical formulas.
