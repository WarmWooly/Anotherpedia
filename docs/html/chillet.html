Chillet

Chillet is a ferret-like Ice element (Palworld) and Dragon element (Palworld) Pals (fictional species) from Palworld. Chillet is similar in structure to a ferret, having a long, cyan body with a white underside. It has four stubby legs, a short snout, two blue eyes, and a pair of ears tipped in a darker blue. It has three dark blue marks on its front and multiple along its tail. Chillet also has three dark blue spines on its back.&pChillet's name is a portmanteau between chill and ferret. Chillet is the 55th Pal in the Paldeck (Palworld). The work suitability (Palworld) for Chillet is 1 for Gathering (Palworld) and 1 for Cooling (Palworld).
