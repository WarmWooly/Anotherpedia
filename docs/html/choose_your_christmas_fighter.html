Choose your Christmas fighter

"Choose your Christmas figher" is an advertisement by Voidpet for the mobile game Voidpet Dungeon set during Christmas. The advertisement was released as a YouTube short on December 23, 2025, featuring several Voidpets taking part in festivities. The song "Happy Santa" by Roman Dudchyk plays in the background. The ad starts with a stage five Kind standing by a fireplace with two scarfed stage one Voidpets as a third (likely Scorn) snakes by in a Santa hat. A stage four Joy creates small magical orbs of fire by the Christmas tree before a Santa-hatted stage three Glee empties a mortar and pestle over a pig roast. Kind returns to the enjoyment of two Voidpets with a bag full of sticks. A stage five Euphoric Merry shakes handbells, boosting Kind and Joy as they stock the fire and illuminate. A stage five Greed places a sign on a display window, reading "HOLIDAY SALE 50% off" as a stage five Charity runs by carrying many presents. A stage five Halcyon Estrangement flies overhead, dropping flyers to stage one Voidpets, reading "merry Christmas".
