Chorus Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|No||Hunger|4 (2 shanks)||Saturation|2.8 (1.4 shanks)||Effects|Effect Resistance (1:00)info>>Chorus bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Chorus flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce chorus bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience. Chorus bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, chorus bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
