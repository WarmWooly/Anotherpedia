Chorus Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Chorus bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Chorus flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce chorus bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience. Chorus bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Chorus bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, chorus bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
