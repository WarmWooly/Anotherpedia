Chorus Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Chorus flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Chorus flour can be baked into chorus bread. Chorus flour can be obtained by milling chorus fruit on a millstone. Chorus flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce chorus bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience.
