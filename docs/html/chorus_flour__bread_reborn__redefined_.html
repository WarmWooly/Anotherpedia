Chorus Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Chorus flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. Chorus flour can be baked into chorus bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Chorus flour can be obtained by milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) chorus fruit (Minecraft) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Chorus flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce chorus bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft).
