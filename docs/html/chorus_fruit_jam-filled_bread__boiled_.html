Chorus Fruit Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled)

Chrous fruit jam-filled bread is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Chorus fruit jam-filled bread can be crafted with 1 chorus fruit jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Chorus fruit jam-filled bread can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
