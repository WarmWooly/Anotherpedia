Chorus Fruit Jam (Boiled)

Chorus fruit jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Chorus fruit jam can be obtained by boiling chorus fruit in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.5 exp. Chorus fruit jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Chorus Fruit Jam-Filled Bread - 1 chorus fruit jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Chorus fruit jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
