Chorus Fruit Jam (Boiled)

<>Chorus fruit jam is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Chorus fruit jam can be obtained by boiling chorus fruit (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.5 experience (Minecraft). Chorus fruit jam is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Chorus Fruit Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled) - 1 chorus fruit jam and 1 bread (Minecraft) (shapeless). Chorus fruit jam can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
