Christmas lights

<><>{{bChristmas lights}} are [[decorative|decorative lights]] [[electric lights]] with even [[spacing]] between bulbs, including [[string lights]] and [[fairy lights]], used during the [[Christmas season]]. Christmas lights are often placed around [[trees|Christmas tree]], on or around [[houses|Christmas house decorations]], and around other fixtures as festive illumination. The term "Christmas lights" may also be used to describe any decorative lighting related to [[Christmas]]. [[Candles|candle]], the original form of Christmas lights, can also fall under this term.
