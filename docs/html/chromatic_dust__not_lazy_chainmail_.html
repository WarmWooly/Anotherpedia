Chromatic Dust (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Chromatic dust is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in creating iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail). Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) a chromatic mass (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a chromastone (Not Lazy Chainmail) will give 1 or 2 chromatic dust respectively.
