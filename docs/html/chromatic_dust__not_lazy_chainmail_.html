Chromatic Dust (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Chromatic dust is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in creating iron composite. Crushing a chromatic mass or a chromastone will give 1 or 2 chromatic dust respectively. Chromatic dust is used in the following crafting recipes:
Chromastone - 2 chromatic dust and 1 glowstone (shapeless).
Music Disc "Neon" - 4 chromatic dust and 1 maille crafted music disc (surround the music disc with the dust). Chromatic dust is used in the following melding recipes:
Chromastone - 2 chromatic dust and 1 glowstone
Experienced Rune - 1 chromatic dust, 1 pseudorock, and 1 bottle o' enchanting
Iron Composite Ingot - 1 chromatic dust, 1 bedrock shard, and 1 iron ingot gal
