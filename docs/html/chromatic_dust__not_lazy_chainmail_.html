Chromatic Dust (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Chromatic dust is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in creating iron composite. Crushing a chromatic mass or a chromastone will give 1 or 2 chromatic dust respectively. Chromatic dust is used in the following crafting recipes:&spChromastone - 2 chromatic dust and 1 glowstone (shapeless).&spMusic Disc "Neon" - 4 chromatic dust and 1 maille crafted music disc (surround the music disc with the dust).
