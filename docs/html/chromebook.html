Chromebook

A Chromebook is a type of electronic device developed by Google with ChromeOS as the device's operating system. It is preinstalled with several Google application, such as Google Chrome. Multiple companies build Chromebooks, including Acer, ASUS, Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Samsung.

Chomebooks are well-known for their prevalence in school, especially with being cheap and easy to use.
