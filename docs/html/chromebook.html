Chromebook

A Chromebook is a type of electronic device developed by Google with ChromeOS as the device's operating system. It is preinstalled with several Google apps, such as Google Chrome. Multiple companies build Chromebooks, including Acer, ASUS, Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Samsung.&pChomebooks are well-known for their prevalence in schools, especially with being cheap and easy to use.
