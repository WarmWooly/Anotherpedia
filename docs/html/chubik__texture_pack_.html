Chubik (texture pack)

<><>{{bChubik}}, also known as {{bChubikcraft}}, {{bThe Chubik Challenge}}, or {{bChubik: A Survival Challenge}}, is a {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[texture pack|Minecraft texture pack]] created by [[JustusFT]] posted onto [[Minecraft Forum]] and [[Planet Minecraft]] on September 27, 2015, based on the 2009 [[artwork]] {{i[[chubik]]}}.<><><> Chubik would later be uploaded to [[CurseForge]] the following year.<> The texture pack features [[blocks|block (Minecraft)]], [[items|item (Minecraft)]], and [[mobs|mob (Minecraft)]] being retextured to a plain white with the name of the block/item/mob written in black text. Each texture is 512x512 in size. The texture pack is advertised as a [[challenge]] for experienced players, with goals such as [[beating the game|Beating Minecraft]] and [[competing in a build-off|Minecraft build-off]] with the pack enabled. The novelty of the pack made it widely popular amongst the [[playerbase|Minecraft playerbase]], especially between 2015 and 2016. Various [[{{iMinecraft}} YouTubers|Minecraft YouTuber]], including [[Logdotzip]], [[ibxtoycat]], and [[a6d]], made videos on the Chubik texture pack.<><><> The latest version of Chubik is for [[1.9|Minecraft: Java Edition 1.9]], however, a successor made by iplaygamesbad_, [[Chubik Remade]], updates the texture pack to modern versions.
