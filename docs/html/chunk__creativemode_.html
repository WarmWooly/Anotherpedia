Chunk (CreativeMode)

Chunk is the AI-generated mascot for CreativeMode, announced on its Discord server by Wilson Spearman on March 10, 2026. Chunk is a white cube with a pair of red buttons on its right side, a joystick with a red bulb on top, and a display with a pair of yellow eyes on its front. Chunk is shown to be hovering through unknown means.
