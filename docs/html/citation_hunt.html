Citation Hunt

Citation Hunt is an open source website created by eggpi (developer) and website hosting on Wikimedia Toolforge to find and fix citation needed Wikipedia tags on Wikipedia. The website takes the snippet of an article (Wikipedia) with a citation needed tag, allowing the user to go to the page to fix it. Notes may also be added to the snippet, either mentioning that the citation needed tag is old or that the citation is present within the article's lead section. Users may also search for categories of pages to add citations for, and a built-in leaderboard tracks added citations over the past month.
