Claim

<>{{tFor other uses of the term claim, see [[claim (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>A {{bclaim}} is a [[statement]] where the [[person]] is saying that some [[fact]] or [[knowledge]] is [[true]], especially with little to no [[evidence]]. These claims then can be [[proven|prove]] or [[disproven|disprove]] by using evidence and [[logic]] to reach a [[conclusion|logical conclusion]], either proving it to be true or [[false]]. Claims are often treated with [[skepticism]] because of the failure to overcome the [[burden of proof]] to make the claim [[legitimate]].
