Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Class D (CD) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who sit in a containment cell, get tested on, and escape the facility. CD members should focus on waiting to be taken by Research Department (SCP: Devolved Revamp) to be tested on, riot, and escaping the facility. The CD has 5 levels, with each giving a new title. They only get the Class D Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp).<> CDs spawn in a room labeled "Class D" in Sector 2 of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The room requires a Level 2 Keycard to enter. The Class D room has two floors. The first floor has four bench tables with two benches on each side of it, and there are a total of eight Class D cells on the first floor. There is also a red line on the floor where if a Class D passes it, they will be marked for termination. Past the red line lie two doors into Sector 2, as well as two plants and three security stands. The top floor can be accessed by going up a ramp, and there are handrail across the top floor, as well as two plants and four benches. Another eight Class D cells are on the top floor, totalling sixteen Class D cells between the two floors, and the red line is also on the second floor, as well as one door on each side of the second floor that goes into Sector 2.
