Class D Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Class D Keycard in SCP: Devolved Revamp is a keycard given to members of the Class D at level 1, it is the lowest ranked keycard in the game not able to unlock any doors in the Facility besides Office 1 and Office 2 in Sector 1.
