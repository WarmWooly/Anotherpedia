Clip-on vaporizer

A clip-on vaporizer, also called a baby vaporizer or baby vapor, is a small vaporizer that is attached to a baby shirt or onesie. The vaporizer is used to relieve coughing and nasal congestion from a common cold. A small pad with essential oil is opened from a sealed packaging and inserted into the clip, where it will aerate around the baby's nose. After a specified time (~12 hour), the pad will need to be replaced.
