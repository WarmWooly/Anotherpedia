Clip-on vaporizer

A clip-on vaporizer, also called a baby vaporizer or baby vapor, is a small vaporizer that is attached to a baby's shirt or onesie. The vaporizer is used to relieve coughing and stuffy noses from a cold. A small pad with essential oils is opened from a sealed package and inserted into the clip, where it will aerate around the baby's nose. After a specified time (~12 hours), the pad will need to be replaced.
