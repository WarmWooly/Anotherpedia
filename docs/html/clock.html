Clock

A {{bclock}} is a device used to measure and display the passing of time. Clocks are used by people to scheduling and time management, to perform task at specific times of the day (such as eating or sleep), and to determine when a task or process will be completed. While the history of timekeeping devices were sundial and water clock, modern clocks used are analog clock or digital clock.
