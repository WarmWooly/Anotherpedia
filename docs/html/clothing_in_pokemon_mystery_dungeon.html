Clothing in {{iPokémon Mystery Dungeon}}

<><>The {{i[[Pokémon]]}} spin-off games {{i[[Pokémon Mystery Dungeon]]}} have a large majority of [[Pokémon|Pokémon (fictional species)]] wearing neckwear of some sort, including [[scarfs|scarf]], [[bowties|bowtie]], [[neckerchiefs|neckerchief]], and most common of all, [[bandanas|bandana]]. It is seen in a variety of locations, such as on [[cover art]], [[promotional images|promotional image]], and in-game [[characters|character]]. There is no given reason for the Pokémon to be wearing cosmetic gear such as bandanas, most likely to breathe more life into the characters due to the nature of the game. Pokémon in these games do not wear other non-neck clothing items.<>
