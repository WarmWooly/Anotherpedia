Clubs Deuce voice acting

"Clubs Deuce voice acting" is a YouTube video made by Afterglow Ampharos on February 17, 2013, where he voices the Homestuck character Clubs Deuce. The passages and imagry are taken from the Homestuck: Intermission pages 3076--3081. The pages depict Clubs Deuce after successfully capturing Doze for interrogation, before trying different methods to get Doze to crack. Someone also voices Spades Slick, but the identity is unknown. The video is 1 minute and 36 seconds in length.
