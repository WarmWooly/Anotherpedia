Coal (Mindustry)

Coal is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Mechanical Drill or higher. The description of Coal is the following:Used for fuel and refined material production.It has an Explosiveness of 20%, a Flammability of 100%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. On destruction, large fires can break out, making it valuable to keep Waves and Tsunamis nearby. It is used in the early-game for power, ammo, and to produce items such as Graphite and Silicon.
