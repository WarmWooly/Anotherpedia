Coal (Mindustry)

Coal is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Mechanical Drill (Mindustry) or higher. The description of Coal is the following:It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 20%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 100%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. On destruction, large fires can break out, making it valuable to keep Wave (Mindustry) and Tsunami (Mindustry) nearby. It is used in the early-game for power (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and to produce items such as graphite (mindustry) and silicon (mindustry).
