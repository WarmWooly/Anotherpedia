Coal (Minecraft)

<>Coal is an item (Minecraft) in Minecraft that is often used in smelting (Minecraft). When Coal Ore (Minecraft) is mining (Minecraft) with a pickaxe (minecraft), it will drop 1 coal. When mined with Fortune (Minecraft), it has a chance to drop an extra piece of coal with each level, with a maximum of 4 coal using Fortune III. Silk Touch (Minecraft) will prevent the ore from dropping coal. Coal can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 block of coal (Minecraft), which will produce 9 coal. Coal ore can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or blast furnace (Minecraft) to produce 1 coal, alongside experience (Minecraft).
