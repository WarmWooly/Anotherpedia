Coal (Minecraft)

<>Coal is an item in Minecraft that is often used in smelting. When coal ore is mined with a pickaxe, it will drop 1 coal. When mined with Fortune, it has a chance to drop an extra piece of coal with each level, with a maximum of 4 coal using Fortune III. Silk Touch will prevent the ore from dropping coal. Coal can be crafted with 1 block of coal, which will produce 9 coal. Coal ore can be smelted in a furnace or blast furnace to produce 1 coal, alongside < 30% chance).(text=u-d0.1note>> experience. The chances to find coal in suspicious blocks are the following (same for Bedrock and Java unless specified):&spIn ocean ruins, a suspicious block has a 13.3% chance of containing 1 coal.&spIn trail ruins, a suspicious block has a 2.2%s-p(Java only) chance of containing 1 coal. Wither skeletons have a 33.3% chance of dropping 1 coal on death. When slain with Looting, it has a chance to drop 1 more coal per level. The chance for it to drop coal is 1 - 2 / (Looting + 3), which equals out to 1/3, 1/2, 3/5, and 2/3 for no Looting, I, II, and III respectively. The chance to get more coal with Looting is equally spread (ex. Looting II --> 40% 0 coal, 20% 1 coal, 20% 2 coal, 20% 3 coal).
