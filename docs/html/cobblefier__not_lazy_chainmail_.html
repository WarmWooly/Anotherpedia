Cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

For the general category of items used to alter the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail), see cobblefiers ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
<>The cobblefier is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as a base for crafting other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail) for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail). Cobblefiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 cobblestone (Minecraft) and 1 paper (Minecraft) (shapeless). Cobblefiers are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Andesitifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 1 dioritifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Basaltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 1 blue ice (Minecraft), and 1 soul sand (Minecraft)/soul soil (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the ice above, and the soul block below).
Broken Cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 1 water bucket (Minecraft), and 1 lava bucket (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the lava in the middle, and the water above).
Calcifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 2 bone block (Minecraft), and 2 diamond (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the diamonds above and below, and the bone blocks to the left and right).
Crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 1 piston (Minecraft), and 1 block of iron (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the iron in the middle, and the piston above).
Deepifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 8 cobbled deepslate (Minecraft) (surround the cobblefier with cobbled deepslate).
Dioritifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 8 quartz (Minecraft) (surround the cobblefier with quartz).
Dirtifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 8 dirt (Minecraft) (surround the cobblefier with dirt).
Dripifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 1 wetifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Endifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 4 chorus fruit (Minecraft), and 4 ender pearl (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the pearls on the sides, and the fruit in the corners).
Hauntifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 1 flint and steel (Minecraft), and 1 soul sand (Minecraft)/soul soil (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier on top, the flint and steel in the middle, and the soul block below).
Marinefier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 8 prismarine shard (Minecraft) (surround the cobblefier with prismarine shards).
Mossifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 2 moss block (Minecraft)/vines (Minecraft), and 2 bone meal (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the bone meal above and below, and the plants to the left and right).
Smeltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier, 1 furnace (Minecraft), and 1 block of coal (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier in the center, the furnace above, and the coal below).
Wetifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 cobblefier and 1 moss'n'mossifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless). Cobblefiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create cobblestone. The generator will create cobblestone even without the cobblefier. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
