Cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

For the general category of items used to alter the Cobblestone Generator, see cobblefiers ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
The cobblefier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a base for crafting other cobblefiers for the Cobblestone Generator. Cobblefiers can be crafted using 1 cobblestone and 1 paper (shapeless). Cobblefiers are used in the following crafting recipes:
Andesitifier - 1 cobblefier and 1 dioritifier (shapeless).
Basaltifier - 1 cobblefier, 1 blue ice, and 1 soul sand/soul soil (place the cobblefier in the center, the ice above, and the soul block below).
Broken Cobblefier - 1 cobblefier, 1 water bucket, and 1 lava bucket (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the lava in the middle, and the water above).
Calcifier - 1 cobblefier, 2 bone blocks, and 2 diamonds (place the cobblefier in the center, the diamonds above and below, and the bone blocks to the left and right).
Crushifier - 1 cobblefier, 1 piston, and 1 block of iron (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the iron in the middle, and the piston above).
Deepifier - 1 cobblefier and 8 cobbled deepslate (surround the cobblefier with cobbled deepslate).
Dioritifer - 1 cobblefier and 8 quartz (surround the cobblefier with quartz).
Dirtifier - 1 cobblefier and 8 dirt (surround the cobblefier with dirt).
Dripifier - 1 cobblefier and 1 wetifier (shapeless).
Endifier - 1 cobblefier, 4 chorus fruit, and 4 ender pearls (place the cobblefier in the center, the pearls on the sides, and the fruit in the corners).
Hauntifier - 1 cobblefier, 1 flint and steel, and 1 soul sand/soul soil (place the cobblefier on top, the flint and steel in the middle, and the soul block below).
Marinefier - 1 cobblefier and 8 prismarine shards (surround the cobblefier with prismarine shards).
Mossifier - 1 cobblefier, 2 moss blocks/vines, and 2 bone meal (place the cobblefier in the center, the bone meal above and below, and the plants to the left and right).
Smeltifier - 1 cobblefier, 1 furnace, and 1 block of coal (place the cobblefier in the center, the furnace above, and the coal below).
Wetifier - 1 cobblefier and 1 moss'n'mossifier (shapeless). Cobblefiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create cobblestone. The generator will create cobblestone even without the cobblefier. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
