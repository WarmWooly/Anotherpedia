Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The cobblestone generator is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to generate items, including cobblestone. The cobblestone generator can be crafted with 1 crude engine, 3 cobblestone, 3 iron ingots, 1 water bucket, and 1 lava bucket (fill the top row with cobblestone and bottom row with iron, place the engine in the middle and the buckets to the left and right). Crafting a cobblestone generator will award the advancement Life Generator. The cobblestone generator requires an industrial belt to function, with better belts increasing throughput. A cobblestone generator can take cobblefiers, which modifies the output, but none is required to create cobblestone. Different modifiers will apply a penalty to the rate at which items are generated as a multiplier (ex. 4x is 4 times slower than base).
