</Wooden pickaxe (Minecraft)||blast resistance (Minecraft)|6||hardness (Minecraft)|1.5||Luminous (Minecraft)|No||Opacity (Minecraft)|Yes||Waterlogging (Minecraft)|Yes||{{bFlammable (Minecraft)|No||Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)|Noinfo>>The cogwheel is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Create (Minecraft mod) used in carrying rotational power (Create). It can both carry rotation along its shaft to the front and back of it and to its sides to any connected cogwheels, large cogwheel (Create), and other blocks requiring rotational power. A cogwheel can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 shaft (Create) and 1 planks (Minecraft) (shapeless). A cogwheel can be crafted with a mechanical mixer (Create) mixing 1 shaft (Create) and 1 planks (Minecraft) in a basin (Create). A cogwheel can be created when a deployer (Create) places a planks (Minecraft) onto a shaft (Create) on a depot (Create) or mechanical belt (Create). When making a precision mechanism (Create), there is a 3.4% chance of a cogwheel being produced instead. A cogwheel can transfer rotational power (Create) to adjacent interlocking cogwheels. Cogwheels can also connect to shaft (Create), blocks with embedded shafts (i.e. water wheel (Create)), and blocks with embedded cogwheels (i.e. millstone (Create)). When a cogwheel transfers rotational power to another cogwheel, the rotation direction (Create) of the rotation is reversed. This can be used to alter the effects of machines (such as making an encased fan (Create) suck instead of blow) or change the direction of mechanical belt (Create).

When placing cogwheels, they can be quickly placed adjacent to each other, with a ghost image showing where the cogwheel will be placed. If the player is wearing engineer's goggles (Create), a particle effect will show the direction of the placed cogwheel. If a cogwheel goes above 256 RPM, is placed going in the same direction as another cogwheel, or is placed going the opposite direction of a shaft, it will automatically break and drop. When a cogwheel is diagonal to a large cogwheel (Create), the RPM of the cogwheels gets halved/doubled. If a large cogwheel is powering a cogwheel, the RPM will double, and if the cogwheel is powering a large cogwheel, the RPM will halve. This gearshifting can be used to increase or decrease the RPM to increase the speed of machines or decrease the stress (Create) used. When a cogwheel and a large cogwheel are connected, the advancement (Minecraft) Shifting Gears (Create) will be awarded. Cogwheels can be encased using andesite casing (Create) and brass casing (Create). This serves both as a decorative covering to hide or integrate cogwheels into builds, and to prevent shafts/cogwheels from connecting to the cogwheel. A wrench (Create) can be used to create openings for shafts to connect to the encased cogwheel. Cogwheels are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
