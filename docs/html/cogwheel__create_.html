It can both carry rotation along its shaft to the front and back of it and to its sides to any connected cogwheels, [[large cogwheels|large cogwheel (Create)]], and other blocks requiring rotational power.<><>A cogwheel can be [[crafted|crafting (Minecraft)]] with 1 [[shaft|shaft (Create)]] and 1 [[plank|planks (Minecraft)]] (shapeless).<>A cogwheel can be crafted with a [[mechanical mixer|mechanical mixer (Create)]] mixing 1 [[shaft|shaft (Create)]] and 1 [[plank|planks (Minecraft)]] in a [[basin|basin (Create)]].<>A cogwheel can be created when a [[deployer|deployer (Create)]] places a [[plank|planks (Minecraft)]] onto a [[shaft|shaft (Create)]] on a [[depot|depot (Create)]] or [[mechanical belt|mechanical belt (Create)]].<>When making a [[precision mechanism|precision mechanism (Create)]], there is a 3.4% chance of a cogwheel being produced instead.<><><>A cogwheel can transfer [[rotational power|rotational power (Create)]] to adjacent interlocking cogwheels. Cogwheels can also connect to [[shafts|shaft (Create)]], blocks with embedded shafts (i.e. [[water wheels|water wheel (Create)]]), and blocks with embedded cogwheels (i.e. [[millstones|millstone (Create)]]). When a cogwheel transfers rotational power to another cogwheel, the [[direction|rotation direction (Create)]] of the rotation is reversed. This can be used to alter the effects of machines (such as making an [[encased fan|encased fan (Create)]] suck instead of blow) or change the direction of [[mechanical belts|mechanical belt (Create)]].&pWhen placing cogwheels, they can be quickly placed adjacent to each other, with a ghost image showing where the cogwheel will be placed. If the player is wearing [[engineer's goggles|engineer's goggles (Create)]], a particle effect will show the direction of the placed cogwheel. If a cogwheel goes above 256 RPM, is placed going in the same direction as another cogwheel, or is placed going the opposite direction of a shaft, it will automatically break and drop.<><>When a cogwheel is diagonal to a [[large cogwheel|large cogwheel (Create)]], the RPM of the cogwheels gets halved/doubled. If a large cogwheel is powering a cogwheel, the RPM will double, and if the cogwheel is powering a large cogwheel, the RPM will halve. This gearshifting can be used to increase or decrease the RPM to increase the speed of machines or decrease the [[stress|stress (Create)]] used.
