Colt M4A1 (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The Colt M4A1 is a primary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Mobile Task Force at level 1. While it does mediocre damage with a fast firerate, its longer reload and high vertical recoil can make it difficult to keep steady in battle.
