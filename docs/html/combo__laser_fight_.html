Combo (Laser Fight)

A combo in Laser Fight is an experience bonus given for completing various movement actions in quick succession within a specific order. Rolls are the only exception, as they are a unique variation of sliding out of a fall break. These simple chains are awarded by repeating a simple move multiple times (slides, dives, and vaults).
