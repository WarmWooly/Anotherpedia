Connection

For other terms related to connection, see connection (disambiguation).
A connection is a physical or conceptual association between objects, people, or ideas. Connections, in the physical sense, may be an intermediate object that keeps things together (i.e. a rope tied around a fence is connected to it). Connections may be a recognition of a shared property or state (i.e. seeing how two different objects are the same color). The connection between things and how they interact is known as a relationship.
