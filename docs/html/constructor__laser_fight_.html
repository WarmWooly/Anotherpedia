Constructor (Laser Fight)

The Constructor is a large block-placing gun in Laser Fight. The blocks placed last for a long time and can be used to block off parts of the map. They can also be used to climb or launch to higher locations as well. Killing someone with the Constructor will award the badge Dizzy the Cement Mixer.
