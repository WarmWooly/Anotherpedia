For the video game, see {{aiContent Warning (video game).}}
A content warning (CW) is a warning that the content of a work or where works are shared may be distressing, offensive, inappropriate, or triggering. A trigger warning is given before content based on certain fears or traumas, while a content warning can apply to a broader range of topics. Content warning may be given through standardized means, like Parental Advisory and other content ratings, or informally as a disclaimer by a creator or distributor.
