contradan_cosplay

Daniel Giovannucci, known online as contradan_cosplay (also written as Contradan Cosplay or Contradan) is an American cosplayer who attends various conventions, especially within the Boston area. He's most notable for his cosplay of Doug Dimmadome, complete with a massive hat, where he hands out "Dimm-Dollars" with his face and social media accounts. He has attended PAX and Anime Boston multiple times. He is most active on his Instagram and Facebook accounts, but he also streams on Twitch.
