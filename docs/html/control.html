Control

For other terms related to control, see control (disambiguation).&spControl is the ability to influence how someone or something behaves. This can come in many forms, such as flipping a switch to change the state of a system, asking other people to do specific actions, or being able to enact decisions with less restrictions. The thing that controls is known as a controller, may it be a person, animal, or automatic system.
