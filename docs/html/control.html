Control

<>{{tFor other terms related to control, see [[control (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>{{bControl}} is the [[ability]] to [[influence]] how someone or something [[behaves|behavior]]. This can come in many forms, such as flipping a [[switch]] to change the [[state]] of a [[system]], [[asking|ask]] other people to do specific [[actions|action]], or being able to enact [[decisions|decision]] with less [[restrictions|restriction]]. The thing that controls is known as a [[controller]], may it be a person, [[animal]], or [[automatic system]].
