Control Room (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Control Room is a room in the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp that is used to keep track of the Facility status and control the alarm system. The room is located in Sector 1 of the Facility labeled "Control Room". The room requires a Level 4 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp) to enter.

In all four corners of the room lie server systems, and in the middle of the room, there are two desks, each with pairs of computers and chairs. On top of the left desk, a mug can be seen, and above the door, a analog clock is also visible. Three TVs can be seen on the back wall of the room, containing the generator room (SCP: Devolved Revamp) fuel level, the Facility status, and the main gate's structural stability. At the top of the room, there are two LED panel that light the room up, and in the back middle of the room, the control panel can be seen. There are two chairs at the panel and four buttons in this order from left to right: Red, Orange, Blue, and White.
