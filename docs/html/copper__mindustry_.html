Copper (Mindustry)

Copper is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Mechanical Drill or higher. It can also be harvested from a Separator. The description of Copper is the following:Used in all types of construction and ammunition.It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in the early-game buildings, ammo, and units. It is also used to produce Surge Alloy.
