Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The copper chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Copper Chain". The chain takes 8 copper chain links to craft. The copper chain can be crafted with a block of copper chains (makes 4). Right-clicking an exposed copper chain or a waxed copper chain with an axe will turn the block into a copper chain. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. Copper chains can be crushed into 8 copper chain links. Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a copper chain will become an exposed copper chain.
