Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The copper chain can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Copper Chain". The chain takes 8 copper chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. The copper chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with a block of copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4). Right-clicking an exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a copper chain. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement).
