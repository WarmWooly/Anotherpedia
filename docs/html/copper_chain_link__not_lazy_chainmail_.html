Copper Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper chain link is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Copper chain links can be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) using pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) from a copper coil (Not Lazy Chainmail) or an unfinished copper project (Not Lazy Chainmail). While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Copper chain links can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes a copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), oxidized copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), waxed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), waxed exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), waxed weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), or waxed oxidized copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 8 chain links). In a Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail), 9 copper chain links can be recycled into a copper nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail). copper chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail) enchanted with Chain Repair (Not Lazy Chainmail) will automatically take a copper chain link from the inventory (Minecraft) to repair durability (Minecraft) when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Copper chain links can be converted into Forge Energy (Minecraft modding) in a Chain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail).
