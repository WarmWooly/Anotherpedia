Copper Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Copper chain links can be snipped using pliers from a copper coil or an unfinished copper project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Copper chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes a copper chain, exposed copper chain, weathered copper chain, oxidized copper chain, waxed copper chain, waxed exposed copper chain, waxed weathered copper chain, or waxed oxidized copper chain (makes 8 chain links). Copper chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:&spCopper Chain - 8 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Belt - 128 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Boots - 256 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Bracelet - 64 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Chestplate - 512 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Gloves - 192 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Helmet - 320 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 copper chain links&spCopper Chainmail Leggings - 448 copper chain link&spCopper Chainmail Necklace - 128 copper chain links&spCopper Tool Chain - 192 copper chain links&spMusic Disc "Company" - 32 copper chain links For Novice-level chainmaillers, there is a 22.22% chance to trade 64 copper chain links for 3 emeralds. In a Recycler, 9 copper chain links can be recycled into a copper nugget. Copper chainmail armor enchanted with Chain Repair will automatically take a copper chain link from the inventory to repair durability when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Copper chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator.
