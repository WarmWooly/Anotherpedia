Copper Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Copper chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Copper Industrial Belt". The copper chainmail industrial belt takes 256 copper chain links to craft. Crafting the copper chainmail industrial belt will award the advancement Upgrades, People, Upgrades! The chances to find a copper chainmail industrial belt in a winding jig are the following:&spIn a winding jig ruins, there is a 14.26% chance to contain a copper chainmail industrial belt.&spIn a chainmailler home, there is a 27.27% chance to contain a copper chainmail industrial belt. Copper chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:&spCopper Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 copper chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 copper chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot).<> The copper chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:&spAuto Mailler&spAuto Snipper&spAuto Winder&spBelt Generator&spChain Generator&spCobblestone Generator&spConveyor Warper&spCrusher&spItem Aggregator&spMelder&spRecycler&spWinding Jig Copper chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished copper chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Copper chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining copper chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Copper chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Copper chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on copper chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels. Copper chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy in a belt generator, generating 2 FE/t. gal
