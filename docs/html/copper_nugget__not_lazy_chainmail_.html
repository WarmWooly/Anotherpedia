Copper Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The copper nugget is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting (Minecraft) low-tier chainmail materials. Copper nuggets can be crafting (Minecraft) with a copper ingot (Minecraft), yielding 9 nuggets. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain copper nuggets. Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of copper nuggets from sieving.<> Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail) 9 copper chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) will return a copper nugget.
