Copper Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The copper nugget is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting low-tier chainmail materials. Copper nuggets can be crafted with a copper ingot, yielding 9 nuggets. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain copper nuggets. Separation can increase the chance/drops of copper nuggets from sieving.<> Recycling 9 copper chain links will return a copper nugget. The chances to find a copper nugget in chests are the following:
In a winding jig ruins, a chest has an 89.26% chance to contain 1-3 copper nuggets. Copper nuggets are used in the following crafting recipes:
Copper Ingot - 9 copper nuggets (fill the grid).
Copper Nunchucks - 4 copper nuggets, 1 copper tool chain, and 2 sticks (nuggets go in all four corners, the sticks to the left and right, and the tool chain on the bottom).
Copper Spiked Chain Carpet x3 - 6 copper nuggets and 3 copper chain carpets (carpets fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor x3 - 6 copper nuggets and 3 copper chain conveyors (conveyors fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Copper Wire - 8 copper nuggets (fill the grid besides the middle slot).
Exposed Copper Spiked Chain Carpet x3 - 6 copper nuggets and 3 exposed copper chain carpets (carpets fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Exposed Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor x3 - 6 copper nuggets and 3 exposed copper chain conveyors (conveyors fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
