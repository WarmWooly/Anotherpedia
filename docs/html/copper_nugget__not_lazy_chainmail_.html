Copper Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bcopper nugget}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used for crafting (Minecraft) low-tier chainmail materials. Copper nuggets can be crafting (Minecraft) with a copper ingot (Minecraft), yielding 9 nuggets. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain copper nuggets.
