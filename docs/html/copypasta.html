Copypasta

<><>{{bCopypasta}}, derived from [[copy|copy (computing)]] and [[pasta]] (a play on [[paste|paste (computing)]]), is an [[internet phenomenon]] where [[text]] is [[copied and pasted|cut, copy, and paste]] across [[chatrooms|chatroom]], [[forumns|forumn]], [[text messaging applications|text messaging application]], and other locations where [[people]] can openly [[communicate|communication]] [[online]]. Most copypastas are surrounded in [[irony]], making a [[joke]] based on an [[interaction]], [[object]], person, or [[character]]. They are [[bombastic]] and [[flashy]], with the main [[goal]] to [[confuse|confusion]] [[readers|reader]]. A significant portion of copypastas are [[defamitory]] and use [[profanity]] for an increased [[shock factor|shock value]].
