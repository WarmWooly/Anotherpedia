Core (optical fiber)

<><><>A {{bcore}} in an [[optical fiber]] is the centermost piece, surrounded by a [[cladding|cladding (optical fiber)]], a [[coating|coating (optical fiber)]], sometimes a [[strength member|strength member (optical fiber)]], and a [[jacket|jacket (optical fiber)]]. The core is made of [[glass]] or [[transparent]] [[plastic]] to allow [[light]] to pass through, with the cladding refracting the light as it passes through the core.
