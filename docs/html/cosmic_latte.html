Cosmic latte

<><>{{bCosmic latte}} is the [[name]] for a [[color]] found by [[astronomers|astronomer]] in the [[Johns Hopkins University]]. The original [[paper|scientific paper]] in 2002 by [[Karl Glazebrook]] and [[Ivan Baldry]] found that the average color of the [[universe]], as [[observed|observation]] from [[Earth]], was a greenish-white color, which was later [[corrected|correction]] in 2003 where it was found to be nearly [[white]]. The [[hex triplet]] for cosmic latte is {{b#FFF8E7}}.
