Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush

The Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush is a small Pokémon stuffed animal of the Pokémon Cradily under the Sitting Cuties plush collection in the fourth wave on January 30, 2021.<> The plush can be purchased on Pokémon Center for $16.99 USD <>.<> The plush is composed of several sewn-together pieces with embroidered eyes and body patterns. Weighted stuffing beads are used to keep the plush sitting upright. The plush stands at 5--6 in. based on the angle of its head. <>, the Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush has a rating of 4.9/5 stars. Multiple reviews suggest that the product is top-heavy due to the design of Cradily's head.
