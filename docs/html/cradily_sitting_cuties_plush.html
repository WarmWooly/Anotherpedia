Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush

The Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush is a small Pokémon stuffed animal of the Pokémon (fictional species) Cradily under the Sitting Cuties product collection in the Sitting Cuties releases on January 30, 2021. The plush can be purchased on Pokémon Center for $16.99 United States dollar <>. The plush is composed of several sewing pieces with embroidery eyes and body patterns. Weighted stuffing beads are used to keep the plush sitting upright. The plush stands at 5--6 inch based on the angle of its head. <>, the Cradily Sitting Cuties Plush has a rating of 4.9/5 star (rating). Multiple reviews suggest that the product is top-heavy due to the design of Cradily's head. Here are a few ratings:
