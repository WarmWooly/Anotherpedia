Create (Minecraft mod)

{{bCreate}} is a Minecraft Minecraft modding focused around Minecraft automation, while also covering Minecraft building, Minecraft transportation, and other aspects through its dynamic contraption (Create) system. The mod has a contributors of Create, with the owner of the project being simibubi. The mod is widely popular, garnering over a hundred million downloads on CurseForge in late-2024. It adds various Create blocks and Create items for Decoration in Minecraft and automation, utilizing rotational force (Create) through blocks like shaft (Create) and cogwheel (Create) to transfer stress unit (Create). Create is available on Forge (Minecraft mod loader) and NeoForge, primarily on Minecraft 1.20.1 and Minecraft 1.21.1.
