Create (Minecraft mod)

{{bCreate}} is a Minecraft mod focused around automation, while also covering building, transportation, and other aspects through its dynamic contraption system. The mod has a team of developers and artists, with the owner of the project being simibubi. The mod is widely popular, garnering over a hundred million downloads on CurseForge in late-2024. It adds various blocks and items for decoration and automation, utilizing rotational force through blocks like shafts and cogwheels to transfer stress. Create is available on Forge and NeoForge, primarily on 1.20.1 and 1.21.1.
