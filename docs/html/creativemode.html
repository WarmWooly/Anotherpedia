CreativeMode

CreativeMode (<>) is a generative artificial intelligence Minecraft mod creator, founded in 2024 and released in 2025. The service can create mods for blocks, items, mobs, and structures <>, with biomes and complete mod packages "coming soon". It can create the mechanics and textures with code and image generation from a text prompt. The company was founded by Wilson Spearman, Jeffrey Tsaw, and Coleman Smith.
