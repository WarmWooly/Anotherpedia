Crimson Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Crimson bread is food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. It can be eaten twice as fast as most other foods. crimson flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce crimson bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft). Crimson bread can be eaten as food (Minecraft). As it has effects, crimson bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. Crimson bread only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
