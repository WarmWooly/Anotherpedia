Crimson Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Crimson bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. It can be eaten twice as fast as most other foods. Crimson flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce crimson bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience. Crimson bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Crimson bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, crimson bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. Crimson bread only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
