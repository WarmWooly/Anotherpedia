Crimson Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Crimson flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Crimson flour can be baked into crimson bread. Crimson flour can be obtained by milling crimson fungi on a millstone. Crimson flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce crimson bread, taking 4 or 2 seconds respectively and giving experience.
