Crimson Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Crimson flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. Crimson flour can be baked into crimson bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Crimson flour can be obtained by milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) crimson fungus (Minecraft) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Crimson flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce crimson bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), taking 4 or 2 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft).
