Crine (slang)

Crine (pronounced cryin') is slang for crying, similar to lol or lmao, appearing across TikTok, often in the form of "I'm crine" with the loudly crying face emoji (😭). Crine has been around since the early-2010s, but it mainly grew in popularity in 2025 and 2026 along with Gen Alpha slang like ahh (slang) and lowkenuinely. Crine tends to be used for situations that are absurdity.
