Crude Engine (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crude engine is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting machines and items. Crude engines can be melded in a Melder using 1 block of iron, 1 kelp/leather industrial belt, and 1 piston. Crude engines are used in the following crafting recipes:&spChain Generator - 1 crude engine, 5 cobblestone, 2 rose gold ingots, and 1 recycler (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).&spChainsaw - 1 crude engine, 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 1 iron ingot, and 2 iron nuggets (place the iron ingot in the middle, the engine to the left, the nuggets in the two slots below the engine and ingot, and the belt in the top right corner).&spChainsword - 1 crude engine, 1 netherite sword, 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt, and 2 iron composite ingots (place the sword in the middle, the iron composite to the bottom and left, the belt in the top-right corner, and the engine in the bottom-left corner).&spCobblestone Generator - 1 crude engine, 3 cobblestone, 3 iron ingots, 1 water bucket, and 1 lava bucket (place the engine in the middle, fill the bottom row with iron and the top row with cobblestone, and place the buckets to the left and right).&spConveyor Manager x4 - 1 crude engine, 4 spool blocks, and 4 rose gold ingots (place the engine in the middle, the spool blocks on the sides, and the rose gold in the corners).&spSort Dropper x4 - 1 crude engine, 1 dropper, and 1 chain conveyor (place the dropper in the middle, with the engine below and the conveyor above).
