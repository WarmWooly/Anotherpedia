Crush'n'crush'n'redifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crush'n'crush'redifier is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce red sand (Minecraft). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 crush'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 4 red dye (Minecraft) (surround the crush'n'crushifier with the dye). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Crush'n'crush'n'smeltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crush'n'crush'n'redifier and 1 smeltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create red sand (Minecraft). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers take 16x as long to generate one sand compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
