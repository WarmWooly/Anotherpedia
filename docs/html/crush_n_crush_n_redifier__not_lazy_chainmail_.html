Crush'n'crush'n'redifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crush'n'crush'redifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce red sand. Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers can be crafted using 1 crush'n'crushifier and 4 red dye (surround the crush'n'crushifier with the dye). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers are used in the following crafting recipes:&spCrush'n'crush'n'smeltifier - 1 crush'n'crush'n'redifier and 1 smeltifier (shapeless). Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create red sand. Crush'n'crush'n'redifiers take 16x as long to generate one sand compared to one cobblestone. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector alongside the 51 other cobblefiers, the player will be awarded the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
