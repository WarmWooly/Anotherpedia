Crush'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The crush'n'crushifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce sand. Crush'n'crushifiers can be crafted using 2 crushifiers (shapeless). Crush'n'crushifiers are used in the following crafting recipes:
Crush'n'crush'n'hauntifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 1 hauntifier (shapeless).
Crush'n'crush'n'redifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 4 red dye (surround the crush'n'crushifier with the dye).
Crush'n'crush'n'smeltifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 1 smeltifier (shapeless). Crush'n'crushifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create sand. Crush'n'crushifiers take 16x as long to generate one sand compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
