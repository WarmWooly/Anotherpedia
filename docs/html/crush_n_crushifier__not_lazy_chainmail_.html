Crush'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crush'n'crushifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce sand. Crush'n'crushifiers can be crafted using 2 crushifiers (shapeless). Crush'n'crushifiers are used in the following crafting recipes:&spCrush'n'crush'n'hauntifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 1 hauntifier (shapeless).&spCrush'n'crush'n'redifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 4 red dye (surround the crush'n'crushifier with the dye).&spCrush'n'crush'n'smeltifier - 1 crush'n'crushifier and 1 smeltifier (shapeless). Crush'n'crushifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create sand. Crush'n'crushifiers take 16x as long to generate one sand compared to one cobblestone. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector alongside the 51 other cobblefiers, the player will be awarded the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
