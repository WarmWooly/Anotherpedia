Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crusher is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to crush item (Minecraft). The crusher can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), 4 cobblestone (Minecraft), 1 block of iron (Minecraft), and 1 piston (Minecraft) (place the block of iron in the middle, piston above the iron, fill the bottom row with rose gold, then fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
