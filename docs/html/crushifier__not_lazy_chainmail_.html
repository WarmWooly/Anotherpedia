Crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The crushifier is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce gravel (Minecraft). Crushifiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 piston (Minecraft), and 1 block of iron (Minecraft) (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the iron in the middle, and the piston above). Crushifiers are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Crush'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 crushifiers (shapeless).
Deep'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier and 1 deepifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Deep'n'crush'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier and 1 deep'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Dirt'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier and 1 dirtifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
End'b'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier and 1 endifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Haunt'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier and 1 hauntifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless).
Wet'n'crush'n'smeltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 crushifier, 1 smeltifier (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 wetifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless). Crushifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create gravel (Minecraft). Crushifiers take 8x as long to generate one gravel compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
