Crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The crushifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce gravel. Crushifiers can be crafted using 1 cobblefier, 1 piston, and 1 block of iron (place the cobblefier on the bottom, the iron in the middle, and the piston above). Crushifiers are used in the following crafting recipes:
Crush'n'crushifier - 2 crushifiers (shapeless).
Deep'n'crushifier - 1 crushifier and 1 deepifier (shapeless).
Deep'n'crush'n'crushifier - 1 crushifier and 1 deep'n'crushifier (shapeless).
Dirt'n'crushifier - 1 crushifier and 1 dirtifier (shapeless).
End'n'crushifier - 1 crushifier and 1 endifier (shapeless).
Haunt'n'crushifier - 1 crushifier and 1 hauntifier (shapeless).
Wet'n'crush'n'smeltifier - 1 crushifier, 1 smeltifier, and 1 wetifier (shapeless). Crushifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create gravel. Crushifiers take 8x as long to generate one gravel compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
