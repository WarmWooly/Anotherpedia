Cryofluid (Mindustry)

{{bCryofluid}} is a liquid (Mindustry) in {{iMindustry}} on Serpulo (Mindustry) and can be obtained through pools of cryofluid tiles<> on the surface using a Mechanical Pump (Mindustry) or better or by mixing Titanium (Mindustry) and Water (Mindustry) in a Cryofluid Mixer (Mindustry). The description of Cryofluid is the following:It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a heat capacity (Mindustry) of 90%, a viscosity (Mindustry) of 50%, and a temperature (Mindustry) of 25%. Cryofluid can be used as a liquid ammo (Mindustry), to generate power (Mindustry) in a Differential Generator (Mindustry), Thorium Reactor, or Impact Reactor (Mindustry), to create tier 4 and 5 unit (Mindustry), and to boost the fire rate of turret (Mindustry). When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit the Freezing (Mindustry) effect.
