Cryofluid (Mindustry)

Cryofluid is a liquid in Mindustry on Serpulo and can be obtained through pools of cryofluid tiles on the surface using a Mechanical Pump or better or by mixing Titanium and Water in a Cryofluid Mixer. The description of Cryofluid is the following:Used as a coolant in reactors, turrets and factories.It has an Exposiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Heat Capacity of 90%, a Viscosity of 50%, and a Temperature of 25%. Cryofluid can be used as a liquid ammo, to generate power in a Differential Generator, Thorium Reactor, or Impact Reactor, to create tier 4 and 5 units, and to boost the fire rate of turrets. When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit the Freezing effect.
