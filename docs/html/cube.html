Cube

A cube is a third dimension shape with 8 vertex connected with 12 line, making 6 square face (geometry). Each line is the same length and each forms a right angle. The width and height are equal to its length. The surface area is six multiplication the length multiplied by itself (l{{s-u2}} × 6 or l × w × 6). The volume is the length multiplied by itself twice (ls-u3 or l × w × h).
