Cube

<><>A {{bcube}} is a [[3D|third dimension]] [[shape]] with 8 [[vertices|vertex]] connected with 12 [[lines|line]], making 6 [[square]] [[faces|face (geometry)]]. Each line is the same [[length]] and each forms a [[90° angle|right angle]]. The [[width]] and [[height]] are [[equal]] to its length. The [[surface area]] is six [[times|multiplication]] the length multiplied by itself ({{il{{s-u2}}}} × 6 or {{il}} × {{iw}} × 6). The [[volume]] is the length multiplied by itself twice ({{il}}{{s-u3}} or {{il}} × {{iw}} × {{ih}}).
