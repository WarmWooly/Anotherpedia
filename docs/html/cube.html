Cube

A {{bcube}} is a third dimension shape with 8 vertex connected with 12 line, making 6 square face (geometry). Each line is the same length and each forms a right angle. The width and height are equal to its length. The surface area is six multiplication the length multiplied by itself ({{il{{s-u2}}}} × 6 or {{il}} × {{iw}} × 6). The volume is the length multiplied by itself twice ({{il}}{{s-u3}} or {{il}} × {{iw}} × {{ih}}).
