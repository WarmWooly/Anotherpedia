Culture

For the process of growing cell (biology), see cell culture.
Culture is the collective art and knowledge of human within a society, either bound to a region or nation, or as the entirety of humanity. Culture is built around social norms, belief, habit, law, institution, and power structure. People may be defined by their cultural associations, cultural heritage aspects of their culture. Enculturation is the process of a person learning the dynamics and aspects of a culture and adapting to such.
