d20

A d20 or 20-sided die is a dice with 20 side (an icosahedron). number are placed on each side of the die, where when roll, one of the sides will be face-up (the result of the roll). There is a 5% chance for any single number to appear on the die. Some d20s may have a unique symbol in place of the 1 and/or 20 due to their significance of being the highest and lowest values on the die.

d20s are often used in wargame, tabletop role-playing game (notably in the d20 System), board game, and as counter (collectible card games) in collectible card game, often alongside other-shaped dice. While dice can be made to have more sides than 20, like the d100 and d120, the d20 keeps a balance between being easy to roll, easy to read, and being granularity enough for most representations of complex events.
