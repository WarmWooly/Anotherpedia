Dark Long Knapped Flint Brick Stairs (Knapped)

<>Dark long knapped flint brick stairs are a stair in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Dark long knapped flint brick stairs can be crafted with 6 dark long knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center; makes 4). Dark long knapped flint brick stairs can be cut from dark long knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter.
