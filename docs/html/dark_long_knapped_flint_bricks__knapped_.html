Dark Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<>Dark long knapped flint bricks are a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used for brickwork-related decoration. Dark long knapped flint bricks can be crafting (Minecraft) with 8 long knapped flint bricks (Knapped) and 1 black dye (Minecraft) (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks; makes 8). Dark long knapped flint bricks can be cut from dark knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark basketweave knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark english knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark long chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark pinwheel knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark short knapped flint bricks (knapped), dark square knapped flint bricks (knapped), or dark stacked knapped flint bricks (knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft). Dark long knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Dark Long Knapped Flint Brick Slab (Knapped) x6 - 3 long knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).
Dark Long Knapped Flint Brick Stairs (Knapped) x4 - 6 long knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center). Dark long knapped flint bricks are used in the following stonecutter (Minecraft) recipes:
