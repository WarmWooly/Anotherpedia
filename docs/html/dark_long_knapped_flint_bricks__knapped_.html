Dark Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<>Dark long knapped flint bricks are a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Dark long knapped flint bricks can be crafted with 8 long knapped flint bricks and 1 black dye (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks; makes 8). Dark long knapped flint bricks can be cut from dark normal, basketweave, chevron, english, long chevron, pinwheel, short, square, or stacked knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter.
