Dark Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Dark long knapped flint bricks are a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Dark long knapped flint bricks can be crafted with 8 long knapped flint bricks and 1 black dye (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks; makes 8). Dark long knapped flint bricks can be cut from dark normal, basketweave, chevron, english, long chevron, pinwheel, short, square, or stacked knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter. Dark long knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Dark Long Knapped Flint Brick Slab x6 - 3 long knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).
Dark Long Knapped Flint Brick Stairs x4 - 6 long knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center). Dark long knapped flint bricks are used in the following stonecutting recipes:
