Dark maze

For other terms related to dark maze, see dark maze (disambiguation), and for blind maze, see blind maze (disambiguation).
A dark maze, blind maze, or pitch-black maze is a maze where its entirety is black or another solid color, or which the player is blind, making it impossible to discern corridors. These mazes can be constructed in real life or within video game. A dark maze is exceedingly challenging compared to a standard maze due to the inability to see, making it much more likely that corridors will be missed and that players will circle back to already treaded areas. tool given to the player are very useful, such as coordinates and a way to mapping out explored areas.
