Dawizard

For the website, see DA-Wizard.
Dawizard was an editing error within Encyclopedia Magica caused by an book editor replacing "mage" with "wizard", including in words such as image and damage (resulting in "iwizard" and "dawizard" respecively). After the error was book publishing in 1994, future prints would fix this error.
