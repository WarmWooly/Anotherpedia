Death (Laser Fight)

Death is a fail state for the player in Laser Fight (Roblox game), causing them to lose their current streak and any activated buffing kill streak (Laser Fight). Upon death, the player will ragdoll as skulls emerge from their body. If the death is caused by a laser, they will be zapped in place instead. The respawn screen will appear, informing the player of how they met their fate, a respawn button, and a button back to the menu. There are many ways for death to occur, including getting shot by a gun (Laser Fight), fall damage (Laser Fight), reset (Laser Fight), or falling off the map (Laser Fight). Dying for your first time awards the badge (Roblox) ded lol (Laser Fight). On death, a message will appear on the respawn screen for the player.
