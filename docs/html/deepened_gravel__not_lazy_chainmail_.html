Deepened Gravel (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Deepened gravel is a gravity-affected block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that generates among gravel in deepslate in the Overworld. Deepened gravel can generate in gravel between y-levels 3 to -64. Deepened gravel can be obtained when a Crusher crushes deepslate. A Cobblestone Generator with a deep'n'crushifier will produce deepened gravel. Deep'n'crushifiers take 16x as long to generate one deepened gravel compared to one cobblestone.
