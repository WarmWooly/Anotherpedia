Deepened Sand (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Deepened sand is a gravity-affected block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Deepened sand can be obtained when a Crusher crushes deepened gravel. A Cobblestone Generator with a deep'n'crush'n'crushifier will produce deepened sand. Deep'n'crush'n'crushifiers take 32x as long to generate one deepened sand compared to one cobblestone. Deepened sand can be sieved, producing various valuable items. Separation can increase the chance/drops from sieving deepened sand.<>
