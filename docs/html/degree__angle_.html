Degree (angle)

A degree, shown with the symbol °, is a mathematical term used to describe the measurement of an angle. A full rotation angle of an angle is 360 degrees. A degree is equal to pi/180 radian, or roughly 0.01745 radians. It is also 60 minute of arc and 3600 second of arc.

A round measuring device known as a protractor is used to measure degrees in the reality.
