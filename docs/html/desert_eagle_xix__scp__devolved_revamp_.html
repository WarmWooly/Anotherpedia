Desert Eagle XIX (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The Desert Eagle XIX (or Deagle) is a gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Administrative Branch at level 1. Its low ammo capacity and firerate are supplemented by a very high damage per bullet. Considering that it's the only weapon AB members have, it's better than nothing!
