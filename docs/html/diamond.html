Diamond

For the mineral in the sandbox game aiMinecraft, see diamond (aiMinecraft).
A diamond is a form of carbon with the atoms arranged in a crystal structure. Diamonds have the most hardness of any natural material. Because of this, it is commonly used in industrial applications, such as cutting or polishing tools. Although they are more often cut and used to adorn jewelry, or as a sign of wealth, due to their rarity.
