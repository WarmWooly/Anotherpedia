Diamond (Minecraft)

<>The diamond is an rare gemstone item (Minecraft) in Minecraft that is used in crafting (Minecraft) powerful tool (Minecraft), weapons (Minecraft), diamond armor (Minecraft), and functional block (Minecraft). Diamonds are also used for duplicating smithing template (Minecraft). Diamonds, especially in Block of Diamond (Minecraft), are seen as signs of wealth and are often used as a currency among Minecraft server. When obtaining a diamond, the advancement (Minecraft) Diamonds! (Minecraft advancement) or the achievement (Minecraft) DIAMONDS! (Minecraft achievement) When Diamond Ore (Minecraft) is mining (Minecraft) with an iron pickaxe (minecraft) or better, it will drop 1 diamond. When mined with Fortune (Minecraft), it has a chance to drop an extra diamond with each level, with a maximum of 4 diamonds using Fortune III. Silk Touch (Minecraft) will prevent the ore from dropping a diamond. Diamonds can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 block of diamond (Minecraft), which will produce 9 diamonds. Diamond ore can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or blast furnace (Minecraft) to produce 1 diamond, alongside 1 experience (Minecraft).
