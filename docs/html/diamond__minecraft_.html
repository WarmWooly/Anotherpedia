Diamond (Minecraft)

<>The diamond is an rare gemstone item in Minecraft that is used in crafting powerful tools, weapons, armor, and functional blocks. Diamonds are also used for duplicating smithing templates. Diamonds, especially in block form, are seen as signs of wealth and are often used as a currency among multiplayer servers. When obtaining a diamond, the advancement Diamonds! or the achievement DIAMONDS! When diamond ore is mined with an iron pickaxe or better, it will drop 1 diamond. When mined with Fortune, it has a chance to drop an extra diamond with each level, with a maximum of 4 diamonds using Fortune III. Silk Touch will prevent the ore from dropping a diamond. Diamonds can be crafted with 1 block of diamond, which will produce 9 diamonds. Diamond ore can be smelted in a furnace or blast furnace to produce 1 diamond, alongside 1 experience.
