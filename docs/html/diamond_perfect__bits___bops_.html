Diamond Perfect (Bits & Bops)

A Diamond Perfect in Bits & Bops is the highest completion of a minigame or mixtape. It is indicated with a blue-and-white Perfect stamp on the postcard and a blue crown on the minigame or mixtape. A cyan crown will also be listed on the save file, with the number matching the total Diamond Perfects completed. The text and photo for the postcard will be the same as Perfect, and the rewards are also the same (a souvenir, record, and video), if they are not already obtained. If a Diamond Perfect is completed after a Perfect, it will override the video's contents, even after obtaining.

A Diamond Perfect is achieved by hitting all the notes on time, which is denoted with a special indicator compared to a hit that is slightly ahead or behind (for example, the snout of the Seal in Flipper Snapper having an orange/yellow indicator around it). If a single note or more is off or completely missed, then a Diamond Perfect can't be achieved. There is no indicator mid-game to determine if a run is on track for a Diamond Perfect.
