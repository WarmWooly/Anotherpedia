Diamond Perfect (Bits & Bops)

A Diamond Perfect in Bits & Bops is the highest completion of a minigames (Bits & Bops) or mixtapes (Bits & Bops). It is indicated with a blue-and-white Perfect stamp on the postcards (Bits & Bops) and a blue crown (symbol) on the minigame or mixtape. A cyan crown will also be listed on the save files (Bits & Bops), with the number matching the total Diamond Perfects completed. The text and photo for the postcard will be the same as Perfect (Bits & Bops), and the rewards are also the same (a souvenir (Bits & Bops), records (Bits & Bops), and videos (Bits & Bops)), if they are not already obtained. If a Diamond Perfect is completed after a Perfect, it will override the video's contents, even after obtaining.

A Diamond Perfect is achieved by hitting all the notes on time, which is denoted with a special indicator compared to a hit that is slightly ahead or behind (for example, the snout of the Seal (Bits & Bops) in Flipper Snapper (Bits & Bops) having an orange/yellow indicator around it). If a single note or more is off or completely missed, then a Diamond Perfect can't be achieved. There is no indicator mid-game to determine if a run is on track for a Diamond Perfect.
