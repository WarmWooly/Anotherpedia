Dice

Dice (singular noun: die) are small, semi-rollable object that are tossed onto tables to get a random number or value. They are often used in things like board game, TTRPG, or luck-based games, such as in casino.

Dice will be a shape that has the same sides so as to have an equal chance of getting any value on the dice. Traditionally, dice are cubes with dot (also known as pips) adding up to a number on each side; numbers or symbol may be carved on each side. Dice may be abbreviated as a d + (number of sides), such as a d20 or d12. Common shapes of dice include a tetrahedron (d4), cube (d6), octahedron (d8), dodecahedron (d12), or a icosahedron (d20).

Loaded dice are dice that are in some way messed with to increase or guarantee the chance of rolling a specific value, with things such as rounded faces, inner weights, or off-square faces.
