Dictionary of Affixes

The Dictionary of Affixes is a website by the etymologist Michael Quinion used to definition affix (prefix, suffix and infix). The website consists of an alphabetical order section to find affixes (with J and K combined into one section), alongside information about the website, the webmaster, and other affix-adjacent content. There is also a hyperlink to his other website World Wide Words and a website donation section.
