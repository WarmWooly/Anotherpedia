Dino Pet

For the simulation game, see {{aiDino Pets.}}
The Dino Pet (trademarked as DinoPet) is a plastic culture-holding toy that is loosely shaped like a dinosaur, which is currently sold by Urbz since their procurement of BioPop in 2017. It was released in 2014 under BioPop after a Kickstarter campaign. The toy holds a culture of bioluminescent dinoflagellate, which glow a cyan-blue color when shaken or agitated. It has four legs, a body, and a head, with most of the toy being fillable. It requires sunlight and nutrients to sustain the culture, with additional packages of nutrients and dinoflagellates also sold.
