Dinossom

Dinossom is a dinosaur-like plant-adorned Grass element (Palworld) and Dragon element (Palworld) Pals (fictional species) in Palworld. Dinossom has a tall, blobby build, with two arms and legs with three digits each. Its claw on its hands and feet are a red, while hasing a white belly. Its back is a deeper green, with small plant-like tufts along its tail, which ends in a curl. Dinossom's head consists of a pair of magenta eyes with a short snout, four leaves, four pink metals with a tan underside, and a yellow crown-shaped blossom on top with six points. It also has two horns hanging backwards, also ending in curls.&pDinossom's name is a portmanteau of dinosaur and blossom. Dinossom is the 64th Pal in the Paldeck (Palworld). The work suitability (Palworld) for Dinossom is 2 for Planting (Palworld) and 2 for Lumbering (Palworld). Dinossom Lux is an Electric element (Palworld) and Dragon element. It is yellow instead of green, with brown leaves, a vibrant red, tan, and pink-topped petals, and a brown blossom. Dinossom Lux is 64B in the Paldeck. Instead of Planting, Dinossom Lux has a work suitability of 2 for Generating Electricity (Palworld). The Paldeck entries for Dinossom and Dinossom Lux describe idiom which have been picked up, likely mirroring reality idioms that also prodominently utilize animal. The entry for Dinossom describes the idiom "step on a Dinossom's tail", meaing to enrage someone. Real-life counterparts would be to "get under someone's skin" or to "drive someone up the wall". The entry for Dinossom Lux describes the idiom "struck by a Dinossom Lux's bolt", which means to near-death instance. There are many counterparts to this idiom, such as to have a "close call", to "live to tell the tale", to "dodge a bullet", and to escape "by the skin of one's teeth".
