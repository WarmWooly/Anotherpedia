Dioxygen

Dioxygen, also known as diatomic oxygen, molecular oxygen, dioxidene, oxygen gas, or just oxygen, is an allotrope of oxygen consisting of two atoms, forming the chemical compound Os-b2. Dioxygen is present on Earth in the form of air from the atmosphere, taking up nearly 21% of it. Dioxygen is used by a large variety of life to breathe and sustain functions in their bodies.
