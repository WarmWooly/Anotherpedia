Dioxygen

Dioxygen, also known as diatomic oxygen, molecular oxygen, dioxidene, oxygen gas, or just oxygen, is an allotrope of oxygen consisting of two atom, forming the chemical compound chemical formula. Dioxygen is present on Earth in the form of air from the atmosphere, composition of Earth's atmosphere nearly 21% of it. Dioxygen is used by a large variety of life to breathing and sustain functions in their body.
