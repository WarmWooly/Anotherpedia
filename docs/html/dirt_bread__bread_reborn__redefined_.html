Dirt Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Dirt bread is food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. dirt flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce dirt bread, taking 2 or 1 second respectively and giving experience (Minecraft). Dirt bread can be used in a breadmaking station (Bread Reborn: Redefined) to make a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined), adding its stats to the platter. Dirt bread can be eaten as food (Minecraft). As it has effects, dirt bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
