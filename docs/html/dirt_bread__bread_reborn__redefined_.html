Dirt Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|No||Hunger|2 (1 shank)||Saturation|0 (0 shanks)||Effects|Dirty (1:00)info>>Dirt bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Dirt flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce dirt bread, taking 2 or 1 second respectively and giving experience. Dirt bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, dirt bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
