Dirt Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Dirt flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. Dirt flour can be baked into dirt bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Dirt flour can be obtained by milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) coarse dirt (Minecraft), dirt (Minecraft), grass block (Minecraft), mycelium (Minecraft), podzol (Minecraft), or rooted dirt (Minecraft) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Dirt flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce dirt bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), taking 2 or 1 second respectively and giving experience (Minecraft).
