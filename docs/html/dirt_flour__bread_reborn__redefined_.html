Dirt Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Dirt flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Dirt flour can be baked into dirt bread. Dirt flour can be obtained by milling coarse dirt, dirt, grass blocks, mycelium, podzol, or rooted dirt on a millstone. Dirt flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce dirt bread, taking 2 or 1 second respectively and giving experience.
