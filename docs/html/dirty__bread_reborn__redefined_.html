Dirty ({{iBread Reborn: Redefined}})

<><>{{bDirty}} is an [[effect|effect (Minecraft)]] in {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|modding (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Bread Reborn: Redefined]]}} that turns [[grass blocks|grass block (Minecraft)]] into [[dirt|dirt (Minecraft)]] and grants [[invisibility|invisibility (Minecraft)]] while on dirt. The source of dirty is from eating [[dirt bread|dirt bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]]. When the affected [[mob|mob (Minecraft)]] walks on a grass block, [[mycelium|mycelium (Minecraft)]], or [[podzol|podzol (Minecraft)]], it has an 85% chance to turn into dirt, 10% for [[coarse dirt|coarse dirt (Minecraft)]], and 5% for [[rooted dirt|rooted dirt (Minecraft)]]. While standing on a dirt, coarse dirt, or rooted dirt block, the mob gains invisibility for 10 [[ticks|tick (Minecraft)]] (0.5 seconds). If the mob enters [[water|water (Minecraft)]] or is under [[rain|rain (Minecraft)]], the effect is removed. If the mob is a [[player|player (Minecraft)]], they will be awarded the [[advancement|advancement (Minecraft)]] [[Squeaky Clean|Squeaky Clean (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]].
