Discarding (Balatro)

Discarding is an action in Balatro to remove cards from the hand. Discarding is most often used to draw from the deck again to get a better hand, but it can also be used to trigger other effects. Up to 5 cards can be selected at a time to be discarded, with each discard costing 1 of the discards.
