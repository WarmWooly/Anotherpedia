Discarding (Balatro)

{{bDiscarding}} is an action in {{iBalatro}} to remove playing cards (balatro) from the hand (Balatro). Discarding is most often used to draw from the deck (balatro) again to get a better poker hand (balatro), but it can also be used to trigger other effects. Up to 5 cards can be selected at a time to be discarded, with each discard costing 1 of the discards. Jokers (Balatro), Vouchers (Balatro), Boss Blind (Balatro), and other effects may trigger or change the discards and discarding.<>
