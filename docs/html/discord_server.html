Discord server

A Discord server is a collection of text channels, voice chats, and/or forums categorized as one community on Discord. Any user can create a Discord server and share it with other users. Servers that can be searched publicly through Discord are called communities. Servers tend to have a singular topic or theme, such as a specific piece of media or content creator.

The owner of a server has the highest authority, with the ability to create, delete, and edit content freely within the server. They may give other users additional permissions to also manage or moderate, and servers often have a set of rules to adhere to. Users may be banned from servers, preventing their ability to join or view the server. Users may have roles to differentiate their purpose within the server, as a reward/achievement, or to get notifications based on user-made criteria.
