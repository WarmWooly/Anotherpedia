Discord server

A Discord server is a collection of text channel, voice chat, and/or forum categorized as one community on Discord (application). Any user can create a Discord server and share it with other users. Servers that can be searched publicly through Discord are called Discord community. Servers tend to have a singular topic or theme, such as a specific piece of media or content creator.

The owner of a server has the highest authority, with the ability to create, delete, and edit content freely within the server. They may give other users additional permissions to also manage or Discord moderator, and servers often have a set of Discord server rules to adhere to. Users may be ban from servers, preventing their ability to join or view the server. Users may have Discord role to differentiate their purpose within the server, as a reward/achievement, or to get notification based on user-made criteria.
