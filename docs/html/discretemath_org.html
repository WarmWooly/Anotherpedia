discretemath.org

discretemath.org is a discrete structures/discrete mathematics website for the textbook Applied Discrete Structures by Al Doerr and Ken Levasseur. The content of the site is under Creative Commons <> and is managed by Ken Levasseur <>. Links to the complete texts, PDF downloads for the books, and Lulu.com links to purchase the physical books are available from the site. Additionally, the site features an embedded MediaWiki.
