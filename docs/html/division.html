Division

<>{{tFor other terms relating to division, see [[division (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>{{bDivision}} is one of the four basic [[arithmetic]] operations, alongside [[addition]], [[subtraction]], and [[multiplication]]. The operation describes the process of taking a [[whole]] and breaking it up into equal [[parts|part]]. The [[number]] being divided is called the [[dividend]], which is divided by the [[divisor]], with the result called the [[quotient]].
