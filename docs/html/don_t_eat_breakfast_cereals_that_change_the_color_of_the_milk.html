Don't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk

<><>"{{bDon't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk}}" is the 36th rule in the [[book]] {{i[[Food Rules: An Eater's Manual]]}} by [[Michael Pollan]]. The saying is used to convey that it is better to avoid [[cereals|cereal]] that changes the [[color]] of [[milk]] added to it because of the [[chemical additives|synthetic food additive]] in the cereal. It precedes {{i[[The whiter the bread, the sooner you'll be dead]]}} and comes after {{i[[Eat sweet foods as you find them in nature]]}}.
