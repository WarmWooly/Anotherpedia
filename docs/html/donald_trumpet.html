Donald Trumpet

For the website, see TrumpDonald.org.
Donald Trumpet refers to internet meme that comes from the wordplay of merging the names of Donald Trump and the trumpet. It follows similar memes made of president of the United States, including Obama Prism and Joe Bidome, with Trump's likeness merged with a trumpet. Variations include plastering Trump's face or hair on a trumpet or stretching Trump's face across a trumpet.
