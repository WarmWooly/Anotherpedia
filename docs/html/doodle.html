Doodle

A doodle is a simple drawing made while preoccupied. Doodles can vary greatly, from shapes or other marks, to people, animals, characters, or other objects. Children tend to produce many doodles on worksheets or notes while bored or daydreaming to flex thier creativity. One of the most notable doodles from childlore is the Cool S.
