Doodle

A doodle is a simple drawing made while preoccupied. Doodles can vary greatly, from shape or other mark, to people, animal, character, or other object. Children tend to produce many doodles on worksheet or notes while boredom or daydreaming to flex thier creativity. One of the most notable doodles from childlore is the Cool S.
