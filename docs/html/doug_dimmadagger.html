Doug DimmaDagger

<><>The {{bDoug DimmaDagger}}, also known as the {{bDimmadirk}}, is a [[dagger]] based on the [[Fairly OddParents]] [[character]] [[Doug Dimmadome|Doug Dimmadome (Fairly OddParents)]], where the [[blade]] is [[concealed|conceal]] under his [[tall hat|Doug Dimmadome's Hat]]. It was [[forged|forging]] by [[Fluid Forge Blacksmithing]] and was [[sold]] for $115.00 before becoming unavailable. It has a [[handle]] in the shape of Doug Dimmadome, where the [[hat]] acts as a [[sheath]] for the blade. When someone had the DimmaDagger, you know you're going to [[Dimmadie|death]].
