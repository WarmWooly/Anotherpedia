Doug DimmaDagger

The {{bDoug DimmaDagger}}, also known as the {{bDimmadirk}}, is a dagger based on the Fairly OddParents character Doug Dimmadome (Fairly OddParents), where the blade is conceal under his Doug Dimmadome's Hat. It was forging by Fluid Forge Blacksmithing and was sold for $115.00 before becoming unavailable. It has a handle in the shape of Doug Dimmadome, where the hat acts as a sheath for the blade. When someone had the DimmaDagger, you know you're going to death.
