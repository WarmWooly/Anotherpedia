Dragon

A dragon is a Myth flying reptile. They are popular with many Culture worldwide and come in a variety of forms. Dragons in European dragon typically have four leg on a full body, wing, horn, and the ability to fire breath, being well-known for chaos and destruction. Meanwhile, Chinese dragon have a more serpent-shaped body, with shorter legs and are much more often used as a method of describing wisdom and peace.
