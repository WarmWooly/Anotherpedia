Dragonite

{{bDragonite}} is a Reptile Dragon Type (Pokémon) Pokémon (fictional species) from the Generation I (Pokémon). Dragonite is considered a Pseudo-legendary Pokémon. Being similar to a chubby dragon, Dragonite has a tall stature with a pair of arms and legs adorned with three digits each. The body is a rich orange, while the stomach and underside are tanned segments. Dragonite also has a pair of relatively small wings with a cyan inside. Its head consists of a horn above its crown, a pair of wispy, yellow antennae with a thick snout, and a pair of faintly green eyes.&pDragonite's official size is 7 foot (unit), 3 inch, while weighing 463 Pound. Shiny (Pokémon) Dragonites are a dull green instead of orange, alongside having purple wings instead of cyan. The color of Dragonite's underside remains the same.&pDragonite is possibly a portmanteau of {{idragon}} and {{iknight}}. Dragonite is extremely rare living around ocean. Dragonite is able to break the sound barrier, with the ability to circle the Earth in 16 hour, which at around the equator would require a maximum speed of ~1550 Miles per hour (slightly faster than Mach 2). It roughly has human-level intellect, suggesting an Encephalization quotient around 7. Despite a lack of orbs, Dragonite can also change the weather. Dragonite, known by locals as a "sea guardian", seemingly has an altruism demeanor. Dragonite has saved people from drowning, alongside aiding ship when lost or damage. Because of this, figurehead (boat) of Dragonite have been adorned onto ships. When the environment is environmental damage, Dragonite will decimate the harmer. It is unclear if Dragonite harms living things involved in the harm, especially due to the uncontrollable nature of its rampage when angered. The hexadecimal value for Dragonite in the original games is 5A (90 in decimal (number system)). A Dragonite has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Dragonite is the 149th Pokémon in the Pokédex (Pokémon) and has the Pokédex color brown.&pDragonite evolution (pokémon) from Dragonair (pokémon) at level (pokémon) 55, which evolves from a level 30 Dratini (Pokémon). Dragonite's leveling rate is "Slow". When a Dragonite held item (pokémon) Dragoninite, it can mega evolution (pokémon) into Mega Dragonite, overall increasing its stats. Dragonite takes on a closer appearance to a Dragonair, now sporting a white horn, a longer tail, and a chromatic orb near its end. It also has small white wing "sprouts" on its thighs and shoulders, and a pair of large white feathery wings on its head. Its eyes also become blue, and its underbelly now has fewer, pointed lines. Mega Dragonite's weight increases to 639.3 lbs.
