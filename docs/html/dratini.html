Dratini

Dratini is an aquatic Reptile Dragon Type (Pokémon) Pokémon (fictional species) from the Generation I (Pokémon). Dratini resembles a serpent with a light blue coloring and a white underside. Dratini has a pair of large eyes that are often reflecting off light with a purple iris. Dratini also has a large snout, a pair of 3-pronged fins/wings on its head, and a flat horn.

Dratini's official size is 5 foot (unit), 11 inch, while weighing 7.3 Pound. Shiny (Pokémon) Dratinis are pink in color instead of blue.

Early on in material promoting Pokémon, Dratini was referred to as Dragoon. It is believed that Dratini may be a portmanteau of dragon and teeny or mini. This is despite the fact that a Dratini is List of All Pokémon as tall as Walter White Walter White (Breaking Bad). Dratinis can be found near or inside bodies of moving water. Dratini is considered a bottom feeder, eating food collected at the bottom of the bodies of water. Dratinis are known to Shedding their skin often due to "life energy buildup". After shedding, Dratinis often hide behind rocks and Waterfall. Dratini glows after shedding its skin. Dratini used to be considered a Myth creature due to its illusive nature. Shed skins of Dratini were found, but there used to be no definitive evidence. A Angler has Catching a Dratini after a 10-hour struggle, and is considered a recent event in the Pokémon universe. This is paired with a small colony being found, as well as DNA evidence that determines that the shed skins were from Dratini.

Shed skins have been Tanning and turned into a luxury commodity. It is unclear if poaching is a threat to Dratini. Some Culture believe that Dratini's horn is a symbol of good luck. The hexadecimal value for Dratini in the original games is 58 (88 in decimal (number system)). A Dratini has a 50/50 chance to be male or female.
