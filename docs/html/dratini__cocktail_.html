Dratini (cocktail)

For the meme, see Dratini in a martini.&spA Dratini is a variation on the martini based around the Pokémon Dratini made by the Tipsy Bartender. The naming is based on the similarity between Dratini and martini. To prepare the Dratini, first, fill a mixing glass with ice cubes until cold. Then, fill it with the following:&sp1.5 oz (~45 mL) of gin (specifically Bombay Sapphire Gin)&sp1.5 oz (~45 mL) of Hpnotiq&sp A splash (~2.5 mL) of a blue curaçao&spStir the mixture and transfer it to a cocktail glass, ice-free.&spAs a garnish, place a dollop of whipped cream on the side of the glass.
