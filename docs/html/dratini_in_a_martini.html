Dratini in a martini

<>{{tFor the [[beverage]], see [[Dratini (cocktail)]].}}&sp<>{{bDratini in a martini}}, also known as {{bDratini on a martini}} or {{bDratini Martini}}, is a play off the [[elf on the shelf meme|elf on the shelf (meme)]]. It is because [[Dratini|Dratini (Pokémon)]] and a [[martini]] [[rhyme]], as required by the elf on the shelf meme. <>{{tFor more examples, see [[Gallery of Dratini in a martini]].}}&sp<>Because of the [[silly]] and [[absurd]] nature of the [[meme]], alongside the [[cultural|culture]] powerhouse that is {{i[[Pokémon]]}}, it causes Dratini in a martini to outlive all other elf on the shelf memes. It has become so widespread that there are [[artworks|artwork]], [[pins|pin]], [[stickers|sticker]], [[sculptures|sculpture]], [[tattoos|tattoo]], and other forms of a Dratini in a martini. The meme about the Dratini in a martini, however, is not connected to the [[cocktail]] [[Dratini|Dratini (cocktail)]], where the Dratini is a martini.
