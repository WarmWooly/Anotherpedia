This page covers jewelry which depicts the Pokémon Dratini, such as rings, necklaces, earrings, pendents and other relevent articles. This is mainly because of the popularity garnered by the Pokémon, alongside the recognizability caused by the size of the Pokémon franchise. Most jewelry related to Dratini is fan-made and not officially licensed. Rings are the most common form because of the serpent shape of Dratini easily lending itself to wrap around a finger. Jewelry of Dratini is usually small, not bigger than an inch or so, and takes the full form of Dratini.
