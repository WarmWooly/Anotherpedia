Dripifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The dripifier is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce dripstone (Minecraft). Dripifiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 wetifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless). Dripifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create dripstone (Minecraft). Dripifiers take 4x as long to generate one dripstone compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
