Dripifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The dripifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce dripstone. Dripifiers can be crafted using 1 cobblefier and 1 wetifier (shapeless). Dripifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create dripstone. Dripifiers take 4x as long to generate one dripstone compared to one cobblestone. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector alongside the 51 other cobblefiers, the player will be awarded the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
