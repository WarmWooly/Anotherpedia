Dripifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The dripifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce dripstone. Dripifiers can be crafted using 1 cobblefier and 1 wetifier (shapeless). Dripifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create dripstone. Dripifiers take 4x as long to generate one dripstone compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
