Droll

For the comedic sketch, see droll (sketch), and for the songwriter, see James Droll.
Droll can be an adjective, noun, or verb, underlying a general sense of humor, whimsy, or oddity. As an adjective, it could describe a silly quality of an object. As a noun, it works as a synonym for jesters, comedians, and the like. As a verb, it is used for the action of joking or making fun of something.
