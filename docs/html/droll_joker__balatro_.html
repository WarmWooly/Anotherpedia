Droll Joker (Balatro)

Droll Joker is a Jokers (Balatro) in Balatro that gives a +10 multiplier (Balatro) when a Poker Hands (Balatro) is contained within a hand (Balatro) (Flush, Straight Flush, Flush House, and Flush Five). Droll Joker directly aids with benefiting from Flushes, especially when using the Checkered Deck where half of the deck are Hearts (Balatro) and the other half Spades (Balatro).
