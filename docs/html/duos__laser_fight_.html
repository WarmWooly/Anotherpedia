Duos ({{iLaser Fight}})

<><>{{bDuos}} is a [[coop|coop (Laser Fight)]] in {{i[[Laser Fight|Laser Fight (Roblox game)]]}} where players join into 8 teams of 2. When selecting a team, other players will be able to view who has joined. The teams are red, blue, green, yellow, orange, purple, pink, and cyan. Players can't join filled teams. Teammates can't be harmed by each other's bullets from [[guns|gun (Laser Fight)]] or from [[streaks|kill streak (Laser Fight)]] (such as [[Turret|Turret (Laser Fight)]] or [[Nuke|Nuke (Laser Fight)]]).
