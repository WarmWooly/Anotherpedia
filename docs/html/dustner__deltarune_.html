Dustner ({{iDeltarune}})

<>{{tThis page contains [[spoilers|spoiler]] for the [[game|video game]] {{ai[[Deltarune]]}}.}}&sp<>A {{bDustner}} is a [[fan term]] from {{i[[Deltarune]]}} for [[Darkners|Darkner (Deltarune)]] whose [[Light World|Light World (Deltarune)]] form are of a [[monster's|Monster (Deltarune)]] [[dust|monster dust (Deltarune)]], which is comparable to a [[corpse]] for when a monster dies. While it used to be a [[prediction|Deltarune predictions]] that a Darkner would be made of dust, it was confirmed upon the release of [[Chapter 4|Deltarune Chapter 4]] where the character [[Gerson|Gerson (Deltarune)]] appeared in a [[Dark World|Dark World (Deltarune)]]. In the Light World, Gerson is deceased and only a pile of dust, proving that Darkners can come from a monster's dust, and the effects of a Dark World can even "revive" a monster for the duration of the Dark World's existence.
