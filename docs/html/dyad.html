Dyad

<>{{tNot to be confused with [[DIAD|Dominantly inherited Alzheimer's disease]].}}&sp<>A {{bdyad}} is a [[group]] of two [[people]] or a [[construct]] of two [[parts|part]]. It is often used to describe the [[relationship]] between two individuals in [[sociology]]. Common examples are a [[mother]] and [[child]] and a [[student]] and [[teacher]].
