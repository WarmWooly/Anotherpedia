Earphone

An earphone is an electrical device worn around or within the ear to play audio using a electrodynamic speaker driver. Earphones can be connected to many audio source, including radio, telephone call, and music transmitted from a smartphone or computer. Earphones can come as a singular device for one ear or a pair of connected earphones (see headphones). Earphones may be connected to a microphone to audio transmission as well.
