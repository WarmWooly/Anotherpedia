Eat sweet foods as you find them in nature

"Eat sweet foods as you find them in nature" is the 35th rule in the book Food Rules: An Eater's Manual by Michael Pollan. The saying is used to convey to avoid sugary food because of how sugar addiction it can be. It precedes Don't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk and comes after Sweeten and salt your food yourself.
