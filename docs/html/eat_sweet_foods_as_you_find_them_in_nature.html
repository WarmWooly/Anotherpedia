Eat sweet foods as you find them in nature

<><>"{{bEat sweet foods as you find them in nature}}" is the 35th rule in the [[book]] {{i[[Food Rules: An Eater's Manual]]}} by [[Michael Pollan]]. The saying is used to convey to avoid [[sugary foods|sugary food]] because of how [[addictive|sugar addiction]] it can be. It precedes {{i[[Don't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk]]}} and comes after {{i[[Sweeten and salt your food yourself]]}}.
