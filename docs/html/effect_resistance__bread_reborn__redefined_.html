Effect Resistance (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Effect resistance is an effect (Minecraft) in Minecraft modding (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined that removes levitation (Minecraft), poison (Minecraft), and wither (Minecraft effect) while applied. The source of effect resistance is from eating chorus bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) or a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined) containing such. Other effects do not get removed from effect resistance.
