Effective accelerationism

Effective accelerationism (e/acc) is a mid-2020s pro-technology ideology that supports the rapid expansion and acceleration of technology, mainly AI progress. e/acc members tend to push towards less restriction for GenAI and agents, with support primarily from AI corperations. Members of the movement see the growth of AI as humanitarian to reach the singularity using AGI. Opponents to the movement are called decels, which are those who want to limit and keep AI usage accountable through safety measures to prevent mass death or extinction from AI misuse.
