Effective accelerationism

Effective accelerationism (e/acc) is a mid-2020s technologic utopianism ideology that supports the rapid expansion and acceleration of technology, mainly AI progress. e/acc members tend to push towards less AI restrictions for GenAI and AI agent, with support primarily from List of corperations producing artificial intelligence. Members of the movement see the growth of AI as humanitarian to reach the technological singularity using artificial general intelligence. Opponents to the movement are called decelerationist, which are those who want to limit and keep AI accountability through AI safety to prevent existential risk from artificial intelligence from AI misuse.
