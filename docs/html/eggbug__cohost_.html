eggbug (cohost)

eggbug, also written egg bug, was the mascot of the social media platform cohost. Its color is a mix of purple and maroon, sporting two large dot eyes, a w-shaped mouth, two wings, and six stubby legs. The overall shape of eggbug resembles an egg, hence the name.
