Ejecta

<>{{tFor the 2014 [[sci-fi horror]] [[film]], see [[{{aiEjecta}} (film)]].}}&sp<>{{bEjecta}} refers to any [[material]] that has been [[forced|force]] out of an [[object]]. Ejecta is often created by [[volcanic eruptions|volcanic eruption]] or [[meteoric impacts|meteoric impact]]. [[Particles|particle]] released from [[supernovae|supernova]] may also be considered ejecta.
