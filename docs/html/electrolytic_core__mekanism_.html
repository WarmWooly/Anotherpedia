Electrolytic Core (Mekanism)

The electrolytic core is an item in the Minecraft mod Mekanism used for crafting, mainly the electrolytic separator. The electrolytic core can be crafted with 2 osmium dust, 1 iron dust, 1 gold dust, and 5 enriched alloy (the alloy goes in an X pattern, where the osmium are on the top and bottom, the iron is to the left, and the gold is to the right). The electrolytic core is used in the following crafting recipies:&spElectrolytic Separator - 1 electrolytic core, 4 iron ingots, 2 redstone, and 2 enriched alloy (the iron ingots are in the corners, with the redstone on the top and bottom and the energetic alloys on the sides, where the middle slot is filled with the electrolytic core).&spGas-Burning Generator - 1 electrolytic core, 2 enriched alloy, 4 iron osmium, and 2 steel casings (the osmium goes in the corners, with the alloy on the top and bottom and the casings to the left and right, where the core is in the center).
