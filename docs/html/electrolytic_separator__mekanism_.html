Electrolytic Separator (Mekanism)

The electrolytic separator is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Mekanism used to electrolysis liquid (Minecraft) to other chemicals. The electrolytic separator can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 iron ingot (Minecraft), 2 redstone (Minecraft), 2 enriched alloy (Mekanism), and 1 electrolytic core (Mekanism) (the iron ingots are in the corners, with the redstone on the top and bottom and the energetic alloys on the sides, where the middle slot is filled with the electrolytic core). The electrolytic separator, which runs on 400 power (mekanism), currently has three reactions it can perform:
2 millibuckets Water (Minecraft) --> 2 mB Hydrogen (Mekanism) + 1 mB Oxygen (Mekanism)
2 mB Heavy Water (Mekanism) --> 2 mB Deuterium (Mekanism) + 1 mB Oxygen (Mekanism)
10 mB Brine (Mekanism) --> 1 mB Sodium (Mekanism) + 1 mB Chlorine (Mekanism)
