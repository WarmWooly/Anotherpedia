Elf on the Shelf (meme)

The meme about the Elf on the Shelf is how the words rhyme, such as elf and shelf. The format of the meme consists of a character or object on another object, such as an elf that is on a shelf. Many open the meme with now get ready for as if the meme is a step up from the elf on the shelf. They often don't explain what the character or object is, requiring the viewer to guess based on their prior knowledge, acting as a puzzle. Occasionally, when there is a container the object is in the, and not on the, container. There are a wide variety of examples of the meme, including:
Duck on a truck
Drake on a cake
Snape on a grape
LEGO on an Eggo
Tide on a slide
Santa on a Fanta
Woody in a hoodie
Yoda on a soda
Chrome on a gnome

Luigi on a Fiji
Vader on a tater
RuPaul on a U-Haul
Skeleton on gelatin
Link on a sink
Bart in a cart
Shaggy on a baggie
Vegeta on a cheetah
Dratini in a martini Main page: Dratini in a martini
The most prolific variation on the Elf on the Shelf meme has been the Pokémon Dratini in a martini, garnering artworks, pins, stickers, sculptures, tattoos, and various other creative works. This is because of the popularity of the Pokémon franchise and the silly idea behind a Pokémon being tied with an alcoholic beverage.
