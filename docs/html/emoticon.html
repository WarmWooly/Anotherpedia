Emoticon

An {{bemoticon}} is a string of text used to represent emotion. Emoticon is a portmanteau of {{iemotion}} and {{iicon (computing)}}. Some common emoticons are :), :D, :P, D:, :(, >:(, OwO (emoticon), and (^.^). Emoticons are often used in chatroom, text messaging, and other electronic communication.
