Emoticon

<><>An {{bemoticon}} is a string of [[text]] used to represent [[emotions|emotion]]. Emoticon is a [[portmanteau]] of {{iemotion}} and {{i[[icon|icon (computing)]]}}. Some common emoticons are [[:)]], [[:D]], [[:P]], [[D:]], [[:(]], [[>:(]], [[OwO|OwO (emoticon)]], and [[(^.^)]]. Emoticons are often used in [[chatrooms|chatroom]], [[text messaging]], and other [[electronic communications|electronic communication]].
