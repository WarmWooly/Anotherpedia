Emotion detection (superpower)

Emotion detection or emotion sensing is a superpower where the user can sense and determine the current emotion of another person or animal depending on the user's capabilities. If the user can change or affect emotions, it is known as emotion manipulation (superpower) or empathy (superpower) instead. Users with emotion manipulation often have emotion detection alongside it, such as Mantis (Marvel Cinematic Universe) in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
