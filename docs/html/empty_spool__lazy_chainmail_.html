Empty Spool (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Empty Spool ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
<>The empty spool is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Lazy Chainmail used to craft the iron spool (Lazy Chainmail) for bulk winding jig (Lazy Chainmail). Empty spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 stick (Minecraft) and 2 wooden slab (Minecraft) (place the stick in the slabs above and below it; makes 4). Empty spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Spool (Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it). In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 empty spool can fuel for 5 second (0.5 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 empty spools, 320 seconds, 32 items). gal
