Empty Spool (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Empty Spool ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
The empty spool is an item in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used to craft the iron spool for bulk winding. Empty spools can be crafted with 1 stick and 2 wooden slabs (place the stick in the slabs above and below it; makes 4). Empty spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it). In a furnace, blast furnace, or smoker, 1 empty spool can fuel for 5 seconds (0.5 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (64 empty spools, 320 seconds, 32 items). gal
