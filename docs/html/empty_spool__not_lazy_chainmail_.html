Empty Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The empty spool is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to store multiple items together as spools for bulk winding. Empty spools can be crafted with 1 stick and 2 wooden slabs (place the stick in the slabs above and below it; makes 4). Empty spools can be obtained when a Crusher crushes spool blocks. A bright empty spool can be smoked in a smoker to produce an iron spool, taking 1 second. Empty spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Auto Winder - 2 empty spools, 4 iron composite ingots, and 2 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with iron composite, the left and right slots with cobblestone, the top corners with empty spools, and the top slot with iron composite).
Belt Generator - 2 empty spools, 1 block of iron, and 6 cobblestone (place the iron in the center, the spools to the left and right, and the cobblestone in the remaining rows).
Bright Empty Spool x4 - 4 empty spools and 1 glowstone dust (place the dust in the middle and surround it with spools).
Copper Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 copper ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Gold Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 gold ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Iron Composite Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 iron composite ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Iron Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Netherite Spool - 1 empty spool and 2 netherite ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots above and below it).
Rose Gold Spool - 1 empty spool and 8 rose gold ingots (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Spool Block - 1 empty spool and 2 wooden slabs (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Empty spools are used in the following melding recipes:
