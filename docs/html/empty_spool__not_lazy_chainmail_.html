Empty Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The empty spool is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to store multiple items together as spools (Not Lazy Chainmail) for bulk winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 stick (Minecraft) and 2 wooden sbal (Minecraft) (place the stick in the slabs above and below it; makes 4). Empty spools can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). A bright empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce an iron spool, taking 1 second. Empty spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Auto Winder (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 empty spools, 4 iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 cobblestone (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with iron composite, the left and right slots with cobblestone, the top corners with empty spools, and the top slot with iron composite).
Belt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 empty spools, 1 block of iron (Minecraft), and 6 cobblestone (Minecraft) (place the iron in the center, the spools to the left and right, and the cobblestone in the remaining rows).
Bright Empty Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 4 empty spools and 1 glowstone dust (Minecraft) (place the dust in the middle and surround it with spools).
Copper Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 copper ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Gold Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 gold ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Iron Composite Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Iron Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Netherite Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 2 netherite ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots above and below it).
Rose Gold Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 8 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the spool in the middle and place the ingots around it).
Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 empty spool and 2 wooden slab (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Empty spools can be Haunted (Not Lazy Chainmail) into bright empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail).
