Empty Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bempty spool}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used to store multiple items together as spools (Not Lazy Chainmail) for bulk winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 stick (Minecraft) and 2 wooden sbal (Minecraft) (place the stick in the slabs above and below it; makes 4). Empty spools can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). A bright empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce an iron spool, taking 1 second.
