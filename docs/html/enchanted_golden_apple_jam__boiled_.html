Enchanted golden apple jam is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:

Enchanted golden apple jam can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. As it has effects, enchanted golden apple jam can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
