Enchanted Golden Apple Jam (Boiled)

Enchanted golden apple jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Enchanted golden apple jam can be obtained by boiling an enchanted golden apple in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 3 exp. Enchanted golden apple jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Enchanted Golden Apple Jam-Filled Bread - 1 enchanted golden apple jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Enchanted golden apple jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. As it has effects, enchanted golden apple jam can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
