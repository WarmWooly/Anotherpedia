Enchanted Golden Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Enchanted golden bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Enchanted golden flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce enchanted golden bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 10 experience. The advancement Notch Bread will also be awarded. Enchanted golden bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, enchanted golden bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
