Enchanted Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Enchanted golden flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Enchanted golden flour can be baked into enchanted golden bread. Enchanted golden flour can be obtained by brewing an enchanted golden apple onto flour. Enchanted golden flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce enchanted golden bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 10 experience. The advancement Notch Bread will also be awarded.
