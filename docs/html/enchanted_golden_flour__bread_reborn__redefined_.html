Enchanted Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>{{bEnchanted golden flour}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iBread Reborn: Redefined}}. Enchanted golden flour can be baked into enchanted golden bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Enchanted golden flour can be obtained by brewing (Minecraft) an enchanted golden apple (Minecraft) onto flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Enchanted golden flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce enchanted golden bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 10 experience (Minecraft). The advancement (Minecraft) Notch Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will also be awarded.
