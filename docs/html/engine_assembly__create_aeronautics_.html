Engine Assembly (Create Aeronautics)

The engine assembly is an item in the Minecraft mod Create Aeronautics used for creating the portable engine. The engine assembly is made by processing an iron sheet by putting it through a mechanical saw and a mechanical press 8 times. During the process, it is in the form of an incomplete engine assembly.
