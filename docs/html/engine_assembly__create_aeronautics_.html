Engine Assembly (Create Aeronautics)

<>The engine assembly is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Create Aeronautics used for creating the portable engine (Create Aeronautics). The engine assembly is made by processing an iron sheet (Create) by putting it through a mechanical saw (Create) and a mechanical press (Create) 8 times. During the process, it is in the form of an incomplete engine assembly (Create Aeronautics).
