Enhancements (Balatro)

Enhancements are Modifiers that can be added to playing cards in Balatro. Only one Enhancement can be on a card at a time. If a new Enhancement is added to a card that already has one, the previous Enhancement will be overwritten. Purchasing cards from the Store or from booster packs are ways to get cards with Enhancements, but they may also come from Tarot Cards and Spectral Cards.
