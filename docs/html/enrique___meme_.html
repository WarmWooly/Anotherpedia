Enrique! (meme)

Enrique! is a meme of a woman calling out to a person named Enrique after a chocolate flan breaks apart and falls out of a dish while she's recording it. The original video was posted to TikTok by the user Marta Catalan on November 1, 2023. She says the following in the video:Al final ha quedado flan--¡Ah, joder! ¡Enrique!It translates to "It ended up being flan--Oh, damn! Enrique!", as Marta calls to her boyfriend.

It became a popular meme sound effect used on TikTok and YouTube Shorts during mid-to-late 2025. It has since become closely associated with the YouTuber Caldruki due to their extensive use of the sound effect in most of their meme-related short videos.
