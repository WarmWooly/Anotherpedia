Epic TDX Edit (Regular Speed)

"Epic TDX Edit (Regular Speed)" was a YouTube short made by Gatling_Peashooter21 on December 26, 2024, where towers in the Roblox game Tower Defense X are panned over. The song "California Gurls" was played over the footage, which was recorded with OBS Studio and edited in CapCut. The towers that appeared in the video were the Slammer (Default skin), Sniper (Default skin), EDJ (Tower Battles skin), Juggernaut (Eradicator skin), and Golden Juggernaut (Default skin).<> The video has since been made unavailable <>.
