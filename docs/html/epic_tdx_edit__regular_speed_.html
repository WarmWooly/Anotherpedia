Epic TDX Edit (Regular Speed)

"{{bEpic TDX Edit (Regular Speed)}}" was a YouTube short made by Gatling_Peashooter21 on December 26, 2024, where tower (Tower Defense X) in the Roblox game {{iTower Defense X}} are panned over. The song "California Gurls" was played over the footage, which was recorded with OBS Studio and edited in CapCut. The towers that appeared in the video were the Slammer (Tower Defense X) (Default skin), Sniper (Tower Defense X) (Default skin), EDJ (Tower Defense X) (Tower Battles skin), Juggernaut (Tower Defense X) (Eradicator skin), and Golden Juggernaut (Tower Defense X) (Default skin).<> The video has since been made unavailable <>.
