Eric (Rhiane Turtonator)

Eric is an original character based on Smiling Friends created by Rhiane Turtonator who is posted to various social media, including DevientART, Twitter, and Reddit. Eric's most prominent appearance was within the YouTube video "SMILING FRIENDS ( fan animation )". Eric is an orange critter (Smiling Friends) with humanoid arms, legs, and body. His head is long and pointed, with thick hair, a wide mouth, and eyes often hidden by glasses. He is often wearing a suit or other formal wear. Eric is 60 year old. His last name starts with "G". He would be the 5th Smiling Friend. Eric is shipping (fandom) with Mr. Boss (Smiling Friends), with Eric shown to be close with him and his son Jason (Smiling Friends).

Eric seems to be able to create a drug that forces people to smile immediately upon consumption. When he consumes it, blood is shown dripping from his mouth. It is currently unknown whether others taking it would have the same side effect. Eric is shown both working at Smiling Friends Inc. (Smiling Friends) and Frowning Friends Inc. (Smiling Friends) at different times.
