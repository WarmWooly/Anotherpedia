Et cetera

Et cetera is a Latin term, abbreviated as etc., that means "and the rest". This is formed by et, which means and, and cetera, which means the rest. It is used to describe that there are more objects in a list, such as the following:The parents made sure that their kids ate healthy, feeding them broccoli, spinach, brussels sprouts, et cetera.
