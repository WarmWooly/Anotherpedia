Eternal Flame (Hexaria)

Not to be confused with the effect, Eternal Burn ({{aiHexaria).}}
Eternal Flame is an ultra-rare card in the Roblox game Hexaria which applies a permanent source of damage on a target. Name: Eternal Flame
Target: Enemy within 3 tiles with line of sight
Quickplay: No
Bit cost: 4
Effects: Applies the effect Eternal Burn on a target. The Eternal Burn deals damage based on 1% of the target's maximum Health (ex. 300 HP --> +3 damage), with a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 6.
Card text:Ignite the opponent with a mysterious flame that never burns out. This Eternal Burn deals 1 to 6 damage each turn, based on the target's maximum Health.Rarity: Ultra-rare
