Ethylene (Mekanism)

Ethylene is a gas in the Minecraft mod Mekanism used for the creation of other chemicals, alongside ore refinery. It can be stored in gas tanks and transported with pressurized tubes. In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber, the following reactions can be made to obtain ethylene:
10 mB Water + 100 mB Hydrogen + 2 Bio Fuel --> 1 Substrate + 100 mB Ethylene In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made to obtain ethylene:
1 mB Liquid Ethylene --> 1 mB Ethylene In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber, the following reactions can be made using ethylene:
200 mB Water + 100 mB Ethylene + 1 Substrate --> 8 Substrate + 10 mB Oxygen In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made using ethylene:
1 mB Ethylene --> 1 mB Liquid Ethylene
