Evolution

<><>{{bEvolution}} is the gradual change of [[DNA]] within the [[population]] of a [[species]] that is [[passed on|heredity]] between generations. All [[lifeforms|lifeform]] likely evolved from the [[last universal common ancestor]] through random [[mutations|DNA mutation]] in DNA.
