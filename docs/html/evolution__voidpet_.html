Evolution (Voidpet)

For the combinational mechanic in {{aiVoidpet Dungeon, see Ascension (aiVoidpet Dungeon).}}
Evolution is a process within the Voidpet franchise where a Voidpet changes its form and increases in power and ability. Each Voidpet starts at stage one and can be evolved up to stage five. Few Voidpets also have higher forms beyond their fifth stage (not covered here). Each form grows in complexity, with an additional set of eyes per stage. In Voidpet Garden, a Voidpet must reach a certain level before they can evolve, with each evolution also costing an increasing amount of void matter (VM). Plants also evolve with the same level and VM requirements.<> <>
In Voidpet Dungeon, a Voidpet can be evolved using special items, which will increase their max level. Rarer Voidpets will require more items to evolve, but also have a higher base cap per level. Each evolution increases the stats of the Voidpet.<>
