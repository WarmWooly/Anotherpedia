Ew brother ew

<><><>{{bEw brother ew}}, also {{bEw, Brother, Ew!}} and {{bEw brother ew what's that?}}, is a [[meme|internet meme]] originating from the 2019 [[video]] [[lecture]] "[[Are You a Man?! Very Powerful Speech!]]" spoken by [[Mohamed Hoblos]]. The meme has Hoblos in the center of the frame unedited, saying: "Ew, brother, ew! What's that? What's that brother?" During the "brother ew," Hoblos extends his arms out and looks away in [[disgust]], adding to the humorous nature of the meme. It became a prominent [[sound|TikTok sound]] on [[TikTok]] and a [[reaction video]] on [[Twitter]] in 2024.
