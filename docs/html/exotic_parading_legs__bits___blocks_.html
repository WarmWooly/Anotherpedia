Exotic Parading Legs (Bits & Blocks)

Exotic parading legs are a feet curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that applies buffs on eating food. The exotic parading legs can be crafted with 1 parading legs, 1 Jungle Mixtape, and 1 leaves (shapeless). When the exotic parading legs are worn in a feet slot, eating food will also apply Speed and Haste II for 30 seconds.
