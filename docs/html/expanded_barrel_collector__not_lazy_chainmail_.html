Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The expanded barrel collector is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to automatically store item (Minecraft) that land above it. The expanded barrel collector can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 barrel collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 block of iron composite chains (Minecraft) (place the barrels to the left and right of the chains) When an item (Minecraft entity) lands above the expanded barrel collector, it will automatically store the dropped item inside itself. The expanded barrel collector has 63 slots to store items within, 36 more than a barrel collector (Not Lazy Chainmail). The expanded barrel collector can be opened with right-click, even with the top covered. Storing all 52 cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail) then opening the expanded barrel collector awards the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail). In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 expanded barrel collector can fuel for 30 second (3 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 expanded barrel collectors, 1920 seconds, 192 items).
