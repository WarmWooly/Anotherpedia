Expanding Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Expanding leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Expanding leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +0.33 Attack Knockback. Expanding leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 1 piston. Journeyman-level leatherworkers have a 50% chance to trade 1 leather padding and 3 emeralds for 1 expanding leather padding. In a leathersmithing table, expanding leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each expanding leather padding adds +0.33 Attack Knockback,< +0.33, 2 --> +0.67, and 3 --> +1 respectively to make it more appealing to look at when at max level.(text=noteCountnote>> with a full set of armor with 12 expanding leather paddings giving +4 Attack Knockback.
