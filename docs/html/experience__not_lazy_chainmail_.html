Experience (Not Lazy Chainmail)

For the enchantment and its associated rune, see experienced ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail) and experienced rune (aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}&sp<>Experience is a placeholder item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that results when the output of a process produces an experience orb. When experience is "obtained", an experience orb with a value of 1 is generated instead. If multiple experience would be gained in a single operation, they are combined together. Experience can be collected by sieving sculk with an iron sieve mesh (4 drops with a 100%, 80%, 50%, and 20% chance respectively). Separation can increase the chance/drops of experience from sieving.
