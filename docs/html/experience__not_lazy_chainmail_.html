Experience (Not Lazy Chainmail)

For the enchantment and its associated rune, see experienced ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail) and experienced rune (aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
Experience is a placeholder item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that results when the output of a process produces an experience orb. When experience is "obtained", an experience orb with a value of 1 is generated instead. If multiple experience would be gained in a single operation, they are combined together. Experience can be collected by sieving sculk with an iron sieve mesh (4 drops with a 100%, 80%, 50%, and 20% chance respectively). Separation can increase the chance/drops of experience from sieving. Items can be used on the experience accumulator to infuse them, with its cost shown with the Experience item. The following are the infusion recipes:
Bottle + 10 Experience --> Bottle o' Enchanting
Item with Mending + Experience --> Repaired item (1 experience per 2 durability)
