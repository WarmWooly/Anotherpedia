Experience Accumulator (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The experience accumulator is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to automatically store experience orb (Minecraft) that land above it. The experience accumulator can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 copper chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail), 2 copper ornate glass pane (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 iron composite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the copper carpets in the bottom row, the panes to the sides, the alloy in the corners, and the iron composite carpet above). When an experience orb (Minecraft entity) lands above the experience accumulator, it will automatically store the dropped experience inside itself. It can store a total of 2,147,483,647 experience points (the 32-bit integer limit). A player (Minecraft) sneaking (Minecraft) on the block will cause it to take experience (Minecraft) from the player and fill the accumulator. The experience accumulator will visibly fill up, increasing the light level (Minecraft) emitted by the block in the following way:<> Experience can be harvested from the experience accumulator using an open hand. Using it will take enough experience to get to the next level. If the player is sneaking while harvesting, all the experience from the accumulator will be extracted. Breaking the experience accumulator will lose all the stored experience. Items can be used on the experience accumulator to infuse them. Infusing a bottle awards the advancement (Minecraft) Not Lazy Bottles (Not Lazy Chainmail). The following are the infusion recipes:
Bottle (Minecraft) + 10 Experience (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Bottle o' Enchanting (Minecraft)
Item with Mending (Minecraft) + Experience (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Repaired item (1 experience per 2 durability (Minecraft))
