Exposed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The exposed block of copper chains is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The exposed block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become an exposed block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking a weathered block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into an exposed block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). Exposed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 4 exposed blocks of copper chains, 1 (bright spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail)) spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 (waxed exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)) exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 piston (Minecraft), and 1 lever (Minecraft) (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Exposed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 exposed block of copper chains (shapeless).
Exposed Copper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 exposed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).
Waxed Exposed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 exposed block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail).
