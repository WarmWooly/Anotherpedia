Exposed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The exposed block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The exposed block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 exposed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a block of copper chains will become an exposed block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking a weathered block of copper chains or a waxed exposed block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into an exposed block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. Exposed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spExposed Copper Chain x4 - 1 exposed block of copper chains (shapeless).&spExposed Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 exposed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).&spWaxed Exposed Block of Copper Chains - 1 exposed block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (shapeless). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed exposed block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, an exposed block of copper chains will become a weathered block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into a block of copper chains.
