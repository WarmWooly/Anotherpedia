Exposed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The exposed copper chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The exposed copper chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become an exposed copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking a weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into an exposed copper chain.
