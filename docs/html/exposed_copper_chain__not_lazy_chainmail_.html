Exposed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The exposed copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The exposed copper chain can be crafted with an exposed block of copper chains (makes 4). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a copper chain will become an exposed copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking a weathered copper chain or a waxed exposed copper chain with an axe will turn the block into an exposed copper chain. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. Exposed copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Chain Launcher - 1 exposed copper chain, 1 (bright) spool block, 4 (waxed) exposed blocks of copper chains, 1 piston, and 1 lever (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Exposed Block of Copper Chains - 4 exposed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Exposed Copper Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 exposed copper chains (fill the middle and bottom rows).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Mangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Spruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Warped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 exposed copper chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain - 1 exposed copper chain and 1 honeycomb (shapeless). Exposed copper chains can be crushed into 8 copper chain links. Right-clicking an exposed copper chain with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed exposed copper chain. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, an exposed copper chain will become a weathered copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking an exposed copper chain with an axe will turn the block into a copper chain.
