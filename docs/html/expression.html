Expression

<>{{tFor other terms related to expression, see [[expression (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>An {{bexpression}} is any [[event]] related to [[oral communication]], such as [[talking]]. These include singular [[words|word]] and [[sentences|sentence]] used to convey [[meaning]]. An expression may also be a string of words not made to be taken [[literally|literal]], such as [[idioms|idiom]], including "[[A dime a dozen]]" and "[[Raining cats and dogs]]".
