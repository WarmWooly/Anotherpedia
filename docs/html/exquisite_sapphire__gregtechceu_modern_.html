Exquisite Sapphire (GregTechCEu Modern)

<>The exquisite sapphire is an item in the Minecraft mod GregTechCEu Modern from processing sapphire needed to progress from MV to HV. The exquisite sapphire is used to make a sapphire lens, which is then used to engrave a silicon wafer into a ULPIC wafer. In a laser engraver with a glass/nether star lens, 2 flawless sapphires can be fused into an exquisite sapphire. The process requires 72,000 EU over 15 seconds (240 EU/t) on HV.<> In a sifter, purified sapphire ore has a 5% chance<> to produce an exquisite sapphire. The process requires 6,400 EU over 20 seconds (16 EU/t) on LV. Exquisite sapphires are used in the following crafting recipes:&spFlawless Sapphire x2 - 1 exquisite sapphire and 1 hammer (shapeless). In a cutter, an exquisite sapphire can be cut into 2 flawless sapphires with the following fluids:&sp3 mB of Distilled Water - 480 EU over 1.5 secs (16 EU/t) on LV.&sp1 mB of Lubricant - 320 EU over 1 sec (16 EU/t) on LV.&sp4 mB of Water - 640 EU over 2 secs (16 EU/t) on LV. In a lathe, an exquisite sapphire can be lathed into a sapphire lens and 2 sapphire dust. The process requires 72,000 EU over 120 secs (30 EU/t) on LV. In a macerator, an exquisite sapphire can be ground into 4 sapphire dust. The process requires 160 EU over 4 secs (2 EU/t) on ULV.
