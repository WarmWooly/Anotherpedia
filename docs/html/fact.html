Fact

<><>A {{bfact}} is [[information]] that is [[proven|proof]] to be [[true]]. Facts are one of the most important forms of information, guiding how [[people]] [[think|thinking]] and [[feel]] about [[events|event]], [[topics|topic]], other people, and the [[world|Earth]]. Facts can be changed or discredited if new information going against the fact shows that the fact is no longer true.
