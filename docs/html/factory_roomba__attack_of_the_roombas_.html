Factory Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

<>The Factory Roomba is an enemy in Attack of the Roombas that is immobile, but that generates Minion Roomba (Attack of the Roombas) over time. Every 10 seconds (or 12 seconds if it just spawned), it will generate a Minion Roomba to chase the player (Attack of the Roombas). The main purpose of the Factory Roomba is to prevent the player from camping (video games) in a single location.
