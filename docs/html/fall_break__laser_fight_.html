Fall break (Laser Fight)

A fall break is a movement (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game) triggered by jump (Laser Fight) (codeSpace) right before landing from a 35--89 stud drop where fall damage (Laser Fight) would normally ragdoll the player. Jumping too soon or too late will cause the fall break to fail, and spamming jumps before landing will only guarantee ragdolling. Fall breaks can't prevent a player from dying from a lethal height (90+ studs). Landing a fall break will award codedistance/10 rounded down in experience (Laser Fight), giving between 3 and 8. If the fall break is after a dive (Laser Fight) (a belly flop (Laser Fight); gives 1 exp), sliding afterwards will perform a belly roll (Laser Fight), which gives 2 exp.
