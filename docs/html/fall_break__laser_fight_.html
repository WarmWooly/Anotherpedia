Fall break (Laser Fight)

A fall break is a movement action in Laser Fight triggered by jumping (codeSpace) right before landing from a 35--89 stud drop where fall damage would normally ragdoll the player. Jumping too soon or too late will cause the fall break to fail, and spamming jumps before landing will only guarantee ragdolling. Fall breaks can't prevent a player from dying from a lethal height (90+ studs). Landing a fall break will award codedistance/10 rounded down in exp, giving between 3 and 8.
