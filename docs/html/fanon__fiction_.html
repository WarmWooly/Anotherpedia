Fanon (fiction)

A {{bfanon}}, combining the terms "fan" and "canon", is information not canon (fiction) (not explicitly shown), but widely accepted as such by a fanbase. This can be because of the information's continuity (fiction) matching with the plot/lore or being implied within the source material. Fanon that becomes popular enough can be spun off into fanwork and grow its own fan community around it.
