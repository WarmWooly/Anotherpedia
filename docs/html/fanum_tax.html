Fanum tax

Fanum tax is 2022--2023 slang for taking food from a friend (a "tax") which was popularized by the Twitch streamer Fanum (streamer) that has since become a part of Gen Alpha slang. The use of the fanum tax in Twitch stream, such as with Fanum and Kai Cenat. Its use is now mostly irony, being used with other Gen Alpha slang for a hurmor effect (such as "skibidi rizz fanum taxed").
