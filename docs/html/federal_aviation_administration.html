Federal Aviation Administration

The {{bFederal Aviation Administration}} (Abbreviation) is an government agency under the United States related to any aerial transportation, primarily commercial aircraft and other aspects of civil aviation. They also regulation flight inspection, pilot certification, and more. The current Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration (who has a term length of 5 year) is Michael Whitaker started on October 27, 2023.
