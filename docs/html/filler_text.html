Filler text

{{bFiller text}}, also known as {{bplaceholder text}}, is a placeholder comprised of text to fill space and to have a preview of what a final product would appear as when using text. It saves the effort required to writing real text and is used to quickly check a typeface or font, test a layout (graphic design), and stress test spam filter. A popular filler text used in graphic design is {{iLorem ipsum}}.
