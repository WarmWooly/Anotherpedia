Final Boss Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

The Final Boss Roomba is the last boss enemy in Attack of the Roombas, having the abilities of the Factory Roomba, Cannon Roomba, Trapper Roomba, and Healer Boss Roomba. Every 10 seconds (or 12 seconds if it just spawned), a Minion Roomba will be generated. Every 8.33 seconds, a Trap Roomba will be generated. Every 5 seconds, it will shoot a rubber ball. Every 2 seconds, the Final Boss Roomba will heal 1 HP if damaged. The Final Boss Roomba will only appear once, and upon defeating it, will grant the player a decreased reload time. To prevent an infinite score farm, each time the Final Boss Roomba heals, 1 score is removed.
