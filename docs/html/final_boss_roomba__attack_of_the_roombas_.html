Final Boss Roomba ({{iAttack of the Roombas}})

<><>||{{bStats}}||{{bHealth}}|100 HP||{{bSpeed}}|300%||{{bSize}}|175%||{{bMin.&spKills}}|100 killsinfo>>The {{bFinal Boss Roomba}} is the last boss enemy in {{i[[Attack of the Roombas]]}}, having the abilities of the [[Factory Roomba|Factory Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]], [[Cannon Roomba|Cannon Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]], [[Trapper Roomba|Trapper Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]], and [[Healer Boss Roomba|Healer Boss Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]]. Every 10 seconds (or 12 seconds if it just spawned), a [[Minion Roomba|Minion Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]] will be generated. Every 8.33 seconds, a [[Trap Roomba|Trap Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)]] will be generated. Every 5 seconds, it will shoot a rubber ball. Every 2 seconds, the Final Boss Roomba will heal 1 HP if damaged. The Final Boss Roomba will only appear once, and upon defeating it, will grant the [[player|player (Attack of the Roombas)]] a decreased reload time. To prevent an infinite score farm, each time the Final Boss Roomba heals, 1 score is removed.
