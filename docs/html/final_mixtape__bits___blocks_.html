Final Mixtape (Bits & Blocks)

The Final Mixtape is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the mixtape of the same name. It can be displayed on a mixtape stand. The Final Mixtape can be crafted with 1 Jungle Mixtape, 1 Sky Mixtape, 1 Ocean Mixtape, and 1 Fire Mixtape (shapeless). The Final Mixtape is used in the following crafting recipes:
Party Ball - 1 Final Mixtape, 1 Beach Ball, and 1 paper (shapeless).
