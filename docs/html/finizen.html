Finizen

Finizen is a dolphin Water type Pokémon from the ninth generation. Finizen closely resembled dolphins, being covered in a pale blue across most of its body. It has two fins on the side, a tail fin split in two, and a dorsal fin. The underbelly extending to its snout to before the tail, and around the eyes, are white. There is also a small wavy white tip on its dorsal fin and a wavy stripe consisting of six directional changes (with a pair of semi-circles in the first two troughs) on both sides of its body. Its eyes are slightly raised and consist of an even split of a blue and pink iris. It also has a blowhole and a water-colored ring around the joint to its tail fin.&pFinizen's official size is 4 ft and 3 in., while weighing 132.7 lbs. Shiny Finizen are a more light blurple color with blue and green eyes.&pFinizen's name comes from fin and potentially citizen. Finizen plays with other Finizen using its ring. It can also use ultrasonic waves to detect emotions. The ring is made of seawater and blowhole goo. It hunts Dragalge for food, and like real dolphins, live in pods. A Finizen has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Finizen is the 963rd Pokémon in the Pokédex and has the Pokédex color blue.&pFinizen can evolve at level 38 while connected to a Union Circle into Palafin.
