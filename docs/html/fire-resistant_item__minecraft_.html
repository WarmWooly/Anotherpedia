Fire-resistant item ({{iMinecraft}})

<><>A {{bfire-resistant item}} in {{i[[Minecraft]]}} is an [[item|item (Minecraft)]], that when thrown into [[fire|fire (Minecraft)]] or [[lava|lava (Minecraft)]] as an [[entity|item (Minecraft entity)]], will not burn and will not be destroyed. Fire-resistant items will slowly float in lava like items in [[water|water (Minecraft)]]. Only [[netherite|netherite (Minecraft)]]-related items have fire resistance, while all others will be destroyed as normal. Fire-resistant items can still be destroyed by other means, such as [[explosions|explosion (Minecraft)]], [[cacti|cactus (Minecraft)]], and being thrown into the [[void|void (Minecraft)]].
