Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)

A {{bfire-resistant item}} in {{iMinecraft}} is an item (Minecraft), that when thrown into fire (Minecraft) or lava (Minecraft) as an item (Minecraft entity), will not burn and will not be destroyed. Fire-resistant items will slowly float in lava like items in water (Minecraft). Only netherite (Minecraft)-related items have fire resistance, while all others will be destroyed as normal. Fire-resistant items can still be destroyed by other means, such as explosion (Minecraft), cactus (Minecraft), and being thrown into the void (Minecraft).
