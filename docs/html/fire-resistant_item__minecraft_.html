Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)

A fire-resistant item in Minecraft is an item, that when thrown into fire or lava as an entity, will not burn and will not be destroyed. Fire-resistant items will slowly float in lava like items in water. Only netherite-related items have fire resistance, while all others will be destroyed as normal. Fire-resistant items can still be destroyed by other means, such as explosions, cacti, and being thrown into the void.
