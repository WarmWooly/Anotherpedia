Fire/Fight Dex

For the firefighting Personal Protective Equipment company, see Fire-Dex.
Fire/Fight Dex or firefightdex (<>) is a blog (Tumblr) on Tumblr, mostly dedicated to depicting Pokémon (fictional species) as Fire type (Pokémon) and/or Fighting type (Pokémon), alongside responding to other comments/questions, selling Pokémon-related merchandise, and other miscellaneous content. The first post made on the Fire/Fight Dex was of Bulbasaur as a Fire type on September 26, 2016. The blog is run by Marco Fanjul, a 2D artist and 3D artist and animator. <>, Marco is currently on hiatus from the blog to focus on his professional career as a 3D artist/animator.
