Fire/Fight Dex

For the firefighting PPE company, see Fire-Dex.&spFire/Fight Dex or firefightdex (<>) is a blog on Tumblr, mostly dedicated to depicting Pokémon as Fire and/or Fighting type, alongside responding to other comments/questions, selling Pokémon-related merchandise, and other miscellaneous content. The first post made on the Fire/Fight Dex was of Bulbasaur as a Fire type on September 26, 2016.<> The blog is run by Marco Fanjul, a 2D and 3D artist and animator. <>, Marco is currently on hiatus from the blog to focus on his professional career as a 3D artist/animator.<>
