Firework Amulet (Bits & Blocks)

The firework amulet is a necklace curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that heals and applies buffs upon taking damage from a firework rocket. The firework amulet can be crafted with 1 Firework Festival and 1 string (shapeless). When the firework amulet is worn in a necklace slot, taking damage from a firework will apply Instant Health for a tick and Resistance II for 10 seconds. The firework amulet is used in the following crafting recipes:
Aurora Firework Amulet - 1 firework amulet and 1 Ocean Mixtape (shapeless).
