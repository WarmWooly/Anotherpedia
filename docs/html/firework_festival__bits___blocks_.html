Firework Festival (Bits & Blocks)

Firework Festival is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Firework Festival can be crafted with 1 minigame case and 2 firework rockets (shapeless). Firework Festival is used in the following crafting recipes:
Firework Amulet - 1 Firework Festival and 1 string (shapeless).
Ocean Mixtape - 1 Firework Festival, 1 mixtape case, 1 Pop-Up Kitchen, 1 Hammer Time!, and 1 Molecano (shapeless).
