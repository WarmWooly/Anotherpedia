Firework Festival (Bits & Bops)

Firework Festival is the tenth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is controlling when fireworks go off. The minigame has fireworks being launched upwards. The player must hit Button 1 on the third beat of a firework launching. Throughout the level, the camera will pan to different locations, and at the very end, the fireworks will be off-screen.

If the player is on time, a white ring will appear around the center of the firework. If they are slightly off, no outline will appear. Being really off will cause the firework to fizzle out. If an input is made really early, a fizzle effect will play mid-launch, with the firework still being able to go off. If there is no input, a disappointing sound will play as the firework disappears.
