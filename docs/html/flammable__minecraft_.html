Flammable (Minecraft)

Flammable is a property of block (Minecraft) in Minecraft that allows a block to be destroyed after a prolonged period of exposure to fire (Minecraft). This is different from a block's ability to catch fire from lava (Minecraft); however, in most cases, flammable blocks can catch fire from lava, and blocks like crafting table (Minecraft) and chest (Minecraft) can catch fire despite not burning up. Different blocks will have different odds of catching fire and being destroyed by fire (such as short grass (Minecraft) being much more likely to burn than logs (Minecraft)). Other blocks, like netherrack (Minecraft), can sustain fire forever, but are not flammable because they do not burn away.
