Flavin-containing monooxygenase 3

Flavin-containing monooxygenase 3, abbreviated as FMO3, is a flavoprotein enzyme naturally occurring in humans. The structure is dictated by the FMO3 gene and is the most prominent of the flavin-containing monooxygenases in the liver of adults.
