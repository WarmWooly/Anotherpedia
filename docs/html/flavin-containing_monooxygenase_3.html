Flavin-containing monooxygenase 3

Flavin-containing monooxygenase 3, abbreviation as FMO3, is a flavoprotein enzyme naturally occurring in human. The enzyme structure is dictated by the FMO3 gene and is the most prominent of the flavin-containing monooxygenase in the liver of adult.
