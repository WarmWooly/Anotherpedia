Flint Tile (Knapped)

<>The flint tile is a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for decoration. Flint tiles can be crafted with 4 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 stone/smooth stone/smooth sandstone/smooth red sandstone (surround the stone with the bricks on all sides). Flint tiles are used in the following crafting recipes:&spKnapped Logo - 1 flint tile and 1 flint (shapeless). The flint tile can be cut into flint stone in a Stonecutter.
