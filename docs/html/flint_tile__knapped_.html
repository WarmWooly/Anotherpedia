Flint Tile (Knapped)

<>The {{bflint tile}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iKnapped (Minecraft mod)}} used for decoration. Flint tiles can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 knapped flint brick (Knapped) and 1 stone (Minecraft)/smooth stone (Minecraft)/smooth sandstone (Minecraft)/smooth red sandstone (Minecraft) (surround the stone with the bricks on all sides). Flint tiles are used in the following crafting recipes:&spKnapped Logo (Knapped) - 1 flint tile and 1 flint (Minecraft) (shapeless). The flint tile can be cut into flint stone (knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft).
