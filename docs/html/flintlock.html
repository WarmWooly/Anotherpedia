Flintlock

<><>A {{bflintlock}} is a type of [[firearm]] that uses [[flint]] and a [[metal]] to [[ignite|ignition]] [[gunpowder]] or [[black powder]]. Flintlocks started to appear around the 1500s in [[Western Europe]]. Multiple types of firearms, such as [[pistols|pistol]], [[rifles|rifle]], and [[muskets|musket]] were developed with flintlocks.
