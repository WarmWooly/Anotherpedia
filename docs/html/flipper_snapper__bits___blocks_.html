Flipper Snapper (Bits & Blocks)

Flipper Snapper is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Flipper Snapper can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 lever, and 1 glass (shapeless). Flipper Snapper is used in the following crafting recipes:
Beach Ball - 1 Flipper Snapper and 1 slimeball (shapeless).
Jungle Mixtape - 1 Flipper Snapper, 1 mixtape case, 1 Sweet Tooth, 1 Rock, Paper, Showdown!, and 1 Pantry Parade (shapeless).
