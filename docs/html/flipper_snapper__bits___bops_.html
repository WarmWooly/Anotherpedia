Flipper Snapper (Bits & Bops)

Flipper Snapper is the first minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a cat taking photos of a seal bouncing a ball. The minigame has the cat taking photos of the seal when its nose hits the ball. The player must hit Button 1 in time with the seal and ball meeting, with bubbles appearing in the water and an exclamation mark appearing above the cat's head. When three exclamation marks appear, the player must take the photo three times in a row.

During the level, the boat moves off to the left, then returns with a seagull controlling the camera while the cat lies in an inflatable tube with a hot dog (which the seal steals). The seal and cat swap places at one point, and later, the cat comes from above to boop the seal. The level ends with the ball popping on the seal, covering its face as it sinks.

If the player is on time, a thick orange bounce outline will appear in the photo. If they are slightly off, no outline will appear. Being really off will cause the photo to have blue lines above it, with a picture of the incorrect shot or a picture of the cat's feet. If there is no input, a blue dashed outline of a missing photo will appear.
