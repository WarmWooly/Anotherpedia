Flour (Age of Steam Workshop Expansion)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Age of Steam Workshop Expansion used to make bread, replacing the vanilla crafting recipe. On a running millstone, 1 wheat can be milled into 2 flour. Flour can be smelted in a furnace to produce 1 bread, alongside experience.
