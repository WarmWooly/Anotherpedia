Flour (Age of Steam Workshop Expansion)

<>Flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Age of Steam Workshop Expansion used to make bread (Minecraft), replacing the vanilla crafting (Minecraft). On a running millstone (Age of Steam Workshop Expansion), 1 wheat (Minecraft) can be milled into 2 flour. Flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) to produce 1 bread (Minecraft), alongside experience (Minecraft).
