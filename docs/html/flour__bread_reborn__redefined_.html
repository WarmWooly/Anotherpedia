Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

For the type of item, see flours ({{aiBread Reborn: Redefined).}}
<>Flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. Flour can be baked into bread (Minecraft). Flour can be obtained by milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) wheat (Minecraft) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce bread (Minecraft), taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft). The advancement (Minecraft) Baking Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will also be awarded. In a brewing stand (Minecraft), flour is used in the following recipes:
Enchanted Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) - 1 enchanted golden apple (Minecraft) --> 1--3 flour
Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) - 1 golden apple (Minecraft) --> 1--3 flour
