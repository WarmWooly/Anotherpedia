Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

For the type of item, see flours ({{aiBread Reborn: Redefined).}}&sp<>Flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Flour can be baked into bread. Flour can be obtained by milling wheat on a millstone. Flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving < 30% chance).(text=u-d0.1note>> experience. The advancement Baking Bread will also be awarded.
