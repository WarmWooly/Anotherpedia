Flours (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

For the item, see flour ({{aiBread Reborn: Redefined).}}
<>The flours are a type of item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. The flours are used to be smelting (Minecraft) or smoker (Minecraft) into bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), with eleven different types of flour. Nine of the eleven flours can be obtained through milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined): flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), dirt flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), crimson flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), warped flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), redflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), glowflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), gunflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), chorus flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined), and sculk flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined). The golden flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) and enchanted golden flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) can be obtained by brewing (Minecraft) a golden apple (Minecraft) or enchanted golden apple (Minecraft) onto flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined). The flours can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft), which will turn them into bread (Minecraft).
