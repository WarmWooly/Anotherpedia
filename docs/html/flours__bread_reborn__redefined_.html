Flours (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

For the item, see flour (aiBread Reborn: Redefined).&sp<>||Stack size|64||Fire/lava&spresistance|Noinfo>>The flours are a type of item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. The flours are used to be smelted or smoked into bread, with eleven different types of flour. Nine of the eleven flours can be obtained through milling on a millstone: flour, dirt flour, crimson flour, warped flour, redflour, glowflour, gunflour, chorus flour, and sculk flour. The golden and enchanted golden flours can be obtained by brewing a golden apple or enchanted golden apple onto flour. The flours can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker, which will turn them into bread.
