Flow Worms (Bits & Blocks)

For the mob, see flow worm ({{aiBits & Blocks).}}
Flow Worms is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Flow Worms can also be used on a flow worm to revert it to its default appearance. Flow Worms can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 glowstone dust, 1 dirt/coarse dirt/rooted dirt, and 1 moss block/grass block/grass/tall grass/fern/vines (shapeless). Flow Worms is used in the following crafting recipes:
Hollow Dirt - 1 Flow Worms and 1 dirt/coarse dirt/rooted dirt (shapeless).
Sky Mixtape - 1 Flow Worms, 1 mixtape case, 1 B-Bot and the Fly Girls, 1 Meet & Tweet, and 1 Steady Bears (shapeless).
