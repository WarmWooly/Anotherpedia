Flow Worms (Bits & Bops)

Flow Worms is the sixth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is the titular creature bouncing along other flow worms. The minigame has a set of eight flow worms, with the seventh (colored red/magenta) being controlled by the player. The player must hit Button 1 in time with the bouncing flow worms to keep it bouncing along. There are three types of flow worms to bounce: slow, normal, and fast. Normal flow worms follow the BPM of the song, fast are twice that, and slow are half that. Some patterns will have the faster flow worms outpace the slower ones, requiring the player to ensure that they are tracking all the flow worms on screen.

Partway through the minigame, only a spotlight showing two flow worms at a time will be visible, tracking a slow flow worm while a normal one catches up. Afterwards, the entire level will be set to a nighttime theme with a background crowd, with the main set of flow worms glowing. Hitting the final flow worm will cause it to land onto a flower above.

If the player is on time, a thick bounce outline will appear. If they are slightly off, only thin lines will appear. Fully rushing or dragging will cause the player to visibly bend away as the flow worm falls to the ground. The flow worm will also fall if there is no input.
