Flow Worms (song)

"Flow Worms" is an instrumental song rhythm game song for the Bits & Bops minigames (Bits & Bops) Flow Worms (Bits & Bops), created by Zorsy and made available by Tempo Lab Games in late-2025. The song is 2 minutes and 4 seconds in length, and is both available on music streaming service and through a purchasable soundtrack on Steam (service).
