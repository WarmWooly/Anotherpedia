Flow Worms (song)

"Flow Worms" is an instrumental rhythm game song for the Bits & Bops minigame of the same name, created by Zorsy and made available by Tempo Lab Games in late-2025. The song is 2 minutes and 4 seconds in length, and is both available on music streaming platforms and through a purchasable soundtrack on Steam.
