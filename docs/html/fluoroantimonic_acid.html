Fluoroantimonic acid

Fluoroantimonic acid is a mixture between hydrogen fluoride and antimony pentafluoride. Fluroantimonic acid comes in the form of a colorless liquid. Fluoroantimonic acid's chemical formula is SbHFs-b6. It is considered corrosive due to how acidic and a health hazard due to being lethal on digestion or inhalation, severe skin burns, and a potential carcinogen.&pFluoroantimonic acid is considered a superacid due to being trillions of times more acidic than 100% sulfuric acid, with a Hs-b0 of -28.
