FN FAL (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The FN FAL is a primary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Chaos Insurgency at level 3. It has very high damage, but its abysmal firerate, limited ammo capacity, and longer reload time severely weaken the gun's viability.
