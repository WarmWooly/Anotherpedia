Food (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

Food is a roleplay item in SCP: Devolved Revamp. Located in Sector 1 of the Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp), there is a cafeteria and a Lunch Lady (SCP: Devolved Revamp) who players can interact with and obtain food from. Players may grab as much food as they want of any type. The available food options are Pizza, Hamburger, and Sandwich. Food serves no purpose in the game outside of roleplaying.
