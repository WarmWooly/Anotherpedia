Fox News' firing of Tucker Carlson

<>{{tFor the [[book]] covering the firing, see [[{{aiThe Firing of Tucker Carlson by Fox News: Best Analysis of Who Tucker is, Why He was Fired, and His Future}}]].}}&sp<>On April 24th, 2023, [[Tucker Carlson]] was [[fired]] from [[Fox News]], a decision made by the [[CEO]] of Fox News Media [[Suzanne Scott]] and the CEO of [[Fox Corperation]] [[Lachlan Murdoch]]. [[Scandals|scandal]] such as the [[Dominion lawsuit|Dominion Voting Systems v. Fox News Network]] filed 2 years prior which settled for three-quarters of a million stirred the waters for the decision to fire Tucker Carlson from the network. Tucker Carlson has since created his own [[subscription streaming service]], the [[Tucker Carlson Network]], on December 11, 2023, after leaving Fox News.
