Freezing (Mindustry)

Freezing is a status effect (Mindustry) in Mindustry that can be obtained by being in pools of Cryofluid (Mindustry) or by being shot with a liquid turret (Mindustry) loaded with Cryofluid. unit (Mindustry) effected with Freezing have a health (Mindustry) multiplier of 80% and a speed (Mindustry) multiplier of 60%. If the effect Blasted (Mindustry) is applied while Freezing, the unit takes an additional 18 armor-piercing damage. Burning (Mindustry) and Melting (Mindustry) will override Freezing (ex. getting Burning/Melting while Freezing) and can be overridden by Freezing (ex. getting Freezing while Burning/Melting).
