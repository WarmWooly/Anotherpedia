Freezing (Mindustry)

Freezing is a status effect in Mindustry that can be obtained by being in pools of Cryofluid or by being shot with a liquid turret loaded with Cryofluid. Units effected with Freezing have a health multiplier of 80% and a speed multiplier of 60%. If the effect Blasted is applied while Freezing, the unit takes an additional 18 armor-piercing damage. Burning and Melting will override Freezing (ex. getting Burning/Melting while Freezing) and can be overridden by Freezing (ex. getting Freezing while Burning/Melting).
