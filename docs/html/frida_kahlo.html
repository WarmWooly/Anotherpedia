Frida Kahlo

Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo y Calderón was a Mexican painter and a member of the Mexican Communist Party. Some of her most well-known paintings were The Two Fridas, Henry Ford Hospital, and Self-Portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird. She was born on July 6, 1907, and died on July 13, 1954, at the age of 47. Frida's husband was Diego Rivera. Her mother was Matilde Calderón y González, and her father was Guillermo Kahlo.
