Friendslop

<><>{{bFriendslop}} is [[slang]] for a [[video game]] where the main appeal is drawn by playing with [[friends|friend]] and the interactions generated though elements such as requiring [[teamwork]] and using [[proximity voice chat]]. Common examples of friendslop include {{i[[Lethal Company]]}}, {{i[[Content Warning|Content Warning (video game)]]}}, {{i[[R.E.P.O.]]}}, and {{i[[Peak|Peak (video game)]]}}, which rely on players working together to achieve a [[goal]] while also giving a plethora of opportunities to mess with each other using the existing [[game mechanics|game mechanic]]. Friendslop is often used to criticize games that fall under its categorization, with proponents claiming that the [[gameplay]] of friendslop is lacking.
