Frisbee Flinger (Laser Fight)

The Frisbee Flinger is a frisbee-throwing gun in Laser Fight. The frisbees can bounce off walls and skid across floors, making an effective weapon for controlling space and keeping opponents on the move. Sometimes a frisbee gets thrown a little early or late, having it veer off from the intended target.
