Frisbee Flinger (Laser Fight)

<>The Frisbee Flinger is a frisbee-throwing gun (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game). The frisbees can bounce off walls and skid across floors, making an effective weapon for controlling space and keeping opponents on the move. Sometimes a frisbee gets thrown a little early or late, having it veer off from the intended target.
