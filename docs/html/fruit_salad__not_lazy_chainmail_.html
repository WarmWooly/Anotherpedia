Fruit Salad (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Fruit salad is a mixed food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail. Fruit salad can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 glazed apple (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 glazed glow berries (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 glazed sweet berries (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Fruit salad can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. The bowl is returned after eating.
