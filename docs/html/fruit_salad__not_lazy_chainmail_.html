Fruit Salad (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Fruit salad is a mixed food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Fruit salad can be crafted with 1 glazed apple, 1 glazed glow berries, 1 glazed sweet berries, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Fruit salad can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. The bowl is returned after eating.
