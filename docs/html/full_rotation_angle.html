Full rotation angle

<><>A {{bfull rotation angle}} is an [[angle]] of 360 [[degrees|degree (angle)]], or 2[[&mpi|pi]] [[radians|radian]]. The angle goes in a full [[circle]], ending back to where it was started. Without aid from an [[arrow]] showing rotation, it would look identical to a [[zero degree angle]].
