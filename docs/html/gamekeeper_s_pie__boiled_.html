Gamekeeper's Pie (Boiled)

Gamekeeper's pie is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Gamekeeper's pie can be crafted with 2 boiled carrots, 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled rabbit, and 1 bone broth (shapeless). Gamekeeper's pie can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. A bone-filled bowl is returned to the player after eating for reuse.
