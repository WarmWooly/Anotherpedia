Gantry

A gantry is an overhead support structure made out of steel to hold up something. Gantries can have legs extending to the ground to be affix or connected to wheel (especially on railroad track) to make the gantry mobile. Gantry crane utilize a gantry to hold a crane above a payload. Gantry (transport) are also seen holding highway sign, railway signal, and traffic camera above road and along railway.
