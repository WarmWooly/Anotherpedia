Garbage Disposal (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The garbage disposal is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to destroy items. The garbage disposal can be crafted with 1 cactus, 3 sand, and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with sand, place the cactus in the middle, and fill the rest with cobblestone). Crafting a garbage disposal will award the advancement Compulsive Decluttering. Items that are dropped above the garbage disposal will be destroyed. There is no way to recover items lost using this method.
