Gartic Phone

{{iGartic Phone}} is a multiplayer game browser game of Telephone Pictionary released in 2020 by Onrizon Social Games. The game has a player write a sentence, followed by someone drawing it, with another player writing what they think the drawing is, repeating between the two phases until all players read/write about another player's prompt. Other gamemode changes up the gameplay significantly, such as making an animation, trying to create a knock-off of another player's drawing with progressively less time, and a mode where different players draw the head, body, and legs of a combined character. Gartic Phone has multiple spin-off, including GarticTV and Gartic Show.
