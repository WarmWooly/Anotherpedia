Garvey fastener

<><>A {{bGarvey fastener}}, also called a {{bGarvey tag}} or a {{bGarvey}}, is a [[metal]] or [[plastic]] [[fastener]] used on [[textiles|textile]] (such as [[clothing]]). They are often produced as [[transparent]], white, or black, and are almost exclusively [[sold|selling]] in [[bulk|bulk purchase]]. These fasteners may hold [[tags|tag]] which includes information of the [[product|product (business)]]. They may also be used to keep multiple articles of clothing together during [[packaging]] and [[shipping]]. It is also useful for preventing tampering or [[theft]] of products. The main [[producer]] of the Garvey fastener is [[Garvey Products, Inc.]]
