Garvey fastener

A {{bGarvey fastener}}, also called a {{bGarvey tag}} or a {{bGarvey}}, is a metal or plastic fastener used on textile (such as clothing). They are often produced as transparent, white, or black, and are almost exclusively selling in bulk purchase. These fasteners may hold tag which includes information of the product (business). They may also be used to keep multiple articles of clothing together during packaging and shipping. It is also useful for preventing tampering or theft of products. The main producer of the Garvey fastener is Garvey Products, Inc.
