Garvey fastener

A Garvey fastener, also called a Garvey tag or a Garvey, is a metal or plastic fastener used on textiles (such as clothing). They are often produced as transparent, white, or black, and are almost exclusively sold in bulk. These fasteners may hold tags which includes information of the product. They may also be used to keep multiple articles of clothing together during packaging and shipping. It is also useful for preventing tampering or theft of products. The main producer of the Garvey fastener is Garvey Products, Inc.
