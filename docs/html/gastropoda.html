Gastropoda

{{bGastropoda}}, also known as {{bgastropods}} and {{bslugs and snails}}, is a class (biology) of invertebrate under the phylum Mollusca, containing slug and snail that live in freshwater, saltwater, and on land animal. Gastropoda has the second (ordering) most name species, only behind Insecta (insect). There are currently 721 family (biology) of known Gastropoda.
