Gem alert

For the meme with a magnifying glass, see gem alert magnifying glass.
A gem alert is a wojak meme format as a reaction to a high-quality post or sent meme. Several variations of the gem alert meme exist, often with some wojak who is around gems and/or comprised of gems. The gems tend to be a lighter blue in color with a brilliant cut, similar to diamonds. It's similar to the term "hidden gem", both using the value of gemstones in comparison with the content they are attached to.
