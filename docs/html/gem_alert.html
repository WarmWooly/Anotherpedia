Several variations of the gem alert meme exist, often with some wojak who is around gem and/or comprised of gems. The gems tend to be a lighter blue in color with a brilliant (gemstone cut) cut (gems), similar to diamond. It's similar to the term "hidden gem", both using the value of gemstones in comparison with the content they are attached to.
