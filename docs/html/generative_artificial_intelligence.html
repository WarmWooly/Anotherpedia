Generative artificial intelligence

Generative artificial intelligence (generative AI/GenAI) refers to artificial intelligence used to create unique content, including text, images, audio, and video. It utilizes a large set of related training data to mimick creativity of a human. Generative AI especially became prevalent after the AI boom in the early-2020s, featuring chatbots, image generators, and implementation into software.
