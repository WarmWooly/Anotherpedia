Genre

<><>A {{bgenre}} is a [[categorization]] of [[art]], [[literature]], [[music]], [[film]], [[game|video game]], or other [[creative work]]. Genres are defined based on their [[similarities|similarity]] in regards to [[style]], [[form]], and [[subject]]. Genres are used to allow [[consumers|consumer]] to identify what type of [[content]] they wish and select works based on that content type.
