George Washingtoad

George Washingtoad is a toad that appears in The Super Mario Bros. Super Show! in the episode "The Koopas are Coming! The Koopas are Coming!" released in late-1989, resembling George Washington. Mario, Luigi, Princess Toadstool, and Toad aid George Washingtoad in the episode to defend the Thirteen Mushroom Colonies from Redcoat Koopa (Bowser) and his army, with the colonies being a stand-in for the Thirteen Colonies and Bowser and his army in place of the British.
