George Washingtoad

{{bGeorge Washingtoad}} is a toad (fictional species) that appears in {{iThe Super Mario Bros. Super Show!}} in the tv episode "The Koopas are Coming! The Koopas are Coming!" released in late-1989, resembling George Washington. Mario, Luigi, Princess Peach, and Toad (character) aid George Washingtoad in the episode to defend the Thirteen Mushroom Colonies from Redcoat Koopa (Bowser) and his army, with the colonies being a stand-in for the Thirteen Colonies and Bowser and his army in place of the British military in the Revolutionary War.
