Gilded Rose (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gilded rose is a secret block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as decoration. Unlike other blocks, it is hidden from the Creative menu. The gilded rose can be crafted with 1 rose bush and 8 gold nuggets (place the roses in the center and surround with the nuggets). The gilded rose can be planted like other flowers on grass blocks, dirt, coarse dirt, rooted dirt, farmland, podzol, mycelium, moss blocks, mud, or muddy mangrove roots. The gilded rose cannot be pollinated, composted, or crafted into suspicious stew.
