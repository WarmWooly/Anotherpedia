The gilded rose can be planted like other flower (Minecraft) on grass block (Minecraft), dirt (Minecraft), coarse dirt (Minecraft), rooted dirt (Minecraft), farmland (Minecraft), podzol (Minecraft), mycelium (Minecraft), moss block (Minecraft), mud (Minecraft), or muddy mangrove roots (Minecraft).
