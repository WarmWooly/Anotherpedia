Glass

<><>{{bGlass}} is a [[brittle]] [[material]] which is often [[transparent]]. The most common method of [[production]] is to mix [[sand]] with [[soda ash]] and [[lime|lime (material)]] and to rapidly cool after heating. Glass is often used in [[construction]] (mainly [[windows|window]]), [[drinkwear]], and [[storage]] ([[jars|jar]], [[beakers|beaker]], [[test tubes|test tube]], [[etc.|et cetera]]).
