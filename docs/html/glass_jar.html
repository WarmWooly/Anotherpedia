Glass jar

A glass jar is a type of jar constructed from glass. Most glass jars today are made by inserting molten glass into a glass mold in the shape of a jar within a factory. Glass jars come in different shapes, sizes, and thicknesses based on their use case and storage capacity, often measured in ounce or mililiter. A common type of glass jar is known as the Mason jar, which has a screw thread metal jar lid to keep it sealed for food preservation. Lids can also be made of cork, wood, and glass, some with a rubber or silicone seal. Other uses for glass jars are to hold loose object and to be decoration.
