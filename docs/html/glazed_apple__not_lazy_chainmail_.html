Glazed Apple (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The glazed apple is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Glazed apples can be crafted with 1 sugar water bottle and 1 apple (shapeless, a glass bottle is returned). Glazed apples are used in the following crafting recipes:
Fruit Salad - 1 glazed apple, 1 glazed glow berries, 1 glazed sweet berries, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Glazed apples can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
