Glep (Smiling Friends)

Glep is a character in the animated series Smiling Friends who works for Smiling Friends Inc. alongside Alan, Pim, and Charlie. He was born on July 9, 329, making him <> years old. He is currently seen in every episode of Smiling Friends <>. Glep is relatively short Critter, standing at 1 ft and 6 in. tall, with a pair of small arms and legs. He is Screamin' Green in color. His mouth is comprised of two large portions that open and close similarly to a beak. His eyes are large, covering a significant portion of the sides of his head. He is usually seen in a purple wizard hat. He is only able to speak gibberish (voiced by Zach Hadel), but other people close to Glep can understand him. Glep, being unable to speak English, often is carried by his actions to convey emotions and humor. He is fond of his tablet and his Rubik's Cube but is less open to criticism and accusations made against him. He also tends toward sleeping on the job. He doesn't like Ed Sheeran. He is able to spit over long distances with high accuracy. He isn't seen interacting with his coworkers often, but he does have a wife Marge Simpson (a reference to Marge in The Simpsons) and to have a grandson, Gleppie, in a flash-forward to the future. He lives on 1352 Green Palm Terrace, Pennsylvania, and his Social Security number is 999-45-6789.
