Glep (Smiling Friends)

Glep is a character in the animated series Smiling Friends who works for Smiling Friends Inc. alongside Allan, Pim, and Charlie. He was born on July 9, 329, making him <> years old. He is seen in most episodes of Smiling Friends, but he rarely has a leading role. His most notable appearance is within The Glep Ep, where the episode focuses on who Glep is. Glep is relatively short critter, standing at 1 ft and 6 in. tall, with a pair of small arms and legs. He is Screamin' Green in color. His mouth is comprised of two large portions that open and close similarly to a beak. His eyes are large, covering a significant portion of the sides of his head. He is usually seen in a purple wizard hat, but he changes clothing to fit his task or job. Glep also is seen wearing costumes, including cosplay. He tends to speak gibberish (voiced by Zach Hadel) with the occasional short English remark, but other characters can understand him. Glep, being unintelligible for viewers, often is carried by his actions to convey emotions and humor. He is fond of his iPad and his Rubik's Cube, but is less open to criticism and accusations made against him. He also tends toward sleeping on the job. He is able to spit over long distances with high accuracy and enjoys dancing. He isn't seen interacting with his coworkers often, but he does have a wife Marge Simpson (a reference to Marge in The Simpsons) and to have a grandson, Gleppie, in a flash-forward to the future. He lives in a mansion on 1352 Green Palm Terrace, Pennsylvania, and his Social Security number is 999-45-6789. He also has a driver's license and is on the organ donor registry.
