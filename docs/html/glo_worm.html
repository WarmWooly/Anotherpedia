Glo Worm

The Glo Worm or Gloworm is a stuffed toy made for babies and toddlers that has the appearance of a human-worm hybrid wearing pajamas. It was designed by Hasbro's Playskool and was first released in 1982. Its head is made of vinyl. The toy can be squeezed to activate, making it glow and play music. It is powered with batteries inside the toy.
