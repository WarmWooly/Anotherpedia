Globe Trotters (Bits & Bops)

Globe Trotters is the sixteenth and last minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is an alien between two others trotting across planets. The minigame has the player walking between two other aliens in unison. The player must hit Button 1 in time to take a step. After the first signal noise, the player must "leap" (make two steps) and walk offbeat, with a second signal having the player leap again and return to normal. As the level progresses, the camera will zoom in and out while changing rotation. Towards the end of the level, more planets appear that the aliens march on.

If the player is on time, a thick blue outline will appear at the player's foot. If they are slightly off, no outline will appear. Being really off will cause the alien to slam into the one in front (too fast) or get bounced by the one behind (too slow/no input). When the player leaps on time, blue lines will appear.
