Glow Berry Jam (Boiled)

<>Glow berry jam is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Glow berry jam can be obtained by boiling glow berries (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). Glow berry jam is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Glow Berry Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled) - 1 glow berry jam and 1 bread (Minecraft) (shapeless). Glow berry jam can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
