Glow Berry Jam (Boiled)

Glow berry jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Glow berry jam can be obtained by boiling glow berries in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Glow berry jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Glow Berry Jam-Filled Bread - 1 glow berry jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Glow berry jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
