Glow Worm, or simply Worm, is a Just Shapes and Beats OC of the Plants created by harlows and posted onto DeviantART on April 16, 2019. Glow Worm takes on the same 4-segment design as other Plants, going from a darker blue to a light green, with her hands matching the green color. She is described to be the sister of Sproket and is always trying to be positive, despite getting ignored by others. She can also glow in the dark.
