Glowflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Glowflour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Glowflour can be baked into glowstone bread. Glowflour can be obtained by milling glowstone on a millstone. Each glowstone makes 2 glowflour. Glowflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce glowstone bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience.
