Glowflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>{{bGlowflour}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iBread Reborn: Redefined}}. Glowflour can be baked into glowstone bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Glowflour can be obtained by milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) glowstone (Minecraft) on a millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Each glowstone makes 2 glowflour.
