Glowstone Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Glowstone bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Glowflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce glowstone bread, taking 10 or 5 second respectively and giving experience. Glowstone bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Glowstone bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, glowstone bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
