Glowworm

For the stuffed toy, see glo worm.
A glowworm, glow-worm, or glow worm can refer to multiple insects which are bioluminescent, primarily larvae and wingless adults. There are no species of worm that are glowworms. Glowworms include firefly larvae and (usually) wingless females, some bioluminescent beetles, and some fungus gnats. The color is dependent on the species, with a wide range from orange to blue.
