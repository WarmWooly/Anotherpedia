Goff, Kansas

Goff is a town in Nemaha County, Kansas. <>, it has a population of 106. The town has an area of 0.21 mi2.

Goff was founded in 1880 by Edward H. Goff, an official from the Central Branch Union Pacific Railroad, while building a railroad through the territory. It became incorporated in 1887.

Ever since the boom in the early 1900s, the population of Goff has been steadily declining.
