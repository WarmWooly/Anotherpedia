Gold

<>{{tFor the [[color]], see [[gold (color)]].}}&sp<>{{bGold}} is a shiny [[metal]] [[element|chemical element]] with a notable yellowish-orange color. Gold found on [[Earth]] is often in the form of [[nuggets|gold nugget]] or grains in [[bodies of water|body of water]] or in [[rocks|rock]] (such as in [[veins|ore vein]]). Gold is well-known to be [[valuable]] and a [[status symbol]] for [[wealth]]. It is 79th on the [[Periodic Table of Elements]], also depicted as [[Au|Atomic Symbol]].
