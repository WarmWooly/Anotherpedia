Gold

For the color, see gold (color).
Gold is a shiny metal element with a notable yellowish-orange color. Gold found on Earth is often in the form of nuggets or grains in bodies of water or in rocks (such as in veins). Gold is well-known to be valuable and a status symbol for wealth. It is 79th on the Periodic Table of Elements, also depicted as Au.
