Gold

For the color, see gold (color).
Gold is a shiny metal chemical element with a notable yellowish-orange color. Gold found on Earth is often in the form of gold nugget or grains in body of water or in rock (such as in ore vein). Gold is well-known to be valuable and a status symbol for wealth. It is 79th on the Periodic Table of Elements, also depicted as Atomic Symbol.
