<>The gold chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. A gold chain naturally generates in a chainmailler home (Not Lazy Chainmail). The gold chain can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. The gold chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with a block of gold chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4). Gold chains have an 11.54% chance of being found in suspicious sand (Minecraft) within chainmail ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail).
