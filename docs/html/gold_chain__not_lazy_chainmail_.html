Gold Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The gold chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. A gold chain naturally generates in a chainmailler home. The gold chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 gold chain links to craft. The gold chain can be crafted with a block of gold chains (makes 4). Gold chains have an 11.54% chance of being found in suspicious sand within chainmail ruins.
Gold chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Block of Gold Chains - 4 gold chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Gold Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 gold chains (fill the middle and bottom rows).
Gold Lasso - 2 gold chains and 1 gold tool chain (place the tool chain in the top right corner and the chains in the middle and bottom right corner).
Gold Rope x4 - 1 gold chain, 1 gold tool chain, and 1 iron ingot (place the tool chain in the middle, chain below, and iron above).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 gold chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
