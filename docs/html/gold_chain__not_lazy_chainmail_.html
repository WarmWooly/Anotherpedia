Gold Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. A gold chain naturally generates in a chainmailler home. The gold chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 gold chain links to craft. The gold chain can be crafted with a block of gold chains (makes 4).
