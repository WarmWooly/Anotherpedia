Gold Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Gold chain links can be snipped using pliers from a gold coil or an unfinished gold project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Gold chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes a gold chain (makes 8 chain links). Gold chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:&spGold Chain - 8 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Belt - 128 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Boots - 256 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Bracelet - 64 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Chestplate - 512 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Gloves - 192 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Helmet - 320 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 gold chain links&spGold Chainmail Leggings - 448 gold chain link&spGold Chainmail Necklace - 128 gold chain links&spGold Tool Chain - 192 gold chain links&spMusic Disc "Bounce" - 32 gold chain links For Novice-level chainmaillers, there is a 22.22% chance to trade 64 gold chain links for 5 emeralds. In a Recycler, 9 gold chain links can be recycled into a gold nugget. Gold chainmail armor enchanted with Chain Repair will automatically take a gold chain link from the inventory to repair durability when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Gold chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator.
