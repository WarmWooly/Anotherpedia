Gold Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold chain link is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Gold chain links can be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) using pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) from a gold coil (Not Lazy Chainmail) or an unfinished gold project (Not Lazy Chainmail). While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Gold chain links can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes a gold chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 8 chain links).
