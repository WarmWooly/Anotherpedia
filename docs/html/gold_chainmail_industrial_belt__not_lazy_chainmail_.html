Gold Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Gold chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Gold Industrial Belt". The gold chainmail industrial belt takes 256 gold chain links to craft. Crafting the gold chainmail industrial belt will award the advancement Upgrades, People, Upgrades! The chances to find a gold chainmail industrial belt in a winding jig are the following:&spIn a chainmailler home, there is a 18.18% chance to contain a gold chainmail industrial belt. Gold chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:&spGold Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 gold chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 gold chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). The gold chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:&spAuto Mailler&spAuto Snipper&spAuto Winder&spBelt Generator&spChain Generator&spCobblestone Generator&spConveyor Warper&spCrusher&spItem Aggregator&spMelder&spRecycler&spWinding Jig Gold chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished gold chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Gold chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining gold chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Gold chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Gold chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on gold chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels.
