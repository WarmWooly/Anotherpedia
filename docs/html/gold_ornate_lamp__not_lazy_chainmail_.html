Gold Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bgold ornate lamp}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as redstone (Minecraft)-powered light (Minecraft). The gold ornate lamp can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 gold chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 redstone lamp (Minecraft) (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the gold ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the gold ornate lamp takes 0 tick (Minecraft) to turn off. {{gal}}
