Gold Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold ornate lamp is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as redstone-powered lighting. The gold ornate lamp can be crafted with 2 gold chain carpets and 1 redstone lamp (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the gold ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the gold ornate lamp takes 0 redstone ticks to turn off. gal
