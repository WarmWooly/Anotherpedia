Gold Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Malleability|160info>>The gold spool is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into gold coils, acting as the bulk alternative to the gold wire. Gold spools can be crafted with 1 empty spool and 8 gold ingots (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Gold spools can be obtained when a Crusher crushes gold spool blocks. A bright gold spool can be smoked in a smoker to produce a gold spool, taking 1 second. Gold spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Gold Spool Block - 1 gold spool and 2 wooden slabs (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Gold spools can be used on a winding jig or in an auto winder to be turned into 10 gold coils.
