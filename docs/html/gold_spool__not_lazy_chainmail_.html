Gold Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bgold spool}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used to be wound into gold coil (Not Lazy Chainmail), acting as the bulk alternative to the gold wire (Not Lazy Chainmail). Gold spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 8 gold ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Gold spools can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes gold spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). A bright gold spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce a gold spool, taking 1 second. Gold spools are used in the following crafting recipes:&spGold Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 gold spool and 2 wooden slab (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Gold spools can be used on a winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) or in an auto winder (Not Lazy Chainmail) to be turned into 10 gold coil (Not Lazy Chainmail).
