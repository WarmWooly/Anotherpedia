Gold Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The gold spool block is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as decoration. The gold spool block can be crafted with 1 gold spool and 2 wooden slabs (spool in the center, slabs above and below). A bright gold spool block can be smoked in a smoker to produce a gold spool block, taking 1 second. Gold spool blocks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Bright Gold Spool Block x4 - 4 gold spool blocks and 1 glowstone dust (place the glowstone dust in the center and surround it with the spool blocks). Gold spool blocks can be crushed into 1 gold spool. Spool blocks can be haunted into bright gold spool blocks. gal
