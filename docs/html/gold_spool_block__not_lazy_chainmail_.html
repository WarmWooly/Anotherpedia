Gold Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bgold spool block}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as decoration. The gold spool block can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 gold spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 2 wooden slab (Minecraft) (spool in the center, slabs above and below). A bright iron spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce a gold spool block, taking 1 second. Gold spool blocks are used in the following crafting recipes:&spBright Gold Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 4 gold spool blocks and 1 glowstone dust (Minecraft) (place the glowstone dust in the center and surround it with the spool blocks). Gold spool blocks can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into 1 gold spool (Not Lazy Chainmail). Spool blocks can be Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail) into bright gold spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). {{gal}}
