Gold Wire (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The gold wire is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into a gold coil. The bulk alternative to the gold wire is the gold spool. Gold wire can be crafted with 8 gold nuggets (place in a circle). Novice-level chainmaillers have a 22.22% chance to trade 3 emeralds for 1 gold wire. The chances to find a gold wire in chests are the following:&spIn a winding jig ruins, a chest has an 50.16% chance to contain a gold wire.<>&spIn a chainmailler home, a chest has a 51.77% chance to contain a gold wire.<> Gold wires have a 15.38% chance of being found in suspicious sand within chainmail ruins. Gold wire can be used on a winding jig or in an auto winder to be turned into an gold coil.
