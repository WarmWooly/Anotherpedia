The bulk alternative to the gold wire is the gold spool (Not Lazy Chainmail). Gold wire can be crafting (Minecraft) with 8 gold nugget (Minecraft) (place in a circle). Novice-level chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) have a 22.22% chance to trade 3 emerald (Minecraft) for 1 gold wire. The chances to find a gold wire in chest (Minecraft) are the following:
In a winding jig ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail), a chest has an 50.16% chance to contain a gold wire.
In a chainmailler home (Not Lazy Chainmail), a chest has a 51.77% chance to contain a gold wire. Gold wires have a 15.38% chance of being found in suspicious sand (Minecraft) within chainmail ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail). Gold wire can be used on a winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) or in an auto winder (Not Lazy Chainmail) to be turned into an gold coil (Not Lazy Chainmail).
