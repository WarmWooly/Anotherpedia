Golden Apple Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled)

Golden apple jam-filled bread is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Golden apple jam-filled bread can be crafted with 1 golden apple jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Golden apple jam-filled bread can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. As it has effects, golden apple jam-filled bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
