Golden Apple Jam (Boiled)

Golden apple jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Golden apple jam can be obtained by boiling a golden apple in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 1 exp. Golden apple jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Golden Apple Jam-Filled Bread - 1 golden apple jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Golden apple jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. As it has effects, golden apple jam can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
