Golden Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Golden bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Golden flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce golden bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 1 experience. The advancement A Golden Opportunity will also be awarded. Golden bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, golden bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
