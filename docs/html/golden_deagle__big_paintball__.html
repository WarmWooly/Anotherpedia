Golden Deagle (BIG Paintball!)

The Golden Deagle is a weapon (BIG Paintball!) that was unlockable during the 2019 Christmas events (BIG Paintball!) in the Roblox game BIG Paintball! It is nearly identical to the Deagle (BIG Paintball!) in stats, acting as a gold reskin, but its main effect is the bonus 5 credit (BIG Paintball!) (7-8 on mobile/console) the player earns per kill. It also causes hit players to become golden statues of themselves. As the event it came from has passed, it can no longer be obtained.
