Golden Desert Eagle XIX (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The Golden Desert Eagle XIX (or Golden Deagle) is a develiper guns (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp, with no method for normal players to obtain. Despite having half the firerate of the Desert Eagle XIX (SCP: Devolved Revamp), the Golden Desert Eagle XIX deals twice the damage per bullet, with headshots taking out over half a player's health per hit.
