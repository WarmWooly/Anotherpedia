Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Golden flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Golden flour can be baked into golden bread. Golden flour can be obtained by brewing a golden apple onto flour. Golden flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce golden bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 1 experience. The advancement A Golden Opportunity will also be awarded.
