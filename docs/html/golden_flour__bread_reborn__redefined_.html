Golden Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Golden flour is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. Golden flour can be baked into golden bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Golden flour can be obtained by brewing (Minecraft) a golden apple (Minecraft) onto flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined). Golden flour can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce golden bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined), taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving 1 experience (Minecraft). The advancement (Minecraft) A Golden Opportunity (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will also be awarded.
