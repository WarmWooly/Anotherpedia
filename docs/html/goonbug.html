Goonbug

For other terms relating to Goonbug, see Goonbug (disambiguation).
Goonbug is a beetle-like Grass type (Clash of Critters) Critter (Clash of Critters). Its character design seems to take inspiration from Dynastinae and Coccinellidae. Goonbug is notable outside of Clash of Critters due to its name having the slang term "goon (slang)", spreading in early- to mid-2026. Goonbug is a small, light green creature with an oval-shaped body. Its front half has a pair of horns, with the top one larger than the bottom and yellow/orange tips. Its backside has a dark cyan shell with yellow/orange spots, with a pair of white wings underneath. In the middle are a pair of large, cyan eyes on each side, with four small legs along the bottom.

Glitter (Clash of Critters) Goonbugs have a purple shell, horn tips, and eyes with a lighter green body color. Goonbug can evolution (Clash of Critters) into Capobeetle, who then can evolve into Donscarab.
