Gore (surveying)

A {{bgore}} is a section of land, often small, that is overlooked or conflicted between surveyor. In the United States, a gore is considered unincorporated territory in a county that is not in a town, which may or may not be population. Gores can be absorbed into nearby towns. The majority of gores are located in New England, such as Beuls Gore, Vermont, Warren's Gore, Vermont, Hibberts Gore, Maine, and Massachusetts Gore, Maine.
