Gore (surveying)

<><>A {{bgore}} is a section of [[land]], often small, that is overlooked or conflicted between [[surveyors|surveyor]]. In the [[United States]], a gore is considered [[unincorporated territory]] in a [[county]] that is not in a [[town]], which may or may not be [[populated|population]]. Gores can be absorbed into nearby towns. The majority of gores are located in [[New England]], such as [[Beuls Gore|Beuls Gore]], [[Vermont]], [[Warren's Gore|Warren's Gore]], Vermont, [[Hibberts Gore|Hibberts Gore]], [[Maine]], and [[Massachusetts Gore|Massachusetts Gore]], Maine.
