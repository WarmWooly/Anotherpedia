Gradian

<><>A {{bgradian}}, shown with {{igon}} or {{igrad}}, is a [[mathematical]] term used to describe the [[measure|measurement]] of an [[angle]]. A [[full rotation|full rotation angle]] of an angle is 400 gradians. A gradian is equal to 0.9 [[degrees|degree (angle)]], [[&mpi|pi]]/200 [[radians|radian]] (~0.01571 radians), and 1/400 [[turns|turn (angle)]].
