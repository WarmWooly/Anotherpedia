Graphite (Mindustry)

Graphite is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir that can be produced in a Graphite Press or Multi-Press using Coal or Coal and Water respectively on Serpulo. It can also be harvested from a Separator and a Disassembler. For Erekir, Graphite can generate in Graphitic Walls, which can be mined with the Evoke, Plasma Bore or higher.
