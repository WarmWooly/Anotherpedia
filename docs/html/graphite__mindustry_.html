Graphite (Mindustry)

Graphite is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) that can be produced in a Graphite Press (Mindustry) or Multi-Press (Mindustry) using coal (Mindustry) or Coal and Water (Mindustry) respectively on Serpulo. It can also be harvested from a Separator (Mindustry) and a Disassembler (Mindustry). For Erekir, Graphite can generate in Graphitic Wall (Mindustry), which can be mined with the Evoke (Mindustry), Plasma Bore (Mindustry) or higher. The description of Graphite is the following:Used in electrical components and turret ammunition.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used in the early-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry).
