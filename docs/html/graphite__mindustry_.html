Graphite (Mindustry)

Graphite is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) that can be produced in a Graphite Press (Mindustry) or Multi-Press (Mindustry) using coal (Mindustry) or Coal and Water (Mindustry) respectively on Serpulo.
