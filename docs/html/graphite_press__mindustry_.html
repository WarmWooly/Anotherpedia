Graphite Press (Mindustry)

A Graphite Press is a building (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) that is used to create graphite (Mindustry). The description of the Graphite Press is the following:Compresses coal into graphite. It has 160 health (Mindustry), taking 0.97 build time (Mindustry), while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 75 copper (Mindustry) and 30 lead (Mindustry). It can store up to 10 item (Mindustry). To create 1 Graphite, it requires 2 Coal (Mindustry). It has a production time of 1.5 seconds. To research (Mindustry) it, you must have the Mechanical Drill (Mindustry) researched. It costs 240 Copper and 900 Lead to research it.
