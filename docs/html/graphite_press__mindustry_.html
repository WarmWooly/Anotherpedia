Graphite Press (Mindustry)

A Graphite Press is a building in Mindustry on Serpulo that is used to create Graphite. The description of the Graphite Press is the following:Compresses coal into graphite. It has 160 health, taking 0.97 seconds to build, while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 75 Copper and 30 Lead. It can store up to 10 items. To create 1 Graphite, it requires 2 Coal. It has a production time of 1.5 seconds. To research it, you must have the Mechanical Drill researched. It costs 240 Copper and 900 Lead to research it.
