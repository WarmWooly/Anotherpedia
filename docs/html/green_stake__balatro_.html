Green Stake (Balatro)

Green Stake is the third available Stake in Balatro, where: ;;Blinds have faster scaling per Ante (requiring more chips to win)|Small Blind will not award any money;; The White and Red Stake must be defeated before unlocking the Green Stake. Upon defeating a Deck with the Green Stake, the Black Stake will become unlocked, alongside the Painted Deck.
