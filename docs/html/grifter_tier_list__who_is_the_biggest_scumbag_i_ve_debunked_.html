Grifter Tier List: Who is the Biggest Scumbag I've Debunked?

"Grifter Tier List: Who is the Biggest Scumbag I've Debunked?" is a YouTube video posted by Professor Dave Explains on June 27, 2025, where Dave goes over 35 "grifters" that he has debunked and rates them on a tierlist for how scummy they are/were. The video is 1 hour, 10 minutes, and 41 seconds in length. Dave utilized a system of breaking down four aspects of the grifters to get an average estimate of where they would go on the tierlist. Dave did not use inline rankings, so anyone within the same rank is roughly even to each other. The ranks are D, D-, F, F-, F--, and Scum, with D being the "best" grifter and Scum being the worst. Dave went through the entries in alphabetical order.

Dave's ranking methodology was the following:;;Each person would receive a score from D to Scum for four metrics: ::Harmfulness -- How much harm the grifter can/has done.|Malice -- How malicious/aware is the grifter of their lies.|Toxicity -- How toxic is the grifter.|Qualifications -- How much education the grifter has completed.::|A roughly estimated average of the scores will determine placement on the final ranking.|Some people may have such a bad score in one metric that it overrides others (such as harmfulness over toxicity).;; <> <>
