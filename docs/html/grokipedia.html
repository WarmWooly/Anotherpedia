Grokipedia

Grokipedia is an generative artificial intelligence online encyclopedia created by xAI, which can be found at <>. Grokipedia uses xAI's Grok (chatbot) AI chatbot to generate articles on the site. Unlike traditional wiki, users must suggest edits or articles to be made by Grok, which it will either accept or deny. The site was launched in 2025, with some content List of content forks of Wikipedia from Wikipedia. Grokipedia tends to lean towards Conservative (American politics) views on more controversial issue topics, such as gender, compared to Wikipedia.
