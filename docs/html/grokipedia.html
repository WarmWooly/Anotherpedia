Grokipedia

Grokipedia is an AI-generated online encyclopedia created by xAI, which can be found at <>. Grokipedia uses xAI's Grok chatbot to generate articles on the site. Unlike traditional wikis, users must suggest edits or articles to be made by Grok, which it will either accept or deny. The site was launched in 2025, with some content forked from Wikipedia. Grokipedia tends to lean towards Conservative views on more controversial topics, such as gender, compared to Wikipedia.
