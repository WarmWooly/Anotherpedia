Gun (Laser Fight)

A gun in Laser Fight is any equippable weapon that either is dropped during the round or is given to the player, depending on the mode. Guns can be equipped with codeE, dropped/thrown with codeBackspace, aimed with codeRight Click, and fired with codeLeft Click.
