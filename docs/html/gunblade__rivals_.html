Gunblade (RIVALS)

The Gunblade is a primary weapon in the Roblox game RIVALS, being a mix between medium-range and melee as it switches between acting like a gun and a blade (hence the name). The weapon seems to take inspiration from the Gunblade weapons in the Final Fantasy series. The design is a long rifle with a large blade underneath the barrel.

It has two modes: Gun Mode and Blade Mode, starting in Gun Mode at the beginning of a round. Upon hitting a player or a destructible object, the Gunblade switches modes. In Gun Mode, the player can aim, while in Blade Mode, the player can dash with a short cooldown. In Blade Mode, hitting an opponent will always be critical as long as the player has ammo remaining. On hitting an opponent in Blade Mode with ammo, a shot will be fired that always adds 15 damage, even if the Gunblade is not facing the player. When the player runs out of ammo, the Gunblade stays in Blade Mode.
