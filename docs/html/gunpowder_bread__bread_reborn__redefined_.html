Gunpowder Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Gunpowder bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Gunflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce gunpowder bread, taking 10 or 5 second respectively and giving experience. Gunpowder bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Gunpowder bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, gunpowder bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. When eaten, the advancement My main goal... will be awarded.
