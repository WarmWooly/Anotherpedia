Gyroscopic Mechanism (Create Aeronautics)

<>The gyroscopic mechanism is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Create Aeronautics used for creating block (Minecraft) that detect rotation. The gyroscopic mechanism is made by processing an iron sheet (Create) by putting it through mechanical deployer (Create) with a cogwheel (Create), shaft (Create), and brass nugget (Create) 5 times (using 5 of each listed item in total). During the process, it is in the form of an incomplete gyroscopic mechanism (Create Aeronautics).
