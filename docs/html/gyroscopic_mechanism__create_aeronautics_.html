Gyroscopic Mechanism (Create Aeronautics)

The gyroscopic mechanism is an item in the Minecraft mod Create Aeronautics used for creating blocks that detect rotation. The gyroscopic mechanism is made by processing an iron sheet by putting it through mechanical deployers with a cogwheel, shaft, and brass nugget 5 times (using 5 of each listed item in total). During the process, it is in the form of an incomplete gyroscopic mechanism.
