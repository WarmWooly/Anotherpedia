Hammer Time! (Bits & Bops)

Hammer Time! is the eleventh minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a hammer hitting nails on an instruction sheet. The minigame has boards of wood that slide from left to right with nails to be struck. The player must press Button 1 to hit the nails, matching the pattern of how they were placed. The position of the camera over the instructions changes, zooming in and out, rotating, and having a cat cover part of the instructions. At the end, there is a long delay between when the nails are placed and when they reach the hammer.

If the player is on time, a large burst will appear in the photo. If they are slightly off, only lines will appear. Being really off will cause the nail to become bent. If there is no input, the nail will tilt over.
