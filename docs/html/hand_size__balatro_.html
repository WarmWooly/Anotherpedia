Hand size (Balatro)

Hand size is a value used to determine the amount of playing cards that can be drawn into the hand in Balatro. The hand size will also determine how many cards can be displayed when opening a Tarot or Spectral Pack. The normal hand size is set to 8. Larger hands are often beneficial as they can make it more likely to draw cards for larger hand types and can hold more cards with held-in-hand effects (ex. Steel Card, Blue Seal). Hand size does not limit the maximum amount of cards that can be held in the hand. Besides in The Serpent Boss Blind, after every played poker hand, cards from the deck will be drawn until the hand is full or the deck runs out of cards.
