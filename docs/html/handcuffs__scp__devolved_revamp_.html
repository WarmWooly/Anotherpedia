Handcuffs (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Handcuffs are a primary tool within SCP: Devolved Revamp for the Administrative Branch at level 3 to arrest Facility players who break the rules. Arresting a player on any other team will put them on Class D for 1 minute. The cuffs will have a 60-second cooldown after use before they can be used again.Reasons for Foundation Staff Arrests:
- Breaching: intentionally letting out SCPs
- Conspiracy: helping Class D escape or cross-teaming with the Chaos Insurgency
- Negligence: refusing orders to perform expected and reasonable, role-specific duties
