Hard Boiled Egg (Boiled)

The hard boiled egg is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A hard boiled egg can be obtained by boiling a soft boiled egg in a boiler. It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.1 exp. Hard boiled eggs are used in the following crafting recipes:
Deviled Egg - 1 hard boiled egg and 1 egg (shapeless). Hard boiled eggs can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
