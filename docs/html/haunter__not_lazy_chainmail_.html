Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The haunter is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to haunt items into a "haunted" version. The haunter can be crafted with 5 cobblestone, 3 soul sand/soul soil, and 1 soul campfire (campfire in the middle, soul blocks in the bottom row, and fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Items that pass over the haunter's opening will be transformed into their "haunted" version.
