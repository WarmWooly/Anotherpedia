Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The haunter is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to haunt items into a "haunted" version. The haunter can be crafted with 5 cobblestone, 3 soul sand/soul soil, and 1 soul campfire (campfire in the middle, soul blocks in the bottom row, and fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Items that pass over the haunter's opening will be transformed into their "haunted" version. These are the recipes the haunter can be used to perform:
Copper Spool Block --> Bright Copper Spool Block
Gold Spool Block --> Bright Gold Spool Block
Iron Composite Spool Block --> Bright Iron Composite Spool Block
Iron Spool Block --> Bright Iron Spool Block
Netherite Spool Block --> Bright Netherite Spool Block
Rose Gold Spool Block --> Bright Rose Gold Spool Block
Spool Block --> Bright Spool Block
Red Mushroom --> Crimson Fungus
Exposed Copper Spool Block --> Exposed Bright Copper Spool Block
TNT/Minecart with TNT --> KABOOM! (explodes)
Brick --> Nether Brick
Brick Slab --> Nether Brick Slab
Brick Stairs --> Nether Brick Stairs
Bricks --> Nether Bricks
Brick Wall --> Nether Brick Wall
Stone/Cobblestone/Mossy Cobblestone/Deepslate/Cobbled Deepslate --> Netherrack
Oxidized Copper Spool Block --> Oxidized Bright Copper Spool Block
Campfire --> Soul Campfire
Lantern --> Soul Lantern
Sand/Red Sand/Suspicious Sand --> Soul Sand
Dirt/Coarse Dirt/Rooted Dirt/Grass Block/Podzol/Mycelium/Moss Block/Mud/Muddy Mangrove Roots --> Soul Soil
Torch --> Soul Torch
Brown Mushroom --> Warped Fungus
Waxed Copper Spool Block --> Waxed Bright Copper Spool Block
Waxed Exposed Copper Spool Block --> Waxed Exposed Bright Copper Spool Block
Waxed Oxidized Copper Spool Block --> Waxed Oxidized Bright Copper Spool Block
Waxed Weathered Copper Spool Block --> Waxed Weathered Bright Copper Spool Block
Weathered Copper Spool Block --> Weathered Bright Copper Spool Block
