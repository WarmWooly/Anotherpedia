Head-on

Not to be confused with HeadOn, {{aiHead On (1998 film), or aiHead-On (2004 film).}}
Head-on is a term used to describe when a thing (typically a person) is facing forward. It is often used when discussing collisions, such as the following example:When she hit the patch of ice on the road, the car skid and slammed into the guardrail head-on.
