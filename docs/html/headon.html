HeadOn

Not to be confused with head-on.
HeadOn is a brand of homeopathic headache relief owned by Miralus Healthcare. It has no clinical trail proving their claim of headache relief, only being a pseudoscientific placebo. It had a notable advertisement where they say the phrase "HeadOn: Apply directly to the forehead" three times.

The product resembles a glue stick, with a twistable bottom to push the wax up the tube. Active ingredients it includes are iris versicolor and bryonia alba, with inactive ingredients of diazolidinyl urea, ethyl alcohol, iodopropynyl butylcarbamate, menthyl lactate, propylene glycol, sodium stearate, steareth-21, tetrasodium EDTA, triethanolamine, and water. It has the National Drug Code of 67567-0306. HeadOn is applied topically to the forehead.
