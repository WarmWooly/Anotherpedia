Headphones

Headphones are a pair of wearable device connected earphone. This may be in the form of a headband, a part to wrap around the ear, or inserted within. It can be used to listening to audio file and/or be connected to electronic device. Headphones are often used to listen to music or other speech, such as in video, especially for personal use. Headphones may be connected using an AUX cable or via Bluetooth.
