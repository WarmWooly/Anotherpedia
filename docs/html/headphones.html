Headphones

Headphones are a pair of wearable connected earphones. This may be in the form of a band, a part to wrap around the ear, or inserted within. It can be used to listen to audio files and/or be connected to electronic devices. Headphones are often used to listen to music or other speech, such as in videos, especially for personal use. Headphones may be connected using an AUX cable or via Bluetooth.
