Headphones

{{bHeadphones}} are a pair of earphone with a band connecting the phones, which is also used to rest against the head. It can be used to listening to audio file and/or be connected to electronic device. Headphones are often used to listen to music or other speech, such as in video, especially to prevent others from being forced to listen. Many headphones are attached to an AUX cable, but others connect using Bluetooth.
