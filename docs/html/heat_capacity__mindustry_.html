Heat Capacity (Mindustry)

Heat Capacity is a characteristic of liquid (Mindustry) in Mindustry formerly used to determine how fast a liquid would be used as a coolant in the Thorium Reactor (Mindustry), but <>, is used to determine the benefit to turret (Mindustry) fire rate. Both turrets on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) can use liquid cooling. Only Water and Cryofluid can be used for cooling, so Heat Capacity in other liquids is meaningless. Cryofluid is unavailable on Erekir, so Erekir turrets cannot be cooled through Cryofluid.
