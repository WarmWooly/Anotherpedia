Heated Sparks (Hexaria)

Heated Sparks is a card rarity (Hexaria) card in the Roblox game Hexaria which applies burn (Hexaria) to an enemy for a Bit (Hexaria). Its card design and text is a reference to Flint and Steel (A Minecraft Movie) as seen in A Minecraft Movie. Compared to Ember (Hexaria), it deals 6 less damage (Hexaria) with 1 less range, making it a strictly worse card.
