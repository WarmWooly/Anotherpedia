Heated Sparks (Hexaria)

Heated Sparks is a common card in the Roblox game Hexaria which applies burn to an enemy for a Bit. Its card design and text is a reference to flint and steel as seen in A Minecraft Movie. Compared to Ember, it deals 6 less damage with 1 less range, making it a strictly worse card. Name: Heated Sparks
Target: Enemy within 2 tiles with line of sight
Quickplay: No
Bit cost: 1
Effects: Applies burn for 3 turns with a strength of 7, dealing a total of 21 damage.
Card text:Flint and Steel! Shoot sparks out setting the target on fire for 21 damage over 3 turns.Rarity: Common
