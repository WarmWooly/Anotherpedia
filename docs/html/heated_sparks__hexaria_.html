Heated Sparks (Hexaria)

Heated Sparks is a card rarity (Hexaria) card in the Roblox game Hexaria which applies burn (Hexaria) to an enemy for a Bit (Hexaria). Its card design and text is a reference to Flint and Steel (A Minecraft Movie) as seen in A Minecraft Movie. Compared to Ember (Hexaria), it deals 6 less damage (Hexaria) with 1 less range, making it a strictly worse card. Name: Heated Sparks
Target: Enemy within 2 tile (Hexaria) with line of sight (Hexaria)
Quickplay (Hexaria): No
Bit cost: 1
Effects: Applies burn (Hexaria) for 3 turns with a strength of 7, dealing a total of 21 damage.
Card text:Flint and Steel! Shoot sparks out setting the target on fire for 21 damage over 3 turns.Rarity: Common
