Hellish Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Hellish leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Hellish leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +1 Hellishness and -0.1 Swim Speed. With Hellishness, upon entering fire, lava, or a campfire, Fire Resistance will be applied for 2 * Hellishness seconds. Initial and lingering damage do not get prevented. Hellish leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 2 hellish glass. In a leathersmithing table, hellish leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each hellish leather padding adds +1 Hellishness and -0.1 Swim Speed, with a full set of armor with 12 hellish leather paddings giving +12 Hellishness and -1.2 Swim Speed. Shields will only add Hellishness and remove Swim Speed when held in the main hand or offhand.
