Hellish Sand (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Hellish sand is a gravity-affected block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Hellish sand can be obtained when a Crusher crushes netherrack. A Cobblestone Generator with a haunt'n'crushifier will produce hellish sand. Haunt'n'crushifiers take 16x as long to generate one hellish sand compared to one cobblestone. Hellish sand can be smelted in a furnace to produce hellish glass, taking 10 seconds and giving 1 experience. Hellish sand can be sieved, producing various Nether-based items. Separation can increase the chance/drops from sieving hellish sand.<>
