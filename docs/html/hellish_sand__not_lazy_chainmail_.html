Hellish Sand (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>{{bHellish sand}} is a falling block (Minecraft) block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}}. Hellish sand can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes netherrack (Minecraft). A Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) with a haunt'n'crushifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) will produce hellish sand. Haunt'n'crushifiers take 16x as long to generate one hellish sand compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). Hellish sand can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) to produce hellish glass (Not Lazy Chainmail), taking 10 seconds and giving 1 experience (Minecraft). Hellish sand can be sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail), producing various Nether (Minecraft)-based items. Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops from sieving hellish sand.<>
