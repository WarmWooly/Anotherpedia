Hippocamp

<><>{{bHippocamp}} is the 14th discovered [[moon]] from [[Neptune]], discovered on July 1, 2013. It was also the smallest moon of Neptune until the discovery of [[S/2021 N 1]] in 2021 and announcement in 2024. It [[orbits|orbit]] Neptune every 22 [[hours|hour]] and 48 [[minutes|minute]]. It was potentially created after a [[comet]] impacted the moon [[Proteus]] billions of [[years|year]] ago.
