Hippocamp

Hippocamp is the 14th discovered moon from Neptune, discovered on July 1, 2013. It was also the smallest moon of Neptune until the discovery of S/2021 N 1 in 2021 and announcement in 2024. It orbit Neptune every 22 hour and 48 minute. It was potentially created after a comet impacted the moon Proteus billions of year ago.
