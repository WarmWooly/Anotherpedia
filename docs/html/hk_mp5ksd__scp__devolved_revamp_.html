HK MP5KSD (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The HK MP5KSD is a secondary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Site Security at level 4. Its high firerate and decent damage allow the user to efficiently eliminate opponents, despite the lower ammo cap compared to the FN P90.
