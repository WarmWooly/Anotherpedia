Holiday Skin Pack 2015 (Minecraft)

The Holiday Skin Pack 2015 is a Christmas-themed skin pack available for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition, Pocket Edition, and legacy consoles. It currently costs 310 Minecoins with a rating of nearly 5 stars <>. The pack included many skins, such as Sweater Steve, Sweater Alex, Parka Steve, Mrs. Clause, Santa Clause, Red-Nose, Mother Christmas, Father Christmas, Toy Crafter, Gingerbread Creeper, Tasty Gingerbread, Tomte, Puffed Padded, Snug Snoozy, and Casual Comfy.
