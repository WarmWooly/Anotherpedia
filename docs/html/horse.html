Horse

A horse (with the name of {{bEquus ferus caballus}}) is a four-legged animal mammal that has been domestication by human which belongs in the family (taxonomy) of Equidae. Horses evolve from an ancestor (taxonomy) over 45 million years ago from a small animal close to the Eohippus. It is believed that humans started horse domestication around 4000 Before the Common Era, with it becoming widespread in 3000 BCE. Horses are well known for their ability to running at high speed, often between 10 to 16 miles per hour, but they can get up to over 25 mph when galloping. Horses aging up to 25 to 30 year old, and are fully animal maturity by five. Horses grazing on grass and grain.
