Horse Race Tests

For the fan game based on the series, see {{aihorse race tests (fan game).}}
{{iHorse Race Tests}} is a simulated video game web series on Twitter by the user <> starting on April 1, 2025. The series follows various crudely-drawn pixel art horses racing on 2D maps bouncing around randomly to achieve a simple objective, such as collecting food or shooting a basketball into a hoop. As the series has gone on, production quality (such as audio and colorblind friendliness) has increased. <>, Horse Race Tests is on its second season. There is a large fanbase around the series, often discussing the lore and the various horses, creating fanart of the series, and "betting" on which horses will win in subsequent races.
