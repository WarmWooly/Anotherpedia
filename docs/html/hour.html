Hour

<><>An {{bhour}} is a period of [[time]] which is 1/24th of a [[day]] and is comprised of 60 [[minutes|minute]]. When displayed, an hour is comprised of two digits: {{b01}}:00 (the leading 0 is often omitted when displayed). In [[military time]], it would be seen as {{b01}}00.
