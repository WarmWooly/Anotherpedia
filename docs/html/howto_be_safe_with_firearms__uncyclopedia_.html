HowTo:Be Safe With Firearms is an article on Uncyclopedia as a part of its HowTo (Uncyclopedia), describing firearm safety in a satire and humor fashion. It starts describing in first-person about the reader purchase an AK-47 from a pawn shop, before pulling the reader into the core of the article about firearm safety. After the opening, it has several sections listing important information related to firearm safety. This section describes what to do when seeing a gun. It recommends lunging for it to prevent others from obtaining the gun, empowerment the lunger and giving them control of the situation. This section discusses the safety (firearm) on guns, primarily how it shouldn't be utilized because it could accidentally be flicked off and cause a misfire. The HowTo claim that it is safer to keep the safety of a gun off at all times in case an enemy strikes. This section provides information about firearm loading guns, such as that they should always be loaded. It shouldn't be unloaded, because in the case of attempting to shooting someone, if the gun isn't loaded, it will take too long to load before being gunshot wound. They recommend checking that the gun is loaded at least twice a day. This section informs the reader to always keep their finger on the trigger (firearm). This, similar to keeping the gun loaded and the safety off, is used to be able to fire at a target with the least delay possible. This section goes over gun accessorization, such as sunglasses to look less suspicious and to reduce seen muzzle flash, hearing protection to protect the ear from the gunshots, wearing old clothing or a raincoat to prevent blood staining, and a fast car to look cool (style) in.
