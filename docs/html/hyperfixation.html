Hyperfixation

<>{{bHyperfixation}}, also written as hyper fixation, relates to the fixation on a singular topic, idea, or object, to an overbearing extent, making it more difficult to communication with person. Hyperfixation is most relevant in those diagnosis with ADHD, autism, and other mental disorder. The main problem with hyperfixation is the potential ostracization that may be caused by the topic or repetition discussion of a single topic.
