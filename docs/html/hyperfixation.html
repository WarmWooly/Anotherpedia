Hyperfixation

<><><>{{bHyperfixation}}, also written as hyper fixation, relates to the [[fixation]] on a singular [[topic]], [[idea]], or [[object]], to an overbearing extent, making it more difficult to [[communicate|communication]] with [[others|person]]. Hyperfixation is most relevant in those [[diagnosed|diagnosis]] with [[ADHD]], [[autism]], and other [[mental disorders|mental disorder]]. The main [[problem]] with hyperfixation is the potential [[ostracization]] that may be caused by the [[topic]] or [[repeated|repetition]] [[discussion]] of a single topic.
