i herd u liek mudballz (Not Lazy Chainmail)

i herd u liek mudballz is an advancement in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that is earned when the player hits an Axolotl with a launched mudball. The name is a reference to the meme "i herd u liek mudkips". It is the child to the advancement We're Blasting Off Again!. The description is as follows:
