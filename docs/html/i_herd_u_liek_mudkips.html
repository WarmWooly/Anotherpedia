There are mixed accounts on which of the two sites the meme started. In October 2004, the group <> was formed, with the owner of the group posting the phrase "so i herd u like mudkips" to others in early 2005. <>In 2005, a [[copypasta]] from 4chan describing a kid spontaneously humping a Mudkip [[stuffed animal]] when prompted used the phrase "So I heard you like Mudkips..." for comedic effect.<> Variations typically include various misspellings, such as "mudkipz" and "herd".
