Icy Cart Handle (Bits & Blocks)

The icy cart handle is a head curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that allows players to self-power minecarts and to get buffs in a minecart. The icy cart handle can be crafted with 1 cart handle and 1 Ocean Mixtape (shapeless). When the icy cart handle is worn in a head slot, minecarts will keep their momentum even without powered rails. Unpowered powered rails will still cause the minecart to slow down. Riding in a minecart will also apply Fire Resistance and Resistance II.
