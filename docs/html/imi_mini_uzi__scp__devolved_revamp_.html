IMI Mini UZI (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The IMI Mini Uzi is a secondary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Mobile Task Force at level 4. With a high ammo capacity, fast firerate, and high damage, the UZI can shred through SCPs and players alike.
