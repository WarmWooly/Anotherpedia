Imminent Explosion ({{iBread Reborn: Redefined}})

<><>{{bImminent explosion}} is an [[effect|effect (Minecraft)]] in {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|modding (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Bread Reborn: Redefined]]}} that causes an [[explosion|explosion (Minecraft)]] when it ends. The source of imminent explosion is from eating [[gunpowder bread|gunpowder bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]]. Even if the effect ends prematurely, such as by drinking [[milk|milk bucket (Minecraft)]], the explosion will still occur. The [[resistance|resistance (Minecraft)]] IV from gunpowder bread greatly reduces dealt [[damage|damage (Minecraft)]], making the effect survivable, even when unarmored.
