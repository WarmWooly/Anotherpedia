Incentive compatibility

<><>{{bIncentive compatibility}} refers to when being [[honest|honesty]] will result in a better, or not worse, [[outcome]] compared to being [[dishonest|dishonesty]]. By telling the [[truth]] or acting in a manner that is [[positive]], the participant will be better off regardless of what others do. An example would be a person looking for [[mattresses|mattress]] with little [[money]] and a [[mattress store]] trying to reduce their [[stock]]. It would be beneficial for the person to look for a [[cheap]] mattress and for the mattress store to [[discount]] their mattresses, allowing both sides to [[win]].
