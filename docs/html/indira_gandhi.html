Indira Gandhi

{{bIndira Priyadarshini Gandhi}} was the third Prime Minister of India between 1966 and 1977, and then from 1980 to 1984. She was born on November 19, 1917, and was Assassination of Indira Gandhi on October 31, 1984, at the age of 66. Indira's husband was Feroze Gandhi. She had two son, Rajiv Ghandi and Sanjay Ghandi. Her mother was Kamala Nehru, and her father was Jawaharlal Nehru.
