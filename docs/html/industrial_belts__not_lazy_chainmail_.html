Industrial belts (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Industrial belts are a type of item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail. There are 8 unique industrial belts; 2 that can be crafting (Minecraft) and 6 that can be chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail). Belts that are more expensive are faster than cheaper belts and have increased durability. The following is information on the industrial belts:<> kelp industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) and leather industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be crafting (Minecraft) using 4 dried kelp (Minecraft)/leather (Minecraft) (place material in the shape of a ring). Crafting one of these industrial belts will award the advancement Slow But Steady (Not Lazy Chainmail). Industrial belts (besides kelp industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) and leather industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)) can be created through Chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the setting "Industrial Belt". Industrial belts take 256 chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Crafting the copper chainmail industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail), iron chainmail industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail), or gold chainmail industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) will award the advancement Upgrades, People, Upgrades! (Not Lazy Chainmail) and crafting the iron composite chainmail industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) will award the advancement Gotta Go Fast (Not Lazy Chainmail). Crafting/melding usage information about industrial belts are located within each specific industrial belt's page. Winding Jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) An industrial belt can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into an unfinished chainmail projects (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 256 chain links (Not Lazy Chainmail). An industrial belt can be repaired through combining (Minecraft) by crafting (Minecraft), using a grindstone (Minecraft), or using an anvil (Minecraft). Combining two of the same industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience (Minecraft) returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Industrial belts can be enchanting (Minecraft) with Unbreaking (Minecraft), Mending (Minecraft), and Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft) using an enchanted book (Minecraft) on an anvil (Minecraft). Industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The runes (Not Lazy Chainmail) Automated Permanence Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be cast on industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (Not Lazy Chainmail) (unbreakable), costing 2 experience (Minecraft).
