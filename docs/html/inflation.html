Inflation

<>{{tFor the increase in the cost of goods and services, see [[economic inflation]].}}&sp<>{{bInflation}} is the [[action]] of [[filling]] an [[expandable]] [[object]] (a [[tire]] or a [[balloon]]) with a [[gas]] or [[liquid]] ([[air]] or [[water]]). Inflation can also be used to describe as an [[increase]] of a [[value]] or an [[exaggeration]]. [[Inflation|Economic inflation]] is often used to describe the increase of [[price]] for [[goods]] and [[services|service]]. The reverse of inflation is [[deflation]]. When the inflation is drastic, it is known as [[hyperinflation]].
