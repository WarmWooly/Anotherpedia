Intersection (road)

An intersection on a road is a location where two or more roads meet at a single point. There are a wide variety of types and use cases for an intersection on a road. Many use sign (road), traffic lights, and road markings to show locations to stop or slow down, who has the right of way (driving), when someone can go, where pedestrian can cross, and other use cases.
