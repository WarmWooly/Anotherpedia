Inverted Sorter (Mindustry)

An Inverted Sorter is a building (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) that is used to sort items on conveyor (mindustry). The description of the Inverted Sorter is the following: It has 40 health (Mindustry), taking 0.12 build time (Mindustry), while taking a 1x1 space. It costs 2 copper (Mindustry) and 2 lead (Mindustry). To use, place the sorter down in front of a conveyor. Then, left click on the sorter, opening a menu that will display a list of item (mindustry) that you have in your database (mindustry). If an item enters the sorter and is the item selected, it will be evenly output on the left and right to where it entered. If the item that entered is not the one selected, it will be output on the other side. To research (Mindustry) it, you must have the Sorter (Mindustry) researched. It costs 100 Copper and 100 Lead to research it.
