Inverted Sorter (Mindustry)

An Inverted Sorter is a building in Mindustry on Serpulo that is used to sort items on conveyors. The description of the Inverted Sorter is the following:Similar to a standard sorter, but outputs selected items to the sides instead. It has 40 health, taking 0.12 seconds to build, while taking a 1x1 space. It costs 2 Copper and 2 Lead. To use, place the sorter down in front of a conveyor. Then, left click on the sorter, opening a menu that will display a list of items that you have in your database. If an item enters the sorter and is the item selected, it will be evenly output on the left and right to where it entered. If the item that entered is not the one selected, it will be output on the other side. To research it, you must have the Sorter researched. It costs 100 Copper and 100 Lead to research it.
