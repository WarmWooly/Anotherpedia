Investopedia

{{bInvestopedia}} is an online encyclopedia regarding finance, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of {{iinvest}} and {{iencyclopedia}}. The Editor-in-Chief of Investopedia is Caleb Silver <>. Investopedia has over 36,000 articles, with a large portion containing definition of financial term. Investopedia was founding in 1999 by Cory Wagner and Cory Janssen, which was then sold to Forbes Media in 2007, then sold to Bread Financial in 2010, then joined into Dotdash Meredith in 2018.
