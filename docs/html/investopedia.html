Investopedia

Investopedia is an online encyclopedia regarding finance, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of invest and encyclopedia. The Editor-in-Chief of Investopedia is Caleb Silver <>. Investopedia has over 36,000 articles, with a large portion containing definitions of financial terms. Investopedia was founded in 1999 by Cory Wagner and Cory Janssen, which was then sold to Forbes in 2007, then sold to ValueClick in 2010, then joined into Dotdash in 2018.
