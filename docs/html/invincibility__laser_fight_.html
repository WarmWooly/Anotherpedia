Invincibility (Laser Fight)

For the kill streak, see invincibility ({{aiLaser Fight kill streak).}}
Invincibility is an effect that can be temporarily applied to a player in the Roblox game Laser Fight. It is applied when the player spawns in and when the player uses the invincibility kill streak. Invincibility makes the player immune to dying from guns, turrets, and nextbots, while also nullifying the effects of fall damage. It does not protect against falling outside of the map, entering a kill zone (such as the water on Highway), or from resetting. When spawning, the player will be invincible for 5 seconds. The kill streak will apply invincibility for 4, 7, 10, 15, and 25 seconds for levels 1--5, respectively. Blue shield particles saying "protected" will spawn around the player for the duration of their invincibility.
