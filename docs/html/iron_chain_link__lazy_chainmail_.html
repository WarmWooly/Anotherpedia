Iron Chain Link (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Chain Link ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
The iron chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Iron chain links can be snipped from an iron coil or an unfinished project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Iron chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:
Chain - 8 iron chain links
Chainmail Boots - 256 iron chain links
Chainmail Chestplate - 512 iron chain links
Chainmail Helmet - 320 iron chain links
Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 iron chain links
Chainmail Leggings - 448 iron chain link
