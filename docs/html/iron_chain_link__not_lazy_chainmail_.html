Iron Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Iron chain links can be snipped using pliers from an iron coil or an unfinished iron project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Iron chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes a chain (makes 8 chain links). Iron chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:&spChain - 8 iron chain links&spChainmail Boots - 256 iron chain links&spChainmail Chestplate - 512 iron chain links&spChainmail Helmet - 320 iron chain links&spChainmail Leggings - 448 iron chain link&spIron Chainmail Belt - 128 iron chain links&spIron Chainmail Bracelet - 64 iron chain links&spIron Chainmail Gloves - 192 iron chain links&spIron Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 iron chain links&spIron Chainmail Necklace - 128 iron chain links&spIron Tool Chain - 192 iron chain links&spMusic Disc "Attack of the Roombas" - 32 iron chain links For Novice-level chainmaillers, there is a 22.22% chance to trade 64 iron chain links for 3 emeralds. In a Recycler, 9 iron chain links can be recycled into an iron nugget. Iron chainmail armor enchanted with Chain Repair will automatically take an iron chain link from the inventory to repair durability when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Iron chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator. Using an iron composite chainmail industrial belt constantly fed with iron chain links, 15 FE/t can be generated.
