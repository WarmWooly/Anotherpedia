Iron Chainmail Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron chainmail belt}} is a belt curios (Not Lazy Chainmail) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} that buffs the wearer while sneaking (Minecraft). The iron chainmail belt can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Belt". The belt takes 128 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Journeyman-level chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) have a 50% chance to trade 3 emerald (Minecraft) for 1 iron chainmail belt. When the iron chainmail belt is worn in a belt slot and the wearer is sneaking (Minecraft), they will gain the effect (Minecraft) Speed (Minecraft) I and Strength (Minecraft) I. The Speed and Strength will increase depending on the number of chainmail armor pieces (chainmail helmet, chainmail chestplate (Minecraft), chainmail leggings (Minecraft), and chainmail boots (Minecraft)) the wearer has equipped. With 2 armor pieces equipped, the effects given are Speed II and Strength II, and with all 4 armor pieces equipped, the effect given is Speed III and Strength III. Iron chainmail belts can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished iron chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 128 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail).
