Iron Chainmail Gloves (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Iron chainmail gloves are a hand curio in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that has a chance to apply Slowness II on attacked mobs. Iron chainmail gloves can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Gloves". The gloves take 192 iron chain links to craft. Journeyman-level chainmaillers have a 50% chance to trade 2 emeralds for 1 iron chainmail gloves. When iron chainmail gloves are worn in a hand slot, they will have a 20% chance to apply Slowness II for 10 seconds to attacked mobs. The chance will increase depending on the number of chainmail armor pieces (chainmail helmet, chainmail chestplate, chainmail leggings, and chainmail boots) and/or iron chain cover-upgraded pieces the wearer has equipped. The chance increases to 25%, 33%, 50% and 100% for 1--4 pieces respectively. Iron chainmail gloves can be crushed into unfinished iron projects containing 192 chain links.
