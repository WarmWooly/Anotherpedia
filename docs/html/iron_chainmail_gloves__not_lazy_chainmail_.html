Iron Chainmail Gloves (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Iron chainmail gloves are a hand curios (Not Lazy Chainmail) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail that has a chance to apply Slowness (Minecraft) II on attacked mobs. Iron chainmail gloves can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Gloves". The gloves take 192 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Journeyman-level chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) have a 50% chance to trade 2 emerald (Minecraft) for 1 iron chainmail gloves. When iron chainmail gloves are worn in a hand slot, they will have a 20% chance to apply Slowness (Minecraft) II for 10 seconds to attacked mobs. The chance will increase depending on the number of chainmail armor pieces (chainmail helmet, chainmail chestplate (Minecraft), chainmail leggings (Minecraft), and chainmail boots (Minecraft)) and/or iron chain cover (Not Lazy Chainmail)-upgraded pieces the wearer has equipped. The chance increases to 25%, 33%, 50% and 100% for 1--4 pieces respectively. Iron chainmail gloves can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished iron chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 192 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail).
