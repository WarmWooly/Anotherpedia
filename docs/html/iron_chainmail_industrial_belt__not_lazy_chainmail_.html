Iron Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Speed|x3info>>The iron chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Iron chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Industrial Belt". The iron chainmail industrial belt takes 256 iron chain links to craft. Crafting the iron chainmail industrial belt will award the advancement Upgrades, People, Upgrades! The chances to find an iron chainmail industrial belt in a winding jig are the following:
In a winding jig ruins, there is a 14.26% chance to contain an iron chainmail industrial belt.
In a chainmailler home, there is a 45.45% chance to contain an iron chainmail industrial belt. Iron chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chainsaw - 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 1 crude engine, 1 iron ingot, and 2 iron nuggets (place the iron ingot in the middle, the engine to the left, the nuggets in the two slots below the engine and ingot, and the belt in the top right corner).
Iron Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 iron chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). The iron chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:
Auto Mailler
Auto Snipper

Winding Jig Iron chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished iron chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Iron chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining iron chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Iron chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Iron chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on iron chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels. Iron chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy in a belt generator, generating 3 FE/t.
