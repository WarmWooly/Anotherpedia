Iron Chainmail Necklace (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron chainmail necklace is a necklace curios (Not Lazy Chainmail) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail that reflects damage (Minecraft) dealt to the wearer back to the attacker. The wearer still takes damage as well. The iron chainmail necklace can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Necklace". The necklace takes 128 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Journeyman-level chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) have a 50% chance to trade 2 emerald (Minecraft) for 1 iron chainmail necklace. Iron chainmail necklaces have a 3.85% chance of being found in suspicious sand (Minecraft) within chainmail ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail). When the iron chainmail necklace is worn in a necklace slot, damage (Minecraft) dealt to the wearer will be reflected back onto the attacker based on the number of Not Lazy Chainmail advancements the wearer has obtained. The way reflected damage is calculated is through codedamage dealt * (advancements * 0.05). For example, if the wearer has 10 advancements and takes 8 damage, they will reflect 4 damage back to the attacker. If the damage is from a player (Minecraft), the damage reflected is divided by 5 (only 1% per advancement). As there are a total of 52 advancements in Not Lazy Chainmail, the wearer can reflect back as much as 260% of the attacker's damage (52% from players). The wearer will still be dealt the normal amount of damage.
