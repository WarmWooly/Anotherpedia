Iron Coil (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Coil ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
The iron coil is an item in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used to be snipped into iron chain links. When an iron wire or spool is wound in a winding jig, an iron coil is created. While wires only give one coil, spools give back 10. Spools are ~11% more efficient than wires when being wound into coils. Pliers can be used to snip the iron coil into iron chain links. An iron coil can be snipped 64 times, creating a stack of iron chain links.
