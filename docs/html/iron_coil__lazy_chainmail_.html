Iron Coil (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Coil ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
<>The iron coil is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Lazy Chainmail used to be pliers (Lazy Chainmail) into iron chain link (Lazy Chainmail). When an iron wire (Lazy Chainmail) or iron spool (Lazy Chainmail) is wound in a winding jig (Lazy Chainmail), an iron coil is created. While wires only give one coil, spools give back 10. Spools are ~11% more efficient than wires when being wound into coils. Pliers (Lazy Chainmail) can be used to snip the iron coil into iron chain link (Lazy Chainmail). An iron coil can be snipped 64 times, creating a stack (Minecraft) of iron chain links.
