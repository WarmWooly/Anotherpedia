Iron Coil (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron coil is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) into iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail). When an iron wire (Not Lazy Chainmail) or (bright iron spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)) iron spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) or material is wound in a winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) or auto winder (Not Lazy Chainmail), an iron coil is created. While wires only give one coil, spools give back 10. Spools are ~11% more efficient than wires when being winded into coils. Novice-level chainmailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) have a 22.22% chance to trade 1 iron wire (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 emerald (Minecraft) for 1 iron coil. In a chainmailler home (Not Lazy Chainmail), a chest has a 51.77% chance to contain an iron coil. Main page: Snipping ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
Pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be used to snip the iron coil into iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) of the same material. An iron coil can be snipped 64 times, creating a stack (Minecraft) of iron chain links. Better pliers decrease the snips required to harvest all the chain links from a coil. The auto snipper (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be used to automate the process of snipping.
