Iron Composite Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistence|Noinfo>>The iron composite chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The iron composite chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Composite Chain". The chain takes 8 iron composite chain links to craft. The iron composite chain can be crafted with a block of iron composite chains (makes 4). Iron composite chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Block of Iron Composite Chains - 4 iron composite chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Iron Composite Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 iron composite chains (fill the middle and bottom rows).
Iron Composite Lasso - 2 iron composite chains and 1 iron composite tool chain (place the tool chain in the top right corner and the chains in the middle and bottom right corner).
Iron Composite Rope x4 - 1 iron composite chain, 1 iron composite tool chain, and 1 iron ingot (place the tool chain in the middle, chain below, and iron above).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Mangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Spruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Warped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 iron composite chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems). Iron composite chains can be crushed into 8 iron composite chain links.
