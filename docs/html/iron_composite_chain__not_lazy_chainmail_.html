Iron Composite Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron composite chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The iron composite chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Composite Chain". The chain takes 8 iron composite chain links to craft. The iron composite chain can be crafted with a block of iron composite chains (makes 4).
