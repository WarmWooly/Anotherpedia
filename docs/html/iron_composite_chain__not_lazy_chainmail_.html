Iron Composite Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron composite chain}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The iron composite chain can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Composite Chain". The chain takes 8 iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. The iron composite chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with a block of iron composite chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4).
