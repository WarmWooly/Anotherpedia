Iron Composite Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The iron composite chain carpet is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The iron composite chain carpet can be crafted with 2 blocks of iron composite chains (place side by side; makes 3). Iron composite chain carpets are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Composite Chain Conveyor x16 - 2 iron composite chain carpets, 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, and 1 redstone torch (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot).
Iron Composite Ornate Glass - 2 iron composite chain carpets and 1 glass (carpets on top and bottom with the glass inbetween).
Iron Composite Ornate Lamp - 2 iron composite chain carpets and 1 redstone lamp (carpets on top and bottom with the lamp inbetween).
Iron Composite Sieve Mesh - 1 iron composite chain carpet and 8 obsidian (place the carpet in the center and surround with the obsidian).
Iron Composite Spiked Chain Carpet x3 - 3 iron composite chain carpets and 6 iron composite nuggets (carpets fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid). Iron composite chain carpets are used in the following melding recipes:
Separation Rune - 1 iron composite chain carpet, 1 pseudorock, and 1 lapis lazuli
