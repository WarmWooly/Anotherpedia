Iron Composite Chain Cover (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The iron composite chain cover is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Iron composite chain covers are used to upgrade chainmail armor in a leathersmithing table, making the armor type "Iron Composite". Iron composite chain covers can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 1 block of iron composite chains. In a leathersmithing table, iron composite chain covers can be applied to chainmail armor. While up to 3 can be applied to a single item, the effects do not stack. Any curios that scale based on worn iron composite chainmail armor will also scale for armor with an iron composite chain cover.
