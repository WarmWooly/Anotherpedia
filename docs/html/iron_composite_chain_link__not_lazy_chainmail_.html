Iron Composite Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron composite chain link}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in maille crafting. Iron composite chain links can be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) using pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) from an iron composite coil (Not Lazy Chainmail) or an unfinished iron composite project (Not Lazy Chainmail). While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Iron composite chain links can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes an iron composite chain (Not lazy Chainmail) (makes 8 chain links).
