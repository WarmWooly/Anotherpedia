Iron Composite Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The iron composite chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Iron composite chain links can be snipped using pliers from an iron composite coil or an unfinished iron composite project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Iron composite chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes an iron composite chain (makes 8 chain links). Iron composite chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:
Iron Composite Chain - 8 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Belt - 128 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Boots - 256 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Bracelet - 64 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Chestplate - 512 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Gloves - 192 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Helmet - 320 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Chainmail Leggings - 448 iron composite chain link
Iron Composite Chainmail Necklace - 128 iron composite chain links
Iron Composite Tool Chain - 192 iron composite chain links
Music Disc "Super Beats" - 32 iron composite chain links In a Recycler, 9 iron composite chain links can be recycled into an iron composite nugget. Iron composite chainmail armor enchanted with Chain Repair will automatically take an iron composite chain link from the inventory to repair durability when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Iron composite chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator. Using an iron composite chainmail industrial belt constantly fed with iron composite chain links, 500 FE/t can be generated.
