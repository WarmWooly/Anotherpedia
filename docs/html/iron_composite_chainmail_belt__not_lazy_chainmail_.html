Iron Composite Chainmail Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The iron composite chainmail belt is a belt curio in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that destorys blocks around the player for mining. The iron composite chainmail belt can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Composigte Belt". The belt takes 128 iron composite chain links to craft. When the iron composite chainmail belt is worn, any Stone, Deepslate, and Lava in a 3-block radius around the player will be destroyed, with Stone and Deepslate dropping Cobblestone and Cobbled Deepslate, and the Lava being removed. While all Lava will be cleared instantly, each Stone/Deepslate will take 1 tick to break. Each iron composite chainmail armor piece worn will increase the number of blocks broken per tick by 1, up to 5 blocks/tick. It is most useful for clearing out underground spaces to find ores or to stop Lava in the Nether. Iron composite chainmail belts can be crushed into unfinished iron composite chainmail projects containing 128 chain links.
