Iron Composite Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron composite chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Composite Industrial Belt". The iron composite chainmail industrial belt takes 256 iron composite chain links to craft. Crafting the iron composite chainmail industrial belt will award the advancement Gotta Go Fast. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:&spIron Composite Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 iron composite chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). The iron composite chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:&spAuto Mailler&spAuto Snipper&spAuto Winder&spBelt Generator&spChain Generator&spCobblestone Generator&spConveyor Warper&spCrusher&spItem Aggregator&spMelder&spRecycler&spWinding Jig Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished iron composite chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining iron composite chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on iron composite chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy in a belt generator, generating 32 FE/t.
