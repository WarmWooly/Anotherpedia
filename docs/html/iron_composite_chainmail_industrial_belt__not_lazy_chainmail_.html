Iron Composite Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron composite chainmail industrial belt}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} and one of the eight industrial belts (Not Lazy Chainmail) used to run machines. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be created through Chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Composite Industrial Belt". The iron composite chainmail industrial belt takes 256 iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Crafting the iron composite chainmail industrial belt will award the advancement Gotta Go Fast (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron composite chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:&spIron Composite Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) x16 - 1 iron chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust (Minecraft), 1 redstone torch (Minecraft), and 2 iron composite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). The iron composite chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:&spAuto Mailler (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spAuto Snipper (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spAuto Winder (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spBelt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spChain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spCobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spConveyor Warper (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spCrusher (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spItem Aggregator (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spMelder (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spRecycler (Not Lazy Chainmail)&spWinding Jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished iron composite chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 256 iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining (Minecraft) by crafting (Minecraft), using a grindstone (Minecraft), or using an anvil (Minecraft). Combining iron composite chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience (Minecraft) returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can be enchanting (Minecraft) with Unbreaking (Minecraft), Mending (Minecraft), and Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft) using an enchanted book (Minecraft) on an anvil (Minecraft). Iron composite chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The runes (Not Lazy Chainmail) Automated Permanence Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be cast on iron composite chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (Not Lazy Chainmail) (unbreakable), costing 2 experience (Minecraft). Iron composite chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy (Minecraft modding) in a Belt Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail), generating 32 FE/tick (Minecraft).
