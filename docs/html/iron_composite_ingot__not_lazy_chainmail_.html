Iron Composite Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron composite ingot is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting (Minecraft) high-tier chainmail materials and automated machines. There are multiple recipes to obtain iron composite ingots:
9 iron composite nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> 1 iron composite ingot
1 block of iron composite (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> 9 iron composite ingots Iron composite ingots can be melded in a Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail) using 1 iron ingot (Minecraft), 1 chromatic dust (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 bedrock shard (Not Lazy Chainmail). Melding an iron composite ingot will award the advancement (Minecraft) I'm Blue Da Ba Dee (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron composite ingots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Auto Mailler (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 3 iron composite ingots, 1 pressure plate (Minecraft), and 5 cobblestone (Minecraft) (place the plate in the middle, fill the bottom row with iron composite, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Auto Snipper (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 3 iron composite ingots, 1 iron ingot (Minecraft), and 5 cobblestone (Minecraft) (place the iron in the middle, fill the bottom row with iron composite, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Auto Winder (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 4 iron composite ingots, 2 cobblestone (Minecraft), and 2 empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) (fill the bottom row with iron composite, place the spools in the top corners and the cobblestone to the sides, fill the top-middle cell with the remaining iron composite).
Block of Iron Composite (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 9 iron composite ingots (fill the grid).
Chainsword (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 netherite sword (Minecraft), 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the sword in the middle, the iron composite to the bottom and left, the belt in the top-right corner, and the engine in the bottom-left corner).
Conveyor Warper (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the alloy in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Iron Composite Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) x9 - 1 iron composite ingot (place anywhere in grid).
Iron Composite Pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 block of iron composite chains (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 obsidian (Minecraft) (place the block in the middle, then the iron composite above and to the right, and the obsidian to the bottom and the left).
Iron Composite Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 8 iron composite ingots and 1 empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the spool in the middle, surround with iron composite). Iron composite ingots are used in the following melding recipes:
Strengthened Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 iron composite ingot and 1 leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) In an anvil, iron composite ingots can be used to repair iron composite chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron composite ingots can be used as an item to apply a selected power for a beacon (Minecraft), consuming one ingot. Iron composite ingots can be used in a smithing table (Minecraft) to trimming (Minecraft) armor.
