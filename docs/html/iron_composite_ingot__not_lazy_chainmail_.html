Iron Composite Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The iron composite ingot is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting high-tier chainmail materials and automated machines. There are multiple recipes to obtain iron composite ingots:
9 iron composite nuggets --> 1 iron composite ingot
1 block of iron composite --> 9 iron composite ingots Iron composite ingots can be melded in a Melder using 1 iron ingot, 1 chromatic dust, and 1 bedrock shard. Melding an iron composite ingot will award the advancement I'm Blue Da Ba Dee. Iron composite ingots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Auto Mailler - 3 iron composite ingots, 1 pressure plate, and 5 cobblestone (place the plate in the middle, fill the bottom row with iron composite, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Auto Snipper - 3 iron composite ingots, 1 iron ingot, and 5 cobblestone (place the iron in the middle, fill the bottom row with iron composite, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Auto Winder - 4 iron composite ingots, 2 cobblestone, and 2 empty spools (fill the bottom row with iron composite, place the spools in the top corners and the cobblestone to the sides, fill the top-middle cell with the remaining iron composite).
Block of Iron Composite - 9 iron composite ingots (fill the grid).
Chainsword - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 netherite sword, 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt, and 1 crude engine (place the sword in the middle, the iron composite to the bottom and left, the belt in the top-right corner, and the engine in the bottom-left corner).
Conveyor Warper - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 telemagnetic alloy, and 2 chain conveyors (place the alloy in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Iron Composite Nugget x9 - 1 iron composite ingot (place anywhere in grid).
Iron Composite Pliers - 2 iron composite ingots, 1 block of iron composite chains, and 2 obsidian (place the block in the middle, then the iron composite above and to the right, and the obsidian to the bottom and the left).
Iron Composite Spool - 8 iron composite ingots and 1 empty spool (place the spool in the middle, surround with iron composite). Iron composite ingots are used in the following melding recipes:
Strengthened Leather Padding - 1 iron composite ingot and 1 leather padding In an anvil, iron composite ingots can be used to repair iron composite chainmail armor. Iron composite ingots can be used as an item to apply a selected power for a beacon, consuming one ingot. Iron composite ingots can be used in a smithing table to trim armor.
