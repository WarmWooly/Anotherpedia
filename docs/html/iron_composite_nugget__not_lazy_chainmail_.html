Iron Composite Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron composite nugget}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used for crafting (Minecraft) high-tier chainmail materials and machines. Iron composite nuggets can be crafting (Minecraft) with an iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), yielding 9 nuggets. Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail) 9 iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) will return a iron composite nugget.
