Iron Composite Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron composite nugget is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting (Minecraft) high-tier chainmail materials and machines. Iron composite nuggets can be crafting (Minecraft) with an iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), yielding 9 nuggets. Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail) 9 iron composite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) will return a iron composite nugget. Iron composite nuggets are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Composite Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 9 iron composite nuggets (fill the grid).
Iron Composite Nunchucks (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 4 iron composite nuggets, 1 iron composite tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 obsidian (Minecraft) (nuggets go in all four corners, the obsidian to the left and right, and the tool chain on the bottom).
Iron Composite Spiked Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 6 iron composite nuggets and 3 iron composite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) (carpets fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Iron Composite Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 6 iron composite nuggets and 3 iron composite chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) (conveyors fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Iron Composite Wire (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 8 iron composite nuggets (fill the grid besides the middle slot).
Item Aggregator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 6 iron composite nuggets, 2 telemagnetic alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 bright spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail) (fill top and bottom rows with nuggets, the left and right with alloy, and the center with the spool block).
