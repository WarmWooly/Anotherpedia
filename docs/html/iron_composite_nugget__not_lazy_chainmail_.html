Iron Composite Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The iron composite nugget is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting high-tier chainmail materials and machines. Iron composite nuggets can be crafted with an iron composite ingot, yielding 9 nuggets. Recycling 9 iron composite chain links will return a iron composite nugget. Iron composite nuggets are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Composite Ingot - 9 iron composite nuggets (fill the grid).
Iron Composite Nunchucks - 4 iron composite nuggets, 1 iron composite tool chain, and 2 obsidian (nuggets go in all four corners, the obsidian to the left and right, and the tool chain on the bottom).
Iron Composite Spiked Chain Carpet x3 - 6 iron composite nuggets and 3 iron composite chain carpets (carpets fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Iron Composite Spiked Chain Conveyor x3 - 6 iron composite nuggets and 3 iron composite chain conveyors (conveyors fill the bottom row, the nuggets take the rest of the grid).
Iron Composite Wire - 8 iron composite nuggets (fill the grid besides the middle slot).
Item Aggregator - 6 iron composite nuggets, 2 telemagnetic alloy, and 1 bright spool block (fill top and bottom rows with nuggets, the left and right with alloy, and the center with the spool block).
