Iron Composite Pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Iron composite pliers are an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for snipping chain links from coils and unfinished projects. Iron composite pliers can be crafted with 2 iron composite ingots, 1 block of iron composite chains, and 2 obsidian (place the block in the middle, then the iron composite above and to the right, and the obisidan to the bottom and the left). Hold the iron composite pliers in the offhand with the coil or unfinished project to be snipped in the main hand. To remove a chain from the coil/unfinished project, right-click anywhere. This will use the pliers and create four chain links of the coil's/unfinished project's material type. When a coil/unfinished project goes down to 0 remaining chains, it will be removed. The Auto Snipper is a block that can be used to automatically snip coils/unfinished projects. By inputting the iron composite pliers, an industrial belt, and coils/unfinished projects, the coils/unfinished projects will be automatically snipped. The iron composite pliers will still lose durability while automatically snipping. Iron composite pliers are ideally not used in combat. Each attack will cost 2 durability. Iron composite pliers deal 2 damage and have an attack cooldown of 1 (1 second or 20 ticks). Iron composite pliers can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining iron composite pliers will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the iron composite pliers, costing experience. Iron composite pliers can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing, using an enchanted book on an anvil. Unbreaking may also be obtained in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on iron composite pliers to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels.
