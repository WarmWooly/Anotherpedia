Iron Flail (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The iron flail is a weapon in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail, mainly used for its higher damage and unique enchantments. The iron flail can be crafted with 1 iron tool chain, 1 block of iron chains, and 2 sticks (place a stick in the center and the bottom-left, the chain in the top-right, and the block below that). The iron flail is made for slow attacks, with each attack costing 2 durability. The iron flail deals 9 damage and has an attack cooldown of 1 (1 second or 20 ticks). Iron flails can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining iron flails will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the two iron flails, costing experience. Iron flails can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, Curse of Vanishing, Launch, Ignite, and Dissolve using an enchanted book on an anvil.
