Iron Flail (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron flail is a weapon (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail, mainly used for its higher damage and unique enchantment (Minecraft). The iron flail can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 iron tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), 1 block of iron chains (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 stick (Minecraft) (place a stick in the center and the bottom-left, the chain in the top-right, and the block below that). The iron flail is made for slow attacks, with each attack costing 2 durability. The iron flail deals 9 damage and has an attack cooldown of 1 (1 second or 20 tick (Minecraft)). Iron flails can be repaired through combining (Minecraft) by crafting (Minecraft), using a grindstone (Minecraft), or using an anvil (Minecraft). Combining iron flails will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience (Minecraft) returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the two iron flails, costing experience. Iron flails can be enchanting (Minecraft) with Unbreaking (Minecraft), Mending (Minecraft), Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft), Launch (Not Lazy Chainmail), Ignite (Not Lazy Chainmail), and Dissolve (Not Lazy Chainmail) using an enchanted book (Minecraft) on an anvil (Minecraft). Unbreaking, Launch, Ignite, and Dissolve may also be obtained in an enchanting table (Minecraft).
