Iron Lasso (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron lasso is a weapon (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail, mainly used to apply effects through its unique enchantment (Minecraft). The iron lasso can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 iron tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 2 chain (Minecraft) (place the stick in the center and bottom-left and the tool chain in the top-right). The iron lasso is not made to deal much damage, with each attack costing 2 durability. The iron lasso deals 0.5 damage and has an attack cooldown of 3 (0.33 seconds or 6 tick (Minecraft)). The lasso also has a 2.5-second effect cooldown, applying Slowness (Minecraft) II to hit mobs for 5 seconds. Iron lassos can be repaired through combining (Minecraft) by crafting (Minecraft), using a grindstone (Minecraft), or using an anvil (Minecraft). Combining iron lassos will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience (Minecraft) returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the two iron lassos, costing experience. Iron lassos can be enchanting (Minecraft) with Unbreaking (Minecraft), Mending (Minecraft), Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft), Yoink (Not Lazy Chainmail), Disorient (Not Lazy Chainmail), and Web (Not Lazy Chainmail) using an enchanted book (Minecraft) on an anvil (Minecraft). Unbreaking, Yoink, Disorient, and Web may also be obtained in an enchanting table (Minecraft).
