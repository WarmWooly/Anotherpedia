Iron Lasso (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The iron lasso is a weapon in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail, mainly used to apply effects through its unique enchantments. The iron lasso can be crafted with 1 iron tool chain and 2 chains (place the stick in the center and bottom-left and the tool chain in the top-right). The iron lasso is not made to deal much damage, with each attack costing 2 durability. The iron lasso deals 0.5 damage and has an attack cooldown of 3 (0.33 seconds or 6 ticks). The lasso also has a 2.5-second effect cooldown, applying Slowness II to hit mobs for 5 seconds. Iron lassos can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining iron lassos will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the two iron lassos, costing experience. Iron lassos can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, Curse of Vanishing, Yoink, Disorient, and Web using an enchanted book on an anvil.
