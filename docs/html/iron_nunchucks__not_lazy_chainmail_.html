Iron Nunchucks (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Iron nunckucks are a weapon (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail, mainly used for landing quick attacks with little cooldown. Iron nunchucks are made for quick jabs, with each attack costing 2 durability. Iron nunchucks deal 4 damage and have an attack cooldown of 4 (0.2 seconds or 4 tick (Minecraft)). Iron nunchucks can be repaired through combining (Minecraft) by crafting (Minecraft), using a grindstone (Minecraft), or using an anvil (Minecraft). Combining iron nunchucks will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience (Minecraft) returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the iron nunchucks, costing experience. Iron nunchucks can be enchanting (Minecraft) with Unbreaking (Minecraft), Mending (Minecraft), Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft), Disorient (Not Lazy Chainmail), Train (Not Lazy Chainmail), and Defend (Not Lazy Chainmail) using an enchanted book (Minecraft) on an anvil (Minecraft).
