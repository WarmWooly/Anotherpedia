Iron Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{biron ornate lamp}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as redstone (Minecraft)-powered light (Minecraft). The iron ornate lamp can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 iron chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 redstone lamp (Minecraft) (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the iron ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the iron ornate lamp takes 0 tick (Minecraft) to turn off.
