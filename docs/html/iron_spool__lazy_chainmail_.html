Iron Spool (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Spool ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
<>The iron spool is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into iron coil (Lazy Chainmail), acting as the bulk alternative to the iron wire (Lazy Chainmail). Iron spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 empty spool (Lazy Chainmail) and 8 iron ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Iron wire can be used on a winding jig (Lazy Chainmail) to be turned into 10 iron coil (Lazy Chainmail). It will also return an empty spool (Lazy Chainmail).
