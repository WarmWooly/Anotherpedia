Iron Spool (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Spool ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
The iron spool is an item in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into iron coils, acting as the bulk alternative to the iron wire. Iron spools can be crafted with 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingots (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Iron wire can be used on a winding jig to be turned into 10 iron coils. It will also return an empty spool.
