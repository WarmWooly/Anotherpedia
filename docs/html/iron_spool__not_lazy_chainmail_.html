Iron Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron spool is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into iron coil (Not Lazy Chainmail), acting as the bulk alternative to the iron wire (Not Lazy Chainmail). Iron spools can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 empty spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 8 iron ingot (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Iron spools can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes iron spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail). A bright iron spool (Not Lazy Chainmail) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a smoker (Minecraft) to produce an iron spool, taking 1 second. Iron spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 iron spool and 2 wooden slab (Minecraft) (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Iron spools can be used on a winding jig (Not Lazy Chainmail) or in an auto winder (Not Lazy Chainmail) to be turned into 10 iron coil (Not Lazy Chainmail).
