Iron Spool (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Malleability|640info>>The iron spool is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into iron coils, acting as the bulk alternative to the iron wire. Iron spools can be crafted with 1 empty spool and 8 iron ingots (place the spool in the middle and surround it with the ingots). Iron spools can be obtained when a Crusher crushes iron spool blocks. A bright iron spool can be smoked in a smoker to produce an iron spool, taking 1 second. Iron spools are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Spool Block - 1 iron spool and 2 wooden slabs (place the spool in the middle and place the slabs above and below it). Iron spools can be used on a winding jig or in an auto winder to be turned into 10 iron coils.
