Iron Spool Block (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron spool block is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as decoration. The iron spool block can be crafted with 1 iron spool and 2 wooden slabs (spool in the center, slabs above and below). A bright iron spool block can be smoked in a smoker to produce an iron spool block, taking 1 second. Iron spool blocks are used in the following crafting recipes:&spBright Iron Spool Block x4 - 4 iron spool blocks and 1 glowstone dust (place the glowstone dust in the center and surround it with the spool blocks). Iron spool blocks can be crushed into 1 iron spool. Spool blocks can be haunted into bright iron spool blocks.
