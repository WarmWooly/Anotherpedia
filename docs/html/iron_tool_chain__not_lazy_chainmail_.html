Iron Tool Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The iron tool chain is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting various tools and weapons. The iron tool chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Tool Chain". The tool chain takes 192 iron chain links to craft. Iron tool chains can be obtained when a Crusher crushes an iron whip, iron flail, iron lasso, or iron nunchucks. Iron tool chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Iron Flail - 1 iron tool chain, 1 block of iron chains, and 2 sticks (place the sticks in the middle and bottom left slots, the tool chain in the top right slot, and the block in the center right slot).
Iron Lasso - 1 iron tool chain and 2 chains (place the tool chain in the top right corner and the chains in the middle and bottom right corner).
Iron Nunchucks - 1 iron tool chain, 4 iron nuggets, and 2 sticks (nuggets go in all four corners, the sticks to the left and right, and the tool chain on the bottom).
Iron Rope x4 - 1 iron tool chain, 1 chain, and 1 iron ingot (place the tool chain in the middle, chain below, and iron above).
Iron Whip - 1 iron tool chain, 1 leather, and 1 stick (place the leather in the middle, the stick in the bottom left, and the tool chain in the top right). Iron tool chains can be crushed into unfinished iron chainmail projects containing 192 chain links.
