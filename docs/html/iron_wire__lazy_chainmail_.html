Iron Wire (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Iron Wire ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
The iron wire is an item in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into an iron coil. The bulk alternative to the iron wire is the iron spool. Iron wire can be crafted with 8 iron nuggets (place in a circle). Iron wire can be used on a winding jig to be turned into an iron coil.
