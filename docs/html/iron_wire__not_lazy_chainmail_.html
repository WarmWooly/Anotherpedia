Iron Wire (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The iron wire is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to be wound into an iron coil. The bulk alternative to the iron wire is the iron spool. Iron wire can be crafted with 8 iron nuggets (place in a circle). Novice-level chainmaillers have a 22.22% chance to trade 2 emeralds for 1 iron wire. The chances to find an iron wire in chests are the following:&spIn a winding jig ruins, a chest has an 89.26% chance to contain an iron wire.<>&spIn a chainmailler home, a chest has a 51.77% chance to contain an iron wire.<> Iron wires have a 15.38% chance of being found in suspicious sand within chainmail ruins. Iron wire can be used on a winding jig or in an auto winder to be turned into an iron coil.
