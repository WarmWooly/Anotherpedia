Island (road)

An {{bisland}} on a road is an area between lane (road) to control the movement of traffic. Islands are typically raised above the road, making a physical obstacle that must be navigated around. Islands can be used for pedestrian safety, decoration, and more.&pIslands are often found in parking lot, intersection (road) (such as a roundabout), and on roadways.
