Island (road)

<><>An {{bisland}} on a [[road]] is an area between [[lanes|lane (road)]] to control the movement of [[traffic]]. Islands are typically raised above the road, making a physical obstacle that must be navigated around. Islands can be used for [[pedestrian]] [[safety]], [[decoration]], and more.&p<>Islands are often found in [[parking lots|parking lot]], [[intersections|intersection (road)]] (such as a [[roundabout]]), and on roadways.
