Item (Mindustry)

An item in Mindustry is any resource that can be collected and transported on conveyor belt (Mindustry). Items are used to build building (Mindustry), to produce liquid (Mindustry) or other items, as ammunition (Mindustry), or to make new unit (Mindustry). Items can be stored in the Core (Mindustry) or other containers (such as a Vault (Mindustry)). There are currently 20 unique items: Beryllium (Mindustry), Blast Compound (Mindustry), Carbide (Mindustry), Coal (Mindustry), Copper (Mindustry), Graphite (Mindustry), Lead (Mindustry), Metaglass (Mindustry), Oxide (Mindustry), Phase Fabric (Mindustry), Plastanium (Mindustry), Pyratite (Mindustry), Sand (Mindustry), Scrap (Mindustry), Silicon (Mindustry), Spore Pod (Mindustry), Surge Alloy (Mindustry), Thorium (Mindustry), Titanium (Mindustry), and Tungsten (Mindustry).
