Item (Mindustry)

An item in Mindustry is any resource that can be collected and transported on conveyor belts. Items are used to build buildings, to produce liquids or other items, as ammunition, or to make new units. Items can be stored in the Core or other containers (such as a Vault). There are currently 20 unique items: Beryllium, Blast Compound, Carbide, Coal, Copper, Graphite, Lead, Metaglass, Oxide, Phase Fabric, Plastanium, Pyratite, Sand, Scrap, Silicon, Spore Pod, Surge Alloy, Thorium, Titanium, and Tungsten.
