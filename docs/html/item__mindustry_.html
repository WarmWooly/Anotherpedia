Item ({{iMindustry}})

<><>An {{bitem}} in {{i[[Mindustry]]}} is any resource that can be collected and transported on [[conveyor belts|conveyor belt (Mindustry)]]. Items are used to build [[buildings|building (Mindustry)]], to produce [[liquids|liquid (Mindustry)]] or other items, as [[ammunition|ammunition (Mindustry)]], or to make new [[units|unit (Mindustry)]]. Items can be stored in the [[Core|Core (Mindustry)]] or other containers (such as a [[Vault|Vault (Mindustry)]]).
