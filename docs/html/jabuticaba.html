Jabuticaba

Jabuticaba are a round, edible fruit that grows from jaboticaba trees (commonly called the Brazilian grape trees). Unlike other fruits, jabuticaba grows over the trunk of the tree, sometimes covering it completely. The fruit starts green before maturing into a rich purple or black with a white or light pink pulp. It can be used to make juice, jams, and other preserves. The fruit itself only lasts between 3--4 days before beginning to ferment.
