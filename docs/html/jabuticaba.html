Jabuticaba

Jabuticaba are a round, edible fruit that grows from jaboticaba tree (commonly called the Brazilian grape trees). Unlike other fruits, jabuticaba grows over the trunk (botany) of the tree, sometimes covering it completely. The fruit starts green before maturing into a rich purple or black with a white or light pink pulp. It can be used to make fruit juice, fruit jam, and other fruit preserves. The fruit itself only lasts between 3--4 days before beginning to fermentation.
