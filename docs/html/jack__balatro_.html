Jack (Balatro)

Jack is a rank of playing cards in Balatro that is worth 10 Chips when played without Modifiers or Jokers. Jacks are considered to be a face card. The Abandoned Deck removes all face cards, so Jack can't be drawn without modifying the deck. The Erratic Deck randomly selects ranks, so Jacks may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks. The following are Jokers that Jack can trigger because of its rank:<>
Mail-In Rebate allows the player to earn $5 on discard with the rank randomly selected at the start of the round, including Jack. The Idol gives x2 Mult when a card of a randomly selected rank and suit is played, including Jacks of any suit.
