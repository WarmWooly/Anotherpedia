Janet (Squishmallows)

Janet the Jellyfish is a stuffed animal by Squishmallows that resembles a jellyfish. The Squishdate for Janet was September 5, 2020, and has the collector number of 512. Janet is available in sizes 3.5", 5", 7.5", 8", 11", 12", 14", and 16". She is rainbow colored with a tie-dye pattern along most of her body, with the only exception being for her solid white belly. She has a pair of solid black eyes with a small smile. She also has a small wavy extension around the bottom of her body.
