January 2013 Australian bus assault

On January 22, 2013, Raymond Jones assaulted the Buslink driver in Dawrin, Northern Territory, Australia using a watermelon and multiple punches. The cause of the altercation was spurred by the driver asking Jones to pay his fare. The event is known by the name of the first story that broke about the incident by the Australian Broadcasting Corporation, "Watermelon used as weapon in bus assault". Jones was 53 years old at the time, and was intoxicated after drinking 700 mL of Wild Turkey. Allegedly, his children were on the bus during the incident. Jones would plead guilty in court to aggravated assault and disorderly behavior for the incident. Due to the absurdity of the situation, memes about the story were created in 2021 and onwards. The main video, created by Meh_here, titled "watermelon used as weapon in bus assault meme- pvz meme", is a video that lasts 27 seconds with over 4.4k views <>. The content of the video is the bus image from the original article with an overlay of the Melon-pult from Plants vs. Zombies, making fun of the scenario by hinting at the idea that Jones was acting like the plant.
