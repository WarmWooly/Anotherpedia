John Oliver

John William Oliver is a British and American comedian, political commentator, actor, and television host. He was born on April 23, 1977 in Erdington, Birmingham, England, making him <> years old. Formerly a stand-up comedian in the UK, he came to the States for The Daily Show with Jon Stewart from 2006 to 2013, in which he got three Primetime Emmy Awards. Since 2014, he's been the host of HBO's Last Week Tonight with John Oliver, winning sixteen Emmy Awards and two Peabody Awards.
