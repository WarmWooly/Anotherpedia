John Oliver's snake

John Oliver's snake was a reoccurring bit (comedy) in Season 11 (Last Week Tonight with John Oliver) episode 9 of Last Week Tonight with John Oliver titled "UFOs (Last Week Tonight with John Oliver)" where John Oliver lost his snake. It starts with a video clip of Mark O'Connell on WTTV in 2017 stating that the United States Air Force doesn't want to seem incompetent by not being able to explain a UFO, with Oliver trying not to look incompetent to the audience discussing the pet snake that he lost while bringing out an empty snake container. He then brings up his snake for another joke later in the segment, until culminating in the finale where a snake is found in front of Oliver's desk, but it isn't Oliver's snake. The snake's species hasn't been confirmed, but it has been guessed to most likely be the California kingsnake by fans.
