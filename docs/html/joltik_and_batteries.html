Joltik and batteries

Joltik and batteries refers to how the Pokémon Joltik is depicted around electric batteries, with an emphasis on Joltik consuming the batteries. This is in part because of Joltik's small size and its Electric typing, where Joltik eating batteries lines up with its scale and theming. Phrases associated with the phenomenon are "full of battery" (as the Joltik has eaten a lot of batteries) and "I make all batteries useless" (because they drain the batteries). Their consumption is contrasted with the risks posed through the consumption of batteries, as most animals and humans have a negative, potentially lethal reaction to doing so. Joltik may also be shown smoking the battery like a cigarette to consume it.
