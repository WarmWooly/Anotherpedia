Joy (Voidpet)

Joy is a Rare Fire type Healer Voidpet based on the emotion of joy. Joy is also the 42nd Voidpet in the VoidDex. Joy's eye is a rounded crescent facing upwards. Its first stage is blobby compared to other Voidpets, with a thick tail and a rounded snout. Its second stage adds the eyes to its tail as it slightly elongates and gains arms and legs. Its third stage adds a second tail and a pair of hair-like strands, with the new eyes on the top strand. The fourth stage expands its bottom strand and top tail, with eyes near its leg. Its fifth stage adds a longer bottom tail, additional back strands, and eyes on a second strand.

While Joy starts only creating embers, it can evolve to creating long streaks of fire as entertainment for others within the Void. Joy tends to reach its full potential being raised in a positive environment.
