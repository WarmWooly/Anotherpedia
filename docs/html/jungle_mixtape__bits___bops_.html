Jungle Mixtape (Bits & Bops)

Jungle Mixtape is the first mixtape within Bits & Bops, containing Flipper Snapper, Sweet Tooth, Rock, Paper, Showdown!, and Pantry Parade. To match the jungle theme, the following changes were made to each minigame:;;The cat looks like a cheetah and the seal is brown.|The cat and seal are now in a swampy jungle.|The ball that the seal bounces is replaced with a large mangrove apple.|The crocodile is yellow with a pineapple body.|The keeper is wearing yellow and green clothing.|The keeper throws watermelon slices and round fruits.|The keeper and crocodile are standing on trees.|The background for Rock, Paper, Showdown! is jungle-themed.|The hands playing RPS are only silhouettes.|The ants are orange and carry leaves.|The ants walk across a branch.;; The minigames appear in the following order: ::Flipper Snapper|Rock, Paper, Showdown!|Pantry Parade|Sweet Tooth|Flipper Snapper|Rock, Paper, Showdown!|Pantry Parade (carrying leaves)|Sweet Tooth|Rock, Paper, Showdown!|Sweet Tooth|Rock, Paper, Showdown!|Flipper Snapper|Pantry Parade|Flipper Snapper::There are no major changes to the hit indicatiors in the Jungle Mixtape.
