KABOOM! (Not Lazy Chainmail)

KABOOM! is a placeholder item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that results when an explosive item is crushed or haunted. When KABOOM! is "obtained", a TNT-sized exposion occurs instead. When TNT or a Minecart with TNT is crushed in a Crusher, the Crusher explodes. When TNT or a Minecart with TNT is haunted in a Haunter, the Haunter explodes.
