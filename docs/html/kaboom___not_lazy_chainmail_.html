KABOOM! (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>{{bKABOOM!}} is a placeholder item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} that results when an explosive item is Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) or Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail). When KABOOM! is "obtained", a TNT (Minecraft)-sized exposion occurs instead. When TNT (Minecraft) or a Minecart with TNT (Minecraft) is crushed in a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail), the Crusher explodes. When TNT (Minecraft) or a Minecart with TNT (Minecraft) is haunted in a Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail), the Haunter explodes.
