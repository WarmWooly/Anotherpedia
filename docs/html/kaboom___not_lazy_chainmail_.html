KABOOM! (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>KABOOM! is a placeholder item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail that results when an explosive item is Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) or Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail). When KABOOM! is "obtained", a TNT (Minecraft)-sized exposion occurs instead. When TNT (Minecraft) or a Minecart with TNT (Minecraft) is crushed in a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail), the Crusher explodes. When TNT (Minecraft) or a Minecart with TNT (Minecraft) is haunted in a Haunter (Not Lazy Chainmail), the Haunter explodes.
