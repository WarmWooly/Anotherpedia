KETIKA SEORANG CASIS DISURUH NGAJI OLEH KOMANDAN TNI

"KETIKA SEORANG CASIS DISURUH NGAJI OLEH KOMANDAN TNI" (translated to English: WHEN A RECRUIT IS ORDERED TO RECITE BY A TNI COMMANDER) is a YouTube Short posted on October 6, 2022, by Another Pedia. It depicts a recruit reciting as a commander stands nearby. Recruits and other military personnel can be seen marching and wandering in the background, with shouting also overheard throughout the video. It has the tags #tni (for Tentara Nasional Indonesia, the Indonesian National Armed Forces), #angkatanlaut (Navy), #abdinegara (no direct translation), and #shorts. The video is only 50 seconds long.
