Keyboard warrior

<><>A {{bkeyboard warrior}} is a [[person]] on the [[internet]], often [[anonymous]], who makes [[heated|anger]] discussions about topics, usually believing that they are in the [[right|correct]]. Their viewpoints are often strong [[opinions|opinion]] with little basis or [[facts|fact]]. Most keyboard warriors go alone, but rarely, [[mobs|mob]] form to [[flood|spam]] a [[chatroom]] or [[discussion|internet discussion]], drowning out the voices of others with differing opinions. Keyboard warriors do not act the same in person as they do online, using the screen to be free to say what they are really thinking.
