Keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

Keycards are a clearance item within SCP: Devolved Revamp used to unlock doors around the Facility. The level of a player's keycard is always displayed in the bottom-right corner. Each keycard can unlock all doors of previous levels, ordered Class D, Level 1, Level 2, Level 3, and Level 4. The level of a player's keycard is dependent on their role, with higher access granted to players with higher levels. Higher keycards can access deeper areas in the Facility and SCPs locked throughout.
