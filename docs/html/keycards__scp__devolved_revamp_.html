Keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

Keycards are a clearance item within SCP: Devolved Revamp used to unlock doors around the Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The level of a player's keycard is always displayed in the bottom-right corner. Each keycard can unlock all doors of previous levels, ordered Class D Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp), Level 1 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp), Level 2 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp), Level 3 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and Level 4 Keycard (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The level of a player's keycard is dependent on their role (SCP: Devolved Revamp), with higher access granted to players with higher levels. Higher keycards can access deeper areas in the Facility and SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp) locked throughout.
