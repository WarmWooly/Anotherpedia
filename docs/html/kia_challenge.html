Kia Challenge

The Kia Challenge was a Challenge (social media) where the participants would break into and Grand Theft Auto a car on social media platform such as TikTok and YouTube.

The main cause was a lack of an Car Immobilizer, which prevented theft via bypassing the ignition. Between 2011 and 2021, some cars manufactured by Hyundai and Kia lacked immobilizers, which made them much easier to steal. The Kia Boys, also known as the Kia Boyz, are a collective group of teenager who would hacking into the car, before joyriding until they crashed or dumped the car. A notable location for the activity was Milwaukee, Wisconsin, where the trend started in October 2022.

In the end, there were thousands of cars stolen, with multiple car accident and several death caused by this challenge. Kia and Hyundai Kia Giving The Club to Kia Owners anti-theft device, alongside free software patches. TikTok has stated that they would remove tutorial related to stealing Kias and Hyundais.
