Kiln (Mindustry)

A Kiln is a building in Mindustry on Serpulo that is used to create Metaglass. The description of the Kiln is the following:Smelts sand and lead into metaglass. It has 160 health, taking 1.35 seconds to build, while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 60 Copper, 30 Lead, and 30 Graphite. It can store up to 10 items. To create 1 Metaglass, it requires 1 Lead and 1 Sand. It has a production time of 0.5 seconds. To research it, you must capture The Craters and have the Silicon Smelter researched. It costs 1900 Copper, 900 Lead, and 900 Graphite to research it.
