Kiln (Mindustry)

A {{bKiln}} is a building (mindustry) in {{iMindustry}} on Serpulo (Mindustry) that is used to create Metaglass. The description of the Kiln is the following: It has 160 health (Mindustry), taking 1.35 build time (Mindustry), while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 60 Copper (mindustry), 30 lead (Mindustry), and 30 graphite (Mindustry). It can store up to 10 item (Mindustry). To create 1 Metaglass, it requires 1 Lead and 1 Sand (mindustry). It has a production time of 0.5 seconds. To research (Mindustry) it, you must capturing (Mindustry) The Craters (Mindustry) and have the Silicon Smelter (Mindustry) researched. It costs 1900 Copper, 900 Lead, and 900 Graphite to research it.
