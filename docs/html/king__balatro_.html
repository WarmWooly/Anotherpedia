King (Balatro)

King is a rank of playing cards in Balatro that is worth 10 Chips when played without Modifiers or Jokers. Kings are considered to be a face card. The Abandoned Deck removes all face cards, so King can't be drawn without modifying the deck. The Erratic Deck randomly selects ranks, so Kings may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks.
