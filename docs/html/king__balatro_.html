King (Balatro)

<><>{{bKing}} is a [[rank|ranks (Balatro)]] of [[playing cards|playing cards (Balatro)]] in {{i[[Balatro]]}} that is worth 10 [[Chips|Chips (Balatro)]] when played without [[Modifiers|Modifiers (Balatro)]] or [[Jokers|Jokers (Balatro)]]. Kings are considered to be a [[face card|face cards (Balatro)]]. The [[Abandoned Deck|Abandoned Deck (Balatro)]] removes all face cards, so King can't be drawn without modifying the deck. The [[Erratic Deck|Erratic Deck (Balatro)]] randomly selects ranks, so Kings may be more or less likely depending on the selected ranks.
