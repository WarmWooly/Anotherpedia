Kitchen without gun

"Kitchen without gun" is a song by ins step derived from mixing the song "BGM Radical Galaxy" from Cat Girl Without Salad: Amuse-Bouche and the sketch Kitchen Gun from The Peter Serafinowicz Show, which was uploaded to YouTube on April 27, 2020. The video is currently private, but reuploads of "Kitchen without gun" are a dime a dozen.
