Knapped (Minecraft mod)

{{iKnapped}} is a Minecraft mod made by Warm_Wooly in MCreator on December 18, 2024, adding the item Knapped Flint Brick and new decoration blocks. Knapped is available for 1.20.1 for Forge. Knapped has an addon for Create compatibility, Create: Knapped.
