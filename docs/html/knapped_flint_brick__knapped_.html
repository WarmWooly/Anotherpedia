For the block (Minecraft), see Knapped Flint Bricks ({{aiKnapped).}}
<>The knapped flint brick is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used in crafting the various Knapped blocks in the mod. Knapped flint bricks can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 flint (Minecraft) (shapeless; makes 3). Knapped flint bricks can be cut from flint (Minecraft) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft) (makes 2). Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped) - 4 knapped flint bricks (item) (place in a 2x2 square).
