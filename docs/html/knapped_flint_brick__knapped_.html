Knapped Flint Brick (Knapped)

For the block, see Knapped Flint Bricks (aiKnapped).
<resistance|Noinfo>>The knapped flint brick is an item in the Minecraft mod Knapped used in crafting the various blocks in the mod. Knapped flint bricks can be crafted with 2 flint (shapeless; makes 3). Knapped flint bricks can be cut from flint in a Stonecutter (makes 2). Knapped flint bricks are using in the following crafting recipes:
Flint Tile - 4 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 stone/smooth stone/smooth sandstone/smooth red sandstone (surround the stone with the bricks on all sides).
Knapped Flint Bricks - 4 knapped flint bricks (item) (place in a 2x2 square).
Polished Andesite Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint brick (item)s and 1 polished andesite (shapeless).
Polished Andesite Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 polished andesite, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Polished Diorite Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint brick (item)s and 1 polished diorite (shapeless).
Polished Diorite Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 polished diorite, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Polished Granite Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint brick (item)s and 1 polished granite (shapeless).
Polished Granite Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 polished granite, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Smooth Red Sandstone Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint brick (item)s and 1 smooth red sandstone (shapeless).
Smooth Red Sandstone Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 smooth red sandstone, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Smooth Sandstone Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 smooth sandstone (shapeless).
Smooth Sandstone Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 smooth sandstone, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Smooth Stone Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 smooth stone (shapeless).
Smooth Stone Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 smooth stone, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Smooth Quartz Block Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 smooth quartz block (shapeless).
Smooth Quartz Block Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 smooth quartz block, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
Stone Flushwork Panel - 2 knapped flint bricks (item) and 1 stone (shapeless).
Stone Flushwork Thing - 2 knapped flint bricks (item), 1 stone, and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless). gal
