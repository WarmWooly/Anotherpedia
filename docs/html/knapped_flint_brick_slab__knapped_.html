Knapped Flint Brick Slab (Knapped)

<>The knapped flint brick slab is a slab in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint brick slabs can be crafted with 4 knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row; makes 6). Knapped flint brick slabs can be cut from knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter (makes 2).
