Knapped Flint Brick Stairs (Knapped)

<>Knapped flint brick stairs are a stairs (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint brick stairs can be crafting (Minecraft) with 6 knapped flint bricks (Knapped) (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center; makes 4). Knapped flint brick stairs can be cut from knapped flint bricks (Knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecoraft).
