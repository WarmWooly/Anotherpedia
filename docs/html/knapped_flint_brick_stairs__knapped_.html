Knapped Flint Brick Stairs (Knapped)

Knapped flint brick stairs are a stair in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint brick stairs can be crafted with 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center; makes 4). Knapped flint brick stairs can be cut from knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter.
