Knapped Flint Brick Wall (Knapped)

<>The knapped flint brick wall is a wall in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint brick walls can be crafted with 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the middle and bottom rows; makes 6). Knapped flint brick walls can be cut from knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter.
