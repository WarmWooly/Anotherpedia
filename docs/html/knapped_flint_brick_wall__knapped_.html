Knapped Flint Brick Wall (Knapped)

<>The knapped flint brick wall is a wall (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint brick walls can be crafting (Minecraft) with 6 knapped flint bricks (Knapped) (fill the middle and bottom rows; makes 6). Knapped flint brick walls can be cut from knapped flint bricks (Knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecoraft).
