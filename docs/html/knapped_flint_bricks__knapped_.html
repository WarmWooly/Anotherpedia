Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

For the item (Minecraft), see Knapped Flint Brick ({{aiKnapped).}}
<>Knapped flint bricks are a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint bricks can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 knapped flint brick (Knapped) (place in a 2x2 square). Knapped flint bricks can be cut from basketweave knapped flint bricks (knapped), chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), english knapped flint bricks (knapped), long chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), long knapped flint bricks (knapped), pinwheel knapped flint bricks (knapped), short knapped flint bricks (knapped), square knapped flint bricks (knapped), or stacked knapped flint bricks (knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft). Knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Dark Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped) x8 - 8 knapped flint bricks and 1 black dye (Minecraft) (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks).
Knapped Flint Brick Slab (Knapped) x6 - 3 knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).
Knapped Flint Brick Stairs (Knapped) x4 - 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center).
Knapped Flint Brick Wall (Knapped) x6 - 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the middle and bottom rows). Knapped flint bricks are used in the following stonecutter (Minecraft) recipes:
