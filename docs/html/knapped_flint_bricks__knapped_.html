Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

For the item, see Knapped Flint Brick (aiKnapped).&sp<>Knapped flint bricks are a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Knapped flint bricks can be crafted with 4 knapped flint bricks (item) (place in a 2x2 square). Knapped flint bricks can be cut from basketweave, chevron, english, long chevron, long, pinwheel, short, square, or stacked knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter. Knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:&spDark Knapped Flint Bricks x8 - 8 knapped flint bricks and 1 black dye (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks).&spKnapped Flint Brick Slab x6 - 3 knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).&spKnapped Flint Brick Stairs x4 - 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center).&spKnapped Flint Brick Wall x6 - 6 knapped flint bricks (fill the middle and bottom rows).
