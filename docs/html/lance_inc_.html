Lance Inc.

Lance Inc., or Lance, is a brand of snacks owned by the Snyder's-Lance company, which itself is owned by the Campbell Soup Company. The current headquarters for Lance is in Charlotte, North Carolina. It was founded by Pillip Lance in 1913. On December 2, 2020, Lance Inc. formally merged with Snyder's of Hanover to become Snyder's-Lance Inc., still traded under LNCE. This merger has made Snyder's-Lance the second-largest snack company.
