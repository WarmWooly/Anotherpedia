Lance Inc.

Lance Inc., or Lance, is a brand of snack owned by the Snyder's-Lance company, which itself is owned by the Campbell Soup Company. The current headquarters for Lance is in Charlotte, North Carolina. It was founding by Pillip Lance in 1913. On December 2, 2020, Lance Inc. formally merging with Snyder's of Hanover to become Snyder's-Lance Inc., still traded under LNCE.
