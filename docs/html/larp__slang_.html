Larp (slang)

Larp is slang verb for a person who pretends to be knowledgeable about something, especially on social media. It is similar to terms like faker and poser, appearing in the 2020s based on the acronym LARP. It is derived from how people who live action roleplay (LARP) deliberately pretend to be a character, extending it to being superficial in other aspects of their lives. Someone who larps is a larper, which means they are larping.
