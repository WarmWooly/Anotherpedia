Laundry basket

A laundry basket is a basket that is for the storage and/or transport of laundry in a household. It can both hold laundry to be washed and laundry that has been dried, coming in a variety of shapes, sizes, and materials. Laundry baskets tend to be located within bedrooms or laundry rooms, with many being equipt with handles for easier carrying.
