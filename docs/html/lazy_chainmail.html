Lazy Chainmail

Lazy Chainmail is a Minecraft mod made by Warm_Wooly in MCreator on July 23, 2024, giving an alternative method to obtain Chainmail. Lazy Chainmail is available for 1.20.1 for Forge. The alternative mod, Not Lazy Chainmail, is the full version Lazy Chainmail.
