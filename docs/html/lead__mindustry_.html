Lead (Mindustry)

Lead is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Mechanical Drill or higher. It can also be harvested from a Separator. The description of Lead is the following:It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in the early-game buildings, ammo, and units. It is also used to produce Metaglass, Pyratite, and Surge Alloy.
