Lead (Mindustry)

Lead is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Mechanical Drill (Mindustry) or higher. It can also be harvested from a Separator (Mindustry). The description of Lead is the following:Used in liquid transportation and electrical structures.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used in the early-game building (Mindustry), ammo (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry). It is also used to produce Metaglass (Mindustry), Pyratite (Mindustry), and Surge Alloy (Mindustry).
