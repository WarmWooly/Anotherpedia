Leather Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Speed|x1info>>The leather industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Leather industrial belts can be crafted using 4 leather (place leather in the shape of a ring). Crafting the leather industrial belt will award the advancement Slow But Steady. The chances to find a leather industrial belt in a winding jig are the following:
In a winding jig ruins, there is a 42.86% chance to contain a leather industrial belt. Leather industrial belts are used in the following melding recipes:
Crude Engine - 1 leather industrial belt, 1 block of iron, and 1 piston The leather industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:
Auto Mailler
Auto Snipper
Auto Winder
Belt Generator
Chain Generator
Cobblestone Generator
Conveyor Warper
Crusher
Item Aggregator
Melder
Recycler
Winding Jig Leather industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining leather industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Leather industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Leather industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on leather industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels. Leather chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy in a belt generator, generating 1 FE/t.
