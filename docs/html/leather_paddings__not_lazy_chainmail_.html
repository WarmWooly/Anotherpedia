Leather paddings (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Leather paddings are a type of item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. They are used in a leathersmithing table to be applied to chainmail armor and shields. There are 21 leather paddings, including the base one and the upgraded versions: strengthened, toughened, reinforced, lightweight, expanding, attractive, healthy, swift, aquadynamic, constructive, destructive, elevating, weighted, inflated, rocky, muddy, natural, hellish, vivacious, and draconic. All leather paddings are melded in a Melder.
