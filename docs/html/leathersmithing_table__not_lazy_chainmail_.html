Leathersmithing Table (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The leathersmithing table is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to add leather padding onto chainmail armor or shields. The leathersmithing table can be crafted with 2 rose gold ingots, 2 leather, and 2 planks (place the planks on the bottom, then the rose gold above, then the leather on top). The leathersmtihing table can be used to add various types of leather padding onto chainmail armor or shields to upgrade them. Items to be upgraded will go in the leftmost slot. The upgradable item can then be removed from the rightmost slot. The three slots in the middle are used to add/remove leather padding. Worn armor (or a shield held in the main hand or offhand) will apply the benefits of their padding to the player. In a furnace, blast furnace, or smoker, 1 leathersmithing table can fuel for 30 seconds (3 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (64 leathersmithing table, 1920 seconds, 192 items).
