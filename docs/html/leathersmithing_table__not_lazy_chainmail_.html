Leathersmithing Table (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The leathersmithing table is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to add leather paddings (Not Lazy Chainmail) onto Chainmail armor (Not Lazy Chainmail) or shield (Minecraft). The leathersmithing table can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), 2 leather (Minecraft), and 2 planks (Minecraft) (place the planks on the bottom, then the rose gold above, then the leather on top). The leathersmtihing table can be used to add various types of leather padding onto chainmail armor or shields to upgrade them. Items to be upgraded will go in the leftmost slot. The upgradable item can then be removed from the rightmost slot. The three slots in the middle are used to add/remove leather padding. Worn armor (or a shield held in the main hand (Minecraft) or offhand (Minecraft)) will apply the benefits of their padding to the player. In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 leathersmithing table can fuel for 30 second (3 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 leathersmithing table, 1920 seconds, 192 items).
