Life

Life is the condition of matter that are comprised of at least one cell, requires energy and food, grows and Reproduction, responds to stimulation, Evolution over time, and at some point Death.

Life is found all over Earth, both on land and in ocean. Estimations of species count on Earth go from 3 million to 100 million. A single living entity is known as a lifeform.

Virus and Viroid are often Debate over the life of a virus due to not following all these requirements for life.
