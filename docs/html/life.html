Life

Life is the condition of matter that are comprised of at least one cell, requires energy and nutrients, grows and reproduces, responds to stimulation, evolves over time, and at some point dies.&pLife is found all over Earth, both on land and in oceans. Estimations of species count on Earth go from 3 million to 100 million. A single living entity is known as a lifeform.&pViruses and viroids are often debated due to not following all these requirements for life.
