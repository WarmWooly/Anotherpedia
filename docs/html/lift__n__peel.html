Lift 'n' Peel

<><><>vid>>{{bLift 'n' Peel}} is a [[trademarked|trademark]] [[container seal]] produced by [[Selig Group]]. The seal provides [[tamper evidence]] for containers while allowing easy consumer use of the sealed item. A semi-circle plastic tab that can be lifted is used to peel the seal away from a container. Lift 'n' Peel is used to seal a variety of products, including [[food]], [[drinks|drink]], [[creams|cream]] and [[powders|powder]] for [[personal care]], [[pills|pill]] and other [[medicine]], and for various [[chemicals|chemical]]. Lift 'n' Peel can be used for both [[glass]] [[containers|container]] and many types of [[plastic]] containers. The optimal way to open a Lift 'n' Peel is to pull the tab sideways to prevent the second layer from sticking to the container.
