Lift 'n' Peel

Lift 'n' Peel is a trademark container seal produced by Selig Group. The seal provides tamper evidence for containers while allowing easy consumer use of the sealed item. A semi-circle plastic tab that can be lifted is used to peel the seal away from a container. Lift 'n' Peel is used to seal a variety of products, including food, drink, cream and powder for personal care, pill and other medicine, and for various chemical. Lift 'n' Peel can be used for both glass container and many types of plastic containers. The optimal way to open a Lift 'n' Peel is to pull the tab sideways to prevent the second layer from sticking to the container.
