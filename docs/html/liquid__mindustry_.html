Liquid (Mindustry)

A liquid in Mindustry is any resource (both liquid and gas) that can be collected and transported in Conduit (Mindustry). Liquids are used to produce item (Mindustry) or other liquids, as ammunition (Mindustry), to increase the speed of building (Mindustry), or to make new unit (Mindustry). Liquids can't be stored in the Core (Mindustry), but they can be stored in containers (such as a Liquid Tank (Mindustry)). There are currently 10 unique liquids: Arkycite (Mindustry), Cryofluid (Mindustry), Cyanogen (Mindustry), Hydrogen (Mindustry), Neoplasm (Mindustry), Nitrogen (Mindustry), Oil (Mindustry), Slag (Mindustry), and Water (Mindustry).
