Liquid ({{iMindustry}})

<><>A {{bliquid}} in {{i[[Mindustry]]}} is any resource (both [[liquids|liquid]] and [[gasses|gas]]) that can be collected and transported in [[conduits|Conduit (Mindustry)]]. Liquids are used to produce [[items|item (Mindustry)]] or other liquids, as [[ammunition|ammunition (Mindustry)]], to increase the speed of [[buildings|building (Mindustry)]], or to make new [[units|unit (Mindustry)]]. Liquids can't be stored in the [[Core|Core (Mindustry)]], but they can be stored in containers (such as a [[Liquid Tank|Liquid Tank (Mindustry)]]). There are currently 10 unique liquids: [[Arkycite|Arkycite (Mindustry)]], [[Cryofluid|Cryofluid (Mindustry)]], [[Cyanogen|Cyanogen (Mindustry)]], [[Hydrogen|Hydrogen (Mindustry)]], [[Neoplasm|Neoplasm (Mindustry)]], [[Nitrogen|Nitrogen (Mindustry)]], [[Oil|Oil (Mindustry)]], [[Slag|Slag (Mindustry)]], and [[Water|Water (Mindustry)]].
