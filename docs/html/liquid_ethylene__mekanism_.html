Liquid Ethylene (Mekanism)

Liquid ethylene is a liquid in the Minecraft mod Mekanism. It can be stored in fluid tanks and transported with mechanical pipes. In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made to obtain liquid ethylene:
1 mB Ethylene --> 1 mB Liquid Ethylene In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber, the following reactions can be made with liquid ethylene:
50 mB Liquid Ethylene + 10 mB Oxygen + 1 Substrate --> 1 HDPE Pellet + 5 mB Oxygen In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made with liquid ethylene:
1 mB Liquid Ethylene --> 1 mB Ethylene
