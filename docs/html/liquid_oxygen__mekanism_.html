Liquid Oxygen (Mekanism)

Liquid oxygen is a liquid (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Mekanism. It can be stored in Fluid Tank (Mekanism) and transported with mechanical pipe (Mekanism). In a Rotary Condensentrator (Mekanism), the following reactions can be made to obtain liquid oxygen:
1 mB Oxygen (Mekanism) --> 1 millibuckets Liquid Oxygen In a Rotary Condensentrator (Mekanism), the following reactions can be made with liquid oxygen:
1 mB Liquid Oxygen --> 1 mB Oxygen (Mekanism)
