Live, Laugh, Love

Live, Laugh, Love was a hendiatris popularized between the late 2000s and the early 2010s, being used as a motivational phrase, which has since fallen out of favor and became cheugy. The phrase originated from the poem by Bessie Anderson Stanley in 1904 titled "Success":He achieved success who has lived well, laughed often, and loved muchLive, laugh, love is often seen around heart (symbol), emoji, flower, and butterfly.
