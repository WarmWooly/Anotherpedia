Live Tucker Reaction

{{bLive Tucker Reaction}} is a internet meme of when Fox News' firing of Tucker Carlson Fox News political commentator Tucker Carlson was shown in a small box off to the bottom right corner of Joe Biden first speech in March of 2021 which was airing on {{iTucker Carlson Tonight}}, where viewer could see in real-time Carlson's reaction to the speech. During the speech, Carlson was often puzzled, where he would occasionally nod or head shaking. It spread across platforms such as iFunny, mainly due to the versatility of the Live X Reaction snowclone and the humor that could be produced by making it seem like a notable or recognizable public figure or character was watching and reacting to other known content. {{tFor more images related to Live Tucker Reaction, see Gallery of Live Tucker Reaction.}}&spThe most often variation was of the content Carlson was reacting too, which was often shock value, funny, or sad, that was even more ridiculous because of Carlson's stern expression. Others include the changing of the reactor, such as Sad SpongeBob (meme), dog, Klaud (Star Wars), Saul Goodman (Breaking Bad), Andrew, Gastrodon (Pokémon), Clodsire (Pokémon), Heavy (Team Fortress 2), and more. {{tMain page: Live Slug Reaction}}&spA popular variation on the meme has Klaud from {{iStar Wars: The Rise of Skywalker}} reacting to content often related to romance, such as hug or kiss.
