Live Tucker Reaction

Live Tucker Reaction is a meme of when former Fox News political commentator Tucker Carlson was shown in a small box off to the bottom right corner of Joe Biden's first speech in March of 2021 which was airing on Tucker Carlson Tonight, where viewers could see in real-time Carlson's reaction to the speech. During the speech, Carlson was often puzzled, where he would occasionally nod or shake his head. It spread across platforms such as iFunny, mainly due to the versatility of the Live X Reaction snowclone and the humor that could be produced by making it seem like a notable or recognizable figure or character was watching and reacting to other known content. For more images related to Live Tucker Reaction, see Gallery of Live Tucker Reaction.
The most often variation was of the content Carlson was reacting too, which was often shocking, funny, or sad, that was even more ridiculous because of Carlson's stern expression. Others include the changing of the reactor, such as sad SpongeBob, dogs, Klaud, Saul Goodman, Andrew, Gastrodon, Clodsire, Heavy, and more. Main page: Live Slug Reaction
A popular variation on the meme has Klaud from Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker reacting to content often related to romance, such as hugging or kissing.
