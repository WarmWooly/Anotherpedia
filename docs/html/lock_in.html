Lock in

<>{{tFor the [[slang]], see [[lock in (slang)]].}}&sp<>{{bLock in}} is a [[phrasal verb]] of [[lock]] when describing the [[action]] of keeping [[something]] (such as a [[person]] or [[animal]]) contained to a single [[location]]. Fastening a [[door]], [[window]], or other [[exit]] would be considered locking something in.
