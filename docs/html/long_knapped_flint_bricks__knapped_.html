Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<>Long knapped flint bricks are a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Long knapped flint bricks can be cut from normal, basketweave, chevron, english, long chevron, pinwheel, short, square, or stacked knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter.
