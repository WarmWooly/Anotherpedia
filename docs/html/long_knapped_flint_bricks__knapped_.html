Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<>Long knapped flint bricks are a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Knapped (Minecraft mod) used for brickwork-related decoration. Long knapped flint bricks can be cut from knapped flint bricks (knapped), basketweave knapped flint bricks (knapped), chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), english knapped flint bricks (knapped), long chevron knapped flint bricks (knapped), pinwheel knapped flint bricks (knapped), short knapped flint bricks (knapped), square knapped flint bricks (knapped), or stacked knapped flint bricks (knapped) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft). Long knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:

Long Knapped Flint Brick Slab (Knapped) x6 - 3 long knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).
