Long Knapped Flint Bricks (Knapped)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Long knapped flint bricks are a block in the Minecraft mod Knapped used for brickwork-related decoration. Long knapped flint bricks can be cut from normal, basketweave, chevron, english, long chevron, pinwheel, short, square, or stacked knapped flint bricks in a Stonecutter. Long knapped flint bricks are used in the following crafting recipes:
Dark Long Knapped Flint Bricks x8 - 8 long knapped flint bricks and 1 black dye (place the dye in the center and surround with bricks).
Long Knapped Flint Brick Slab x6 - 3 long knapped flint bricks (fill the bottom row).
Long Knapped Flint Brick Stairs x4 - 6 long knapped flint bricks (fill the left column and bottom row, then place one in the center). Long knapped flint bricks are used in the following stonecutting recipes:
