Long pillow

A long pillow (also called a lumbar pillow or decorative body pillow) is a pillow that is much longer than normal or a body pillow that is explicitly used as decoration. Long pillows tend to be used on couch or bed as a decorative piece, a support for other pillows, or a pillow that can be used by multiple people. Long pillows may be shaped like an extended pillow or as a cylinder (which is also called a "body pillow (pillow shape)").
