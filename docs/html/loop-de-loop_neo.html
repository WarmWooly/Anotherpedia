Loop-de-loop neo

A loop-de-loop neo is a joke neo jump in Minecraft in the spoof on Parkour Civilization titled "Parkour Civilization be like" from div_y, where the "parkour champion" pulls out the loop-de-loop neo to attack Eggbo. A loop-de-loop neo could not be achieved in Survival Mode because the jump requires Creative flight to go all the way around the loop-de-loop. The absurdity of such a jump is what makes it comedic, with the parkour champion just flying around it. Eggbo (internal monologuing): But then, he used the illegal parkour my friend warned me about.
Parkour champion: Watch this, Eggbo!
[The parkour champion jumps, flies around a wooden loop-de-loop, then lands on the other side.]
Eggbo (internal monologuing): Then, he did a loop-de-loop neo! The battle was over.
Parkour champion: What are you going to do, NOW?
Eggbo (internal monologuing): Now there was only one jump I could do to defeat the champion.
