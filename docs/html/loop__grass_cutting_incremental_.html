Loop (Grass Cutting Incremental)

Loops are the first Loop layer of the Universe 1 (Grass Cutting Incremental) new game+ in the game Grass Cutting Incremental. By performing a Loop, the player will obtain a currency called Looplet (Grass Cutting Incremental). The player will also unlock the Loop Tower (Grass Cutting Incremental). After performing a Loop, the player will be brought back to the start of game progression. The multiplier provided by all tiers of Loops is called the Loop Multiplier.
