Loose Deepened Stone (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Loose deepened stones are an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) as a piece of cobbled deepslate (Minecraft). Loose deepened stones can be collected by sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) Deepened Gravel (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an iron sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (6% chance) or a rose gold sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (12% chance). Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of loose deepened stones from sieving. 4 loose deepened stones can be crafting (Minecraft) in a 2x2 square to make cobbled deepslate (Minecraft). Loose deepened stones are used in the following melding recipes:&spRocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 loose deepened stones and 1 leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) Main page: Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}&spWhen held in the offhand, the loose deepened stone can be launched from a Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail). When the loose deepened stone impacts a block, it drops to the ground. If the hit block is glass (Minecraft), the glass block will break. The loose deepened stone deals damage to hit mobs. If a player increases their Rockiness with Rocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail), the damage dealt from a launched loose deepened stone increases. If a Skeleton (Minecraft), Stray (Minecraft), Skeleton Horse (Minecraft), Wither Skeleton (Minecraft), or the Wither (Minecraft) is hit with a launched loose deepened stone, the advancement (Minecraft) Sticks and Stones (Not Lazy Chainmail) is awarded.
