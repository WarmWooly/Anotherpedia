[[Separation|Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] can increase the chance/drops of loose deepened stones from sieving.<><>4 loose deepened stones can be [[crafted|crafting (Minecraft)]] in a 2x2 square to make [[cobbled deepslate|cobbled deepslate (Minecraft)]].<>Loose deepened stones are used in the following melding recipes:&sp[[Rocky Leather Padding|Rocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] - 2 loose deepened stones and 1 [[leather padding|leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]<>{{tMain page: [[Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail}})]]}}&spWhen held in the offhand, the loose deepened stone can be launched from a [[chain launcher|Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. When the loose deepened stone impacts a block, it drops to the ground. If the hit block is [[glass|glass (Minecraft)]], the glass block will break. The loose deepened stone deals damage to hit mobs. If a player increases their Rockiness with [[rocky leather paddings|Rocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], the damage dealt from a launched loose deepened stone increases. If a [[Skeleton|Skeleton (Minecraft)]], [[Stray|Stray (Minecraft)]], [[Skeleton Horse|Skeleton Horse (Minecraft)]], [[Wither Skeleton|Wither Skeleton (Minecraft)]], or the [[Wither|Wither (Minecraft)]] is hit with a launched loose deepened stone, the [[advancement|advancement (Minecraft)]] [[Sticks and Stones|Sticks and Stones (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] is awarded.
