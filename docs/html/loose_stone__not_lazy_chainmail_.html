Loose Stone (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>{{bLoose stones}} are an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} obtained from sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) as a piece of cobblestone (Minecraft). There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain loose stones. Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of loose stones from sieving.<> 4 loose stones can be crafting (Minecraft) in a 2x2 square to make cobblestone (Minecraft). Loose stones are used in the following melding recipes:&spNatural Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 loose stone, 1 leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 moss block (Minecraft) {{tMain page: Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail}})}}&spWhen held in the offhand, the loose stone can be launched from a Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail). When the loose stone impacts a block, it drops to the ground. If the hit block is glass (Minecraft), the glass block will break. The loose stone deals damage to hit mobs. If a player increases their Rockiness with Rocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail), the damage dealt from a launched loose stone increases. If a Skeleton (Minecraft), Stray (Minecraft), Skeleton Horse (Minecraft), Wither Skeleton (Minecraft), or the Wither (Minecraft) is hit with a launched loose stone, the advancement (Minecraft) Sticks and Stones (Not Lazy Chainmail) is awarded.
