Loose Stone ({{iNot Lazy Chainmail}})

<><>||{{bStats}}||{{b[[Rarity|Rarity (Minecraft)]]}}|Common||{{b[[Stack size|Stack size (Minecraft)]]}}|64||{{b[[Fire/lava&spresistance|Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)]]}}|Noinfo>>{{bLoose stones}} are an [[item|item (Minecraft)]] in the {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|mod (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Not Lazy Chainmail]]}} obtained from [[sieving|sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] as a piece of [[cobblestone|cobblestone (Minecraft)]].<><>There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain loose stones. [[Separation|Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] can increase the chance/drops of loose stones from sieving.<><><>4 loose stones can be [[crafted|crafting (Minecraft)]] in a 2x2 square to make [[cobblestone|cobblestone (Minecraft)]].<>Loose stones are used in the following melding recipes:&sp[[Natural Leather Padding|Natural Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] - 1 loose stone, 1 [[leather padding|leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and 1 [[moss block|moss block (Minecraft)]]<>{{tMain page: [[Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail}})]]}}&spWhen held in the offhand, the loose stone can be launched from a [[chain launcher|Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. When the loose stone impacts a block, it drops to the ground. If the hit block is [[glass|glass (Minecraft)]], the glass block will break. The loose stone deals damage to hit mobs. If a player increases their Rockiness with [[rocky leather paddings|Rocky Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], the damage dealt from a launched loose stone increases. If a [[Skeleton|Skeleton (Minecraft)]], [[Stray|Stray (Minecraft)]], [[Skeleton Horse|Skeleton Horse (Minecraft)]], [[Wither Skeleton|Wither Skeleton (Minecraft)]], or the [[Wither|Wither (Minecraft)]] is hit with a launched loose stone, the [[advancement|advancement (Minecraft)]] [[Sticks and Stones|Sticks and Stones (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] is awarded.
