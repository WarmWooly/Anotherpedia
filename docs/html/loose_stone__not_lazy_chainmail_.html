Loose Stone (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Loose stones are an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving as a piece of cobblestone. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain loose stones. Separation can increase the chance/drops of loose stones from sieving.<> 4 loose stones can be crafted in a 2x2 square to make cobblestone. Loose stones are used in the following melding recipes:
Natural Leather Padding - 1 loose stone, 1 leather padding, and 1 moss block Main page: Chain Launcher (aiNot Lazy Chainmail)
When held in the offhand, the loose stone can be launched from a chain launcher. When the loose stone impacts a block, it drops to the ground. If the hit block is glass, the glass block will break. The loose stone deals damage to hit mobs. If a player increases their Rockiness with rocky leather paddings, the damage dealt from a launched loose stone increases. If a Skeleton, Stray, Skeleton Horse, Wither Skeleton, or the Wither is hit with a launched loose stone, the advancement Sticks and Stones is awarded.
