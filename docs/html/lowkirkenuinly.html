Lowkirkenuinly

Lowkirkenuinly is a late-2025 portmanteau of lowkey, the deceased Charlie Kirk, and genuinely used in TikToks for memes, replacing "lowkey" or "genuinely". Lowkirkenuinly is an expansion on lowkenuinly with Charlie Kirk as he's been integrated in meme culture. It takes on the same definition of lowkey.
