Lush cave here

Lush cave here refers to a series of internet meme regarding the Minecraft mechanic where an azalea tree (Minecraft) will always world generation (Minecraft) above a lush cave (Minecraft), growing in popularity in March 2026. The meme tends to appear in two forms: one where the existence of an azalea tree superimposed on an image implies a lush cave, and another showing the azalea tree with a lush cave below it. Deviations on the normal format include showing that manually growing an azalea tree likely means that a lush cave isn't below it, using different things that include "azalea" (such as the azalea plant or the former rapper Iggy Azalea), or metameme regarding the subject. The subreddit r/lushcave is the largest concentration of lush cave here memes, being created on March 7, 2026.
