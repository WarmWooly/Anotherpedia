M249 SAW (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The M249 SAW is a primary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Mobile Task Force at level 5. While its damage and firerate may be lacking, its extreme ammo capacity allows the user to keep the pressure against targets for a long time without reloading.
