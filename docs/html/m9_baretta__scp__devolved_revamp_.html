M9 Baretta (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The M9 Baretta is a secondary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Site Security at level 2. Its decent damage is hampered by the gun's slower firerate overall. It also shares the same stats as the EF Model 5.
