MAGA hat

A MAGA hat is a hat displaying the text "Make America Great Again" or "MAGA." The most popular form is the official red hat from the Trump Store available <>, with the words "MAKE AMERICA GREAT AGAIN" in the front, the American flag on the right, and "TRUMP" on the back, costing $50.00 <>. Other variations, such as black, gray, blue, or camoflauge, do exist in both official and non-official capacities on sites such as Amazon.
