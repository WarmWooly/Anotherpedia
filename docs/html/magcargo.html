Magcargo

<><>{{bMagcargo}} is a [[magma]] [[snail]] [[Fire|fire type (pokémon)]] and [[Rock type|Rock type (pokémon)]] [[Pokémon|Pokémon (fictional species)]] from the [[second generation|Generation II (Pokémon)]].<><>Magcargo is reddish-orange or bright red with a gray shell and a pair of yellow eyes. The area around its foot is covered in boiling magma, while its face is adorned with thick drip-like structures extending past the mouth. The shell is comprised of multiple stone plates of varying shapes and sizes, with a hole in Magcargo's shell emitting a burning flame.&pMagcargo's official size is 2 [[ft|foot (unit)]] and 7 [[in.|inch]], while weighing 121.3 [[lbs|pound]]. [[Shiny|Shiny (Pokémon)]] Magcargo are magenta instead of red with a bronze shell.&pMagcargo's name comes from {{imagma}} and {{iescargot}} ([[French|French language]] for snail).<>Magcargo tends to inhabit [[volcanic craters|volcanic crater]], using the magma inside to sustain its body for thousands of years. Its shell, formed from its skin cooling, is quite brittle, and when disturbed, creates a hole for fire to erupt from. Magcargo's temperature idles around 18,000 °[[F|Fahrenheit]] (~6-7x as hot as normal magma), with [[water]] [[boiling]] instantly into [[vapor|water vapor]] on contact (such as Magcargo being in the [[rain]]).<>A Magcargo has a 50/50 chance to be [[male]] or [[female]]. Magcargo is the 219th Pokémon in the [[Pokédex|Pokédex (Pokémon)]] and has the Pokédex color red.&pMagcargo [[evolves|evolution (pokémon)]] from [[Slugma|Slugma (Pokémon)]] at [[level|leveling (pokémon)]] 38.
