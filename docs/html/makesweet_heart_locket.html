MakeSweet Heart Locket

The MakeSweet Heart Locket is a customizable animation on MakeSweet of a red heart-shaped locket. Text and/or images can be added into the animation to cover the white blank space. The animation shows the heart locket open up, revealing the two blank spaces. The animation has since become a meme as an odd method to express love for a person, character, or object. Within memes, the heart locket also serves as a juxtaposition to the content within, such as a disliked figure, an offensive message, or a random gag.
