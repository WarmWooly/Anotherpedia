Mangrove Apple (Bits & Blocks)

The mangrove apple is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that spawns a smackable entity. The mangrove apple can be crafted with 1 beach ball, 1 Jungle Mixtape, and 1 apple (shapeless). When used, the mangrove apple will be placed. If the player is sprinting when used, the apple will be thrown. When the mangrove apple is struck, it will be flung in the direction the attacker is facing. While it does have 20 health, it heals itself fully every tick. To kill the mangrove apple, the attack would need to do 20 or more damage at once. The mangrove apple will also float in water and lava.

The mangrove apple is immune to fire, fall damage, cacti, drowning, lightning, potion effects, anvils, dragon breath, and Wither. If the mangrove apple crams or suffocates, it will instantly despawn and drop itself.

The mangrove apple can be picked up by sneaking while interacting with it.
