Mareep Poké Plush

The Mareep Poké Plush is a life-size Pokémon stuffed animal of the Pokémon Mareep under the Poké Plush plush collection. The plush could be purchased on Pokémon Center for $499.99 USD, but is out of stock <>. The plush is composed of several sewn-together pieces with embroidered eyes, mouth, and nostril. Mareep's wool is made of a different material compared to the rest of the body. The plush stands at 2 ft. tall (including tail).
