Mareep Poké Plush

The Mareep Poké Plush is a life-size Pokémon stuffed animal of the Pokémon (fictional species) Mareep under the Poké Plush product collection. The plush could be purchased on Pokémon Center for $499.99 United States dollar, but is out of stock <>. The plush is composed of several sewing pieces with embroidery eyes, mouth, and nostril. Mareep's wool is made of a different material compared to the rest of the body. The plush stands at 2 foot (unit) tall (including tail).
