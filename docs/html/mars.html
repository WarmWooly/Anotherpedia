Mars

<><>{{bMars}} is the 4th [[planet]] from the [[Sun]] in the [[Solar System]]. Mars is on average 1.5 [[AU]] away from the Sun. Mars has two natural satellites, known as [[Phobos]] and [[Deimos]]. A day on Mars is 24 [[hours|hour]] and 37 [[minutes|minute]]. It takes 687 [[days|day]], or roughly 669.7 Mars days, for Mars to [[orbit]] around the Sun.
