Mars

Mars is the 4th planet from the Sun in the Solar System. Mars is on average 1.5 AU away from the Sun. Mars has two natural satellites, known as Phobos and Deimos. A day on Mars is 24 hour and 37 minute. It takes 687 day, or roughly 669.7 Mars days, for Mars to orbit around the Sun.
