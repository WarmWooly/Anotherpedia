MartinSugar2k intro

The MartinSugar2k intro is a meme based on the intro to various videos of the YouTuber/Twitch streamer MartinSugar2k. The intro is also used on his Twitch profile. While the intro had started to be used in 2022, it was picked up across Instagram and TikTok in 2025, poking fun at the odd way the intro was animated in terms of driving away new viewers. The intro consists of a purple disk spinning around, before MartinSugar2k's avatar sitting in a chair and holding a PS5 controller. It then zooms in on the avatar as it crudely shakes its arms and the controller, before more effects are overlaid and MartinSugar2k's name appears.
