Massachusetts Commissioner of Education

The commissioner of the Massachusetts Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (abbreviation) is, <>, Pedrio Martinez (DESE commissioner), who was brought in on July 1, 2025. The commissioner is the highest rank in the department. The commissioner is responsible for administrating DESE, working on policy of the department, creating grant and reform, overseeing the budget, collaberation with other government agency and public figure, regulating legal compliance, and recording data and making report of DESE.
