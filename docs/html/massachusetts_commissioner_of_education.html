Massachusetts Commissioner of Education

The commissioner of the Massachusetts Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (DESE) is, <>, Pedro Martinez, who was brought in on July 1, 2025.<> The commissioner is the highest rank in the department. The commissioner is responsible for administrating DESE, working on policies of the department, creating grants and reforms, overseeing the budget, collaberating with other government agencies and public figures, regulating legal compliance, and recording data and making reports of DESE.
