Mechanical Drill (Mindustry)

A Mechanical Drill is a building in Mindustry on Serpulo that is used to extract early-game resources. The description of the Mechanical Drill is the following:When placed on ore, outputs items at a slow pace indefinitely. Only capable of mining basic resources. It has 160 health, taking 0.1 seconds to build, while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 12 Copper. It can store up to 5 liquid units and 10 items. The following resources can be harvested with the drill:
Coal - 0.085 seconds per tile (0.34 sec fully covered)
Copper - 0.09 seconds per tile (0.36 sec fully covered)
Lead - 0.09 seconds per tile (0.36 sec fully covered)
Sand - 0.1 seconds per tile (0.4 sec fully covered)
Scrap - 0.1 seconds per tile (0.4 sec fully covered) The Mechanical Drill can be boosted with Water, where 3 units of Water a second grants a 2.56x speed boost. There are no prior things to research before the Mechanical Drill. It costs 12 Copper to research it.
