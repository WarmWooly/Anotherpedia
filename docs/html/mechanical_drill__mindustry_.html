Mechanical Drill (Mindustry)

A Mechanical Drill is a building (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) that is used to extract early-game resources. The description of the Mechanical Drill is the following:When placed on ore, outputs items at a slow pace indefinitely. Only capable of mining basic resources. It has 160 health (Mindustry), taking 0.1 build time (Mindustry), while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 12 copper (Mindustry). It can store up to 5 liquid unit (Mindustry) and 10 item (Mindustry). The following resources can be harvested with the drill:
Coal (Mindustry) - 0.085 seconds per tile (0.34 sec fully covered)
Copper (mindustry) - 0.09 seconds per tile (0.36 sec fully covered)
Lead (mindustry) - 0.09 seconds per tile (0.36 sec fully covered)
Sand (mindustry) - 0.1 seconds per tile (0.4 sec fully covered)
Scrap (mindustry) - 0.1 seconds per tile (0.4 sec fully covered) The Mechanical Drill can be boosted with Water (Mindustry), where 3 units of Water a second grants a 2.56x speed boost. There are no prior things to research (Mindustry) before the Mechanical Drill. It costs 12 Copper to research it.
