Medkit (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Medkit is the primary tool within SCP: Devolved Revamp for the Paramedic Team, but can also be used by other teams to heal players who are hurt. PT members of higher levels gain +5 HP per use per level (with a max of 30 HP at level 5). Any Facility players can heal any other Foundation player, but can't heal unauthorized Class Ds or Chaos Insurgency members. Any Class D or Chaos Insurgency can heal CD and CI.
