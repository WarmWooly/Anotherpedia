Medkit (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The Medkit is the primary tool within SCP: Devolved Revamp for the Paramedic Team (SCP: Devolved Revamp), but can also be used by other teams to heal player (roblox) who are hurt. PT members of higher levels gain +5 HP per use per level (with a max of 30 HP at level 5). Any Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp) players can heal any other Foundation player, but can't heal unauthorized Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp) or Chaos Insurgency (SCP: Devolved Revamp) members. Any Class D or Chaos Insurgency can heal CD and CI.
