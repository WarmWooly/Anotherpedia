Meet & Tweet (Bits & Blocks)

Meet & Tweet is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Meet & Tweet can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 baked potato, and 1 feather (shapeless). Meet & Tweet is used in the following crafting recipes:
Canary Feather - 1 Meet & Tweet and 1 yellow dye (shapeless).
Sky Mixtape - 1 Meet & Tweet, 1 mixtape case, 1 B-Bot and the Fly Girls, 1 Flow Worms, and 1 Steady Bears (shapeless).
