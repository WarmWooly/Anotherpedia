Meet & Tweet (Bits & Bops)

Meet & Tweet is the seventh minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a canary conversing with a wren. The minigame has the canary responding to the wren after it speaks. The player must hit Button 1 based on the number of chirps given. If the wren chirps twice, then the player immediately responds the next beat, but if the wren chirps three times, then the player waits a beat before responding. At the start of the level, a guide is shown below with Bop's head with the tweet patterns. As the birds converse, speech bubbles appear above showing their conversation in the form of drawings.

If the player is on time, dark lines will be emitted from the canary. If they are slightly off, the lines will be thinner and lighter. Being really off will cause the canary to screech, either making the wren confused (too early/late) or angry (completely off). If there is no input, the wren will also be angry.
