Mekanism (Minecraft mod)

{{bMekanism}} is a Minecraft mod made by bradyaidanc where players can create machines and mechanisms to automate resource collection, items such as jetpacks to increase movement capabilities, and fluids, gasses, and energy. Mekanism is available for 1.20.1, 1.19.2, 1.19.1, 1.19, 1.18.2, 1.16.5, 1.16.4, 1.16.3, 1.16.1, 1.15.2 1.12.2, 1.12.1, 1.11.2, 1.10.2, and 1.7.10. Main pages: {{aiMekanism Additions (aiMinecraft mod), aiMekanism Generators (aiMinecraft mod), and aiMekanism Tools (aiMinecraft mod)}}&spThere are 3 mods that expand upon Mekanism: Mekanism Additions, Mekanism Generators, and Mekanism Tools. Mekanism Additions adds small general additions, such as baby mobs, balloons, and obsidian TNT. Mekanism Generators adds new single block and multiblock generators, alongside new liquids and gasses. Mekanism Tools adds paxels and new tool materials, such as lapis and osmium.
