Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The melder is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to meld item (Minecraft) together. The melder can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 rose gold ingot (not lazy chainmail) and 5 cobblestone (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with rose gold, fill the rest of the grid besides the middle with cobblestone). Crafting a melder will award the advancement (Minecraft) Melding Pot (Not Lazy Chainmail).
