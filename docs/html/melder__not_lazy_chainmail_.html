Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The melder is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to meld items together. The melder can be crafted with 3 rose gold ingots and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with rose gold, fill the rest of the grid besides the middle with cobblestone). Crafting a melder will award the advancement Melding Pot. The melder requires an industrial belt to run, with better belts increasing the speed the melder can meld.
