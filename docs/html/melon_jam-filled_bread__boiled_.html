Melon Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled)

Melon jam-filled bread is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Melon jam-filled bread can be crafted with 1 melon jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Melon jam-filled bread can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
