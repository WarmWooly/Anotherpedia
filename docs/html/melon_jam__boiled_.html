Melon Jam (Boiled)

Melon jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Melon jam can be obtained by boiling a melon slice in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Melon jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Melon Jam-Filled Bread - 1 melon jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Melon jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
