MelonGod

MelonGod is an Undertale/Deltarune YouTuber and Twitch streamer. MelonGod is especially known for their modded playthroughs, many specifically made for MelonGod. They are represented with their own OC of Frisk, stylized with a red and green striped shirt, brown hair with a watermelon hair clip, red-dyed face framing layers, red pupils, and reddish cheeks, which they use as their VTuber model. Most of their YouTube videos are stream highlights from Twitch. They also have a second channel for Minecraft content, MelonGod77.
