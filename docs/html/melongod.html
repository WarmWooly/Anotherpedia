MelonGod

MelonGod is an Undertale/Deltarune YouTuber and Twitch streamer. They were born on October 31, 2007, making them <> years old. MelonGod is especially known for their Undertale modding playthrough, many specifically made for MelonGod. Most of their YouTube videos are stream highlights from Twitch (streaming service). They also have a second channel for Minecraft content, MelonGod77.
