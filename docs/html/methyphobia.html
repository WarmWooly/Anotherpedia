Methyphobia

<>{{tFor the fear of drinking alcohol, see [[potophobia]].}}&sp<>{{bMethyphobia}} is the [[irrational]] [[fear]] of being around [[alcohol]] or [[alcohol intoxication]]. Those who are methyphobic may experience [[anxiety]] when encountering alcoholic beverages or intoxication, even when doing so would not [[harm]] the individual. [[Therapy]] and [[anxiolytics|anxiolytic]] can be used to reduce the effects of methyphobia.
