Methyphobia

For the fear of drinking alcohol, see potophobia.&spMethyphobia is the irrational fear of being around alcohol or alcohol intoxication. Those who are methyphobic may experience anxiety when encountering alcoholic beverages or intoxication, even when doing so would not harm the individual. Therapy and anxiolytic can be used to reduce the effects of methyphobia.
