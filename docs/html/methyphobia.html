Methyphobia

For the fear of drinking alcohol, see potophobia.
Methyphobia is the irrational fear of being around alcohol or alcohol intoxication. Those who are methyphobic may experience anxiety when encountering alcoholic beverages or intoxication, even when doing so would not harm the individual. Therapy and anxiolytics can be used to reduce the effects of methyphobia.
