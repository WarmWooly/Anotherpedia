Mickey-shaped pumpkin

For the decorative pumpkins, see Mickey Mouse pumpkin.
A Mickey-shaped pumpkin is a pumpkin that is grown in a mold in the shape of Mickey Mouse's head. This causes the pumpkin to have two lumps extrude upwards from the round body to fill the mold, creating "ears" on the pumpkin. Mickey-shaped pumpkins are produced in The Land pavilion at Epcot within Disney World, which can be viewed during the Behind the Seeds Tour attraction.
