Microaggression

<><>A {{bmicroaggression}} is an [[insult]], [[invalidation]], or [[offensive behavior]] against a [[person]]. These are often [[conflated|conflate]] with [[actions|action]] that may be well-meaning in nature, but turn out to be harmful to an individual. The term originated in 1970 by [[Chester M. Pierce]] as seen toward [[African Americans|African American]], but it has since spread to include any slight against [[marginalized groups|marginalized group]].
