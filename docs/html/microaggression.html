Microaggression

A {{bmicroaggression}} is an insult, invalidation, or offensive behavior against a person. These are often conflate with action that may be well-meaning in nature, but turn out to be harmful to an individual. The term originated in 1970 by Chester M. Pierce as seen toward African American, but it has since spread to include any slight against marginalized group.
