Microtransactions in Voidpet Dungeon

Microtransactions in {{iVoidpet Dungeon}} are prevalent due to the free-to-play nature of the game. The game has a premium currency, gems, which are difficult to obtain through normal gameplay. Gems are used for many operations to speed up progress, such as purchasing eggs for more Voidpets, immediately collecting resources from the chest, and getting keys to obtain gear or EXP. A one-time starter boost is also purchasable, increasing daily rewards for the first week. Specific items can also be bought immediately with money. An additional track and increased rewards for the battle pass may also be purchased.
