Microtransactions in Voidpet Dungeon

microtransaction in {{iVoidpet Dungeon}} are prevalent due to the free-to-play nature of the mobile video game. The game has a premium currency, gems (Voidpet Dungeon), which are difficult to obtain through normal gameplay. Gems are used for many operations to speed up progress, such as purchasing eggs (Voidpet Dungeon) for more Voidpet (fictional species), immediately collecting resources from the chest (Voipet Dungeon), and getting keys (Voidpet Dungeon) to obtain gear (Voidpet Dungeon) or Bottled EXP (Voidpet Dungeon). A one-time starter pack (microtransaction) is also purchasable, increasing daily rewards for the first week. Specific items (Voidpet Dungeon) can also be bought immediately with money. An additional tracks (Voidpet Dungeon) and increased rewards for the battle pass (Voidpet Dungeon) may also be purchased.
