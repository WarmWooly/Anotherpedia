Microtransactions in Voidpet Garden

Microtransactions in {{iVoidpet Garden}} are prevalent due to the free-to-play nature of the app. The app has a premium currency, gems, which are rarely earned through normal gameplay, and are used to buy higher-quality items and more storage for Voidpets. A one-time starter pack is also purchasable, giving large quantities of rare items and cosmetics. Additionally, a subscription called "Voidpet GIGA" can be bought on a monthly or annual scale, giving more Voidpet storage, increased access to the journal, more item drops, and an increased texting limit.
