Mill Stone (Better Than Wolves)

<resistance|No||Buoyancy|Sinksinfo>>The mill stone is a mechanical block in the Minecraft mod Better Than Wolves used in milling items. It is the first machine the player will create that uses mechanical power. A mill stone can be crafted with 1 gear and 8 stone bricks (place the gear in the center bottom slot and fill the remaining grid with bricks). Upon obtaining the mill stone, the achievement On the Grind will be awarded. A mill stone can be powered using a hand crank placed on top or with a powered axle on the top or bottom of the mill stone. Items can be manually inserted or pushed in with a powered hopper. Up to a stack of one item may be input at a time, and output items will automatically pop out of a random side of a mill stone. Inserted items can be retrieved by right-clicking the mill stone. To prevent items from being lost, have a water stream or eight hoppers surrounding the mill stone.
