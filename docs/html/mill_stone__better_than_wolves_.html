Mill Stone (Better Than Wolves)

<>The mill stone is a mechanical block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Better Than Wolves used in milling items. It is the first machine the player will create that uses mechanical power (Better Than Wolves). A mill stone can be crafting (Better Than Wolves) with 1 gear (Better Than Wolves) and 8 stone bricks (Better Than Wolves) (place the gear in the center bottom slot and fill the remaining grid with bricks). Upon obtaining the mill stone, the Better Than Wolves achievements On the Grind (Better Than Wolves) will be awarded. A mill stone can be mechanical power (Better Than Wolves) using a hand crank (Better Than Wolves) placed on top or with a powered axle (Better Than Wolves) on the top or bottom of the mill stone. Items can be manually inserted or pushed in with a powered hopper (Better Than Wolves). Up to a stack of one item may be input at a time, and output items will automatically pop out of a random side of a mill stone. Inserted items can be retrieved by right-clicking the mill stone.
