Milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<><>{{bMilling}} is a process to turn [[items|item (Minecraft)]] into [[flour|flours (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]] with a [[millstone|millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]] in the {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|mod (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Bread Reborn: Redefined]]}}. Milling can be manually performed by a [[player|player (Minecraft)]] or automated with [[redstone|redstone circuits (Minecraft)]]. While the normal [[millstone|millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]] can only process one item at a time, the [[powered|powered millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]] and [[refined millstones|refined millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined)]] can mill 4 and 16 at a time when powered.
