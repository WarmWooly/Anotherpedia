Milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Milling is a process to turn items into flour with a millstone in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Milling can be manually performed by a player or automated with redstone. While the normal millstone can only process one item at a time, the powered and refined millstones can mill 4 and 16 at a time when powered. Milling an item will award the advancement Mill Away!<>
