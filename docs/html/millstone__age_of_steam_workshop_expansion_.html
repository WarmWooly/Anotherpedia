Millstone (Age of Steam Workshop Expansion)

The millstone is a multiblock in the Minecraft mod Age of Steam Workshop Expansion used to mill items. It takes in mechanical power to run the millstone. A millstone can be crafted with 1 casing, 1 wooden axle, and 4 fences (place the axle in the middle, the casing below, and the fences to the left/right). To assemble the millstone, it will require the millstone, 2 wooden axles, and 6 stone slabs. Place the axles to connect to the central axle and lay the stone slabs along the side of the millstone. Upon completing the assembly, either by right-clicking the millstone or waiting for the game to update, the millstone will be formed. To run a millstone, it must have sufficient mechanical power from an axle connected from either end. The millstone can store 18 items to be processed at a time.
