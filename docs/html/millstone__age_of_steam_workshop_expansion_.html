Millstone (Age of Steam Workshop Expansion)

<>The {{bmillstone is a multiblock (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Age of Steam Workshop Expansion used to mill items. It takes in mechanical power (Age of Steam) to run the millstone. A millstone can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 casing (Age of Steam), 1 wooden axle (Age of Steam), and 4 fence (Minecraft) (place the axle in the middle, the casing below, and the fences to the left/right). To assemble the millstone, it will require the millstone, 2 wooden axle (Age of Steam), and 6 stone slab (Minecraft). Place the axles to connect to the central axle and lay the stone slabs along the side of the millstone. Upon completing the assembly, either by right-clicking the millstone or waiting for the game to update, the millstone will be formed. To run a millstone, it must have sufficient mechanical power (Age of Steam) from an axle connected from either end. The millstone can store 18 item (Minecraft) to be processed at a time.
