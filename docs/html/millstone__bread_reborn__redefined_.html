Millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>The {{bmillstone}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iBread Reborn: Redefined}} used in milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) items. It is the lowest tier of millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined). A millstone can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 andesite slab (Minecraft), 1 granite slab (Minecraft), 2 smooth stone slab (Minecraft), and 2 stick (Minecraft) (place the andesite in the center, the granite below, a stick above, the smooth stone in the bottom corners, and a stick in the top-left corner). Millstones are used in the following crafting recipes:&spPowered Millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined) - 1 millstone, 2 iron ingot (Minecraft), 2 redstone dust (Minecraft), and 1 redstone torch (Minecraft) (place the millstone in the center, the torch below, an iron ingot above, the dust in the bottom corners, and an iron ingot in the top-left corner). {{tMain page: Milling ({{aiBread Reborn: Redefined}})}}&spTo use the millstone, the item to be processed must be held in the main hand (Minecraft). Upon interacting with the millstone, the held item will be consumed, the output will be given to the player, and the millstone will rotate 45°. Manually milling will slowly deplete hunger (Minecraft). When trying to process an item that can't be milled, the message "This item can't be milled!" will appear. The millstone can be mute when placed on wool (Minecraft). With an open hand, the millstone may be turned, going counter-clockwise while sneaking (Minecraft).
