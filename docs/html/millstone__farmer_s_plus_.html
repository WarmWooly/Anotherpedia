Millstone (Farmer's Plus)

<>The millstone is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Farmer's Plus used to mill items. A millstone can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 grindstone (Minecraft) and 1 stone slab (Minecraft) (place the grindstone above the slab). Up to a stack of item (Minecraft) can be inserted into a millstone. To mill, the player must hold down on the millstone. Ground items will then pop out to be collected. When trying to process an item that can't be milled, the message "This doesn't seem millable..." will appear.
