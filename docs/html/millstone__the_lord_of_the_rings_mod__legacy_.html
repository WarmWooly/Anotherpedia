Millstone (The Lord of the Rings Mod: Legacy)

<>The millstone is a utility block in the Minecraft mod The Lord of the Rings Mod: Legacy used to mill items. A millstone can be crafted with 1 iron/bronze ingot, 1 blackroot/mallorn/vanilla stick, and 7 cobblestone (place the stick in the center, the ingot above, and surround with cobblestone).
