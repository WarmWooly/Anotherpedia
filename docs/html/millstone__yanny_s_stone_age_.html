Millstone (Yanny's Stone Age)

<>The millstone is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Yanny's Stone Age used to mill items. A millstone can be crafting (Minecraft) in a flint crafting table (Yanny's Stone Age) with 4 stick (Minecraft), 3 smooth stone slab (Minecraft), 1 granite (Minecraft), and 1 andesite (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with slabs, the left and right columns with sticks, the granite in the middle, and the andesite above; use a flint knife (Yanny's Stone Age) to complete). To open a millstone, sneaking (Minecraft) while using it. The millstone can hold up to a stack of input items at a time. To mill, continue using the millstone until the input items are processed.
