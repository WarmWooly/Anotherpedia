Millstone (Yanny's Stone Age)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The millstone is a block in the Minecraft mod Yanny's Stone Age used to mill items. A millstone can be crafted in a flint crafting table with 4 sticks, 3 smooth stone slabs, 1 granite, and 1 andesite (fill the bottom row with slabs, the left and right columns with sticks, the granite in the middle, and the andesite above; use a flint knife to complete). Millstones are used in the following flint crafting recipes:
Crafting Table - 1 millstone, 1 stone chest, 1 stone axe, 1 stone pickaxe, 3 wooden slabs, 1 bread, and 1 wool (place the bread in the center, the millstone to the left, the chest to the right, the wool above, the slabs in the bottom row, the pickaxe in the top-left corner, and the axe in the top-right corner; use a flint knife to complete). To open a millstone, sneak while using it. The millstone can hold up to a stack of input items at a time. To mill, continue using the millstone until the input items are processed.
