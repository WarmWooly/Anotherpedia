Millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>The millstones are a type of block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined used in milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) items. They come in three tiers: the millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined), the powered millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined), and the refined millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined). All three millstones can be crafting (Minecraft), with the previous tier utilized in its successor's recipe. The advancement (Minecraft) Mechanized Milling (Bread Reborn: Redefined) and Milling Mastery (Bread Reborn: Redefined) are awarded for crafting the powered millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined) and refined millstone (Bread Reborn: Redefined) respectively. Main page: Milling ({{aiBread Reborn: Redefined)}}&spTo use a millstone, the item to be processed must be held in the main hand (Minecraft). Upon interacting with a millstone, the held item will be consumed, the output will be given to the player, and the millstone will rotate 45°. If the powered or refined millstone is powered, up to 4/16 items will be processed at a time. Manually milling without power will slowly deplete hunger (Minecraft). When trying to process an item that can't be milled, the message "This item can't be milled!" will appear. A millstone can be mute when placed on wool (Minecraft). With an open hand, a millstone may be turned, going counter-clockwise while sneaking (Minecraft). When a powered or refined millstone is powered, the millstone will rotate once, processing up to 4/16 Item (Minecraft entity) above it. If the block below it is open, the produced flours (Bread Reborn: Redefined) will fall through; otherwise, it will pop above the millstone. This can be used to automate the milling process by constantly rotating the millstone and allowing items to fall into a hopper (Minecraft).
