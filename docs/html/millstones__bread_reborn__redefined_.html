If the powered or refined millstone is powered, up to 4/16 items will be processed at a time. Manually milling without power will slowly deplete [[hunger|hunger (Minecraft)]]. When trying to process an item that can't be milled, the message "This item can't be milled!" will appear. A millstone can be [[muted|mute]] when placed on [[wool|wool (Minecraft)]].
