Minecraft

{{bMinecraft}} is a video game that was developed by Mojang Studios, which was released in 2009. Originally created by Markus Persson in Java, it has grown to be the best-selling game, with 300 million purchases of the game and becoming one of the largest franchises. Minecraft is considered a 3D sandbox game, allowing players to make their own goals, giving it a large range of versatility. It has a dedicated multiplayer scene and modding scene that keep Minecraft's gameplay fresh while new updates are being developed. <>, it is on update 1.21.4. There are two distinct versions, Java Edition (coded in Java, runs on computers) and Bedrock Edition (coded in C++, runs on most devices, including computers).
