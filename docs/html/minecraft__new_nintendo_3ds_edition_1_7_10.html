Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition 1.7.10

Not to be confused with the update {{aiMinecraft: Java Edition 1.7.10.}}&sp1.7.10 was a major update to Minecraft: New Nintendo 3DS Edition on September 19 and 20, 2018 following 1.6.11 and preceding 1.8.13. It brought the Ender Update, including The End, End Cities, and other End content (excluding shulker boxes, which would appear in 1.9.19). Stability and performance were improved as well. New blocks related to The End were added, including end stone, end stone bricks, end portals, end gateway blocks, the dragon egg, the dragon head, chorus plants, chorus flowers, purpur blocks (and it's varients purpur pillars, purpur slabs, and purpur stairs), end rods, and ender chests. Items was also added which were related to The End, including ender pearls, eyes of ender, dragon breath, lingering potions, chorus fruit, popped chorus fruit, shulker shells, the elytra, and spawn eggs for the endermite, shulker, and polar bear.&pThe wmobs the ender dragon, endermites, shulkers, and polar bears were added alongside these blocks and items. There was also the new dimension The End and structures such as the end city and it's end ship, the exit portal for The End, and the feature chorus plants.&pThere were several new achievements, which are Beam Me Up, Great View From Up Here, Super Sonic, The End., The End?, The End... Again..., The Haggler, and You Need a Mint.
