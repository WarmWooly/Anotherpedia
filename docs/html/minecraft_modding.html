Minecraft modding

For the similar alterations to {{aiMinecraft through the Minecraft marketplace, see aiMinecraft add-ons.}}&sp{{iMinecraft modding}} is the video game modding of the video game Minecraft, adding or updating mechanics, features, and processes of the base game. Mods can add new item (Minecraft), block (Minecraft), mob (Minecraft), and dimension (Minecraft), tweak or improve existing features, video game optimizations for performance, and full conversion mod the gameplay of Minecraft. There have been thousands of mods made, with the modding scene continuing to grow, and some of the most prominent mod makers making a profit off their mods. There are also multiple Minecraft mod loader to search for, compile, and run Minecraft modpack. While Mojang Studios has not released an API for mod making, various community tools (such as Blockbench, Bukkit, and MCreator) have been made to aid and assist the modding process. Modding has even influenced additions added to Minecraft itself, including horse (Minecraft) originally from DrZhark Mo' Creatures<> and piston (Minecraft) created by Hippoplatimus and DiEvAl.<><>
