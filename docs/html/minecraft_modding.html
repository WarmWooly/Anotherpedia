{{iMinecraft}} modding

<>{{tFor the similar alterations to {{aiMinecraft}} through the [[marketplace|Minecraft marketplace]], see [[{{aiMinecraft}} add-ons]].}}&sp<>img>>{{b{{iMinecraft}} modding}} is the [[modding|video game modding]] of the [[game|video game]] {{i[[Minecraft]]}}, adding or updating mechanics, features, and processes of the base game. Mods can add new [[items|item (Minecraft)]], [[blocks|block (Minecraft)]], [[mobs|mob (Minecraft)]], and [[dimensions|dimension (Minecraft)]], tweak or improve existing features, [[optimize|video game optimizations]] for performance, and [[fully convert|full conversion mod]] the gameplay of {{iMinecraft}}. There have been thousands of mods made, with the modding scene continuing to grow, and some of the most prominent mod makers making a [[profit]] off their mods. There are also multiple [[mod loaders|Minecraft mod loader]] to search for, compile, and run [[modpacks|Minecraft modpack]]. While [[Mojang Studios]] has not released an [[API]] for mod making, various community tools (such as [[Blockbench]], [[Bukkit]], and [[MCreator]]) have been made to aid and assist the modding process. Modding has even influenced additions added to {{iMinecraft}} itself, including [[horses|horse (Minecraft)]] originally from [[Dr. Zhark's|DrZhark]] {{i[[Mo' Creatures]]}}<> and [[pistons|piston (Minecraft)]] created by [[Hippoplatimus]] and DiEvAl.<><>
