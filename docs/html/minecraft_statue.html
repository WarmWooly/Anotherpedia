A {{iMinecraft statue}} is a statue constructed to represent content from Minecraft, most often mob (Minecraft) or skin (Minecraft) of player (Minecraft). Statues can be made as a tribute to the game as a whole, a specific mob, or to a person in player form. It can be used as decoration for personal or public display, such as within Minecraft attractions.
