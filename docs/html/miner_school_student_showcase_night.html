Miner School Student Showcase Night

The Miner School Student Showcase Night (also referred to as the CS Student Project Showcase) was an event at the University of Massachusetts Lowell on April 2nd, 2026, from 5:00pm to 7:00pm. The event was hosted at University Crossing (University of Massachusetts Lowell). The event is a collaboration between the Cloud Computing Club (University of Massachusetts Lowell), Association for Computing Machinery (University of Massachusetts Lowell), Cyber Security Club (University of Massachusetts Lowell), AI&ML, Open-Source Club (University of Massachusetts Lowell), and GameDev Club (University of Massachusetts Lowell).

The first hour of the event was dedicated to the senior director of Red Hat, Rashid Khan (Red Hat), alongside co-op and other employee. The second hour was college student-led booth presentation-based project presentation, where students showed off their project to get votes, with the top 5 projects presented awarded prize. Anotherpedia was also at this event!
