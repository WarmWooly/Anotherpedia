Miner School Student Showcase Night

The Miner School Student Showcase Night (also referred to as the CS Student Project Showcase) was an event at the University of Massachusetts Lowell on April 2nd, 2026, from 5:00pm to 7:00pm. The event was hosted at University Crossing. The event is a collaboration between the Cloud Computing Club, ACM, Cyber Security Club, AI&ML, Open-Source Club, and GameDev Club.

The first hour of the event was dedicated to the senior director of Red Hat, Rashid Khan, alongside co-ops and other employees. The second hour was student-led booth-based presentations, where students showed off their projects to get votes, with the top 5 projects presented awarded prizes. Anotherpedia was also at this event!
