Minigame Stand (Bits & Blocks)

The minigame stand is a block in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks used to display the minigame items. The minigame stand can be crafted with 4 sticks (place sticks along the bottom row and middle slot). The minigame stand can hold any of the minigame items, which will be visibly displayed.
