Minigames (Bits & Bops)

Minigames are the primary type of level in Bits & Bops. Each minigame has a different control scheme and characters present, using one or two inputs. After every four minigames, a mixtape combines them all under a single song, with the Final Mixtape including some part of every minigame. When a minigame is beaten, it will get a sticker in the top-right corner (Bop when skipped, blue circle for Cool, green star for Amazing, yellow crown for Perfect, and blue crown for Diamond Perfect). Getting Cool gives a souvenir and unlocks the subsequent level, Amazing gives a record of the song, and Perfect gives a video of the player's completion.
