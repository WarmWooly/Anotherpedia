Mixtape Stand (Bits & Blocks)

The mixtape stand is a block in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks used to display the mixtape items. The mixtape stand can be crafted with 1 glass pane, 2 sticks, and 1 wooden plank (place the glass in the middle, the planks below, and the sticks in the bottom corners). The mixtape stand can hold any of the mixtape items, which will be visibly displayed. The mixtapes will remain animated in the stands.
