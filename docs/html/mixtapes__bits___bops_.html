Mixtapes (Bits & Bops)

For custom mixtapes, see Custom Mixtapes (Bits & Bops).
Mixtapes are the secondary type of level in Bits & Bops, combining multiple minigames together. The first four mixtapes combine the previous minigames together, with an additional mixtape at the end containing all the minigames. The first four mixtapes also provide a set theme, with the minigames present retextured to match the theme. When a mixtape is beaten, it will get a sticker in the top-right corner (Bop when skipped, blue circle for Cool, green star for Amazing, yellow crown for Perfect, and blue crown for Diamond Perfect). Getting Cool gives a souvenir and unlocks the subsequent level, Amazing gives a record of the song, and Perfect gives a video of the player's completion.
