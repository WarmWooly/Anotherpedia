Molar mass

<><>{{bMolar mass}}, represented as [[M|Abbreviation]], is the [[measurement]] of the [[density]] of a [[compound]], measured in [[kg|kilogram]]/[[mol|mole (unit)]] (or [[g|gram]]/mol for smaller use cases). Molar mass is only the [[average]] [[mass]] of a compound due to unaccounted [[isotopes|isotope]]. Molar mass can be used to find how many [[atoms|atom]] an [[object]] contains based on the moles of the substance and the object's [[mass]].
