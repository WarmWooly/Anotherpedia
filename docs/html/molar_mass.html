Molar mass

{{bMolar mass}}, represented as Abbreviation, is the measurement of the density of a compound, measured in kilogram/mole (unit) (or gram/mol for smaller use cases). Molar mass is only the average mass of a compound due to unaccounted isotope. Molar mass can be used to find how many atom an object contains based on the moles of the substance and the object's mass.
