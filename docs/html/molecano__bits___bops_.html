Molecano (Bits & Bops)

Molecano is the twelfth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a mole riding a handcar with a partner. The minigame has the player controlling the cart based on the other mole's actions. The player must hit Button 1 to match their partner's pace, with the signal "one-two" for normal pace and "one-two-three-four" for double time. When a whistle blows, the player must hit Button 2 when their partner lands to stop the handcar. Throughout the level, the scene will change between a calm cave and the inside of an active volcano. The lighting during the cave sections will also change, with some of the screen obscured around the moles.

If the player lands on time, a three-lobed effect will appear. If they are slightly off, it will only be two-lobed. Being really off will cause the mole to flounder in mid-air and fall. If there is no input, the mole will also fall. Missing the break will cause the mole to fall over.
