Month

A month is a length of time used for calendar, originally based on the lunar cycle of the Moon (~29.5 day). It is now fixed between 28 and 31 days depending on the month, with a total of 12 named months in a year in the Gregorian calendar: January (31), February (28/29), March (31), April (30), May (31), June (30), July (31), August (31), September (30), October (31), November (30), and December (31).
