Moon

<><>{{tFor other moons, see [[natural satellite]]. For other terms relating to moon, see [[moon (disambiguation)]].}}&spThe {{bMoon}} is the [[natural satellite]] that [[orbits|orbit]] [[Earth]]. The Moon is, [[on average|lunar distance]], 238,855 [[miles|mile]] (384,400 [[km|kilometer]]) from Earth, making a complete revolution every 27.3 [[days|day]].<> It has a [[radius]] of 1,080 miles (1,740 km), making it less than a third as wide as Earth.<> The Moon undergoes distinct [[phases|lunar phase]] based on the positions of the Moon, Earth, and the [[Sun]], with an average cycle lasting 29.5 days.
