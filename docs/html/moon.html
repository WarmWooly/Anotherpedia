Moon

For other moons, see natural satellite. For other terms relating to moon, see moon (disambiguation).&spThe Moon is the natural satellite that orbits Earth. The Moon is, on average, 238,855 miles (384,400 km) from Earth, making a complete revolution every 27.3 days.<> It has a radius of 1,080 miles (1,740 km), making it less than a third as wide as Earth.<> The Moon undergoes distinct phases based on the positions of the Moon, Earth, and the Sun, with an average cycle lasting 29.5 days.
