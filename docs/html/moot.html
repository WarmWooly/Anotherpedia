Moot

For other terms relating to moot, see moot (disambiguation).&spMoot is a word that can be used both as a verb and an adjective. Something that is moot is something that should be debated or contested, such as in an argument, or that is no longer relevant because it is either hypothetical or unimportant. Something that moots is a thing that has started a debate about another thing or has made a claim that another thing is irrelevant..
