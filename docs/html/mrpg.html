mRPG

<><>{{bmRPG}} was a [[texting|text messaging]]-based [[virtual tabletop]] (VTT) [[mobile app]] for playing [[tabletop role-playing games|tabletop role-playing game]] such as {{i[[Dungeons & Dragons]]}} that allowed for users to send texts, [[images|image]], [[dice rolls|dice simulation]], [[macros|macro (computer science)]], and [[character sheets|character sheet]]. mRPG was started in 2018 by [[Adriano Cola]] and shut down at the end of 2024.<> <>[[Felipe Fabel]] was also noted as one of the "Creators of mRPG" in their shutting down message on the mRPG app. In 2022, the development of mRPG 2Ed (second edition) was announced,<> overhauling the app's design and features with the promise of cross compatability for [[desktop|desktop app]], finishing and launching the mobile app changes the following year. The desktop app was never released to the public.&p<>Various features of mRPG made it notable and useful as a lightweight VTT, mainly dice rolling and a character management system. Rolling dice was done through a custom-built function system that allowed for any number and size of dice to be rolled, to run [[functions|function (computer science)]] on the results, and to display text and [[emoji]] outcomes based on programmed criteria. Campaigns could be openly advertised to all users of the app, having filters for the system used, [[language]] spoken, the [[maturity|content maturity]] of the content, and various other aspects. The character system allowed players to chat as their characters and keep track of their stats and [[inventory|inventory (mechanic)]] on customizable and shareable character sheets. The [[game master]] would also have access to chat as a "GM" or as a spontaneously created [[NPCs|Non-player character]].
