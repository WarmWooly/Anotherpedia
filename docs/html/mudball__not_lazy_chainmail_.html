Mudball (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The mudball is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) or breaking down mud (Minecraft). Mudballs can be crafting (Minecraft) from mud (Minecraft), yielding 4 mudballs. When mud (Minecraft) is crushed in a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail), it will yield 4 mudballs. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain mudballs. Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of mudballs from sieving.<> 4 mudballs can be crafting (Minecraft) in a 2x2 square to make mud (Minecraft). Mudballs are used in the following Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail) recipes:
Muddy Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 mudballs and 1 leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)
Slimeball (Minecraft) x2 - 1 mudball, 1 clay ball (Minecraft), and 1 lime dye (Minecraft) Main page: Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
When held in the offhand, mudballs can be launched from a Chain Launcher (Not Lazy Chainmail). When the mudball hits a mob, it is given Slowness (Minecraft) and Blindness (Minecraft) for 5 seconds. If an Axolotl (Minecraft) is hit with a launched mudball, the advancement (Minecraft) i herd u liek mudballz (Not Lazy Chainmail) is awarded.
