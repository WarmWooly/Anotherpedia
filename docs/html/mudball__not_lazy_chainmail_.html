Mudball (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The mudball is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving or breaking down mud. Mudballs can be crafted from mud, yielding 4 mudballs. When mud is crushed in a Crusher, it will yield 4 mudballs. There are multiple sievable blocks and levels to obtain mudballs. Separation can increase the chance/drops of mudballs from sieving.<> 4 mudballs can be crafted in a 2x2 square to make mud. Mudballs are used in the following melding recipes:
Muddy Leather Padding - 2 mudballs and 1 leather padding
Slimeball x2 - 1 mudball, 1 clay ball, and 1 lime dye Main page: Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}
When held in the offhand, mudballs can be launched from a chain launcher. When the mudball hits a mob, it is given Slowness and Blindness for 5 seconds. If an Axolotl is hit with a launched mudball, the advancement i herd u liek mudballz is awarded.
