Muddy Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Muddy leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Muddy leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +1 Muddiness and -0.01 Movement Speed. Muddiness causes mobs hit by the wearer to be given Blindness for 2 * Muddiness seconds. Muddy leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 2 mudballs. In a leathersmithing table, muddy leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each muddy leather padding adds +1 Muddiness and -0.01 Movement Speed, with a full set of armor with 12 muddy leather paddings giving +12 Muddiness and -0.12 Movement Speed. Shields will only add Muddiness and remove Movement Speed when held in the main hand or offhand.
