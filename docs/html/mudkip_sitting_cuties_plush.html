Mudkip Sitting Cuties Plush

The Mudkip Sitting Cuties Plush is a small Pokémon stuffed animal of the Pokémon Mudkip under the Sitting Cuties plush collection in the fourth wave on January 30, 2021. The plush can be purchased on Pokémon Center for $16.99 USD <>. The plush is composed of several sewn-together pieces with embroidered eyes and nostrils.
