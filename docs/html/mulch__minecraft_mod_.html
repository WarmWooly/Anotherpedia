Mulch (Minecraft mod)

{{iMulch}} is a Minecraft mod (Minecraft) made by Warm_Wooly in MCreator on August 21, 2024, adding mulch (Mulch), mulch slabs (Mulch), wood chips (Mulch), wood chip mix (Mulch), wood pellets (Mulch), wood pulp (Mulch), a wood chipper (Mulch), and a mulch redefiner (Mulch). Mulch is available for Minecraft: Java Edition 1.20.1 for Forge (Minecraft mod loader).
