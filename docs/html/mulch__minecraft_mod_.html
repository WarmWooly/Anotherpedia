Mulch (Minecraft mod)

Mulch is a Minecraft mod made by Warm_Wooly in MCreator on August 21, 2024, adding mulch, mulch slabs, wood chips, wood chip mix, wood pellets, wood pulp, a wood chipper, and a mulch redefiner. Mulch is available for 1.20.1 for Forge.<><>&sp
