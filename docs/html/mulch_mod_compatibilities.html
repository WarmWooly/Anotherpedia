Mulch mod compatibilities

The Minecraft mod Mulch has many mod compatibilities, primarily with its ten additional mulch types for Biomes o'Plenty and additional chipping recipes for items from other mods. The following are the mod compatibilities based on the type of item: The following items can be crafted with mulch with Biomes o'Plenty:
Dead Planks x8 - 4 dead mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Fir Planks x8 - 4 fir mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Hellbark Planks x8 - 4 hellbark mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Jacaranda Planks x8 - 4 jacaranda mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Magic Planks x8 - 4 magic mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Mahogany Planks x8 - 4 mahogany mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Palm Planks x8 - 4 palm mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Redwood Planks x8 - 4 redwood mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Umbran Planks x8 - 4 umbran mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Willow Planks x8 - 4 willow mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square). Most mod compatibilities include additional chipping recipes to obtain wood chips.
