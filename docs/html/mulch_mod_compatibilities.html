Mulch mod compatibilities

The Minecraft Minecraft modding Mulch (Minecraft mod) has many Minecraft mod compatibility, primarily with its ten additional mulch (mulch) types for Biomes o'Plenty and additional wood chipper (Mulch) recipes for item (Minecraft) from other mods. The following are the mod compatibilities based on the type of item: The following items can be crafting (Minecraft) with mulch (Mulch) with Biomes o'Plenty:
Dead Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 dead mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Fir Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 fir mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Hellbark Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 hellbark mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Jacaranda Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 jacaranda mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Magic Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 magic mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Mahogany Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 mahogany mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Palm Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 palm mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Redwood Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 redwood mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Umbran Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 umbran mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square).
Willow Planks (Biomes o'Plenty) x8 - 4 willow mulch (place the mulch in a 2x2 square). Most mod compatibilities include additional wood chipper (Mulch) recipes to obtain wood chips (Mulch).
