Music

{{bMusic}} is the use of sound from vocal and/or instrument to express emotion to the listener. Music is defined by what instruments it uses, the lyric within it, the rhythm (music), melody, and harmony (music). Music may be separated into different genre based on the message or the form (music) of the music. Music is considered to be a cultural universal; an everlasting form of expression among human.
