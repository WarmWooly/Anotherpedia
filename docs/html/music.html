Music

<><>{{bMusic}} is the use of [[sound]] from [[vocals|vocal]] and/or [[instruments|instrument]] to [[express]] [[emotion]] to the [[listener]]. Music is defined by what instruments it uses, the [[lyrics|lyric]] within it, the [[rhythm|rhythm (music)]], [[melody]], and [[harmony|harmony (music)]]. Music may be separated into different [[genres|genre]] based on the [[message]] or the [[form|form (music)]] of the music. Music is considered to be a [[cultural universal]]; an everlasting form of expression among [[humans|human]].
