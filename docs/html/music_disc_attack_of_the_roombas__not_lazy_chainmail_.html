Music Disc Attack of the Roombas (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The music disc "Attack of the Roombas" is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the seven music discs composed by Warm_Wooly added in Not Lazy Chainmail. The song was originally created for Attack of the Roombas before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Iron Music Disc". The disc takes 32 iron chain links to craft. Crafting the music disc "Attack of the Roombas" will award the advancement Musically Inclined. Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be used to craft the Music Disc "Neon" by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust. Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be crushed into unfinished iron chainmail projects containing 32 chain links. When right-clicked into a jukebox, the disc will play "Attack of the Roombas". The song has a duration of 1:45.
