Music Disc Attack of the Roombas (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bmusic disc "Attack of the Roombas"}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} and one of the seven music discs (Not Lazy Chainmail) composed by Warm_Wooly added in {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}}. The song was originally created for {{iAttack of the Roombas}} before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be created through Chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Iron Music Disc". The disc takes 32 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Crafting the music disc "Attack of the Roombas" will award the advancement Musically Inclined (Not Lazy Chainmail). Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be used to crafting (Minecraft) the Music Disc Neon (Not Lazy Chainmail) by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust (Not Lazy Chainmail). Music disc "Attack of the Roombas" can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished iron chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 32 iron chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail). When right-clicked into a jukebox (Minecraft), the disc will play "Attack of the Roombas". The song has a duration of 1:45.
