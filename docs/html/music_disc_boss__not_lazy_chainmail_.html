Music Disc Boss (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Yesinfo>>The music disc "Boss" is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the seven music discs composed by Warm_Wooly added in Not Lazy Chainmail. The song was originally created for Attack of the Roombas before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Boss" can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Netherite Music Disc". The disc takes 32 netherite chain links to craft. Crafting the music disc "Boss" will award the advancement Musically Inclined. Music disc "Boss" can be used to craft the Music Disc "Neon" by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust. Music disc "Boss" can be crushed into unfinished netherite chainmail projects containing 32 chain links. When right-clicked into a jukebox, the disc will play "Boss". The song has a duration of 2:05. gal
