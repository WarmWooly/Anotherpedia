Music disc "Company" can be used to crafting (Minecraft) the Music Disc Neon (Not Lazy Chainmail) by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust (Not Lazy Chainmail). Music disc "Company" can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished copper chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 32 copper chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail).
