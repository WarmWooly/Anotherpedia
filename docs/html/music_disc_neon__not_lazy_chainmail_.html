Music Disc Neon (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bmusic disc "Neon"}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} and one of the seven music discs (Not Lazy Chainmail) composed by Warm_Wooly added in {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}}. The song was originally created for {{iNeon Breakout (Warm_Wooly)}} before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Neon" can be crafting (Minecraft) by surrounding any disc made through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with 4 chromatic dust (Not Lazy Chainmail). Crafting this music disc will award the advancement Chromatic Chords (Not Lazy Chainmail). When right-clicked into a jukebox (Minecraft), the disc will play "Neon". The song has a duration of 3:40.
