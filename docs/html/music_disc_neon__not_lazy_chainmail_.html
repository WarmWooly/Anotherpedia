Music Disc Neon (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The music disc "Neon" is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the seven music discs composed by Warm_Wooly added in Not Lazy Chainmail. The song was originally created for Neon Breakout before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Neon" can be crafted by surrounding any disc made through maille crafting with 4 chromatic dust. Crafting this music disc will award the advancement Chromatic Chords. When right-clicked into a jukebox, the disc will play "Neon". The song has a duration of 3:40.
