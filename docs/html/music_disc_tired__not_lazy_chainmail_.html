Music Disc Tired (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The Music Disc "Tired" is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the seven music discs (Not Lazy Chainmail) composed by Warm_Wooly added in Not Lazy Chainmail. The song was originally created for Tired (Warm_Wooly) before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Tired" can be created through Chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Rose Gold Music Disc". The disc takes 32 rose gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. Crafting the music disc "Tired" will award the advancement Musically Inclined (Not Lazy Chainmail). Music disc "Tired" can be used to crafting (Minecraft) the Music Disc Neon (Not Lazy Chainmail) by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust (Not Lazy Chainmail). Music disc "Tired" can be Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) into unfinished rose gold chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) containing 32 rose gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail). When right-clicked into a jukebox (Minecraft), the disc will play "Tired". The song has a duration 4:02.
