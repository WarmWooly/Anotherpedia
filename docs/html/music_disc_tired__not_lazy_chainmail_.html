Music Disc Tired (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The Music Disc "Tired" is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the seven music discs composed by Warm_Wooly added in Not Lazy Chainmail. The song was originally created for Tired before being repurposed for the mod. Music disc "Tired" can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Rose Gold Music Disc". The disc takes 32 rose gold chain links to craft. Crafting the music disc "Tired" will award the advancement Musically Inclined. Music disc "Tired" can be used to craft the Music Disc "Neon" by surrounding the disc with 4 chromatic dust. Music disc "Tired" can be crushed into unfinished rose gold chainmail projects containing 32 chain links. When right-clicked into a jukebox, the disc will play "Tired". The song has a duration 4:02.
