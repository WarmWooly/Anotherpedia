Music Discs (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>info>>There are 7 unique music discs in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that can be obtained through crafting or maille crafting. All of the music discs were composed by Warm_Wooly.<> The music disc "Neon" can be crafted by surrounding any disc made through maille crafting with 4 chromatic dust. Crafting this music disc will award the advancement Chromatic Chords. Music discs (besides "Neon") can be created through maille crafting with the setting "Music Disc". Music discs take 32 chain links to craft. Maille crafting a music disc will award the advancement Musically Inclined. Music discs (besides "Neon") can be crushed into unfinished chainmail projects containing 32 chain links.
