My Superhero Movie

"{{bMy Superhero Movie}}" is a song from the 2018 film {{iTeen Titans Go! To the Movies}} played by Jacob Jeffries Band, with Jacob Jeffries singing the song as Robin (Teen Titans Go!). The song in the movie conveys Robin's desire to star in his own movie, where he would be able to rake in the spotlight and audience attention while also highlighting Robin's egotistical tendencies. The internet meme It would be so awesome, it would be so cool originates from the first lyric of "My Superhero Movie".
