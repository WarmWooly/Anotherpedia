Natural

<>{{tFor other terms related to natural, see [[natural (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>{{bNatural}} is used to describe [[things|thing]] which come from [[nature]]. Natural is often used as an [[antonym]] for [[synthetic]]. Natural things are not made by [[humans|human]] (besides [[biological processes]] that occur within the human body). The term can also be used for things not made by humans outside of [[Earth]], such as [[stars|star]].
