Natural Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Natural leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Natural leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +1 Photosynthesis. Photosynthesis will cause applied items to slowly regain durability in the day while the sky is visible. Natural leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding, 1 loose stone, and 1 moss block. In a leathersmithing table, natural leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each natural leather padding adds +1 Photosynthesis, with a max of +3 Photosynthesis per item. Shields will only gain the benefits of Photosynthesis when held in the main hand or offhand. Every tick the sky is directly above the player during the day, there is an xs-b/200 chance for a durability point to be restored, where x = Photosynthesis. Photosynthesis only works on the applied item.
