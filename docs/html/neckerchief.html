Neckerchief

{{tNot to be confused with bandana.}}&spA {{bneckerchief}} is an article of clothing worn around the neck. It is often used for Ceremony purposes. They have a standard size of 27 inch. A neckerchief is often associated with Boy Scouts of America and other relevant programs. A neckerchief's design is based on each Troop (Boy Scouts of America) and is not required on the official uniform. These neckerchiefs are able to be used as a triangular bandage in emergency situations, and Scouts are taught how to use them for injuries such as Broken Bone and Burn (injury).
