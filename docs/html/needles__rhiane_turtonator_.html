Needles (Rhiane Turtonator)

Needles is an original character based on Smiling Friends created by Rhiane Turtonator who is posted to various social media, including DevientART, Twitter, and Reddit. Needles' most prominent appearance was within the YouTube video "SMILING FRIENDS ( fan animation )". Needles is a red critter (Smiling Friends) with humanoid arms, legs, and body. There is a noticable dent between Needles' neck and head. He is often seen in a blue hoodie with a backwards baseball cap and pants, but he may also wear more formal wear. Needles is shown working Frowning Friends Inc. (Smiling Friends) alongside Pins (Rhiane Turtonator), Eric (Rhiane Turtonator), and Anti-Boss (Rhiane Turtonator).
