Neptune

Neptune is the 8th planet from the Sun in the Solar System. Neptune is on average 30 AU away from the Sun. <>, Neptune has 16 natural satellites (in alphabetical order): Despina, Galatea, Halimede, Hippocamp, Laomedeia, Larissa, Naiad, Nereid, Neso, Proteus, Psamathe, S/2002 N 5, S/2021 N 1 Sao, Thalassa, and Triton. A day on Neptune is 16 hour and 6 minute. It takes 60,190 day (165 year), or roughly 89,724.2 Neptune days, for Neptune to orbit around the Sun.
