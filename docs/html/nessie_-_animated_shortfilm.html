NESSIE - animated shortfilm

"NESSIE - animated shortfilm" is a short film animation released onto YouTube by Maru-i8q on March 27, 2026. It was animated in RoughAnimator on an iPad. The video includes Jack Stauber's song, "Estranger (Jack Stauber)". It is 2 minutes and 48 seconds in length. A 12-year-old girl, Moko, is shown to be visibly boredom in the classroom before the school bell rings. Moko becomes overwhelmed by the noise of the other student and leaves school to go to the beach. While descending into the ocean, she opens her eyes to a large jellyfish with a person inside, who sticks their hand out to Moko. She flees to the shore, for the blue-skinned girl in the jellyfish (Nessie) to wash up shortly after. Moko sand drawing to try and communicate with Nessie, but they both end up drawing together. The following day, Moko is holding a star (shape) from Nessie in class.
