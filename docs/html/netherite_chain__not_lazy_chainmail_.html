Netherite Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The netherite chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The netherite chain can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Netherite Chain". The chain takes 8 netherite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft.
