Netherite Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The netherite chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The netherite chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Netherite Chain". The chain takes 8 netherite chain links to craft. The netherite chain can be crafted with a block of netherite chains (makes 4).
