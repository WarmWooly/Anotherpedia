Netherite Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Speed|x16info>>The netherite chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Netherite chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Netherite Industrial Belt". The netherite chainmail industrial belt takes 256 netherite chain links to craft. Netherite chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chainsword - 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt, 1 netherite sword, 1 crude engine, and 2 iron composite ingots (place the sword in the middle, the iron composite to the bottom and left, the belt in the top-right corner, and the engine in the bottom-left corner).
Netherite Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 netherite chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). Winding Jig Netherite chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished netherite chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Netherite chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining netherite chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Netherite chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Netherite chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on netherite chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels.
