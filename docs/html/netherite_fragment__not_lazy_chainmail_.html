Netherite Fragment (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The netherite fragment is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving or breaking down netherite scrap that is used in crafting. Recycling 27 netherite chain links will return a netherite fragment. When netherite scrap is crushed in a Crusher, it will yield 3 netherite fragments. Netherite fragments can be collected by sieving Soul Sand/Soil with an iron composite sieve mesh (1% chance). Separation can increase the chance/drops of netherite fragments from sieving.
