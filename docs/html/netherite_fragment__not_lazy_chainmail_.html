Netherite Fragment (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The netherite fragment is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) or breaking down netherite scrap (Minecraft) that is used in crafting (Minecraft). Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail) 27 netherite chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) will return a netherite fragment. When netherite scrap (Minecraft) is crushed in a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail), it will yield 3 netherite fragments. Netherite fragments can be collected by sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) Soul Sand (Minecraft)/Soul Soil (Minecraft) with an iron composite sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (1% chance). Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of netherite fragments from sieving. Netherite fragments are used in the following crafting recipes:
Netherite Nunchucks (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 4 netherite fragments, 1 netherite tool chain (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 obsidian (Minecraft) (place the fragments in the corners, the obsidian to the left and right, and the tool chain on the bottom).
Netherite Scrap (Minecraft) - 4 netherite fragments (place in a 2x2 square).
Netherite Spiked Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 3 netherite fragments, 3 netherite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 3 gold nugget (Minecraft) (gold nuggets for the top row, fragments for the middle, and carpets on the bottom).
Netherite Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 3 netherite fragments, 3 netherite chain conveyors (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 3 gold nugget (Minecraft) (gold nuggets for the top row, fragments for the middle, and conveyors on the bottom).
