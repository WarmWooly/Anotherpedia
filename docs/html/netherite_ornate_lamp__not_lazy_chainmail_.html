Netherite Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The netherite ornate lamp is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used as redstone (Minecraft)-powered light (Minecraft). The netherite ornate lamp can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 netherite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 redstone lamp (Minecraft) (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the netherite ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the netherite ornate lamp takes 0 tick (Minecraft) to turn off. gal
