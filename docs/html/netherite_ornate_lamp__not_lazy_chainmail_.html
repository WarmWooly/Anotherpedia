Netherite Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bnetherite ornate lamp}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as redstone (Minecraft)-powered light (Minecraft). The netherite ornate lamp can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 netherite chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 redstone lamp (Minecraft) (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below).
