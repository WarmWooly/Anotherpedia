New Year's Day

For other celebration, see list of New Year's celebrations.
New Year's Day or New Year is an annual holiday celebrated on the Gregorian calendar January 1st each year following the change in the calendar era. The holiday is closely paired with New Year's Eve, a celebration the day prior on December 31st for the upcoming New Year. It is celebrated with gathering friend and family, feast, and spending time together.
