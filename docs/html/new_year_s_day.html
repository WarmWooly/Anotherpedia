New Year's Day

For other celebrations, see list of New Year's celebrations.
New Year's Day or New Year is an annual holiday celebrated on the Gregorian January 1st each year following the change in the year's number. The holiday is closely paired with New Year's Eve, a celebration the day prior on December 31st for the upcoming New Year. It is celebrated with gathering friends and family, feasting, and spending time together.
