Newton's flaming laser sword

For the song, see Newton's Flaming Laser Sword (song).&spNewton's flaming laser sword, also known as Alder's razor, describes a philosophical razor created by Mike Alder in Philosophy Now. The razor is closely related to Newtonianism. It is titled flaming laser sword to describe how it is sharper than Occam's razor. The full quote for it is the following:It is often boiled down to this:The razor removes impossible questions that are not worth debating. This includes questions such as "If a tree falls in a forest and nobody hears it, did it make a sound?", because there is no way to observe it, making it useless to talk about.
