Nibble (computing)

<><>A {{bnibble}} or {{bnybble}} is a [[unit of information]] comprised of four [[bits|bit]], being half a [[byte]] (with the name being a play on having a [[nibble]] instead of a full [[bite]]). A nibble can hold up to sixteen values (2{{s-u4}}), with one nibble having enough space for a single [[digit]] in [[hexadecimal]] or a [[binary-coded decimal]].
