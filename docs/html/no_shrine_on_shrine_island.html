No shrine on Shrine Island

"No shrine on Shrine Island" is a quote from the YouTuber skittybitty during their video essay "I HATE TEARS OF THE KINGDOM". The quote is a jab at the lack of a shrine of light (The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom) on Shrine Island (The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom) from The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild to its sequel The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, as the former had a shrine which was removed in the latter. During the essay, it was made to show how shrines in the game did not feel as thoughtfully placed in their opinion, especially leaving an island deliberately named "Shrine Island" without a shrine.

Following the video essay, it would become a internet meme among content creator who played The Legend of Zelda games and for skittybitty's audience. This change is also noted on Zelda Wiki and Zelda Dungeon when describing the island in Tears of the Kingdom. A follow-up video, "THE TRUTH ABOUT SHRINE ISLAND", would be made by skittybitty covering more context behind the naming of Shrine Island and other similarly-named locations, making a possible connection to Spirit Island (The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass) from The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass and discussing their personal experience with the meme. skittybitty's island in Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream would be named "Shrine Island" in reference to the quote.
