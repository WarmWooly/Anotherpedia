Normal (Laser Fight)

Normal is a mode (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game) where nothing is changed mechanically. Normal is also the most likely mode to appear as an option while voting. Players will spawn in with the Basic Gun (Laser Fight) and can hold up to 3 guns. Guns will gun spawning (Laser Fight) at their normal rates.
