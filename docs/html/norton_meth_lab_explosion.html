Norton meth lab explosion

In Norton, Massachusetts, in an apartment on 3 Faith Way exploded on February 22nd, 2020 around 12:53 AM. This explosion killed one person, Edward Rooney.

Police and firefighters responded to the event, but when they got there, the fire had subsided. Instruments for cooking methamphetamine were located inside the apartment where the explosion occurred. Rooney had fled the scene. Residents from the neighboring town Attleboro called the police about a man begging for help, which was Rooney. Rooney allegedly was let into their home, before shortly collapsing. He was rushed to the Sturdy Memorial Hospital, where he died at 2:38 AM. Residents in the apartment and other nearby buildings were evacuated to a community center.
