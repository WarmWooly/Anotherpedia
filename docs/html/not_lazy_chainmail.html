Not Lazy Chainmail

Not Lazy Chainmail is a Minecraft mod made by Warm_Wooly in MCreator on July 23, 2024, giving an alternative method to obtain Chainmail, alongside new tools, blocks, and advancements. Not Lazy Chainmail is available for 1.20.1 for Forge. Not Lazy Chainmail requires Curios API. The alternative mod, Lazy Chainmail, is a bare-bones version of Not Lazy Chainmail.
