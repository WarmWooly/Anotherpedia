Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.10

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.1.10}} was a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on May 17th, 2025, that updates copper-related and curio textures. This update follows 1.1.9 and precedes 1.1.11.
