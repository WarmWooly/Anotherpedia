Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.11

Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.11 was a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on August 6th, 2025, that updates gold and netherite-related textures. This update follows 1.1.10 and preceeds 1.1.12.
