Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.12

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.1.12}} was a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on August 9th, 2025, that adds the sculkifier and sculk sieving. This update follows 1.1.11 and precedes 1.1.13. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:;;A new Cobblefier, the Sculkifier, has been added.|Added the placeholder item Experience.;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;The advancement Cobblestone is Everything now requires 52 Cobblefiers instead of 51.|Sculk can now be sieved.|The texture for the Cobblestone Generator has been updated.|Leather paddings on Shields now applies all effects in the main hand as well as the offhand.|When manually sieving, both Luck and Bad Luck are used in calculations, instead of Luck overriding Bad Luck.;;
