Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.13

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.1.13}} was a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on August 11th, 2025, that adds new bright spools and updates the textures of normal spools. This update follows 1.1.12 and precedes 1.2.0. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:;;Added bright variations of normal spools, including the Bright Empty Spool, Bright Iron Spool, Bright Gold Spool, Bright Netherite Spool, Bright Rose Gold Spool, and Bright Iron Composite Spool.;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Bright spools can now be smoked back to normal.|The following blocks/items can now be used as fuel: Empty Spool, Bright Empty Spool, Spool Block, Bright Spool Block, Winding Jig, Conveyor Manager, Item Aggregator, Barrel Collector, Expanded Barrel Collector, Leathersmithing Table, Machine Dampener, and Chainmail Project.|Textures for normal spool items updated (including Empty Spool, Iron Spool, Gold Spool, Copper Spool, Netherite Spool, Rose Gold Spool, and Iron Composite Spool).|Texture for Chromatic Dust updated.|Added Forge tags for rose gold.;;
