Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.3

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.1.3}} was an update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on November 30th, 2024 for leathersmithing. This update follows 1.1.2 and precedes 1.1.4. These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;The iron chainmail bracelet now increases Resistance applied based on the number of chainmail armor pieces worn.|The iron composite chainmail bracelet now increases hunger restored based on the number of iron composite chainmail armor pieces worn.|Reduced netherite chainmail necklace burn time to 10 seconds, but the time increases based on the number of netherite chainmail armor pieces worn.|The rose gold chainmail gloves now increase the heal chance based on the number of rose gold chainmail armor pieces worn.|The gold chainmail belt now increases Speed applied based on the number of gold chainmail armor pieces worn.|Improved the tooltip for the rose gold chainmail belt.|Fixed mutliple gloves/belts not working together when more curio slots are added by other mods. Multiple curios of the same type do not stack effects.|Using a sieve mesh on a sieve dropper now swaps the stored sieve mesh with the one held.|Using a cobblefier on a cobblestone generator now swaps the stored cobblefier with the one held.;;
