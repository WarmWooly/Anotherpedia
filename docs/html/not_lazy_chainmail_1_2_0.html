Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.0

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.0}} was an update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on August 16, 2025. This update added new leather paddings and advancements. This update follows 1.1.13 and precedes 1.2.1. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:&sp;;Fourteen new Leather Paddings were added: Healthy, Swift, Aquadynamic, Constructive, Destructive, Elevating, Weighted, Inflated, Rocky, Muddy, Natural, Hellish, Vivacious, and Draconic.|Four new advancements were added: i herd u liek mudballz, Nethender Portal, Sticks and Stones, and Shower, NOW!;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Texture for Attractive Leather Padding updated.|Texture for Rose Gold Ingot and Rose Gold Nugget updated.|Texture for Block of Iron Composite and textures based on it (Auto Winder, Auto Snipper, and Auto Mailler) updated.|Texture for Winding Jig updated.|Texture for Conveyor Manager updated.|The attribute "Attraction" is now visible when applied to items.;;
