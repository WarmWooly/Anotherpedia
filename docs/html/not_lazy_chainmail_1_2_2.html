Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.2

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.2}} is a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on December 12th, 2025, that adds dynamic textures for the chain launcher and coils. This update follows 1.2.1 and precedes 1.2.3. These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Chainmail launchers can now use all types of chains, blocks of chains, and the bright spool block for crafting.|The type of materials used to craft a chain launcher will be reflected on its texture.|Coils will have their texture visibly decrease when snipping.;;
