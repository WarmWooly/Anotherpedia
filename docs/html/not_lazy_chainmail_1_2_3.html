Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.3

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.3}} is a minor Not Lazy Chainmail updates to the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail released on December 19th, 2025, that adds new leather paddings (Not Lazy Chainmail) and the telebelt (Not Lazy Chainmail). This update follows Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.2 and precedes Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.4. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:
