Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.3

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.3}} is a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on December 19th, 2025, that adds new chain covers and the telebelt. This update follows 1.2.2 and precedes 1.2.4. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:
;;6 new chain covers (leather padding subtype): Iron, Gold, Copper, Netherite, Rose Gold, and Iron Composite.|New Telebelt (takes on the old Iron Composite Chainmail Belt functionality).;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Iron Composite Chainmail Belts will now destroy all Stone, Deepslate, and Lava in a 3-block radius.|Swift Leather Padding has a new texture and is melded with 1 Leather Padding, 1 Sugar, and 1 Feather.|Ascension Rune now has an enchanted glint.|Iron Composite Chainmail Gloves will no longer allow the player to "ride" non-creature entities to prevent crashes (i.e. primed TNT, end crystals).;;
