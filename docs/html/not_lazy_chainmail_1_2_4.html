Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.4

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.4}} is a minor update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on January 5th, 2026, that adds a new rune scraper and 2 new runes. This update follows 1.2.3 and precedes 1.2.5. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:
;;New Rune Scraper item to remove rune enchantments from items.|New runes: Dampen Rune and Muffle Rune.;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Snipping and sieving will no longer have chance-based durability loss.|Maille crafting will no longer have chance-based hunger loss.|Winding now requires at least 1 shank of hunger to perform and consumes hunger.|Pliers durability increase: 64 --> 640.|Sieve mesh durability increase: 64 --> 192.|When quick-swapping a Cobblefier from a Cobblestone Generator, only one will be inserted.|Item Aggregator is now 4x as fast.|Belt Generators and Chain Generators now have the option to output power at a rate of 64 FE/t and 1,000 FE/t, respectively.|Belt Generators are now crafted with a Block of Iron and 2 (Bright) Empty Spools instead of 2 Iron Ingots.|Fixed the improper title in the Telebelt's menu.|Updated the advancement Nethender Portal to use the Telebelt as its icon.|Updated the display models for the Winding Jig, Auto Winder, Conveyor Warper, and Chain Launcher.|New Chainmail Project texture.|Wires, Spools, and the Chainmail Project now have dynamic textures.;;Fixed double sounds for the following:;;Blocks: Auto Snipper, Auto Mailler, Auto Winder, Barrel Collector, Belt Generator, Cobblestone Generator, Conveyor Manager, Conveyor Warper, Crusher, Expanded Barrel Collector, Garbage Disposal, Haunter, Item Aggregator, Leathersmithing Table, Melder, Recycler, Sieve Dropper, Sort Dropper, Winding Jig|Items: Chain Launcher, Chainmail Project, Chainsaw, Chainsword, Curios, Flail, Lasso, Nunchucks, Pliers, Rope, Runes, Sieve Mesh, Vinegar Bottle, Whip;; The advancement Compulsive Decluttering was removed.
