Not Lazy Chainmail 1.2.6

{{iNot Lazy Chainmail 1.2.6}} is an update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on June 16th, 2026, that adds the experience accumulator. This update follows 1.2.5. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:
;;New block: Experience Accumulator|New advancements: All Mods Need Wrenches!; Off I Go, Apparently; Not Lazy Bottles;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;Updated the textures for the Crude Engine, Chainsaw, and Chainsword.|Updated and made the textures dynamic for the Belt Generator, Sieve Dropper, and Auto Mailler.|The Crude Engine now needs a Piston, craftable Industrial Belt, and an Empty Spool to meld.|The Belt Generator now needs 6 stone blocks, 2 Iron Ingots, and 1 Crude Engine to craft.|Pliers no longer take damage when picking up blocks.|Blocks picked up by Pliers will be immediately added to the inventory.|Removed cooldown for rotating blocks.|Non-chain blocks require Manipulating to rotate.|Swapped picking up blocks and rotating blocks while sneaking.|Made Reversed and Manipulating no longer mutually exclusive.|Material spool blocks and chains can now be rotated.|Hopper Droppers will no longer drop items while redstone powered.|Applying an Automated Permanence Rune will fully repair the item.|The advancement Cobblestone is Everything now only requires the player to obtain every Cobblefier.|Ropes now have a tooltip, can be picked up with an empty hand/rope, and can extend into water.|Ropes can no longer 'anchor' themselves on other ropes.|Fixed the Nethender Portal advancement text.|Fixed some spool blocks not being picked up with Manipulating.|Fixed the Rune Scraper not burning in fire/lava.|Fixed the Iron Composite Chainmail Bracelet showing double the restored hunger.|Fixed the singular form of the Iron Composite Chainmail Belt's tooltip not appearing correctly.|Added descriptions for Bleeding and Trailing.|Pliers and Sieve Meshes now make a breaking sound when fully used up.|Iron Composite Chainmail Armor no longer randomly adds Armor Toughness on upgrade.|Fixed the bounding boxes of chains.;;
