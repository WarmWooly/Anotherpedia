Not Lazy Chainmail guide

This is a guide for the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. As Not Lazy Chainmail is not required for the progression in Minecraft, this will be moreso an outline of goals to complete based on the current progression the player is at. Once the player obtains iron, they can interact with the most basic systems of Not Lazy Chainmail, fabricating a set of iron chainmail armor and curios. ;;Collect leather or dried kelp to make a leather industrial belt or a kelp industrial belt|Construct a winding jig and craft a wire|Wind the wire into a coil|Craft a pair of iron pliers to snip the coil into chain links|Craft a chainmail project (requires paper) to maille craft the chain links into armor|Maille craft an industrial belt for faster winding;; ;;Craft a chain launcher to move around with ease|Craft an iron sieve mesh to turn blocks into resources through sieving;; Once the player obtains gold and copper, they can open up a world of automation and improvements with rose gold. ;;Craft rose gold with copper and gold|Craft a melder to create new materials|Craft a crusher to process blocks and items|Meld a diamond, emerald, and amethyst shard to make a chromatic mass, then crush it into chromatic dust|Collect bedrock shards from loose bedrock at the bottom of the world;; ;;Craft chain conveyors to push around mobs and items|Meld a crude engine for several useful recipes|Craft a sort dropper to automate item sorting from conveyors|Use a hopper dropper to pull items from machines|Create a cobblestone generator and add cobblefiers to generate resources, which can be processed in a sieve dropper|Use a machine muffler or machine dampener to decrease the sound emitted from machines|Use a conveyor manager, garbage disposal, and barrel collector to improve automation setups|Make a chainsaw to cleave through trees|Meld bedrock shards together to make pseudorock to create runes, especially the automated permanence rune for automation|Craft a leathersmithing table to add leather padding to chainmail armor to upgrade them|Upgrade your pliers to rose gold pliers and your belt to a rose gold chainmail industrial belt|Craft a rose gold sieve mesh to harvest unique drops from blocks;; With chromatic dust and bedrock shards, the player can make iron composite to increase automation and unlock the best gear in the mod. ;;Automate winding, snipping, and maille crafting|Complete as many advancements as you can to power up the iron chainmail necklace|Make a complete set of netherite or iron composite chainmail armor and upgrade it with leather padding to become unstoppable;; ;;Use the conveyor warper and item aggregator to transcend the boundaries of conveyors with teleportation|Upgrade your barrel collector to an expanded barrel collector to increase automated storage space|Craft a chainsword to cleave through enemies, or meld a laceration rune to apply the effect onto other weapons|Upgrade your pliers to iron composite|Craft an iron composite sieve mesh to yet again unlock even better resource drops from blocks|With the steep cost of a block of iron composite and a nether star, meld an ascension rune to effectively fly with the chain launcher;;
