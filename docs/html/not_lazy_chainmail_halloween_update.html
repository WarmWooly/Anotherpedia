Not Lazy Chainmail Halloween Update

The {{iNot Lazy Chainmail Halloween Update}} (1.1.0) is an update to the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail released on October 31, 2024. This update added new blocks and weapons while also expanding on other mechanics. It follows 1.0.3 and precedes 1.1.1. These are all the new blocks, items, and other features added:
;;Four new Cobblefiers were added: Haunt'n'crimsonifier, Haunt'n'warpifier, Deep'n'crushifier, and Deep'n'crush'n'crushifier.|Added Unfinished Projects.|Added Loose Stone, Loose Deepened Stone, Mudball, Obsidian Shard, and Netherite Fragment.|Added Haunter block and haunting mechanic.|Added Deepened Gravel and Deepened Sand blocks.|Deepened Gravel can generate amongst Gravel at Deepslate depth.|Added Chromatic Mass.|Added Nunchucks.|Added Telemagnetic Alloy and the Item Aggregator block.|Added the Chainsaw.|Added Spool-based varients for the Bright Spool Block to match their Spool Block counterparts.;; These are all the items and features that were fixed, balanced, or updated:;;The advancement Cobblestone Is Everything now requires 51 Cobblefiers instead of 47 (for the 4 new Cobblefiers).|When prematurely ending a Chainmail Project, Chain Links will now be returned in the form of an Unfinished Project.|Pliers can no longer be used as Shears.|Copper Ornate Lamps and its varients no longer have shapeless crafting.|When winding a Wire or a Spool, the percentage is now a whole number (30.0% --> 30%) in its tooltip.|All drops from sieving have been overhauled.|A crusher bug, causing it to crush any item (and destroying it), has been fixed.|Chromatic Dust can no longer be melded, instead requiring Chromatic Mass to be crushed.|Chainsword damage increase (7 --> 9).|Lasso damage decrease (all now deal 0.5).|Whip damage decrease (-2 for Netherite and Iron Composite, -1 for the rest).|Flail and Lasso sounds were changed.|Chain Launcher can now launch other mobs.|Advancement We're Blasting Off Again! now requires a mob to be launched through hitting with a Vinegar-boosted Chain Launcher.|Attractive Padding now only activates while sneaking, has particles to show the collection range, and has an increased base range.|Ascension Rune now has unlimited uses.|Attractive Padding and Conveyor Warper now use Telemagnetic Alloy in their recipes.|Netherite Spiked Chain Carpet and Netherite Spiked Chain Conveyor now use Netherite Fragments in their recipes.|Wax On and Wax Off Minecraft advancements can now be obtained with Not Lazy Chainmail copper blocks.;; The Melder's Guide was removed and can no longer be obtained from the advancement Melding Pot.
