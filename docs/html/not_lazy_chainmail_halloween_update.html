Not Lazy Chainmail Halloween Update

The {{iNot Lazy Chainmail Halloween Update}} (1.1.0) is an Not Lazy Chainmail updates to the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail released on October 31, 2024. This update added new block (Minecraft) and weapon (Minecraft) while also expanding on other mechanics. It follows Not Lazy Chainmail 1.0.3 and precedes Not Lazy Chainmail 1.1.1.
