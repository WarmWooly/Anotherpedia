Not safe for work

<><>{{bNot safe for work}}, [[abbreviated|abbreviation]] as {{bNSFW}}, is any [[content]] which is considered [[taboo]] to such a degree the viewing of such material would violate [[workplace policies|workplace policy]], which may lead to being [[fired|firing]].
