Notepad

For device application that simulate notepads, see notepad (application).
A notepad or pocket notebook is a collection of paper which are paper binding that are primarily used for taking notes. Notepads come in varying shapes and sizes, from those that fit into pocket to larger sheets that are the size of notebook. Notepads may be bound with a spiral binding made of plastic or metal, a wire binding, or with an adhesive book binding. A chipboard is a common addition to the end of a notepad to give a solid surface for writing while held in the hand.
