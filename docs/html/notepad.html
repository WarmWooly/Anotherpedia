Notepad

For applications that simulate notepads, see notepad (application).
A notepad or pocket notebook is a collection of paper which are bound that are primarily used for taking notes. Notepads come in varying shapes and sizes, from those that fit into pockets to larger sheets that are the size of notebooks. Notepads may be bound with a spiral made of plastic or metal, a wire, or with an adhesive. A chipboard is a common addition to the end of a notepad to give a solid surface for writing while held in the hand.
