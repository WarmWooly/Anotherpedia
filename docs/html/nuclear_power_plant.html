Nuclear power plant

<><>A {{bnuclear power plant}} is a [[power generation facility]] that uses [[steam|Water Vapor]] to spin [[turbines|Turbine]] from [[boiling]] [[water]] using [[heat]] generated from [[nuclear fission]]. [[Nuclear reactors|Nuclear Reactor]] are not the same as nuclear power plants. A nuclear reactor is the [[device]] that controls nuclear fission, while a nuclear power plant is the building that uses the heat from a nuclear reactor to boil water.&p<>, there are 54 nuclear power plants in the [[USA|United States]].<> The worst [[failure]] of a nuclear power plant was at [[Chernobyl|Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant Meltdown]]. The most recent failure was at [[Fukushima|Fukushima Nuclear Power Plant Meltdown]].
