Nuclear power plant

A nuclear power plant is a power generation facility that uses steam to spin turbines from boiling water using heat generated from nuclear fission. Nuclear reactors are not the same as nuclear power plants. A nuclear reactor is the device that controls nuclear fission, while a nuclear power plant is the building that uses the heat from a nuclear reactor to boil water.&p<>, there are 54 nuclear power plants in the USA.<> The worst failure of a nuclear power plant was at Chernobyl. The most recent failure was at Fukushima.
