Nuclear power plant

A {{bnuclear power plant}} is a power generation facility that uses Water Vapor to spin Turbine from boiling water using heat generated from nuclear fission. Nuclear Reactor are not the same as nuclear power plants. A nuclear reactor is the device that controls nuclear fission, while a nuclear power plant is the building that uses the heat from a nuclear reactor to boil water.&p<>, there are 54 nuclear power plants in the United States.<> The worst failure of a nuclear power plant was at Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant Meltdown. The most recent failure was at Fukushima Nuclear Power Plant Meltdown.
