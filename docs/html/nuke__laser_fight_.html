Nuke (Laser Fight)

Nuke is a kill streak (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game) that kills all opponents instantly.<> When a Nuke kills a player, their death screen will say "Y's Nuke killed you!", with one of the following death messages appearing in the feed:;;X fought the sun made by Y|X evaporated because of Y|X didn't stand a chance against Y|X was irradiated by Y|X should've hid under a blanket to avoid Y|X was nuked by Y|X was wiped off the map by Y;;
