Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon

Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon is a Monster card in Yu-Gi-Oh!. Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon is a part of the Numbers, Galaxy, Galaxy-Eyes, Tachyon, and Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon archetypes. In the anime, this card was used by Mizael or Mizar as he was known in the English dub. Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon appeared in episodes 82, 83, 84, 87, 96, 97, 125, 127, 128, 130, 134, and 135 of the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL anime. This card did not appear in the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL manga. Effects differ from the card game and anime. This card has currently been through 3 erratum. This card's legality is unlimited in both the Original Card Game and Trading Card Game.
