Numbers Band

For the band, see The Numbers Band.
Numbers Band refers to a genre of videos on YouTube where numbers are animated to music. While there is no defined format, Numbers Band videos tend to have numbers occur in an increasing manner, with a different snippet of music per number. The numbers oscillate between two angles, colors, sizes, or other property. The numbers may be drawn or in the form of text. The name of the video often contains the numbers that will be included (i.e. 21--30). Numbers Band videos are made for children and may overlap with the style of object shows. The most notable creator of Numbers Band videos is sunny bot.
