Nutmeg (soccer)

<><>A {{bnutmeg}}, or {{bmeg}}, in [[soccer]] (association football), and occasionally in other [[sports|sport]], is a term for when a [[soccer ball]] or other object is [[passed|pass (soccer)]] between another player's legs. The main advantage of performing a nutmeg is circumventing the need to go around or over an opponent, saving time and space in close-quarter situations. This [[dribbling|dribbling (soccer)]] skill is not only beneficial when pulled off, but also seen as very impressive. Failing a nutmeg, however, can allow the opponent to take control of the ball and break free.
