Obsidian Shard (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The obsidian shard is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving or breaking down obsidian. When obsidian or crying obsidian is crushed in a Crusher, it will yield 4 obsidian shards. Obsidian shards can be collected by sieving Temporal Sand with an iron sieve mesh (6% chance), a rose gold sieve mesh (12% chance), or an iron composite sieve mesh (20% chance). Separation can increase the chance/drops of obsidian shards from sieving.
