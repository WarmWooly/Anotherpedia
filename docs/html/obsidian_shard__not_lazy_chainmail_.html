Obsidian Shard (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The obsidian shard is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail obtained from sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) or breaking down obsidian (Minecraft). When obsidian (Minecraft) or crying obsidian (Minecraft) is crushed in a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail), it will yield 4 obsidian shards. Obsidian shards can be collected by sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail) Temporal Sand (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an iron sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (6% chance), a rose gold sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (12% chance), or an iron composite sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) (20% chance). Separation (Not Lazy Chainmail) can increase the chance/drops of obsidian shards from sieving. Obsidian shards are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Arrow (Minecraft) x4 - 1 obsidian shard, 1 stick (Minecraft), and 1 feather (Minecraft) (with each item listed stacked on top of each other, from top to bottom)
Fletching Table (Minecraft) - 2 obsidian shards and 4 planks (Minecraft) (make a 2x2 square of planks on the bottom, then place the obsidian shards above it)
Obsidian (Minecraft) - 4 obsidian shards (place in a 2x2 square) For Expert-level fletchers, there is a 66.67% chance to trade 16 obsidian shards for an emerald (Minecraft). Obsidian shards are used in the following Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail) recipes:
Shatter Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 obsidian shard and 1 smooth stone (Minecraft)
