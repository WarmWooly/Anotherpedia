Ocean Mixtape (Bits & Blocks)

The Ocean Mixtape is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the mixtape of the same name. It can be displayed on a mixtape stand. The Ocean Mixtape can be crafted with 1 mixtape case, 1 Pop-Up Kitchen, 1 Firework Festival, 1 Hammer Time!, and 1 Molecano (shapeless). The Ocean Mixtape is used in the following crafting recipes:
Aurora Firework Amulet - 1 Ocean Mixtape and 1 firework amulet (shapeless).
Blueprint - 1 Ocean Mixtape, 1 instruction sheet, and 1 blue dye (shapeless).
Final Mixtape - 1 Ocean Mixtape, 1 Jungle Mixtape, 1 Sky Mixtape, and 1 Fire Mixtape (shapeless).
Frozen Skewer x16 - 1 Ocean Mixtape and 8 skewers (shapeless).
Icy Cart Handle - 1 Ocean Mixtape and 1 cart handle (shapeless).
