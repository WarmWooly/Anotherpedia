Ocean Mixtape (Bits & Bops)

Ocean Mixtape is the third mixtape within Bits & Bops, containing Pop-Up Kitchen, Firework Festival, Hammer Time!, and Molecano. To match the ocean theme, the following changes were made to each minigame:;;The chef is now in a submarine.|The pork and leeks are replaced with salmon and lemon slices.|The fireworks are launched over the ocean during an aurora borealis.|Hammer Time! takes place on a blueprint.|The wood planks are replaced with blocks of ice floating on water.|The hammer is a wooden mallet, and the hand placing nails has a thick mitten on.|The moles are in an ice cavern.|The moles are white with silver helmets.;; The minigames appear in the following order: ::Firework Festival|Pop-Up Kitchen|Firework Festival|Hammer Time!|Molecano|Pop-Up Kitchen|Firework Festival|Hammer Time!|Molecano|Pop-Up Kitchen|Hammer Time!|Firework Festival|Hammer Time!|Firework Festival!|Pop-Up Kitchen::Before each segment of Molecano, a lead-up "one-two" is given.
