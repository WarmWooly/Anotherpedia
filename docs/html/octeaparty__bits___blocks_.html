Octeaparty (Bits & Blocks)

Octeaparty is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Octeaparty can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 sugar, and 1 water bucket (shapeless; a bucket is returned). Octeaparty is used in the following crafting recipes:
Fire Mixtape - 1 Octeaparty, 1 mixtape case, 1 President Bird, 1 Snakedown, and 1 Globe Trotters (shapeless).
Tentacles - 1 Octeaparty and 1 (glow) ink sac (shapeless).
