Octeaparty (Bits & Bops)

Octeaparty is the fifteenth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is an octopus serving tea to patrons. The minigame has the player controlling the octopus to fill teacups and to add sugar cubes. The player must hold Button 2 to fill the teacup once it settles and press Button 1 three times after the octopus shakes it. While every teacup must get tea, the number of times sugar is added is variable. Partway through the level, a patron will repeatedly ask for sugar, with a wide shot showing him holding up the line. Another wide shot will show them either enjoying their tea or gazing at it longingly, depending on whether he got enough sugar or not.

If the player pours on time, lines will appear from the spout. If they are slightly off, no lines will appear. Pouring too much will cause the cup to overflow, and too little will leave the cup partly empty. Pouring without a teacup will cause it to splash towards the ground. If the player adds cubes on time, a large splash effect will appear in the tea. If they are slightly off, a small splash will play. Being really off will cause the cube to bounce off the rim. Dropping cubes without a teacup will cause them to fall to the ground. Not pouring tea or dropping sugar will cause a disappointing sound to play.
