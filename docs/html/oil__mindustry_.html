Oil (Mindustry)

Oil is a liquid in Mindustry on Serpulo and can be obtained through pools of oil tiles on the surface using a Mechanical Pump or better, an Oil Extractor, or by pressing Spore Pods in a Spore Press. The description of Oil is the following:It has an Exposiveness of 120%, a Flammability of 120%, a Heat Capacity of 70%, a Viscosity of 75%, and a Temperature of 50%. Oil can be used to create Plastanium and Coal and as liquid ammo. When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit the Tarred effect.
