Oil (Mindustry)

Oil is a liquid (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and can be obtained through pools of oil tiles on the surface using a Mechanical Pump (Mindustry) or better, an Oil Extractor (Mindustry), or by pressing Spore Pod (Mindustry) in a Spore Press (Mindustry). The description of Oil is the following:Used in advanced material production and as incendiary ammunition.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 120%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 120%, a heat capacity (Mindustry) of 70%, a viscosity (Mindustry) of 75%, and a temperature (Mindustry) of 50%. Oil can be used to create Plastanium (Mindustry) and Coal (Mindustry) and as liquid ammo (Mindustry). When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit (Mindustry) the Tarred (Mindustry) effect.
