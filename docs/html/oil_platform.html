Oil platform

<><>An {{boil platform}}, also called an {{boil rig}}, is a large [[structure]] used to [[harvest|harvesting]] [[natural gas]] and [[petroleum]], often in the [[ocean]]. They can be placed in any large viable body of [[water]], such as [[lakes|lake]] and [[inland seas|inland sea]]. There are multiple types, such as a [[fixed platform]], on an [[artificial island]], or a [[floating oil production system|floating production storage and offloading]].
