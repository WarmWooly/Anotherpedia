Orange

<>{{tFor the [[fruit]], see [[orange (fruit)]].}}&sp<>{{bOrange}} is a [[color]] between [[red]] and [[yellow]]. When mixing red and yellow [[pigments|pigment]] or similar materials (such as [[paint]]), it creates orange. Because it is comprised of two [[primary colors|primary color]], orange is considered a [[secondary color]] in [[color theory]]. Darkening orange creates the color [[brown]].
