Orange Stake (Balatro)

Orange Stake is the seventh available Stake in Balatro, where: ;;Jokers have a 30% chance of having a Perishable Sticker (debuffed after five rounds)|Blinds have even faster scaling per Ante|-1 Discard|Jokers have a 30% chance of having an Eternal Sticker (can't be sold or destroyed)|Blinds have faster scaling per Ante (requiring more chips to win)|Small Blind will not award any money;; The White, Red, Green, Black, Blue, and Purple Stake must be defeated before unlocking the Orange Stake.
