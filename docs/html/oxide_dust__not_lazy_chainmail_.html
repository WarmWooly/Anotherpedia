Oxide Dust (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Oxide dust is a internet meme item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail that plays various funny sounds. Unlike other items, it is hidden in the Creative menu (Minecraft). Oxide dust was inspired by the bear dust (Alex's Mobs) from Alex's Mobs. When sneaking while using a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail) on any oxidized copper block from Minecraft or Not Lazy Chainmail, an oxide dust will be given. When oxide dust is used, a random sound effect will play as the item goes on a short cooldown (Minecraft). When sneaking while using oxide dust, a menu will appear with a button for "All Memes" and a plus and minus button to change the selected meme. Using oxide dust will award the hidden Advancement (Minecraft) Man of Culture (Not Lazy Chainmail). There are a total of 66 internet meme to choose from.
