Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Oxide On is an advancement in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that is earned when the player applies a vinegar bottle directly onto a block made of copper. It is the child to the advancement Chain On and the parent to the advancements Variety is the Pickle of Life, We're Blasting Off Again!, and What a Mouthful!.
