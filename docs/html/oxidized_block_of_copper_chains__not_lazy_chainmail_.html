Oxidized Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The oxidized block of copper chains is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The oxidized block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a weathered block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become an oxidized block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Oxidized blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Oxidized Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 oxidized block of copper chains (shapeless).
Oxidized Copper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 oxidized blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).
Waxed Oxidized Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 oxidized block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed oxidized block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a weathered block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail).
