Oxidized Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The oxidized block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The oxidized block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 oxidized copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a weathered block of copper chains will become an oxidized block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Oxidized blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spOxidized Copper Chain x4 - 1 oxidized block of copper chains (shapeless).&spOxidized Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 oxidized blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).&spWaxed Oxidized Block of Copper Chains - 1 oxidized block of copper chains and 1 honeycomb (shapeless). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed oxidized block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into a weathered block of copper chains.
