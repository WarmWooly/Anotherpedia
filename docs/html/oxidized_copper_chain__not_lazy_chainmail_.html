Oxidized Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The oxidized copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The oxidized copper chain can be crafted with an oxidized block of copper chains (makes 4). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a weathered copper chain will become an oxidized copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking a waxed oxidized copper chain with an axe will turn the block into an oxidized copper chain. This will award the advancement Wax Off. Oxidized copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Mangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oxidized Block of Copper Chains - 4 oxidized copper chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Oxidized Copper Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 oxidized copper chains (fill the middle and bottom rows).
Spruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Warped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Waxed Oxidized Copper Chain - 1 oxidized copper chain and 1 honeycomb (shapeless). Oxidized copper chains can be crushed into 8 copper chain links. Right-clicking an oxidized copper chain with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed oxidized copper chain. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Right-clicking an oxidized copper chain with an axe will turn the block into a weathered copper chain.
