Oxygen (Mekanism)

Oxygen is a gas in the Minecraft mod Mekanism used for breathing and creation of other chemicals, alongside ore refinery. It can be stored in gas tanks and transported with pressurized tubes. In a Electrolytic Separator, the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
2 mB Water --> 2 mB Hydrogen + 1 mB Oxygen
2 mB Heavy Water --> 2 mB Deuterium + 1 mB Oxygen In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber, the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
200 mB Water + 100 mB Ethylene + 1 Substrate --> 8 Substrate + 10 mB Oxygen In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
1 mB Liquid Oxygen --> 1 mB Oxygen In a Chemical Infuser, the following reactions can be made with oxygen:
1 mB Oxygen + 2 mB Sulfur Dioxide --> 2 mB Sulfur Trioxide In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber, the following reactions can be made with oxygen:
1000 mB Water + 1000 mB Oxygen + 1 Block of Coal or Charcoal Block --> 9 Sulfur Dust + 1000 mB Hydrogen
100 mB Water + 100 mB Oxygen + 1 Coal or Charcoal --> 1 Sulfur Dust + 100 mB Hydrogen
100 mB Water + 100 mB Oxygen + 1 Log --> 100 mB Hydrogen
20 mB Water + 20 mB Oxygen + 1 Sawdust --> 20 mB Hydrogen
20 mB Water + 20 mB Oxygen + 1 Planks --> 20 mB Hydrogen
10 mB Water + 10 mB Oxygen + 1 Wooden Slab --> 10 mB Hydrogen
4 mB Water + 4 mB Oxygen + 1 Stick --> 4 mB Hydrogen
50 mB Liquid Ethylene + 10 mB Oxygen + 1 Substrate --> 1 HDPE Pellet + 5 mB Oxygen In a Purification Chamber, the following reactions can be made with oxygen:
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Iron Ore --> 3 Iron Clumps
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Gold Ore --> 3 Gold Clumps
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Tin Ore --> 3 Tin Clumps
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Copper Ore --> 3 Copper Clumps
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Osmium Ore --> 3 Osmium Clumps
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Iron Shard --> 1 Iron Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Gold Shard --> 1 Gold Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Tin Shard --> 1 Tin Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Copper Shard --> 1 Copper Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Osmium Shard --> 1 Osmium Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Silver Shard --> 1 Silver Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Lead Shard --> 1 Lead Clump
200 mB Oxygen + 1 Gravel --> 1 Flint In a Rotary Condensentrator, the following reactions can be made with oxygen:
1 mB Oxygen --> 1 mB Liquid Oxygen Oxygen is used to fuel the Scuba Tank for breathing.
