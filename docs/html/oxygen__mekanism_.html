Oxygen (Mekanism)

Oxygen is a gas (Mekanism) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Mekanism used for breathing and creation of other chemical, alongside ore (Minecraft) refinery. It can be stored in Gas Tank (Mekanism) and transported with pressurized tube (Mekanism). In a Electrolytic Separator (Mekanism), the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
2 millibuckets Water (Minecraft) --> 2 mB Hydrogen (Mekanism) + 1 mB Oxygen
2 mB Heavy Water (Mekanism) --> 2 mB Deuterium (Mekanism) + 1 mB Oxygen In a Pressurized Reaction Chamber (Mekanism), the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
200 mB Water (Minecraft) + 100 mB Ethylene (Mekanism) + 1 substrate (Mekanism) --> 8 substrate (Mekanism) + 10 mB Oxygen In a Rotary Condensentrator (Mekanism), the following reactions can be made to obtain oxygen:
