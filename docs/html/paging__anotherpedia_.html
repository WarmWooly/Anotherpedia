Paging (Anotherpedia)

For the separate areas for information, see page (Anotherpedia).
Paging on Anotherpedia is the action of creating a page (Anotherpedia) about a person, thing, or topic, as a joke. This can be done as a spontaneity act to keep the information on Anotherpedia for all to view. The person targeted is said to be paged.
