PairOfPears3

PairOfPears3 is a YouTuber who reuploads music and plays rhythm games. Most notably, have reuploaded the OST for Windowkill and multiple songs for Friday Night Funkin' mods. They also have gameplay of The Battle Cats, Geometry Dash, Rhythm Heaven, and Bits & Bops, with some of the content created by them. Additionally, they made a fan game between Five Nights at Freddy's and Brawl Stars called Five Nights at Starr Park.
