Pallet rack

<><>A {{bpallet rack}} is a [[rack]] used for holding [[pallets|pallet]] in a [[storage system]]. Multiple pallet racks used together in a system are known as [[pallet racking]]. Pallet racks are nearly exclusively made of [[metal]], where they may have [[plastic]] [[rollers|roller]] to make it easier to [[rack|racking]] and unrack pallets.
