Pantry Parade (Bits & Bops)

Pantry Parade is the fourth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is an ant marching along with others to transport food to a table. The minigame has the player's ant between two others. The player must hit Button 1 in time with the beat to take a step. When a signal plays, the player takes three steps in a row. During the level, the camera zooms in and out, with some parts obscured by other objects. The ant the player controls also changes throughout the level, with no specific ant shown during a wide shot across the kitchen.

If the player is on time, a thick red outline will appear by the ant's foot. If they are slightly off, only two lines will appear. Being really off will cause the ant to slam into the one in front (too fast) or get bounced by the one behind (too slow/no input). In the wide shots, multiple lines together is on time, fewer lines is slightly off, and red squiggles is completely off.
