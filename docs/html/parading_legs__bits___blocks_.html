Parading Legs (Bits & Blocks)

Parading legs are a feet curio in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks that applies a buff on eating food. The parading legs can be crafted with 1 Pantry Parade and 2 sticks (shapeless). When the parading legs are worn in a feet slot, eating food will also apply Speed for 30 seconds. Parading legs are used in the following crafting recipes:
Exotic Parading Legs - 1 parading legs, 1 Jungle Mixtape, and 1 leaves (shapeless).
