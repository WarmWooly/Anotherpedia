Paramedic Team (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Parametic Team (PT) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who can heal other player (Roblox). The PT get a medkit (SCP: Devolved Revamp), which they can use on all other non-Chaos Insurgency (SCP: Devolved Revamp) players (not including themselves). PT members should focus on healing allies in battle or fixing up Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp) after interacting with SCPs (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The PT has 5 levels, with each giving a new title and increasing the healing power of the medkit. At levels 3 and 5, their keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp) access also increases.<> PTs spawn in a room labeled "Paramedic Team" in Sector 1 of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The room requires a Level 1 Keycard to enter. The back of the room has file cabinet, an armchair, and a plant in the left corner of the door. A long gray/white table sits in the center with a medkit, with cabinets to the right containing more medkits, bottles, loose papers, a mug, and a little guy in a jar. Against the front wall is a water cooler, a analog clock, a poster showing information related to facility lighting, and another plant. To the left is a table with a mug and a pair of chairs, a large whiteboard with random math and SCP-999 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), an interactible uniform stand, and a TV with the following information:For Paramedics:
- heal foundation personnel
- treat injured or infected class d after tests
- run from scps
- be wary of hostiles
