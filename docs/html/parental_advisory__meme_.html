Parental Advisory (meme)

Parental Advisory as a internet meme refers to using the Parental Advisory warning label on mundane or silly images as if it were an album cover. This meme format is especially popular on iFunny, where users often request and make Parental Advisory memes based on the content of a post. A common edit made to the image is to turn it grayscale before applying the warning label. While not necessary, most Parental Advisory memes are in a 1:1 aspect ratio (image) to match the dimensions of album covers.
