Parkour Civilization

Parkour Civilization is a Minecraft web series on YouTube created by Evbo, with the first episode released on October 5th, 2021, titled "Minecraft but I join PARKOUR CIVILIZATION". <>, there are a total of 21 episodes of Parkour Civilization that have been officially released. It has since boomed in popularity through Twitter, especially the now-memed phrase from the first episode "No one jumps for the beef." There are two "movies" for Parkour Civilization, compiling the first 10 and last 10 episodes into two movie-length packages. Main page: Minecraft but I join PARKOUR CIVILIZATION
