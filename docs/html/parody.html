Parody

<>{{tNot to be confused with [[satire]].}}&sp<>{{bParody}} is the [[mimicry]] of a [[work's|artistic work]] or [[creator's|creator]] [[style]] in a [[humorous|humor]] fashion which premotes [[irony]] or [[satire]]. Parody may be known as a {{bspoof}}, a {{bsatire}}, a {{blampoon}}, a {{bcaricature}}, or a {{bplay on}}.
