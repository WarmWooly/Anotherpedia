Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

{{tFor the song, see Peanut Butter Jelly Time (song).}}&spA {{bpeanut butter and jelly sandwich}}, abbreviation to {{bpbj}} or {{bpb&j}}, is a sandwich where the contents include peanut butter and jelly. The most common formation is to have a bread slice, peanut butter, jelly, then a top slice. Jelly is often used interchangeably with jam. Other fillings, such as marshmallow creme and sunbutter have been also used as ingredient substitute.
