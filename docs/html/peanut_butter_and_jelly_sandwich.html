Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

<>{{tFor the [[song]], see [[Peanut Butter Jelly Time (song)]].}}&sp<>A {{bpeanut butter and jelly sandwich}}, [[abbreviated|abbreviation]] to {{bpbj}} or {{bpb&j}}, is a [[sandwich]] where the contents include [[peanut butter]] and [[jelly]]. The most common formation is to have a [[slice of bread|bread slice]], peanut butter, jelly, then a top slice. Jelly is often used interchangeably with [[jam]]. Other fillings, such as [[marshmallow creme]] and [[sunbutter]] have been also used as [[ingredient]] [[substitutes|substitute]].
