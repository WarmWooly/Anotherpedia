Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

For the song, see Peanut Butter Jelly Time (song).&spA peanut butter and jelly sandwich, abbreviation to pbj or pb&j, is a sandwich where the contents include peanut butter and jelly. The most common formation is to have a bread slice, peanut butter, jelly, then a top slice. Jelly is often used interchangeably with jam. Other fillings, such as marshmallow creme and sunbutter have been also used as ingredient substitute.
