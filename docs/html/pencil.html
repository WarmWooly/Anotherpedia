Pencil

<><>A {{bpencil}} is a [[tool]] used for [[writing]] with a [[core|pencil core]] of material (often [[graphite]]) surrounded with an outer casing (such as [[wood]] or [[plastic]]). Pencils may also have an attached [[eraser]] to remove [[marks|mark]] made. [[Pencil sharpeners|pencil sharpener]] are used to sharpen pencils for reuse. Some pencils are [[mechanical|mechanical pencil]], where the graphite is fed into the pencil and pushed up for use.
