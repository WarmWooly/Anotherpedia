Pencil

A {{bpencil}} is a tool used for writing with a pencil core of material (often graphite) surrounded with an outer casing (such as wood or plastic). Pencils may also have an attached eraser to remove mark made. pencil sharpener are used to sharpen pencils for reuse. Some pencils are mechanical pencil, where the graphite is fed into the pencil and pushed up for use.
