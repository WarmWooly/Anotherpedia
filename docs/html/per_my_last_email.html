Per my last email

Per my last email refers to a reply done on emails when the sender is restating information present in the previous correspondence with the receiver, such as the following conversation:Sender: Hello Reciever, we need to discuss the new product we are making. Would you be available for lunch on Wednesday?

Reciever: Hi Sender, we can talk about the product. What time were you thinking of meeting at?

Sender: As per my last email, I can do 12:00 PM on Wednesday.Per my last email is considered the professional way to insult the receiver's ability to read by showing that the receiver missed key information in a previous email.
