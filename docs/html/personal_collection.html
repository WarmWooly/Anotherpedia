Personal collection

For the marketing company, see Personal Collection (company).
A personal collection is a collection owned by a person for their own use and enjoyment, mainly through the hobby of collecting. Someone who collects for their personal collection is called a collector. Some object are designed to be collected, known as a collectable. There is a wide variety of personal collections, including coin collecting, stamp collecting, record collecting, and list of collectables.
