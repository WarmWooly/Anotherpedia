Pickled Beetroot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The pickled beetroot is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Pickled beetroots can be crafted with 1 vinegar bottle and 3 beetroots (shapeless, a glass bottle is returned; makes 3). Pickled beetroots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Veggie Salad - 1 pickled beetroot, 1 pickled carrot, 1 pickled potato, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Pickled beetroots can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
