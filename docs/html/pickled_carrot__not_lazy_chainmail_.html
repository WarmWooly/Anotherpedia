Pickled Carrot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The pickled carrot is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Pickled carrots can be crafted with 1 vinegar bottle and 3 carrots (shapeless, a glass bottle is returned; makes 3). Pickled carrots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Veggie Salad - 1 pickled carrot, 1 pickled beetroot, 1 pickled potato, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Pickled carrots can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
