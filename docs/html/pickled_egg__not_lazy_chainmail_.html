Pickled Egg (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The pickled egg is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Pickled eggs can be crafted with 1 vinegar bottle and 3 eggs (shapeless, a glass bottle is returned; makes 3). Pickled eggs can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
