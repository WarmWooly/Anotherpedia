Pins (Rhiane Turtonator)

Pins is an original character based on Smiling Friends created by Rhiane Turtonator who is posted to various social media, including DevientART, Twitter, and Reddit. Pins' most prominent appearance was within the YouTube video "SMILING FRIENDS ( fan animation )". Pins is a green critter (Smiling Friends) with lanky arms and legs and a tall body. His hands each have two fingers. His head consists of a wide mouth, a pair of large eyes, and a single hair (or possibly a free nerve ending, like Pim (Smiling Friends)). He wears a white or off-white bowtie. Pins is shown working Frowning Friends Inc. (Smiling Friends) alongside Needles (Rhiane Turtonator), Eric (Rhiane Turtonator), and Anti-Boss (Rhiane Turtonator).
