Piss filter

For the yellowing of generated images, see AI piss filter, and for the filter used in film and TV, see Mexican filter.
The piss filter is slang for warmer color grading and yellow filter used within video games during the 2000s and early-2010s. Notable examples include Resident Evil 5, Metal Gear Solid 4, and Deus Ex: Human Revolution. The filter tends to be applied in the outdoors within urban areas, possibly resembling smog while giving a sense that an area is run-down. The term is demeaning to represent disdain by players of the filter used in games, with some games getting mods to remove the filter.
