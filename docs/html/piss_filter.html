Piss filter

For the yellowing of generative artificial intelligence image, see AI piss filter, and for the filter used in film and TV series, see Mexican filter.
The piss filter is slang for warmer color grading and yellow photographic filter used within video game during the 2000s and early-2010s. Notable examples include Resident Evil 5, Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots, and Deus Ex: Human Revolution. The filter tends to be applied in the outdoors within urban areas, possibly resembling smog while giving a sense that an area is run-down. The term is demeaning to represent disdain by players of the filter used in games, with some games getting video game modding to remove the filter.
