PKM (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The PKM is a primary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Chaos Insurgency at level 5. While its damage and firerate may be lacking, its extreme ammo capacity allows the user to keep the pressure against targets for a long time without reloading.
