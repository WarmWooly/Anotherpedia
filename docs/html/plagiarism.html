Plagiarism

Plagiarism is the action of copying work or ideas that the plagiarizer did not create, yet to pass it off as their own work or ideas. Plagiarism is almost always considered unethical, where the plagiarizer is often subject to various punishments or sanctions, such as suspension, expulsion from school or work, fines, imprisonment, and other penalties. Plagiarism is prominent, often exploiting trust of the reader/viewer and of the writer/publisher, in order to achieve a goal (such as wealth or influence).
