Planet

A planet is a relatively large sphere astronomical object which orbit a star and has cleared its orbit. The planets in the Solar System (from closest to furthest from the Sun) are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Bodies which orbit planets are known as moon. Bodies that do not meet all the requirements to be a planet may be dwarf planet, asteroid, or comet. Planets outside of the Solar System are known as exoplanet.
