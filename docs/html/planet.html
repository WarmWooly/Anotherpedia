Planet

<><>A planet is a relatively large [[spherical|sphere]] [[astronomical object]] which [[orbits|orbit]] a [[star]] and has cleared its orbit. The planets in the [[Solar System]] (from closest to furthest from the [[Sun]]) are [[Mercury]], [[Venus]], [[Earth]], [[Mars]], [[Jupiter]], [[Saturn]], [[Uranus]], and [[Neptune]]. Bodies which orbit planets are known as [[moons|moon]]. Bodies that do not meet all the requirements to be a planet may be [[dwarf planets|dwarf planet]], [[asteroids|asteroid]], or [[comets|comet]]. Planets outside of the Solar System are known as [[exoplanets|exoplanet]].
