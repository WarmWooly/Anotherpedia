Planet Cards (Balatro)

Planet Cards are a type of Consumables (Balatro) in Balatro used to upgrade poker hands (Balatro). There are a total of 12 Planet Cards based on the planet in the Solar System and other similar celestial body. These cards increase the chips (Balatro) and multiplier (Balatro) earned with specific hands. They are often the primary way to level up poker hands, and they can be found in The Shop (Balatro) or in booster packs (Balatro). They can also be obtained through the Blue Seal (Balatro), Jokers (Balatro), and from Tags (Balatro).
