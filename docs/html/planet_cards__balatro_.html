Planet Cards (Balatro)

Planet Cards are a type of Consumable in Balatro used to upgrade poker hands. There are a total of 12 Planet Cards based on the planets in the Solar System and other similar celestial bodies. These cards increase the chips and mult earned with specific hands. They are often the primary way to level up poker hands, and they can be found in the Shop or in booster packs. They can also be obtained through the Blue Seal, Jokers, and from Tags. Only 9 are immediately available, with Planet X, Ceres, and Eris requiring their respective hand to be played and then to purchase the card in the same run.<>
