Plant Walking (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Plant walking is an effect in Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined that allows the player bone meal nearby blocks while crouched. The source of plant walking is from eating bone bread or a bread platter containing such. For each half second a player is crouched, surrounding blocks will be given bone meal. Each time this effect occurs, 3 seconds are removed from the duration of the effect.
