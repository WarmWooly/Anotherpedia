Plastanium (Mindustry)

Plastanium is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) that can be produced in a Plastanium Compressor (Mindustry) using titanium (Mindustry) and oil (Mindustry). The description of Plastanium is the following:Used in advanced units, insulation and fragmentation ammunition.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 20%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 10%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. On destruction, with enough plastanium, a moderate explosion can be created. It is used in mid- to late-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry). Plastanium is a portmanteau of plastic and titanium.
