Plastanium (Mindustry)

Plastanium is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo that can be produced in a Plastanium Compressor using Titanium and Oil. The description of Plastanium is the following:It has an Explosiveness of 20%, a Flammability of 10%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. On destruction, with enough plastanium, a moderate explosion can be created. It is used in mid- to late-game buildings, ammo, and units. Plastanium is a portmanteau of plastic and titanium.
