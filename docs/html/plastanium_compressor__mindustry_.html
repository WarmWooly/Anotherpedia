Plastanium Compressor (Mindustry)

A Plastanium Compressor is a building (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) that is used to create plastanium (Mindustry). The description of the Plastanium Compressor is the following: It has 320 health (Mindustry), taking 4.74 build time (Mindustry), while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 115 lead (Mindustry), 60 graphite (Mindustry), 80 titanium (Mindustry), and 80 silicon (Mindustry). It can store up to 60 liquid unit (Mindustry) and 10 item (Mindustry). To create 1 Plastanium, it requires 2 Titanium and 15 units of oil (Mindustry). It has a production time of 1 second. To research (Mindustry) it, you must capturing (Mindustry) Windswept Islands (Mindustry) and have the spore press (Mindustry) researched. It costs 3900 Lead, 1900 Graphite, 2600 Titanium, and 2600 Silicon to research it.
