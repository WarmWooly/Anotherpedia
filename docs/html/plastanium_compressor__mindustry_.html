Plastanium Compressor (Mindustry)

A Plastanium Compressor is a building in Mindustry on Serpulo that is used to create Plastanium. The description of the Plastanium Compressor is the following: It has 320 health, taking 4.74 seconds to build, while taking a 2x2 space. It costs 115 Lead, 60 Graphite, 80 Titanium, and 80 Silicon. It can store up to 60 liquid units and 10 items. To create 1 Plastanium, it requires 2 Titanium and 15 units of Oil. It has a production time of 1 second. To research it, you must capture Windswept Islands and have the Spore Press researched. It costs 3900 Lead, 1900 Graphite, 2600 Titanium, and 2600 Silicon to research it.
