Platypet

{{bPlatypet}} is a platypus-like Water type (TemTem) and Toxic type (TemTem) Temtem (fictional creature). Platypet resembles a small child in stature and a platypus in features. Its fur is light blue, with a white belly and two dark blue stripes perpendicular to its back. Platypet has stubby hands and feet, each with three digits. It has a large, flat, black tail, often pointing upwards. Platypet's head is comprised of two large, black eyes, two small black markings to the lower side of the eyes, a large black bill, two spikey fur tufts on the top of its head, and three bulbous fur tufts on each side of its head.&pPlatypet's official size is 2 foot (unit) and 9 inch, while weighing 105.8 pound. Luma (Temtem) Platypets have orange eyes, a pink coat, and a magenta shading. Umbra (Temtem) Platypets are completely black with bright purple eyes and purple shading.&pPlatypet is a portmanteau between {{iplatypus}} and {{ipet}}. In {{iTemtem}}, Platypet was popularized with kids in an in-universe children's cartoon. The cartoon was made to education that Toxic Temtems were as friendly and valid as other types of Temtems. A Platypet has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Platypet is the 7th Temtem in the Tempedia (Temtem).&pPlatypet evolution (Temtem) into Platox after gaining 20 leveling (Temtem), and then into Platimous after an addition 20 levels.
