Plop

<><>A {{bplop}} is an [[onomatopoeia]] of the short [[sound]] produced when an [[object]] enters a [[liquid]] (usually [[water]]) without making a signifigant [[splash]]. Plop, as a [[verb]], may also be used to describe an object being [[dropped|drop]] a short distance without being [[damaged|damage]] and/or [[without care|careless]].
