Pokémon (fictional species)

For the franchise, see {{aiPokémon.}}
Pokémon are a fictional species making up the Pokémon franchise, coming in a variety of shapes and sizes. There are currently 1025 unique Pokémon <>.

Many resembles real-life aniamls, such as turtles (Squirtle, Turtwig), cows (Miltank), dogs (Snubbull, Poochyena, Rockruff), and dolphins (Finizen), being only a small fraction of the animals represented by Pokémon. Others are fantasy creatures, mythological beings, or from fokelore, such as Ninetales (based on the Chinese huli jing), Hoopa (based on the Arabic genie), and Arceus (being a stand-in for God, creating the universe similarly to how it is described in Christianity). Some may even be objects, such as balloons (Drifloon), magnets (Magnemite), ice cream (Vanillite), and swords (Honedge).
