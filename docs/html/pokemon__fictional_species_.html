Pokémon (fictional species)

For the franchise, see {{aiPokémon.}}
Pokémon are a fictional species making up the Pokémon, coming in a variety of shapes and sizes. There are currently 1025 unique Pokémon <>.

Many resembles reality animal, such as turtle (Squirtle (Pokémon), Turtwig (Pokémon)), cow (Miltank (Pokémon)), dog (Snubbull (Pokémon), Poochyena (Pokémon), Rockruff (Pokémon)), and dolphin (Finizen (Pokémon)), being only a small fraction of the animals represented by Pokémon. Others are fantasy creature, mythological being, or from fokelore, such as Ninetales (Pokémon) (based on the Chinese folklore huli jing), Hoopa (Pokémon) (based on the Arabic folklore genie), and Arceus (Pokémon) (being a stand-in for God, creating the universe similarly to how it is described in Christianity). Some may even be object, such as balloon (Drifloon (Pokémon)), magent (Magnemite (Pokémon)), ice cream (Vanillite (Pokémon)), and sword (Honedge (Pokémon)).
