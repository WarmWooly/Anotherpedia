Polished Andesite Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The polished andesite flushwork Panel is a polished andesite flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The polished andesite flushwork Panel can be crafted with 1 polished andesite and 2 knapped flint bricks (item) (shapeless). The polished andesite flushwork Panel can be cut from any other polished andesite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The polished andesite flushwork Panel can be cut into any other polished andesite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
