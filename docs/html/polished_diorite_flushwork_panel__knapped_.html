Polished Diorite Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<>The polished diorite flushwork Panel is a polished diorite flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The polished diorite flushwork Panel can be crafted with 1 polished diorite and 2 knapped flint bricks (item) (shapeless). The polished diorite flushwork Panel can be cut from any other polished diorite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The polished diorite flushwork Panel can be cut into any other polished diorite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
