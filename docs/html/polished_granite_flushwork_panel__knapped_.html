Polished Granite Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<>The {{bpolished granite flushwork Panel}} is a polished granite flushwork (Knapped) block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iKnapped (Minecraft mod)}}. The polished granite flushwork Panel can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 polished granite (Minecraft) and 2 knapped flint brick (Knapped) (shapeless). The polished granite flushwork Panel can be cut from any other polished granite flushwork (Knapped) (besides Polished Granite Flushwork Thing (Knapped)) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft).
