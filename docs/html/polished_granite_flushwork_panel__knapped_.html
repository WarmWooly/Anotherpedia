Polished Granite Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<>The polished granite flushwork Panel is a polished granite flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The polished granite flushwork Panel can be crafted with 1 polished granite and 2 knapped flint bricks (item) (shapeless). The polished granite flushwork Panel can be cut from any other polished granite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The polished granite flushwork Panel can be cut into any other polished granite flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
