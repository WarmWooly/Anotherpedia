Pop-Up Kitchen (Bits & Blocks)

Pop-Up Kitchen is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Pop-Up Kitchen can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 stick, and 1 fish (shapeless). Pop-Up Kitchen is used in the following crafting recipes:
Ocean Mixtape - 1 Pop-Up Kitchen, 1 mixtape case, 1 Firework Festival, 1 Hammer Time!, and 1 Molecano (shapeless).
Skewer x16 - 1 Pop-Up Kitchen and 8 sticks (shapeless).
