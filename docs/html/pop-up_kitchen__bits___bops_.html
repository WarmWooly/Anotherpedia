Pop-Up Kitchen (Bits & Bops)

Pop-Up Kitchen is the ninth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a chef throwing skewers/toothpicks to catch food mid-air. The minigame has the chef throwing skewers in time with the food being at the peak of its throw. The player must hit Button 1 in time for when the dumplings (one) or the pork and leek (three) are at the top. When the group of fried prawns cinematically appears, the player must hold Button 2 to start skewering them all, releasing it once it is done.

If the player is on time, a thick orange outline will appear around the food. If they are slightly off, only a thin outline will appear. Being really off will cause the food to be askew with a blue spiral effect. If there is no input, a disappointing sound will play as the food falls.
