PopCorners (Cinnamon Crunch)

The cinnamon crunch flavor of PopCorners are popped corn chips that has a cinnamon taste to it. It comes in a purple bag. Under the flavor, it depicts a pair of cinnamon sticks atop a small pile of cinnamon. A single bag contains 7 servings, with a serving size of roughly 17 chips, or 28 grams. Each serving contains 130 calories. There are 4.5 grams of total fat, with no saturated or trans fat. It has no cholesterol, 100 milligrams of sodium, 21 grams of total carbohydrates, including no dietary fibers and 3 grams of sugar.
