PopCorners (Cinnamon Crunch)

The cinnamon crunch flavor of PopCorners are popped corn chip that has a cinnamon taste to it. It comes in a purple bag. Under the flavor, it depicts a pair of cinnamon stick atop a small pile of cinnamon. A single bag contains 7 food serving, with a food serving size of roughly 17 chips, or 28 Gram. Each serving contains 130 Calorie. There are 4.5 grams of total Fat (food), with no Saturated fat or trans fat fat. It has no cholesterol, 100 milligram of sodium (food), 21 grams of total Carbohydrate, including no Dietary fiber and 3 grams of sugar. It has 2 grams of protein, with 0.3 milligrams of iron (food) and 30 milligrams of potassium (food). The ingredients are listed as follows:Yellow Corn, Sunflower Oil, Sugar, Cinnamon, Brown Sugar, Salt, Buttermilk, And Natural Flavors.It is also noted that it contains Milk Ingredients.
