Porygon2

Porygon2 is a duck-like man-made Normal type Pokémon from the second generation. Porygon2 partially resembles a duck, comprised of several rounded segments. Porygon's body is red with a blue underbelly. A thin neck connects the body to the head, which has two large eyes and a long, blue beak. Its feet are also two blue bean-shaped offshoots connected to the lower sides of the body. Its blue tail extends from its body, resembling a spoon in shape.&pPorygon2's official size is 2ft, while weighing 71.7 lbs. Shiny Porygon2s are blue and light purple instead of red and blue.&pPorygon2's name is Porygon with the number 2 slapped on the end. Porygon2 is an upgrade of Porygon with modern visuals and a sleek design for spaceflight and exoplanet exploration, despite being unable to move in zero-g. Porygon2 uses artificial intelligence to exhibit new actions and behaviors not programmed. Through its AI, Porygon2 can speak a language that only other Porygon2s can understand. Porygon2 is genderless. Porygon2 is the 233rd Pokémon in the Pokédex and has the Pokédex color red.&pPorygon2 evolves from Porygon, which can then evolve when traded while holding a Dubious Disc into Porygon-Z.
