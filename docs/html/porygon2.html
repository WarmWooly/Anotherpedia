Porygon2

Porygon2 is a duck-like man-made normal type (pokémon) Pokémon (fictional species) from the Generation II (Pokémon). Porygon2 partially resembles a duck, comprised of several rounded segments. Porygon's body is red with a blue underbelly. A thin neck connects the body to the head, which has two large eyes and a long, blue beak. Its feet are also two blue bean-shaped offshoots connected to the lower sides of the body. Its blue tail extends from its body, resembling a spoon in shape.

Porygon2's official size is 2foot (unit), while weighing 71.7 pound. shiny (pokémon) Porygon2s are blue and light purple instead of red and blue.

Porygon2's name is Porygon with the number 2 slapped on the end. Porygon2 is an upgrade of Porygon with modern visuals and a sleek design for spaceflight and exoplanet exploration, despite being unable to move in weightlessness. Porygon2 uses artificial intelligence to exhibit new actions and behaviors not programmed. Through its AI, Porygon2 can speak a language that only other Porygon2s can understand. Porygon2 is genderless Pokémon. Porygon2 is the 233rd Pokémon in the Pokédex (Pokémon) and has the Pokédex color red.
