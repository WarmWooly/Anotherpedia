Pot Lid (Bits & Blocks)

The pot lid is a shield in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks, where blocking has a chance to deal damage to the attacker. The pot lid can be crafted with 1 Snakedown and 1 shield (shapeless). Pot lids can block like shields. If an attack is blocked, there is a 1 in 4 (25%) chance to deal 2 damage to the attacker. The pot lid can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining pot lids will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the pot lids, costing experience. Pot lids can be repaired in an anvil using an iron ingot, restoring 25% of the pot lid's durability, rounded down. The pot lid is used in the following crafting recipes:
Tempered Pot Lid - 1 pot lid and 1 Fire Mixtape (shapeless). Pot lids can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil.
