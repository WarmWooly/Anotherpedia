Potion man

Not to be confused with Potion Man - Ocean Man Parody.
"Potion man" is a Minecraft parody song of "Ocean Man", made by Humphrey Hood Official and posted to YouTube on September 9, 2017. It would later be mentioned in a Reddit post by u/Remarkable-Hour-6501 on r/DeepIntoYouTube on May 9, 2026.
