PPAP (Pen-Pineapple-Apple-Pen)

"PPAP (Pen-Pineapple-Apple-Pen)", also known as Pen Pineapple Apple Pen, was a popular song and a single by the fictional Pikotaro in 2016 that went viral for comparisons to "Gangnam Style" and the spread to fans of Justin Bieber after a tweet on Twitter.
