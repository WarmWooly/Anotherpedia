President Bird (Bits & Blocks)

President Bird is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. President Bird can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 feather, and 1 light blue dye (shapeless). President Bird is used in the following crafting recipes:
Fire Mixtape - 1 President Bird, 1 mixtape case, 1 Snakedown, 1 Octeaparty, and 1 Globe Trotters (shapeless).
Small Tie - 1 President Bird and 1 leather (shapeless).
