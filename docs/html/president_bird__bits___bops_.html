President Bird (Bits & Bops)

President Bird is the thirteenth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a parrot copying the talking points of their aide. The minigame has the player controlling the parrot to copy what their aide says. The player must hit Button 1 for a round speech bubble and hold Button 2 for a square speech bubble. Throughout the level, the broadcast of the parrot will be shown in different places, such as above a crowded street or in a newsroom.

If the player lands on time, lines will appear from the speech bubble. If they are on time to start stressing a point, then lines will appear above the parrot's head. If they are slightly off, no lines will appear. Being really off will cause the parrot to weakly squawk as the bubble shakes. If they are completely off, the parrot will loudly squawk with a negative symbol. If there is no input, a dashed outline of the missing bubble will appear.
