Privacy Visualizer

The Privacy Visualizer is a browser tool made by Reject Convenience to aid in understanding privacy policies, hosted at <>. It simplifies the various elements of user data that is collected, shared, and sold into 14 distinct categories:<>
Each category can be marked as gray (not collected), blue (collected), orange (shared), and red (sold), with each level being more intrusive than the last. The tool is made to help a person reading through a privacy policy to keep track of the data that is being collected, with a summary of the category and the potential benefits and downsides. Reject Convenience uses this tool when he reads privacy policies and references it in other videos. While he personally uses this tool, Reject Convenience also acknowledges a redesigned version on his site.
