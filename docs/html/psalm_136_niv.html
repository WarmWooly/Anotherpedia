Psalm 136 NIV

In the New International Version of the Psalms (Book 5) of Psalms, the 136th chapter (Psalms) reads:s-pPsalm 136:1 NIVGive thanks to the Lord, for he is good.
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:2 NIVGive thanks to the God of gods.
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:3 NIVGive thanks to the Lord of lords:
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:4 NIVto him who alone does great wonders,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:5 NIVwho by his understanding made the heavens,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:6 NIVwho spread out the earth upon the waters,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:7 NIVwho made the great lights—
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:8 NIVthe sun to govern the day,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:9 NIVthe moon and stars to govern the night;
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:10 NIVto him who struck down the firstborn of Egypt
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:11 NIVand brought Israel out from among them
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:12 NIVwith a mighty hand and outstretched arm;
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:13 NIVto him who divided the Red Sea asunder
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:14 NIVand brought Israel through the midst of it,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:15 NIVbut swept Pharaoh and his army into the Red Sea;
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:16 NIVto him who led his people through the wilderness;
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:17 NIVto him who struck down great kings,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:18 NIVand killed mighty kings—
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:19 NIVSihon king of the Amorites
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:20 NIVand Og king of Bashan—
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:21 NIVand gave their land as an inheritance,
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:22 NIVan inheritance to his servant Israel.
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:23 NIVHe remembered us in our low estate
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:24 NIVand freed us from our enemies.
His love endures forever.
s-pPsalm 136:25 NIVHe gives food to every creature.
His love endures forever.

s-pPsalm 136:26 NIVGive thanks to the God of heaven.
