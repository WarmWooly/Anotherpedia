Psalm 136 NIV

In the New International Version of the 5th book of Psalms, the 136th chapter reads:s-p1Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good.
His love endures forever.
s-p2Give thanks to the God of gods.
His love endures forever.
s-p3Give thanks to the Lord of lords:
His love endures forever.

s-p4to him who alone does great wonders,
His love endures forever.
s-p5who by his understanding made the heavens,
His love endures forever.
s-p6who spread out the earth upon the waters,
His love endures forever.
s-p7who made the great lights—
His love endures forever.
s-p8the sun to govern the day,
His love endures forever.
s-p9the moon and stars to govern the night;
His love endures forever.

s-p10to him who struck down the firstborn of Egypt
His love endures forever.
s-p11and brought Israel out from among them
His love endures forever.
s-p12with a mighty hand and outstretched arm;
His love endures forever.

s-p13to him who divided the Red Sea asunder
His love endures forever.
s-p14and brought Israel through the midst of it,
His love endures forever.
s-p15but swept Pharaoh and his army into the Red Sea;
His love endures forever.

s-p16to him who led his people through the wilderness;
His love endures forever.

s-p17to him who struck down great kings,
His love endures forever.
s-p18and killed mighty kings—
His love endures forever.
s-p19Sihon king of the Amorites
His love endures forever.
s-p20and Og king of Bashan—
His love endures forever.
s-p21and gave their land as an inheritance,
His love endures forever.
s-p22an inheritance to his servant Israel.
His love endures forever.

s-p23He remembered us in our low estate
His love endures forever.
s-p24and freed us from our enemies.
His love endures forever.
s-p25He gives food to every creature.
His love endures forever.

s-p26Give thanks to the God of heaven.
His love endures forever.This is following Psalm 135 NIV and before Psalm 137 NIV.
