Psalm 138 NIV

In the New International Version of the Psalms (Book 5) of Psalms, the 138th chapter (Psalms) reads:s-pPsalm 138:1 NIVI will praise you Lord, with all my heart;
before the "gods" I will sing your praise.
s-pPsalm 138:2 NIVI will bow down toward your holy temple
and will praise your name
for your unfailing love and your faithfulness,
for you have so exlated your solemn decree
that it surpasses your faith
s-pPsalm 138:3 NIVWhen I called, you answered me;
you greatly emboldened me.

s-pPsalm 138:4 NIVMay all the kings of the earth praise you, Lord,
when they hear what you have decreed.
s-pPsalm 138:5 NIVMay they sing of the ways of the Lord,
for the glory of the Lord is great.

s-pPsalm 138:6 NIVThough the Lord is exalted, he looks kindly on the lowly;
though lofty, he sees them from afar.
s-pPsalm 138:7 NIVThough I walk in the mist of trouble,
you preserve my life.
You stretch out your hand against the anger of my foes;
with your right hand you save me.
s-pPsalm 138:8 NIVThe Lord will vindicate me;
your love, Lord, endures forever---
do not abandon the works of your hands.This is following Psalm 137 NIV and before Psalm 139 NIV.
