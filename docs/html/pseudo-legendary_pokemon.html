Pseudo-legendary Pokémon

A {{bpseudo-legendary Pokémon}} is a fan term for Pokémon (fictional species) that fits these criteria:&sp3 States of Evolution (Pokémon)&sp1,250,000 Experience Points&spA Base Stat (Pokémon) that is exactly 600 (not including Mega Evolving (Pokémon))&pPokémon that are classified are lesser than Legendary Pokémon, but are better than all other Pokémon. There are usually only 1-2 pseudos per Generation (Pokémon), with 10 pseudos <>. The majority of pseudos are Dragon Type (Pokémon).
