Pseudo-legendary Pokémon

A pseudo-legendary Pokémon is a fan term for Pokémon that fits these criteria:
3 States of Evolution
1,250,000 exp
A base stat that is exactly 600 (not including Mega Evolving)

Pokémon that are classified are lesser than legendaries, but are better than all other Pokémon. There are usually only 1-2 pseudos per generation, with 10 pseudos <>. The majority of pseudos are Dragon type. This list includes:

Dragonite (from Dragonair and Dratini)
Tyranitar (from Pupitar and Larvitar)
Salamence (from Shelgon and Bagon)
Metagross (from Metang and Beldum)
Garchomp (from Gabite and Gible)
Hydreigon (from Zweilous and Deino)
Goodra (from Sliggoo and Goomy)
Kommo-o (from Hakamo-o and Jangmo-o)
Dragapult (from Drakloak and Dreepy)
Baxcalibur (from Arctibax and Frigibax)
