Pseudo-legendary Pokémon

A pseudo-legendary Pokémon is a fan term for Pokémon that fits these criteria:&sp3 States of Evolution&sp1,250,000 exp&spA base stat that is exactly 600 (not including Mega Evolving)&pPokémon that are classified are lesser than legendaries, but are better than all other Pokémon. There are usually only 1-2 pseudos per generation, with 10 pseudos <>. The majority of pseudos are Dragon type.
