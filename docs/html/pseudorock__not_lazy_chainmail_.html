Pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Pseudorock is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to create runes. It shares the same texture as bedrock, acting as a Survival-friendly stand-in for builds. Pseudorock can be melded in a Melder using 3 bedrock shards. Melding pseudorock will award the advancement Fake It 'Til You Make It. Pseudorock is used in the following melding recipes:
Acceleration Rune - 1 pseudorock, 1 block of redstone, and 1 piston
Ascension Rune - 1 pseudorock, 1 block of iron composite, and 1 nether star
Automated Permanence Rune - 1 pseudorock and 1 rose gold ingot
Experienced Rune - 1 pseudorock, 1 chromatic dust, and 1 bottle o' enchanting
Featherweight Rune - 1 pseudorock and 2 phantom membrane
Laceration Rune - 1 pseudorock, 1 crude engine, and 1 netherite chainmail industrial belt
Separation Rune - 1 pseudorock, 1 iron composite chain carpet, and 1 lapis lazuli
Shuffle Rune - 1 pseudorock and 1 telemagnetic alloy Pseudorock can be crushed into 3 bedrock shards.
