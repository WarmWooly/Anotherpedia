Punch (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

Punch is a primary tool within SCP: Devolved Revamp for Class D at level 1 attack other players. Upon usage, the player will be flagged for termination. It's most useful for rioting against other facility members and aiding Chaos Insurgency in freeing them.
