Pupil

<>{{tFor the other definition of pupil, see [[student]].}}&sp<>A {{bpupil}} in the [[eye]] is a black opening in the middle of the [[iris]]. The pupil is controlled by the [[muscles|Muscle]] in the iris.&pThe pupil will grow and shrink based on several [[stimuli]], notably [[light]]. [[Adrenaline]], [[arousal]], and [[brain injuries|Brain Damage]] can also be causes for pupil dilation.
