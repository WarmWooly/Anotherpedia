Pyrope Gemstone (Blue Skies)

<>A {{bpyrope gemstone}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} modding (Minecraft) {{iBlue Skies (Minecraft mod)}} used for making gold (Minecraft)-like tool (Minecraft) and armor (Minecraft) that are not debuffed in the Everbright (Blue Skies) and Everdawn (Blue Skies) dimension (Minecraft). Upon obtaining a pyrope gemstone, the advancement (Minecraft) A Swift Upgrade (Blue Skies) will be awarded. When Pyrope Ore (Blue Skies) is mining (Minecraft) with a stone pickaxe (Minecraft) or better, it will drop 1 pyrope gemstone. Mining pyrope ore with Fortune (Minecraft) increases the number of pyrope gemstones obtained. Silk Touch (Minecraft) will prevent the ore from dropping pyrope gemstones. Pyrope gemstones can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 block of pyrope (Blue Skies), which will produce 9 pyrope gemstones. Pyrope ore can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or blast furnace (Minecraft) to produce 1 pyrope gemstone, alongside < 30% chance).(text={{u-d0.7}}note>> experience (Minecraft).
