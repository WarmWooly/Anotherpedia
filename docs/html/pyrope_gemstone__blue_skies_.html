Pyrope Gemstone (Blue Skies)

<>A pyrope gemstone is an item in the Minecraft mod Blue Skies used for making gold-like tools and armor that are not debuffed in the Everbright and Everdawn dimensions. Upon obtaining a pyrope gemstone, the advancement A Swift Upgrade will be awarded. When pyrope ore is mined with a stone pickaxe or better, it will drop 1 pyrope gemstone. Mining pyrope ore with Fortune increases the number of pyrope gemstones obtained. Silk Touch will prevent the ore from dropping pyrope gemstones. Pyrope gemstones can be crafted with 1 block of pyrope, which will produce 9 pyrope gemstones. Pyrope ore can be smelted in a furnace or blast furnace to produce 1 pyrope gemstone, alongside < 30% chance).(text=u-d0.7note>> experience.
