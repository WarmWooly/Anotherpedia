Pyrope Gemstone (Blue Skies)

<resistance|Noinfo>>A pyrope gemstone is an item in the Minecraft mod Blue Skies used for making gold-like tools and armor that are not debuffed in the Everbright and Everdawn dimensions. Upon obtaining a pyrope gemstone, the advancement A Swift Upgrade will be awarded. When pyrope ore is mined with a stone pickaxe or better, it will drop 1 pyrope gemstone. Mining pyrope ore with Fortune increases the number of pyrope gemstones obtained. Silk Touch will prevent the ore from dropping pyrope gemstones. Pyrope gemstones can be crafted with 1 block of pyrope, which will produce 9 pyrope gemstones. Pyrope ore can be smelted in a furnace or blast furnace to produce 1 pyrope gemstone, alongside experience. The chances to find pyrope gemstones in chests are the following:
In a blinding dungeon, a study chest has a 44.5% chance of containing 1-3 pyrope gemstones.

&tabA key chest has a 57.8% chance of containing 1-2 pyrope gemstones. Pyrope gemstones are used in the following crafting recipes:
Block of Pyrope - 9 pyrope gemstones (fill the grid).
Pyrope Axe - 3 pyrope gemstones and 2 sticks (place the two sticks in a line upward, with the gemstone on top and another gemstone to the left/right of it, and the last gemstone below that).
Pyrope Boots - 4 pyrope gemstones (fill the bottom two rows besides the middle slots).
Pyrope Chestplate - 8 pyrope gemstones (fill all the rows besides the top middle slot).
Pyrope Helmet - 5 pyrope gemstones (fill the bottom two rows besides the bottom middle slot).
Pyrope Hoe - 2 pyrope gemstones and 2 sticks (place the two sticks in a line upward, with the gemstone on top and another gemstone to the left/right of it).
Pyrope Leggings - 7 pyrope gemstones (fill all the rows besides the bottom and center middle slots).
Pyrope Pickaxe - 3 pyrope gemstones and 2 sticks (place the two sticks in a line upward, with the gemstone on top and the other gemstones to the left and right of it).
Pyrope Shovel - 1 pyrope gemstone and 2 sticks (place the two sticks in a line upward, with the gemstone on top).
Pyrope Sword - 2 pyrope gemstones and 1 sticks (place the two gemstones in a line downward, with the stick on the bottom). Pyrope gemstones can be used in a smithing table to trim armor.
