Qwertyuiop (song)

"Qwertyuiop" is an instrumental guitar jazz by Chris Standring in 2008 for the studio album Love and Paragraphs released under his own label Ultimate Vibe Recordings. The song is 4 minutes and 16 seconds in length, primarily featuring the guitar, saxophone, and drums. "Qwertyuiop" is also a part of the compilation album Collector's Series.
