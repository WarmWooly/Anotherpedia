Radian

<><>A {{bradian}}, denoted with {{bθ}}, is a [[unit]] used as a [[measurement]] of an [[angle]] where 1 radian is [[equal]] to 1 unit on a [[unit circle]], or where the [[radius]] is equal to the [[arc length]]. Radians are used in [[mathematics]] and often have the [[abbreviation]] rad. A radian is equal to 180/[[π|pi]] [[degrees|degree (angle)]], or roughly 57.296 degrees.
