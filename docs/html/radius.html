Radius

<>{{tFor othe uses of the term radius, see [[radius (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>{{bRadius}}, denoted with {{br}}, is the [[distance]] between the [[center]] and [[circumference]] of a [[shape]] where the center is the same distance from all directions, such as a [[circle]] or a [[sphere]].
