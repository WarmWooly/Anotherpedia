Radius (bone)

<><><>The {{bradius}} is a [[bone]] in the [[forearm]], paired with the [[ulna]], forming the [[radio-carpel joint]] at the [[wrist]] and the [[radio-ulnar joint]] at the [[elbow]]. The purpose of the radius is to work with the ulna and the [[humerus]] at the elbow to provide [[supination]] and [[pronation]].
