Random Gun (Laser Fight)

The Random Gun is a randomized gun in Laser Fight. Because of the variety of stats that can be changed, the Random Gun can be amazing or complete dogwater depending on the fortune of the user. Once a Random Gun is spawned, its stats will not change. If the Random Gun has a bullet speed of 1150 studs/sec or more, the user will be awarded the badge Railgun.
