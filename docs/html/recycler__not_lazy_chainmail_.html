Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The recycler is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to recycle chain links. The recycler can be crafted with 3 blocks of iron chains, 1 lava bucket, and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with blocks of chains, put the lava in the center, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Crafting a recycler will award the advancement Reduce, Reuse, and.... Recyclers are used in the following crafting recipes:&spChain Generator - 1 recycler, 5 cobblestone, 2 rose gold ingots, and 1 crude engine (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The recycler requires an industrial belt to run, with better belts increasing the speed the recycler can recycle.
