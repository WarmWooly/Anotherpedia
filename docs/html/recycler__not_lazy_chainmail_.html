Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The recycler is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to recycle chain links. The recycler can be crafted with 3 blocks of iron chains, 1 lava bucket, and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with blocks of chains, put the lava in the center, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Crafting a recycler will award the advancement Reduce, Reuse, and.... Recyclers are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Generator - 1 recycler, 5 cobblestone, 2 rose gold ingots, and 1 crude engine (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The recycler requires an industrial belt to run, with better belts increasing the speed the recycler can recycle. These are the recipes that the recycler can be used to perform:
9 Copper Chain Links --> Copper Nugget
9 Gold Chain Links --> Gold Nugget
9 Iron Chain Links --> Iron Nugget
9 Iron Composite Chain Links --> Iron Composite Nugget
80 Netherite Chain Links --> Netherite Scrap
9 Rose Gold Chain Links --> Rose Gold Nugget
