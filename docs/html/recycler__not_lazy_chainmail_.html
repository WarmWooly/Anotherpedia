Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The recycler is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to recycle chain links. The recycler can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 block of iron chains (not lazy chainmail), 1 lava bucket (Minecraft), and 5 cobblestone (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with blocks of chains, put the lava in the center, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Crafting a recycler will award the advancement Reduce, Reuse, and... (Not Lazy Chainmail). Recyclers are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 recycler, 5 cobblestone (Minecraft), 2 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The recycler requires an industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) to run, with better belts increasing the speed the recycler can recycle. These are the recipes that the recycler can be used to perform:
9 Copper Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Copper Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)
9 Gold Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Gold Nugget (Minecraft)
9 Iron Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Iron Nugget (Minecraft)
9 Iron Composite Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Iron Composite Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)
80 Netherite Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Netherite Scrap (Minecraft)
9 Rose Gold Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> Rose Gold Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)
