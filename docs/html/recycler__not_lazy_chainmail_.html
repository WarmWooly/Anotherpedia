Recycler (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The recycler is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to recycle chain links. The recycler can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 block of iron chains (not lazy chainmail), 1 lava bucket (Minecraft), and 5 cobblestone (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with blocks of chains, put the lava in the center, fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). Crafting a recycler will award the advancement Reduce, Reuse, and... (Not Lazy Chainmail). Recyclers are used in the following crafting recipes:&spChain Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 recycler, 5 cobblestone (Minecraft), 2 rose gold ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone). The recycler requires an industrial belt (Not Lazy Chainmail) to run, with better belts increasing the speed the recycler can recycle.
