Red Seal ({{iBalatro}})

<>{{tNot to be confused with [[Red Stake ({{aiBalatro}})]].}}&sp<>A {{bRed Seal}} is a [[Seal|Seals (Balatro)]] for [[playing cards|playing cards (Balatro)]] in {{i[[Balatro]]}}. When a Red Seal is [[scored|scoring card (Balatro)]] or activated, the card is retriggered (adding [[chips|chips (Balatro)]] to the score, triggering [[Enhancements|Enhancements (Balatro)]] and [[Editions|Editions (Balatro)]], activating [[Jokers|Jokers (Balatro)]], [[etc]]). Red Seal can be obtained through the [[Spectral Card|Spectral Cards (Balatro)]] [[Deja Vu|Deja Vu (Balatro)]] or on cards randomly created in [[booster packs|booster packs (Balatro)]] or [[the Shop|The Shop (Balatro)]].
