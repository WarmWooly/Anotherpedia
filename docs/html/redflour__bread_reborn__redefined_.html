Redflour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Redflour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Redflour can be baked into redstone bread. Redflour can be obtained by milling blocks of redstone on a millstone. Each block of redstone makes 3 redflour. Redflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce redstone bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving < 30% chance).(text=u-d0.1note>> experience.
