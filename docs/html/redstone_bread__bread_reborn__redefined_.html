Redstone Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|No||Hunger|3 (1.5 shanks)||Saturation|1.2 (0.6 shanks)||Effects|Haste II (0:30)info>>Redstone bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Redflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce redstone bread, taking 10 or 5 second respectively and giving experience. Redstone bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, redstone bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
