Redstone Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

Redstone bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Redflour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce redstone bread, taking 10 or 5 second respectively and giving experience. Redstone bread can be used in a breadmaking station to make a bread platter, adding its stats to the platter. Redstone bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, redstone bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
