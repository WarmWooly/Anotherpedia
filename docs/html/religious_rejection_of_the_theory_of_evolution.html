Religious rejection of the theory of evolution

The religious rejection of the theory of evolution the opposition to the scientific theory of evolution because of their religious beliefs. Religion are diverse and spread across many culture, where there may be a contradiction between beliefs and religious scripture on the origin of life and the theory of evolution. Religions that may have religious follower that oppose evolution include (but are not limited to) Christianity and evolution, Islam and evolution, and Judaism and evolution.
