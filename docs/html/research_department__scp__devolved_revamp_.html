Research Department (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Research Department (RD) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who can run tests and experiements on Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp) to learn more about the SCPs (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The RD gets an authorize tool (SCP: Devolved Revamp), which they can use only on Class Ds to authorize them permission to go past the red line. RD members should focus on authorizing Class D and using them to run tests on various SCPs within Sectors 2 and 3. The RD has 5 levels, with each giving a new title. At levels 3 and 5, their keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp) access also increases.<> PTs spawn in a room labeled "Research Department" in Sector 2 of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The room requires a Level 2 Keycard to enter. The back wall of the room has many file cabinets, and the middle of the room has two tables and four work benches. On the work benches, there is coffee, papers, and scientific instruments. There are also two whiteboards in the room, one with lots of math and a derpy SCP-999 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and the other with a picture of Maxwell the Cat. Across the room, there is a corkboard with various papers thumb-tacked to it; there is also a plant in the corner of the room and the RD uniform by the front of the room. Two LED panel light up the room and by the RD door there is a TV with the following information:For Research Department:
- conduct tests on scps
- learn about recontainment methods
- provide research for mobile task forces
- ask site Security to escort your tests
- be careful of hostiles
- follow orders from administration
