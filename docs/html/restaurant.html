Restaurant

For buildings made for the express purpose of food creation and distribution, see virtual restaurant.&spA restaurant is a building where people can purchase food and drinks where the meals are prepared and served in the same building. Restaurants may also use take-out and delivery to increase the appeal and accessibility. There are many kinds of restaurant, such as fast-food, family, and fine dining, which each have their own pros and cons.
