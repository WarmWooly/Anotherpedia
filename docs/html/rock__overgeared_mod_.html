Every time the stone is knapped, the flint will have a chance to break. After a random amount of knapping, the stone will turn into [[cobblestone|cobblestone (Minecraft)]] and a rock will fall out.<><><>When using a rock in the [[main hand|main hand (Minecraft)]] and the [[offhand|offhand (Minecraft)]], the player can knap a rock into a stone tool head. Depending on the shape the player makes, a corresponding tool head will be formed. A rock from the main hand will be consumed by knapping. If the player cancels out while knapping, the rock will still be lost. While knapping, a menu will appear, allowing the player to click on parts of the rock to remove, with five different shapes corresponding to each tool head that will register.
