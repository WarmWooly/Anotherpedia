Rock, Paper, Showdown! (Bits & Blocks)

Rock, Paper, Showdown! is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Rock, Paper, Showdown! can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 paper, 1 shears, and 1 stone/cobblestone (shapeless). Rock, Paper, Showdown! is used in the following crafting recipes:
Jungle Mixtape - 1 Rock, Paper, Showdown!, 1 mixtape case, 1 Flipper Snapper, 1 Sweet Tooth, and 1 Pantry Parade (shapeless).
Rock Paper Scissors - 1 Rock, Paper, Showdown! and 1 bottle (shapeless).
