Rock, Paper, Showdown! (Bits & Bops)

Rock, Paper, Showdown! is the third minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is repeatedly playing rock paper scissors. The minigame has the player control a hand to play rock paper scissors. The player must hit Button 1 when the opponent is about to do their third shake, showing their move. The opponent can either shake at a normal rate (following the beat) or at a slow rate.

During the level, different items are used instead of the normal hand gestures, such as foam hands, physical rocks, paper, and scissors, and pencil toppers. Other actions, like fist bumping and holding each other's hands, are also performed. Near the end, the screen gets split into quarters multiple times, eventually leading to the combined total of all the games to make a skin color mosaic of the player's and opponent's hands.

If the player is on time, a thick outline will appear from the hand. If they are slightly off, the outline will be thinner. Being really off will cause the hand to look like broken scissors (too early/late) or an open palm (very off). If there is no input, a disappointing noise will play.
