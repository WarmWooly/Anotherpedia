Role (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

Roles are one of the biggest key features in SCP: Devolved Revamp as they are the main factor that differentiates players from each other. Depending on what role you choose to play as will determine what tasks you have to do and who your allies and enemies are. There are currently 8 different roles to choose from, those being: Class D (CD), Research Department (RD), Maintenance Staff (MS), Paramedic Team (PT), Site Security (SS), Chaos Insurgency (CI), Mobile Task Force (MTF), and Administrative Branch (AD). Every role has 5 levels to it, as stated in the SCP: Devolved Revamp page, and they all require the same amount of XP over a linear path to max out (900 XP). The level of keycard (KC) given covers the ones prior, ordered Class D, Level 1, Level 2, Level 3, and Level 4.
