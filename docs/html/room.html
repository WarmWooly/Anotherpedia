For an open area, see space.&spA room is a part of a building divided using wall and often a floor and ceiling. Rooms serve many purposes, mainly to partition the floorspace of a building into distinct section for different jobs. This may include rooms to sleep in (bedroom), places to cooking and eat food (kitchen and dining room), remove human waste (bathroom), relax (living room) in house.
