Room

For an open area, see space.
A room is a part of a building divided using walls and often a floor and ceiling. Rooms serve many purposes, mainly to partition the floorspace of a building into distinct sections for different jobs. This may include rooms to sleep in (bedrooms), places to create and eat food (kitchens and dining rooms), remove wastes (bathrooms), relax (living rooms) in houses.
