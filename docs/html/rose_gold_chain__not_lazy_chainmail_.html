Rose Gold Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The rose gold chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The rose gold chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Rose Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 rose gold chain links to craft. The rose gold chain can be crafted with a block of rose gold chains (makes 4). Rose gold chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Barrel Collector - 2 rose gold chains, 1 barrel, and 1 hopper (place the chains to the left and right of the barrel, then place the hopper above).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Block of Rose Gold Chains - 4 rose gold chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Chain Launcher - 1 rose gold chain, 1 (bright) spool block, 4 blocks of rose gold chains, 1 piston, and 1 lever (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Mangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Rose Gold Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 rose gold chains (fill the middle and bottom rows).
Rose Gold Lasso - 2 rose gold chains and 1 rose gold tool chain (place the tool chain in the top right corner and the chains in the middle and bottom right corner).
Rose Gold Rope x4 - 1 rose gold chain, 1 rose gold tool chain, and 1 iron ingot (place the tool chain in the middle, chain below, and iron above).
Spruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Warped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 rose gold chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems). Rose gold chains can be crushed into 8 rose gold chain links.
