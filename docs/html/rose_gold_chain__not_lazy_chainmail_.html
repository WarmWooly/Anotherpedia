Rose Gold Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The rose gold chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The rose gold chain can be created through chainmail project (Not Lazy Chainmail) with the settings "Rose Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 rose gold chain link (Not Lazy Chainmail) to craft. The rose gold chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with a block of rose gold chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4). Rose gold chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
