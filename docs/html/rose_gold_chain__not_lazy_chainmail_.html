Rose Gold Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The rose gold chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The rose gold chain can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Rose Gold Chain". The chain takes 8 rose gold chain links to craft. The rose gold chain can be crafted with a block of rose gold chains (makes 4).
