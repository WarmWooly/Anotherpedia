Rose Gold Chain Cover (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The rose gold chain cover is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Rose gold chain covers are used to upgrade chainmail armor in a leathersmithing table, making the armor type "Rose Gold". Rose gold chain covers can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 1 block of rose gold chains. In a leathersmithing table, rose gold chain covers can be applied to chainmail armor. While up to 3 can be applied to a single item, the effects do not stack. Any curios that scale based on worn rose gold chainmail armor will also scale for armor with a rose gold chain cover.
