Rose gold chain links can be snipping (Not Lazy Chainmail) using pliers (Not Lazy Chainmail) from a rose gold coil (Not Lazy Chainmail) or an unfinished rose gold project (Not Lazy Chainmail). While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Rose gold chain links can be obtained when a Crusher (Not Lazy Chainmail) crushes a rose gold chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 8 chain links).
