Rose Gold Chain Link (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The rose gold chain link is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in maille crafting. Rose gold chain links can be snipped using pliers from a rose gold coil or an unfinished rose gold project. While unfinished projects may be variable in size, coils always contain 64 chain links. Rose gold chain links can be obtained when a Crusher crushes a rose gold chain (makes 8 chain links). Rose gold chain links can be used with a chainmail project to maille craft the following items:
Music Disc "Tired" - 32 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chain - 8 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Belt - 128 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Boots - 256 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Bracelet - 64 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Chestplate - 512 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Gloves - 192 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Helmet - 320 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Industrial Belt - 256 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Chainmail Leggings - 448 rose gold chain link
Rose Gold Chainmail Necklace - 128 rose gold chain links
Rose Gold Tool Chain - 192 rose gold chain links For Expert-level chainmaillers, there is a 66.67% chance to trade 64 rose gold chain links for 6 emeralds. In a Recycler, 9 rose gold chain links can be recycled into a rose gold nugget. Rose gold chainmail armor enchanted with Chain Repair will automatically take a rose gold chain link from the inventory to repair durability when the wearer takes damage. Chain Repair can go up to IV, adding 2, 4, 8, and 12 durability per chain link consumed for levels I-IV respectively. The chain link will only be consumed when the maximum value of the chain link can be repaired. Rose gold chain links can be converted into Forge Energy in a chain generator. Using an iron composite chainmail industrial belt constantly fed with rose gold chain links, 40 FE/t can be generated.
