Rose Gold Chainmail Industrial Belt (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The rose gold chainmail industrial belt is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail and one of the eight industrial belts used to run machines. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts can be created through maille crafting with the settings "Rose Gold Industrial Belt". The rose gold chainmail industrial belt takes 256 rose gold chain links to craft. The chances to find a rose gold chainmail industrial belt in a winding jig are the following:&spIn a chainmailler home, there is a 9.09% chance to contain a rose gold chainmail industrial belt. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts are used in the following crafting recipes:&spRose Gold Chain Conveyor x16 - 1 rose gold chainmail industrial belt, 2 redstone dust, 1 redstone torch, and 2 rose gold chain carpets (place the industrial belt in the middle, the carpets to the left and right, the redstone in the bottom corners, and the torch in the middle-bottom slot). The rose gold chainmail industrial belt can be used to run the following machines:&spAuto Mailler&spAuto Snipper&spAuto Winder&spBelt Generator&spChain Generator&spCobblestone Generator&spConveyor Warper&spCrusher&spItem Aggregator&spMelder&spRecycler&spWinding Jig Rose gold chainmail industrial belts can be crushed into unfinished rose gold chainmail projects containing 256 chain links. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts can be repaired through combining by crafting, using a grindstone, or using an anvil. Combining rose gold chainmail industrial belts will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some experience returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the industrial belts, costing experience. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts can be enchanted with Unbreaking, Mending, and Curse of Vanishing using an enchanted book on an anvil. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts cannot be enchanted in an enchanting table. The rune Automated Permanence Rune can be cast on rose gold chainmail industrial belts to give Automated Permanence (unbreakable), costing 2 levels. Rose gold chainmail industrial belts can generate Forge Energy in a belt generator, generating 8 FE/t.
