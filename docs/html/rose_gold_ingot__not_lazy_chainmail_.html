Rose Gold Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The rose gold ingot is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting mid-tier chainmail materials and improved machines. There are multiple recipes to obtain rose gold ingots:&sp3 gold ingots and 1 copper ingot (shapeless) --> 2 rose gold ingots&sp9 rose gold nuggets --> 1 rose gold ingot&sp1 block of rose gold --> 9 rose gold ingots&pCrafting a rose gold ingot will award the advancement Pink is Pretty. Rose gold ingots can be melded in a Melder using 2 gold ingots and 1 copper ingot. Rose gold ingots are used in the following crafting recipes:&spBlock of Rose Gold - 9 rose gold ingots (fill the grid).&spChain Generator - 2 rose gold ingots, 5 cobblestone, 1 crude engine, and 1 recycler (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).&spConveyor Manager x4 - 4 rose gold ingots, 4 spool blocks, and 1 crude engine (place the engine in the middle, the spool blocks on the sides, and the rose gold in the corners).&spCrusher - 3 rose gold ingots, 4 cobblestone, 1 block of iron, and 1 piston (place the block of iron in the middle, piston above the iron, fill the bottom row with rose gold, then fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).&spLeathersmithing Table - 2 rose gold ingots, 2 leather, and 2 planks (place the planks on the bottom, then the rose gold above, then the leather on top).&spMelder - 3 rose gold ingots and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with rose gold, fill the rest of the grid besides the middle with cobblestone).&spRose Gold Nugget x9 - 1 rose gold ingot (place anywhere in grid).&spRose Gold Pliers - 2 rose gold ingots, 1 block of rose gold chains, and 2 sticks (place the block in the middle, then the rose gold above and to the right, and the sticks to the bottom and the left).&spRose Gold Spool - 8 rose gold ingots and 1 empty spool (place the spool in the middle, surround with rose gold). Rose gold ingots are used in the following melding recipes:&spAutomated Permanence Rune - 1 rose gold ingot and 1 pseudorock&spHealthy Leather Padding - 1 rose gold ingot and 1 leather padding In an anvil, rose gold ingots can be used to repair rose gold chainmail armor. Rose gold ingots can be used as an item to apply a selected power for a beacon, consuming one ingot. Rose gold ingots can be used in a smithing table to trim armor.
