Rose Gold Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{brose gold ingot}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used for crafting (Minecraft) mid-tier chainmail materials and improved machines. There are multiple recipes to obtain rose gold ingots:&sp3 gold ingot (Minecraft) and 1 copper ingot (Minecraft) (shapeless) --> 2 rose gold ingots&sp9 rose gold nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> 1 rose gold ingot&sp1 block of rose gold (Not Lazy Chainmail) --> 9 rose gold ingots&pCrafting a rose gold ingot will award the advancement Pink is Pretty (Not Lazy Chainmail). Rose gold ingots can be melded in a Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail) using 2 gold ingots and 1 copper ingot.
