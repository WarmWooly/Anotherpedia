Rose Gold Ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The rose gold ingot is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting mid-tier chainmail materials and improved machines. There are multiple recipes to obtain rose gold ingots:
3 gold ingots and 1 copper ingot (shapeless) --> 2 rose gold ingots
9 rose gold nuggets --> 1 rose gold ingot
1 block of rose gold --> 9 rose gold ingots

Crafting a rose gold ingot will award the advancement Pink is Pretty. Rose gold ingots can be melded in a Melder using 2 gold ingots and 1 copper ingot. Rose gold ingots are used in the following crafting recipes:
Block of Rose Gold - 9 rose gold ingots (fill the grid).
Chain Generator - 2 rose gold ingots, 5 cobblestone, 1 crude engine, and 1 recycler (place the recycler in the middle, then the ingots to the left and right and the engine on the bottom; fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Conveyor Manager x4 - 4 rose gold ingots, 4 spool blocks, and 1 crude engine (place the engine in the middle, the spool blocks on the sides, and the rose gold in the corners).
Crusher - 3 rose gold ingots, 4 cobblestone, 1 block of iron, and 1 piston (place the block of iron in the middle, piston above the iron, fill the bottom row with rose gold, then fill the rest of the grid with cobblestone).
Leathersmithing Table - 2 rose gold ingots, 2 leather, and 2 planks (place the planks on the bottom, then the rose gold above, then the leather on top).
Melder - 3 rose gold ingots and 5 cobblestone (fill the bottom row with rose gold, fill the rest of the grid besides the middle with cobblestone).
Rose Gold Nugget x9 - 1 rose gold ingot (place anywhere in grid).
Rose Gold Pliers - 2 rose gold ingots, 1 block of rose gold chains, and 2 sticks (place the block in the middle, then the rose gold above and to the right, and the sticks to the bottom and the left).
Rose Gold Spool - 8 rose gold ingots and 1 empty spool (place the spool in the middle, surround with rose gold). Rose gold ingots are used in the following melding recipes:
Automated Permanence Rune - 1 rose gold ingot and 1 pseudorock
Healthy Leather Padding - 1 rose gold ingot and 1 leather padding In an anvil, rose gold ingots can be used to repair rose gold chainmail armor. Rose gold ingots can be used as an item to apply a selected power for a beacon, consuming one ingot. Rose gold ingots can be used in a smithing table to trim armor.
