Rose Gold Nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The rose gold nugget is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting mid-tier chainmail materials. Rose gold nuggets can be crafted with a rose gold ingot, yielding 9 nuggets. Recycling 9 rose gold chain links will return a rose gold nugget.
