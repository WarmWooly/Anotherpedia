Rose Gold Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bgold ornate lamp}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as redstone (Minecraft)-powered light (Minecraft). The rose gold ornate lamp can be crafting (Minecraft) with 2 rose gold chain carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 redstone lamp (Minecraft) (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the rose gold ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the rose gold ornate lamp takes 0 tick (Minecraft) to turn off.
