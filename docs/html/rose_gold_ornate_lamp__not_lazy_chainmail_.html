Rose Gold Ornate Lamp (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The gold ornate lamp is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as redstone-powered lighting. The rose gold ornate lamp can be crafted with 2 rose gold chain carpets and 1 redstone lamp (place the lamp in the middle, then the carpets above and below). When powered, the rose gold ornate lamp produces light. Unlike redstone lamps, the rose gold ornate lamp takes 0 redstone ticks to turn off.
