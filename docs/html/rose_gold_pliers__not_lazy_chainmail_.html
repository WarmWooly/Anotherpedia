The rose gold pliers will still lose durability while automatically snipping.<>Rose gold pliers are ideally not used in combat. Each attack will cost 2 durability. Rose gold pliers deal 2 damage and have an attack cooldown of 1 (1 second or 20 [[ticks|tick (Minecraft)]]).<>Rose gold pliers can be repaired through [[combining|combining (Minecraft)]] by [[crafting|crafting (Minecraft)]], using a [[grindstone|grindstone (Minecraft)]], or using an [[anvil|anvil (Minecraft)]]. Combining rose gold pliers will add the two durability values together with an additional 3 durability. Combining will remove all enchantments, with some [[experience|experience (Minecraft)]] returned when combining in a grindstone. Combining in an anvil will keep and combine the enchantments on the rose gold pliers, costing experience.<>Rose gold pliers can be [[enchanted|enchanting (Minecraft)]] with [[Unbreaking|Unbreaking (Minecraft)]], [[Mending|Mending (Minecraft)]], and [[Curse of Vanishing|Curse of Vanishing (Minecraft)]], using an [[enchanted book|enchanted book (Minecraft)]] on an [[anvil|anvil (Minecraft)]].
