Rose Gold Sieve Mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The rose gold sieve mesh is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for sieving blocks into various resources. The rose gold sieve mesh is the middle tier, getting decent quantities of common items with a few unique drops. The rose gold sieve mesh can be crafted with 1 rose gold chain carpet and 8 sticks (place the carpet in the center and surround with sticks). For the sieving loot table and applicable blocks, see sieving (aiNot Lazy Chainmail).
Hold the rose gold sieve mesh in the offhand with the block to be sieved in the main hand. Upon using the rose gold sieve mesh, the block will be consumed, and any items collected will be placed in the inventory or on the ground if there is not enough space. The Sieve Dropper is a block that can be used to automatically sieve items that pass above on a chain conveyor. By inputting the rose gold sieve mesh, the valid blocks will automatically be sieved and dropped below. The rose gold sieve mesh will still lose durability while in a sieve dropper.
