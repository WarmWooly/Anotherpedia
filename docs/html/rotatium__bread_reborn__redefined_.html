Rotatium (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>{{bRotatium}} is a placeholder item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iBread Reborn: Redefined}} that is stored within millstones (Bread Reborn: Redefined) when rotated. A millstone can contain 0--7 rotatium, which it uses to allow redstone comparator (Minecraft) to output a redstone signal (Minecraft) based on the direction of the millstone. Rotatium increases while going clockwise and decreases while going counter-clockwise, with it resetting when the millstone is rotated back to its starting position. Rotatium can't be collected from a millstone, even when the millstone breaks.
