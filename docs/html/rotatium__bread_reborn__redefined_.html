Rotatium (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Rotatium is a placeholder item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined that is stored within millstones when rotated. A millstone can contain 0--7 rotatium, which it uses to allow redstone comparators to output a signal based on the direction of the millstone. Rotatium increases while going clockwise and decreases while going counter-clockwise, with it resetting when the millstone is rotated back to its starting position. Rotatium can't be collected from a millstone, even when the millstone breaks.
