RTX Off / RTX On

RTX Off / RTX On refers to internet meme between two image of similar content that have two levels of image detail, poking fun at the Nvidia RTX that can be used for improved ray tracing (graphics). The meme tends to take on the format of a side-by-side comparison with some normal image and a more realistic rendition/photograph of similar content. The format can also be used to joke about the content itself, such as having "RTX On" of a bad video game or person show up as a pile of garbage.
