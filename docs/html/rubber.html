Rubber

For other terms relating to rubber, see rubber (disambiguation).
Rubber is an elasticity material that can be made natural rubber (using latex sap) or Synthetic rubber (using Elastomer) that is used in a plethora of object, such as tire, rubber glove, rubber balloon, rubber band, eraser, sports ball, and more. The process to hardening rubber is known as vulcanization. Lots of rubber is black, but they can be easily rubber coloring.
