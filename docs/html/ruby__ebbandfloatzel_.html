Ruby (EbbAndFloatzel)

Ruby is a flow worm OC created by EbbAndFloatzel, which was posted to the Bits & Bops Community Hub on January 3, 2026. The artwork is one of the most popular artworks on the Community Hub, and the most popular depicting a flow worm <>. Ruby is a salmon-colored flow worm based on the model from the Sky Mixtape, sporting cyan detatched wings, a smaller halo, and very long antennae. She is directly given the traits "flirty", "cheerful", "forgetful", and "impatient", with the qualities of being an extroverted singer. Her halo loops onto her right antenna. With the label on the abdomen and the design of the character, it is suggestive in nature.
