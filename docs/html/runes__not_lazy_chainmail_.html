Runes (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Runes are a type of item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail. Runes are used for casting onto various tools and armor, applying a specified enchantment (Minecraft). There are two tiers of runes: those using smooth stone (Minecraft), and those using pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail). There are a total of 18 runes. All runes are obtained from melding in a Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail), with the tiers of runes split based on the base material used. The runes that use smooth stone (Minecraft) are Quick Erosion Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), Locating Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), Tracking Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), hunger endurance Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), nimble Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), shatter Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), chain repair Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), dampen rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), and muffle rune (Not Lazy Chainmail). The runes that use pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail) are automated permanence Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), separation Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), laceration Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), experienced Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), acceleration Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), featherweight Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), ascension Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail), and shuffle Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail). To cast a rune onto the item, place the rune in the offhand (Minecraft) and the item to enchant in the main hand (Minecraft), then use the rune.
