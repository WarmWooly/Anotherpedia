Runes ({{iNot Lazy Chainmail}})

<><>||{{bStats}}||{{b[[Rarity|Rarity (Minecraft)]]}}|Common<>||{{b[[Stack size|Stack size (Minecraft)]]}}|64||{{b[[Fire/lava&spresistance|Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)]]}}|Noinfo>>{{bRunes}} are a type of [[item|item (Minecraft)]] in the {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|mod (Minecraft)]] {{i[[Not Lazy Chainmail]]}}. Runes are used for casting onto various tools and armor, applying a specified [[enchantment|enchantment (Minecraft)]]. There are two tiers of runes: those using [[smooth stone|smooth stone (Minecraft)]], and those using [[pseudorock|pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. There are a total of 16 runes.<><>All runes are obtained from melding in a [[Melder|Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], with the tiers of runes split based on the base material used. The runes that use [[smooth stone|smooth stone (Minecraft)]] are [[quick erosion|Quick Erosion Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[locating|Locating Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[tracking|Tracking Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]],<> [[hunger endurance|hunger endurance Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[nimble|nimble Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[shatter|shatter Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and [[chain repair|chain repair Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]]. The runes that use [[pseudorock|pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] are [[automated permanence|automated permanence Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[separation|separation Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[laceration|laceration Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[experienced|experienced Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[acceleration|acceleration Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[featherweight|featherweight Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], [[ascension|ascension Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]], and [[shuffle|shuffle Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]].<><>To cast a rune onto the item, place the rune in the [[offhand|offhand (Minecraft)]] and the item to enchant in the [[main hand|main hand (Minecraft)]], then use the rune. Runes will consume [[experience|experience (Minecraft)]], with all but the [[ascension rune|ascension rune (Not Lazy Chainmail)]] being consumed as well.
