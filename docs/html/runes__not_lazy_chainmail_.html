Runes (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Runes are a type of item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Runes are used for casting onto various tools and armor, applying a specified enchantment. There are two tiers of runes: those using smooth stone, and those using pseudorock. There are a total of 16 runes. All runes are obtained from melding in a Melder, with the tiers of runes split based on the base material used. The runes that use smooth stone are quick erosion, locating, tracking, hunger endurance, nimble, shatter, and chain repair. The runes that use pseudorock are automated permanence, separation, laceration, experienced, acceleration, featherweight, ascension, and shuffle. To cast a rune onto the item, place the rune in the offhand and the item to enchant in the main hand, then use the rune.
