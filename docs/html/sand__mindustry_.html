Sand (Mindustry)

Sand is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir that can be collected from the ground with the Mechanical Drill or higher on Serpulo. Sand can also be obtained from a Disassembler or created through crushing Scrap in a Pulverizer. For Erekir, Sand can be harvested from walls using a Cliff Crusher. The description of Sand is the following:It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used solely for the production of other items, such as Silicon, Metaglass, and Phase Fabric.
