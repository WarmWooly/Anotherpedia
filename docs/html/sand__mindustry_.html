Sand (Mindustry)

{{bSand}} is an item (Mindustry) in {{iMindustry}} on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) that can be collected from the ground with the Mechanical Drill (Mindustry) or higher on Serpulo. Sand can also be obtained from a Disassembler (Mindustry) or created through crushing Scrap (Mindustry) in a Pulverizer (Mindustry). For Erekir, Sand can be harvested from walls using a Cliff Crusher (Mindustry). The description of Sand is the following:It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used solely for the production of other items, such as Silicon (Mindustry), Metaglass (Mindustry), and Phase Fabric (Mindusty).
