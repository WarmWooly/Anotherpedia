Sand ({{iMindustry}})

<><>{{bSand}} is an [[item|item (Mindustry)]] in {{i[[Mindustry]]}} on [[Serpulo|Serpulo (Mindustry)]] and [[Erekir|Erekir (Mindustry)]] that can be collected from the ground with the [[Mechanical Drill|Mechanical Drill (Mindustry)]] or higher on Serpulo. Sand can also be obtained from a [[Disassembler|Disassembler (Mindustry)]] or created through crushing [[Scrap|Scrap (Mindustry)]] in a [[Pulverizer|Pulverizer (Mindustry)]]. For Erekir, Sand can be harvested from walls using a [[Cliff Crusher|Cliff Crusher (Mindustry)]].
