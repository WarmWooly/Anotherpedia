Sandwich

For the term for squeezing, see sandwich (squeeze).
A sandwich is a food where two bread slices are used as the bottom and top of the sandwich, with any filling in between, such as meat, fruits, vegetables, cheeses, or spreads such as peanut butter, jelly, and marshmallow creme.
