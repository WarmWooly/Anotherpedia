Sans Obby

{{iSans Obby}} was a Roblox game by the user PRINCESSHOMSAPIEN. It was a chapter-based game with varying gameplay based on the chapter. Sans Obby is currently only playable by users who are friends with the developer's account and for users over the age of 16. The only playable chapter is Chapter 1: Sans Obby, with Chapter 0: Will I Hear The Clock Stop At The End, Chapter 2: Sans Incrimental, and Chapter 3: Sans May Cry X originally planned. Sans Obby has 3 badges, with only one being obtainable. Sans Obby also has a total of 6 gamepasses, 5 of which are solely for donations with no bearing on gameplay. Sans Obby was originally created on October 24th, 2020, but had received a massive revamp into a pre-alpha state on May 27th, 2024. Development would halt in May 2026 following a change to Roblox, which would have required buying a subscription to allow non-verified users or users under 16 to play. The plot of Sans Obby follows the player as they go on a quest to return Sans' ketchup, which was stolen by former United States president Bara O. Bama. Main page: {{aiChapter 1 (aiSans Obby)}}}}
The first chapter of the game is an unfair rage obby with a mostly linear path to follow. The goal is to make it to the other side of the map while avoiding having your character killed. If your character dies, they will be sent all the way back to the start of the game. However, players may be able to respawn at a checkpoint they have reached using the Checkpoints gamepass, which can be purchased for 50,000 Robux. This gamepass uses the image of a Trollface and has the description of:"Tired of having to redo the entire obby all over again? Tired of the horrifically brutal difficulty of this game? Well, for a small fee, you can get checkpoints!"The obstacles of Chapter 1 include kill bricks, parts with no collision, code doors, disappearing platforms, bottomless pits, puzzles, radiation, lethal green mystery fluid, and the player's own limits.
