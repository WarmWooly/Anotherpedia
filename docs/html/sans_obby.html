{{iSans Obby}} is a Roblox game by the user Conner101ful. It is a video game pacing with varying gameplay based on the chapter. Sans Obby is currently only playable by users who are connections (Roblox) with the developer's account. <>, the only playable chapter is Chapter 1: Sans Obby, with Chapter 0: Will I Hear The Clock Stop At The End, Chapter 2: Sans Incrimental, and Chapter 3: Sans May Cry X in progress. Sans Obby currently has 3 Badges (Roblox), with only one being currently obtainable. Sans Obby currently also has a total of 6 Gamepass (Roblox), 5 of which are solely for donations on Roblox with no bearing on gameplay. Sans Obby was originally created on October 24th, 2020, but had received a massive revamp into a pre-alpha state on May 27th, 2024. The plot of Sans Obby follows the player as they go on a quest to return Sans (Sans Obby) ketchup, which was stolen by former President of the United States Bara O. Bama (Sans Obby). Main page: {{aiChapter 1 (aiSans Obby)}}}}
The first chapter of the game is an unfair rage obby (Roblox) with a mostly linear path to follow. The goal is to make it to the other side of the map while avoiding having your character killed. If your character dies, they will be sent all the way back to the start of the game. However, players may be able to respawn at a checkpoint they have reached using the Checkpoints gamepass, which can be purchased for 50,000 Robux. This gamepass uses the image of a Trollface and has the description of:"Tired of having to redo the entire obby all over again? Tired of the horrifically brutal difficulty of this game? Well, for a small fee, you can get checkpoints!"The obstacles of Chapter 1 include Kill brick (Roblox), parts with no collision, code doors, disappearing platforms, bottomless pits, puzzles, radiation, lethal green mystery fluid, and the player's own limits.
