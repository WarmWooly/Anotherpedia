Satire

<>{{tNot to be confused with [[parody]].}}&sp<>{{bSatire}} is a [[genre]] of [[literature]] and [[art]] in which the [[writer]] or [[artist]] uses [[humor]], [[irony]], and [[exaggeration]] in order to expose [[shortcomings|shortcoming]] or [[vices]]. The main usage of satire is to [[ridicule]] or [[humble]] the [[creator]] of another [[work|artistic work]] or their [[actions|action]].
