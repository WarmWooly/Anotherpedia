For the character, see Satya ({{aiMagic: The Gathering).}}
Satya, Aetherflux Genius, or simply Satya, is a card in Magic: The Gathering that can be used as a commander (Magic: The Gathering). Satya's main gimmik comes from his ability to create copies of other controlled creatures with enough energy (Magic: The Gathering) to sustain the new token creatures on the field.
