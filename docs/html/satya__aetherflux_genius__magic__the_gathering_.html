Satya, Aetherflux Genius (Magic: The Gathering)

For the character, see Satya ({{aiMagic: The Gathering).}}
Satya, Aetherflux Genius, or simply Satya, is a card in Magic: The Gathering that can be used as a commander. Satya's main gimmik comes from his ability to create copies of other controlled creatures with enough energy to sustain the new token creatures on the field. Name: Satya, Aetherflux Genius
Mana Cost: 1*, 1 B, 1 R, 1 W
Types: Legendary, Creature, Human, Artificer
Card Text:Menace, hasteWhenever Satya, Aetherflux Genius attacks, create a tapped and attacking token that's a copy of up to one other target nontoken creature you control. You get two energy counters. At the beginning of the next end step, sacrifice that token unless you pay an amount of energy equal to its mana value.Power/Toughness: 3/5
Rarity: Mythic Rare
