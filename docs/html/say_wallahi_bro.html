Say wallahi bro

Say {{iwallahi bro}} (often stylized as "Say Wallahi bro") is an early-2026 internet meme of the Twitch streamer IShowSpeed yelling "Say wallahi bro" (where wallahi is an Arabic phrase for "by God" or "I swear (oath) to God"), which is being used similar to the slang "on God". It originated in 2023 when IShowSpeed bought a Classic clothing (Roblox) of Shrek (character) for over 18 million Robux, which was around $200,000 United States dollar. He reacted afterwards with an exaggerated form of buyer's remorse, crying while yelling phrases such as "Say you swear to God" and "Say wallahi". It would later be used in memes on TikTok in early 2026, for when information is obvious (especially with no name (brand) products) or shocking (surprise). Longer variations apply various voice changer effects on the phrase.
