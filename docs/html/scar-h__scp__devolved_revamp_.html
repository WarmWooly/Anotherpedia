SCAR-H (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>The SCAR-H is a primary guns (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Mobile Task Force (SCP: Devolved Revamp) at level 3. Despite a lower ammo capacity and slow firerate, its damage per bullet makes the SCAR-H effective in steady hands.
