SCP-409 (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>SCP-409, or Contagious Crystal, is a Keter (SCP: Devolved Revamp) SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp) located within Sector 3 of the Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp. SCP-409 is a blue crystal that is contained inside of a glass case in its containment cell. SCP-409 is currently stationary and inactive. SCP-409's room is located within Sector 3 of the Facility, with the door marked with "scp 409". The room itself has a floor comprised of a dark grey metal pattern, charcoal grey walls, a dark grey ceiling, and several fixtures. There is a desk with two chairs and two sets of file cabinets next to the desk, one having a monitor on top. There are also two storage containers on the back wall of the room. Two LED panel light up the room, and there is a viewing fence between the containment and viewing rooms. In the middle of the containment room lies a small stand with a glass case surrounding SCP-409, which also spawns inside this case. Two LED panels light up the room, and near the bottom of the wall, the number 409 can be seen.
