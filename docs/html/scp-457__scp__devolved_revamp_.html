SCP-457 (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<>SCP-457, or The Burning Man, is a Keter (SCP: Devolved Revamp) SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp) located within Sector 3 of the Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp. SCP-457 is a plain black humanoid figure that is on fire. SCP-457 will approach the nearest player (Roblox), jumping or occasionally teleporting while pathfinding. Upon contacting a player, it will deal damage to the player, hurting them. The player does not have to be moving for it to follow them. The team (SCP: Devolved Revamp) of the player does not change SCP-457's behaviour. SCP-457 can be shot and killed, but it will respawn shortly thereafter. SCP-457's room is located within Sector 3 of the Facility, with the door marked with "scp 457". The room itself has a floor comprised of a dark grey metal pattern, charcoal grey walls, a dark grey ceiling, and several fixtures. There are two fence barrier in the room alongside file cabinets, a monitor, and a locker. There is a viewing fence between the containment and viewing rooms. There are two LED panel that light up the room. The containment room has a burnt spot in the corner of the room where SCP-457 spawns, and near the bottom of the wall, the number 457 can be seen with burn marks on it. Two LED panels light up the room.
