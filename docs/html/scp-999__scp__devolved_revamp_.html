SCP-999 ({{iSCP: Devolved Revamp}})

<><>{{bSCP-999}}, or {{bThe Tickle Monster}}, is a [[Safe|Safe (SCP: Devolved Revamp)]] [[SCP|SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp)]] located within Sector 2 of the [[Facility|Map (SCP: Devolved Revamp)]] in {{i[[SCP: Devolved Revamp]]}}.<><>SCP-999 is an orange blob that always has a [[derpy]] [[smile]], a pair of black eyes, and arms when hugging. SCP-999 will approach the nearest [[player|player (Roblox)]], jumping or occasionally teleporting while [[pathing|pathfinding]]. Upon contacting a player, it will proceed to hug them, restoring [[health|health (Roblox)]] to all nearby players. The player must be moving or hurt for it to follow/hug. The [[team|team (SCP: Devolved Revamp)]] of the player does not change SCP-999's behaviour. SCP-999 can be shot and killed, but it will respawn shortly thereafter.<><>SCP-999's room is located within Sector 2 of the Facility, with the door marked with "scp 999". The room itself has a floor comprised of a [[checkerboard]] pattern of light and dark green, blue walls and ceiling with painted [[stars|star (symbol)]], and several fixtures. A gray pen sits in the middle, where SCP-999 spawns. A pair of 3-person benches lay to the sides, with a potted plant in three of the four corners. The far-left corner has a [[refrigerator]], including SCP-999 magnets (such as the one from [[Classic|SCP-999 (SCP: Devolved Classic)]]). The room is lit with two [[LED panels|LED panel]].<><>SCP-999 appears in other parts of {{iSCP: Devolved Revamp}}, including being a [[mascot]] for the game's icon. SCP-999 also appears on [[whiteboards|whiteboard]] as a [[doodle]] and as a magnet on the kitchen fridge.
