SCP-999 (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

SCP-999, or The Tickle Monster, is a Safe SCP located within Sector 2 of the Facility in SCP: Devolved Revamp. SCP-999 is an orange blob that always has a derpy smile, a pair of black eyes, and arms when hugging. SCP-999 will approach the nearest player, jumping or occasionally teleporting while pathing. Upon contacting a player, it will proceed to hug them, restoring health to all nearby players. While a player is near SCP-999, a pink overlay with particles will cover the screen as romantic music plays (known as a euphoric episode). The player must be moving or hurt for it to follow/hug. The team of the player does not change SCP-999's behaviour. SCP-999 can be shot and killed, but it will respawn shortly thereafter. SCP-999's room is located within Sector 2 of the Facility, with the door marked with "scp 999". The room itself has a floor comprised of a checkerboard pattern of light and dark green, blue walls and ceiling with painted stars, and several fixtures. A gray pen sits in the middle, where SCP-999 spawns. A pair of 3-person benches lay to the sides, with a potted plant in three of the four corners. The far-left corner has a refrigerator, including SCP-999 magnets (such as the one from Classic). The room is lit with two LED panels. By clicking on the middle '9' of "scp 999", the player will be teleported to the Archives. SCP-999 appears in other parts of SCP: Devolved Revamp, including being a mascot for the game's icon. SCP-999 also appears on whiteboards as a doodle and as a magnet on the kitchen fridge and on top of the 2025 Christmas tree.
