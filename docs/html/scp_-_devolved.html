SCP - Devolved

SCP - Devolved is a community on Roblox created by SneakySecretAgent for the experiences SCP: Devolved Classic (also known as SCP: Devolved) and SCP: Devolved Revamp. The community is affiliated with SCP: Devolved Events Team and Project Redrawn &vl SCP - Devolved Development. The community includes developers, testers, and fans of the experiences.
