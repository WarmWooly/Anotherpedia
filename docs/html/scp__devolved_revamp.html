SCP: Devolved Revamp

This page is a work in progress (Anotherpedia) and will be updated!&sp{{bSCP: Devolved Revamp}} is an SCP Foundation Roblox roleplay game developed by SCP - Devolved based on the Roblox game SCP: Devolved Classic. Players may select various role (SCP: Devolved Revamp), use tools and guns (SCP: Devolved Revamp), talk and fight with other players, and interact with various SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp). Main page: Role ({{aiSCP: Devolved Revamp)}}&spRoles are one of the largest things that got revamped in SCP: Devolved Revamp, completely changing the stats, XP required for ranking up, and reward system from SCP: Devolved Classic.&pEvery role in {{bSCP: Devolved Revamp}} has a total of 5 levels, each with its own rank name. Each level between roles requires the same amount of XP to achieve. The player earns 1 XP per minute of playtime for their respective role, and XP is cumulative (XP from the previous level is used for the next).
