SCP: Devolved Revamp

{{bSCP: Devolved Revamp}} is an SCP Foundation Roblox roleplay game developed by SCP - Devolved based on the Roblox game SCP: Devolved Classic. Players may select various role (SCP: Devolved Revamp), use tools and guns (SCP: Devolved Revamp), talk and fight with other players, and interact with various SCP (SCP: Devolved Revamp). SCP: Devolved Revamp released with a new map, simplified down into different sectors.

Outside the facility lies the Mobile Task Force (MTF) helipad and cave, the gate to Sector 1, gate and path to the Chaos Insurgency (CI) area, the CI Camp, and the CI facility.

Sector 1 contains the generator room, control room, maintenance room, medical room, admin room, office rooms, staff cafeteria, kitchen, and gates leading to outside and Sector 2.

Sector 2 contains the viewing room, research department, security department, armory, Class-D containment room, SCP-066 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-513 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-999 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-??? (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and gates leading to Sectors 1 and 3.

Sector 3 contains SCP-035 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-049 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-096 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-173 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-409 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-457 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), SCP-682 (SCP: Devolved Revamp), and gates leading to Sectors 2 and 4 (not yet added). Roles are one of the largest things that got revamped in SCP: Devolved Revamp, completely changing the stats, XP required for ranking up, and reward system from SCP: Devolved Classic.

Every role in {{bSCP: Devolved Revamp}} has a total of 5 levels, each with its own rank name. Each level between roles requires the same amount of XP to achieve. The player earns 1 XP per minute of playtime for their respective role, and XP is cumulative (XP from the previous level is used for the next). XP is also earned by killing other players (1 XP/kill).
