SCP: Devolved Revamp

This page is a work in progress and will be updated!&spSCP: Devolved Revamp is an SCP Roblox roleplay game developed by SCP - Devolved based on the Roblox game SCP: Devolved Classic. Players may select various roles, use tools and weapons, talk and fight with other players, and interact with various SCPs. The following are the changes and additions between Revamp and Classic:;;New map|Rank and level rehaul for all roles|Updated SCP UI and designs|New designs for the map and uniforms|New guns;; Main page: Map (aiSCP: Devolved Revamp)&spSCP: Devolved Revamp released with a new map, simplified down into different sectors.&pOutside the facility lies the Mobile Task Force (MTF) helipad and cave, the gate to Sector 1, gate and path to the Chaos Insurgency (CI) area, the CI Camp, and the CI facility.&pSector 1 contains the generator room, control room, maintenance room, medical room, admin room, office rooms, staff cafeteria, kitchen, and gates leading to outside and Sector 2.&pSector 2 contains the viewing room, research department, security department, armory, Class-D containment room, SCP-066, SCP-513, SCP-999, SCP-???, and gates leading to Sectors 1 and 3.&pSector 3 contains SCP-035, SCP-049, SCP-096, SCP-173, SCP-409, SCP-457, SCP-682, and gates leading to Sectors 2 and 4 (not yet added). Main page: Role (aiSCP: Devolved Revamp)&spRoles are one of the largest things that got revamped in SCP: Devolved Revamp, completely changing the stats, XP required for ranking up, and reward system from SCP: Devolved Classic.&pEvery role in SCP: Devolved Revamp has a total of 5 levels, each with its own rank name. Each level between roles requires the same amount of XP to achieve. The player earns 1 XP per minute of playtime for their respective role, and XP is cumulative (XP from the previous level is used for the next).
