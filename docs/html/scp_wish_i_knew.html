SCP Wish I Knew

SCP Wish I Knew or Ooh Wish I Knew is a meme originally created by Zachary Aghaizu on TikTok in December 2025 to promote his album SO CHOPPED. The meme depicts a black-and-white potentially-generated floating head with white glowing eyes lip synced to the song "OOH WISH I KNEW!" on a black background. It would be used in various memes in reference to the SCP Foundation as if it were an SCP (an anomalous creature) and become incorporated in brainrot. Within memes, it is either what someone turns into after an event or something that chases the poster of the meme. An example is the following:"Me lowkirkenuinly crashing out at hb 67 times before turning into SCP Wish I Knew and degloving them"
