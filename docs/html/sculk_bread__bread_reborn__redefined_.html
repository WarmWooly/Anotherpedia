Darkness (0:10)info>>Sculk bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Sculk flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce sculk bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience. Sculk bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, sculk bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full.
