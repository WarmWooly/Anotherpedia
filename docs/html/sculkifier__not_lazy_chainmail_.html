Sculkifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The sculkifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce sculk. Sculkifiers can be crafted using 1 deepifier, 4 sculk, and 4 echo shards (place the deepifier in the center, the sculk along the sides, and the shards in the corners). Sculkifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create sculk. Sculkifiers take 64x as long to generate one sculk compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
