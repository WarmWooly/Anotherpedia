Sculkifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bsculkifier}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce sculk (Minecraft). Sculkifiers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 deepifier (Not Lazy Chainmail), 4 sculk (Minecraft), and 4 echo shard (Minecraft) (place the deepifier in the center, the sculk along the sides, and the shards in the corners). Sculkifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create sculk (Minecraft). Sculkifiers take 64x as long to generate one sculk compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
