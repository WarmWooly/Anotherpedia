Sculkifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The sculkifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce sculk. Sculkifiers can be crafted using 1 deepifier, 4 sculk, and 4 echo shards (place the deepifier in the center, the sculk along the sides, and the shards in the corners). Sculkifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create sculk. Sculkifiers take 64x as long to generate one sculk compared to one cobblestone. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector alongside the 51 other cobblefiers, the player will be awarded the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
