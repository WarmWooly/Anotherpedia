Seals (Balatro)

Seals are Modifiers that can be added to playing cards in Balatro. Only one Seal can be on a card at a time. If a new Seal is added to a card that already has one, the previous Seal will be overwritten. Purchasing cards from the Store or from booster packs are ways to get cards with Seals, but they may also come from Spectral Cards. There are a total of 4 different Seals.<>
