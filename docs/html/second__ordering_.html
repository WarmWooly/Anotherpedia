Second (ordering)

Second is an element that is behind the first instance in a series. Anything second can't be before any other element besides first.&pIt is commonly seen as a near Succeed or even a Fail to be the second of something, such as the second to cross a finish line or the second to complete a task. Metals such as silver is used as a symbol for getting second place.
