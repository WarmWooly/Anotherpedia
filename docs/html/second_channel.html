Second channel

Not to be confused with two-channel.&spA second channel, secondary channel, or alternate channel is a YouTube channel for a YouTuber that is used to contain content that the creator deems requires another channel to contain. Second channels are used in a wide variety of ways, such as for posting different genres or quality of content, behind-the-scenes footage, vlogs, and VODs. Second channels tend to have fewer subscribers compared to the YouTuber's main channel.
