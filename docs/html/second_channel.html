Second channel

Not to be confused with two-channel.
A second channel, secondary channel, or alternate channel is a YouTube channel for a YouTuber that is used to contain YouTube content that the creator deems requires another channel to contain. Second channels are used in a wide variety of ways, such as for posting different YouTube genres or media quality of content, behind-the-scenes footage, vlog, and video-on-demand. Second channels tend to have fewer YouTube subscriber compared to the YouTuber's main channel.
