Serpulo (Mindustry)

Serpulo is a fictional planet in the indie game Mindustry and is currently the largest planet in the campaign (Mindustry). Serpulo was added to the game in 5.0 (Mindustry) to replace the previous campaign system. It's landscape is covered in mountain, tundra, salt plain, desert, ocean, and spore (Mindustry) plains. The enemy factions (Mindustry) Crux (Mindustry) has control of the planet at the start of the game, with the main objective to create an Interplanetary Accelerator (Mindustry) to spread to other planets (such as Erekir (Mindustry)).
