Serviceware

Serviceware is a software company based in Germany that specializes in service governance. Serviceware was founding in 1998 by Dirk Martin and Harold Popp, originally named PM Computer Services. Dirk Martin is the CEO of Serviceware, with Harold Popp as the CFO and Alexander Becker the COO. <>, Serviceware has 14 locations, including in Germany, Switzerland, England, Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, Bulgaria, and Austria, with their headquarters in Idstein.<>
