Seviper

{{bSeviper}} is a viper-like Poison type (Pokémon) Pokémon (fictional species) from the Generation III (Pokémon). Seviper has a black-ish covering of scales with an assortment of markings, including small yellow protuberance (bumps) along its belly, yellow hexagons with ovals inside along its back and top of its head, a small purple stripe near the end of its tail, and a large purple mark vaguely resembling a scar under its head. At the end of Seviper's body is a sharp triangular blade with a small cutout facing inward and a red edge outward. Seviper's jaw is yellow with two large red fangs extending downwards. The left side of its face is marked in purple. Seviper has two red eyes with yellow markings roughly around/above them. Seviper is often scrunched up, but it can extend its body into a flattened position.&pSeviper's official size is 8 foot (unit) and 10 inch, while weighing 115.7 pound. Shiny (Pokémon) Sevipers have green replace yellow, magenta replace purple, and blue replace red, with no changes to the body color.&pSeviper's name is a portmanteau of {{isever}} (or something similar) and {{iviper}}. Seviper's tail acts as a sharp blade that is able to secrete a lethal venom. It Sharpening blades on rocks its tail on rock to prepare for battle. Seviper tends to hide in tall grasses to strike prey with its fangs or blade. Sevipers and Zangoose are considered "mortal enemies" spanning generations, constantly fighting each other. A Seviper has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Seviper is the 336th Pokémon in the Pokédex (Pokémon) and has the Pokédex color black.&pSeviper has no evolution (Pokémon).
