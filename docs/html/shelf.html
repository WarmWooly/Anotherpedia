Shelf

For the term used to refer to the entirety of the storage system, see shelving.&spA shelf is any flat material, typically wood or metal, which is used for the expressed purpose of holding objects in homes, stores, offices, or other locations. They are raised from the floor and typically are anchored to a wall using brackets, screws, or other fixation tools. The size and material used for the shelf are dependent on the weight they need to support and the available space for the shelf.
