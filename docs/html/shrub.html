For other terms relating to bush, see [[bush (disambiguation)]].}}&sp<>A {{bshrub}} or {{bbush}} is a [[woody|woody plant]] [[perennial]] [[plant]] with multiple [[stems|stem]] under 20--30 [[ft|foot (unit)]]. Their size and stem requirements separate shrubs from [[trees|tree]], which are taller with a single stem. Shrubs are most prominent within [[shrublands|shrubland]], but they are also in [[forests|forest]], [[taigas|taiga]], and sometimes among [[greenery]] in [[gardens]].
