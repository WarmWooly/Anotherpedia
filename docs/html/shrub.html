Shrub

For other terms relating to shrub, see shrub (disambiguation).
"Bush" redirects here. For other terms relating to bush, see bush (disambiguation).
A shrub or bush is a woody perennial plant with multiple stems under 20--30 ft. Their size and stem requirements separate shrubs from trees, which are taller with a single stem. Shrubs are most prominent within shrublands, but they are also in forests, taigas, and sometimes among greenery in gardens.
