Shuckle

Shuckle is a turtle-like Bug and Rock type Pokémon from the second generation. Shuckle somewhat resembles a turtle, with a large red shell with four yellow legs and a head. The shell is rotund with several raised tan openings, alongside multiple tan oval spots. Out of four bottom openings are its noodle-like legs, with its bulbous head emerging from an opening towards the front of its shell. Shuckle's face has two simple line eyes and a mouth.

Shuckle's official size is 2 ft, while weighing 45.2 lbs. Shiny Shuckles have blue shells with silver openings.

Shuckle is a portmanteau of shuck and turtle. Shuckle stores berries in its shell, mixing them with its bodily fluids to make a pulpy juice. Its legs can secrete a liquid that can dissolve rock for nesting, which it also uses for its juice. Shuckle will hide under rocks in its shell to protect itself, feasting on its stored berries and juice. A Shuckle has a 50/50 chance to be male or female. Shuckle is the 213th Pokémon in the Pokédex and has the Pokédex color yellow.
