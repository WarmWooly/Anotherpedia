Sieving (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Sieving is a process to turn block (Minecraft) into more valuable item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail. There are three tiers of sieving, using the iron sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail), rose gold sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail), and iron composite sieve mesh (Not Lazy Chainmail) sieve meshes (Not Lazy Chainmail) respectively. Holding a sievable block with a sieve mesh in the offhand will destroy the block and give it a chance to return items based on the tier of the mesh. Not every tier is a direct improvement from the one prior, as certain items are only available at certain tiers. There are a maximum of four unique items that can be dropped from sieving.
