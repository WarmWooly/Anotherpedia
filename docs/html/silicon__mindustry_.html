Silicon (Mindustry)

Silicon is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir that can be produced in a Silicon Smelter or Silicon Crucible using Sand and Coal or Sand, Coal and Pyratite respectively on Serpulo. For Erekir, Silicon can be produced in a Silicon Arc Furnace using Sand and Graphite. The description of Silicon is the following:Used in solar panels, complex electronics and homing turret ammunition.It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in the early- to mid-game buildings, ammo, and units. It is also used to produce Surge Alloy.

On servers, Silicon is often depleted. This is followed shortly after with players spamming the chat with "Siligone" until the situation is resolved.
