Silicon (Mindustry)

Silicon is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) that can be produced in a Silicon Smelter (Mindustry) or Silicon Crucible (Mindustry) using Sand (Mindustry) and coal (Mindustry) or Sand, Coal and Pyratite (Mindustry) respectively on Serpulo. For Erekir, Silicon can be produced in a Silicon Arc Furnace (Mindustry) using Sand and Graphite (Mindustry). The description of Silicon is the following:Used in solar panels, complex electronics and homing turret ammunition.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used in the early- to mid-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry). It is also used to produce Surge Alloy (Mindustry).

On server (mindustry), Silicon is often depleted. This is followed shortly after with players chat spam the chat with "Siligone" until the situation is resolved.
