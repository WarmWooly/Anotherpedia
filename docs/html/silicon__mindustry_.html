Silicon (Mindustry)

Silicon is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir that can be produced in a Silicon Smelter or Silicon Crucible using Sand and Coal or Sand, Coal and Pyratite respectively on Serpulo. For Erekir, Silicon can be produced in a Silicon Arc Furnace using Sand and Graphite. The description of Silicon is the following:It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in the early- to mid-game buildings, ammo, and units. It is also used to produce Surge Alloy.&pOn servers, Silicon is often depleted.
