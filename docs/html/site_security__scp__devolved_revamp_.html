Site Security (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Site Security (SS) is a role (SCP: Devolved Revamp) within SCP: Devolved Revamp who guard and protect the facility. The SS get weapons across their levels, which they can use on Chaos Insurgency (SCP: Devolved Revamp) and rogue Class D (SCP: Devolved Revamp) players. SS members should focus on eliminating CI, preventing Class Ds from escaping, guarding SCP rooms and sectors, and protecting the facility. The SS has 5 levels, with each giving a new title and weapon. At levels 3 and 5, their keycards (SCP: Devolved Revamp) access also increases.<> SSs spawn in a room labeled "Site Security" in Sector 2 of the map (SCP: Devolved Revamp). The room requires a Level 2 Keycard to enter. The back wall of the room is covered with lockers with four benches in front and even more lockers in front of those benches. A storage container sits by some lockers containing a special Rapid Response Team (SCP: Devolved Classic) uniform for players who owned the RRT gamepass in SCP: Devolved Classic. Two LED panel and one big wall lamp light up the room. At the front wall of the room lies the Site Security uniform, a poster explaining alarm colors, and a TV with the following information:For Site Security:
- you are the first line of defense
- keep the inmates in check
- defend the site from hostiles
- protect the staff
- be careful of scps
- follow orders from administration
