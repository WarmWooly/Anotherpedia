Sky Mixtape (Bits & Blocks)

The Sky Mixtape is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the mixtape of the same name. It can be displayed on a mixtape stand. The Sky Mixtape can also be used on a flow worm to get its mixtape appearance. The Sky Mixtape can be crafted with 1 mixtape case, 1 B-Bot and the Fly Girls, 1 Flow Worms, 1 Meet & Tweet, and 1 Steady Bears (shapeless). The Sky Mixtape is used in the following crafting recipes:
Final Mixtape - 1 Sky Mixtape, 1 Jungle Mixtape, 1 Ocean Mixtape, and 1 Fire Mixtape (shapeless).
Rainbow Skates - 1 Sky Mixtape and 1 ice skates (shapeless).
Windblown Dancing Antenna - 1 Sky Mixtape and 1 dancing antenna (shapeless).
Wren Feather - 1 Sky Mixtape, 1 canary feather, and 1 blue dye (shapeless).
