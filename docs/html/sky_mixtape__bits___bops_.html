Sky Mixtape (Bits & Bops)

Sky Mixtape is the second mixtape within Bits & Bops, containing B-Bot and the Fly Girls, Flow Worms, Meet & Tweet, and Steady Bears. To match the sky theme, the following changes were made to each minigame:;;B-Bot is silver in color, with the Fly Girls wearing red and silver/blue clothing.|B-Bot and the Fly Girls are dancing on the wing of an airplane.|The flow worms are blue/yellow, with the player golden.|All the NPC flow worms have halos and small wings.|The flow worms are dancing atop clouds.|The wren and canary are chatting outside on branches.|Throughout the mixtape, time passes each time Meet & Tweet appears.|The bears are whiter with more saturated outfits.|The bears are skating along a large rainbow.;; The minigames appear in the following order: ::Meet & Tweet (day)|B-Bot and the Fly Girls|Flow Worms (day)|Steady Bears|B-Bot and the Fly Girls|Flow Worms (day to night)|Meet & Tweet (night)|Steady Bears|Flow Worms (night)|Meet & Tweet (dawn)|B-Bot and the Fly Girls|Flow Worms (day)|Meet & Tweet (day)::Before each segment of Steady Bears, a lead-up sound is given.
