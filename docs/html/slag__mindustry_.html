Slag (Mindustry)

Slagr is a liquid in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir and can be obtained through various means. On Serpulo, Slag can be collected by melting Scrap in a Melter. On Erekir, Slag can be collected through pools of slag tiles on the surface using a Reinforced Pump. The description of Slag is the following:It has an Exposiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Heat Capacity of 50%, a Viscosity of 70%, and a Temperature of 100%. On Serpulo, Slag can be used to create Copper, Lead, Graphite, Titanium, Sand, and Thorium, and can be used as a liquid ammo. On Erekir, Slag can be used to create Surge Alloy, to generate heat in a Slag Heater, to generate power in a Pyrolysis Generator, and to destroy items in a Slag Incinerator. When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit the Melting effect.
