Slag (Mindustry)

Slagr is a liquid (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) and can be obtained through various means. On Serpulo, Slag can be collected by melting Scrap (Mindustry) in a Melter (Mindustry). On Erekir, Slag can be collected through pools of slag tiles on the surface using a Reinforced Pump (Mindustry). The description of Slag is the following:Refined in separators into constituent metals.
Consumed in liquid turrets as ammunition.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a heat capacity (Mindustry) of 50%, a viscosity (Mindustry) of 70%, and a temperature (Mindustry) of 100%. On Serpulo, Slag can be used to create Copper (Mindustry), Lead (Mindustry), Graphite (Mindustry), Titanium (Mindustry), Sand (Mindustry), and Thorium (Mindustry), and can be used as a liquid ammo (Mindustry). On Erekir, Slag can be used to create Surge Alloy (Mindustry), to generate heat (Mindustry) in a Slag Heater (Mindustry), to generate power (Mindustry) in a Pyrolysis Generator (Mindustry), and to destroy item (Mindustry) in a Slag Incinerator (Mindustry). When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit (Mindustry) the Melting (Mindustry) effect.
