Slide (Laser Fight)

A slide is a movement action in Laser Fight triggered by pressing codeF while moving at or above 20 studs/sec while grounded. When trying to perform a slide in the air, a dive will be done instead. Sliding is pivotal for quickly navigating the battlefield and getting through small gaps, giving a large speed boost in a single direction. A slide can be interrupted by jumping or by allowing the slide to naturally decay. Holding sprint (codeShift) will increase the duration of the slide. Sliding will slow down over time, ending when the slide is less than the player's walkspeed + 1. A slide awards 1 exp. A slide costs 10 stamina to use and can't be performed when the player has less than 12 stamina. Holding an Ice Ray increases the slide speed multiplier from 3 to 4 and decreases the slide momentum decay from 20% to 10%. After landing a fall break, sliding afterwards will perform a roll instead, being a faster, yet shorter burst of speed. Rolling awards 1 exp. If the fall break is after a dive (a belly flop), sliding afterwards will perform a belly roll, which gives 2 exp. Main page: Lethal ollie/nollie ({{aiLaser Fight)}}
If the player slides at a high enough speed into a ramp, they can be launched far into the air. While this is often dangerous, leading to the possibility of taking fall damage, it looks pretty sick. If the player manages to launch themselves 100 studs in the air from their slide starting position, a lethal nollie will be preformed, awarding 12 exp and the badge Lethal Ollie. When chaining a slide into a dive into a second slide into a second dive (slide --> dive --> slide --> dive), a speed demon will be preformed, awarding 5 exp. Sliding after a wall climb will perform a wall slide, awarding 3 exp. When chaining a vault into a slide into a dive (vault --> slide --> dive), a hop, skip, and a jump will be preformed, awarding 5 exp.
