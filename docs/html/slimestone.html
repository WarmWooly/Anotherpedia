Slimestone

For the {{aiGoBots character, see Slimestone (aiGoBots).}}
Slimestone is a fan term for redstone contraptions utilizing slime/honey blocks, sticky pistons, and observers to create motion in Minecraft. Slime and honey blocks can drag various other blocks and can be pushed by pistons, allowing for machines that automatically update and push themselves along. Flying machines, farms, doors, and other contraptions can be made or optimized utilizing slimestone.
