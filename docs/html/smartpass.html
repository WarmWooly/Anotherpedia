SmartPass

<><>{{bSmartPass}} is a [[website]] and [[software]] used to create and manage [[hall passes|hall pass]] in [[schools|school]], primarily [[middle|middle school]] and [[high schools|high school]]. These passes can be requested by [[students|student]], where [[teachers|teacher]] or [[staff|school staff]] can accept or deny them. These passes can be given [[time limits|time limit]] to prevent extended periods with a student outside of [[class]]. They can also prevent certain students from being in the same place at the same time.
