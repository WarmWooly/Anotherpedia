Smooth Stone Flushwork Body (Knapped)

<>The smooth stone flushwork Body is a smooth stone flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The smooth stone flushwork Body can be cut from any other smooth stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The smooth stone flushwork Body can be cut into any other smooth stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
