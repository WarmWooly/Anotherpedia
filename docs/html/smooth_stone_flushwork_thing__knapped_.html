Smooth Stone Flushwork Thing (Knapped)

<>The smooth stone flushwork Thing is a decorative block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The smooth stone flushwork Thing can be crafted with 1 smooth stone, 2 knapped flint bricks (item), and 1 enchanted golden apple (shapeless).
