Snakedown (Bits & Blocks)

Snakedown is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Snakedown can be crafted with 1 minigame case, 1 decorated pot, and 1 gold ingot (shapeless). Snakedown is used in the following crafting recipes:
Fire Mixtape - 1 Snakedown, 1 mixtape case, 1 President Bird, 1 Octeaparty, and 1 Globe Trotters (shapeless).
Pot Lid - 1 Snakedown and 1 shield (shapeless).
