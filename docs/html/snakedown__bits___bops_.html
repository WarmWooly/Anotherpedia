Snakedown (Bits & Bops)

Snakedown is the fourteenth minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a snake working with a monkey to take back the money from the snakecharmer. The minigame has the player controlling the snake in a pot, popping the lid up with their head. The player must hit Button 1 to intercept a coin or gem. The monkey can throw one coin or three coins at a time, and gems will appear from off-screen with different timing.

During the level, the carpets will start flying. In the air, the monkey will start taking photos of themself while only gems appear. The clouds will then become thick, only showing silhouettes of the characters and the valuables. Near the end, the monkey panics, pulling on its tail, waking up the snakecharmer. The snakecharmer falls as the monkey throws one last coin from off-screen.

If the player lands on time, a blue burst will appear from the pot lid. If they are slightly off, a yellow burst will appear. Being really off will cause the coins to be missed with a burst to the left (early) or right (late). If there is no input, the valuables will fall off-screen.
