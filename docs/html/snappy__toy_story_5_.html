Snappy (Toy Story 5)

<>
Snappy is a minor character in Toy Story 5 who supports Jessie, Buzz, and Woody alongside Atlas and Smarty Pants. She is voice acted by Shelby Rabara and is owned by Blaze in the movie. Snappy is a small pink digital camera with yellow and purple accents. She has one large eye in the form of an adjustable yellow lens with a screen and a yellow-and-purple tripod for legs. Her red right button is for capturing, and the yellow left dial is for adjusting. She has purple rubber grips with stars on her sides, with an eyebrow-like purple rubber grip atop. Her backside is a traditional camera screen with button controls. Snappy can be connected to Smarty Pants with a cable, which allows them to send pictures that Snappy takes. While Jessie is getting batteries for Smarty Pants in Blaze's house, they also find Atlas and Snappy in a drawer. After giving them batteries as well, Snappy and Atlas join them upstairs. While hiding, they witness Blaze getting rejected by another friend, which inspires Jessie to connect her with Bonnie. As Smarty Pants is connected to the same service as Lily and Snappy can connect to him, Snappy takes a photo of Jessie to get Bonnie to meet Blaze.

Jessie initiates playtime with Blaze, with Snappy becoming one of the participants---her first time experiencing such. Within the playtime, Snappy is shown to act as a fly on the wall above the ball.

Later, when Lily throws herself onto a moving truck, Snappy coordinates with Atlas and Smarty Pants to show Lily's sent photos of street signs to pinpoint her location.
