Social media

<><>{{bSocial media}} refers to [[Web 2.0]] [[Internet]] [[applications|internet application]] where [[user-generated content]] can be shared and spread with some form of [[profile|user profile]] system to give [[people]] a way to maintain an image. The primary use is to [[connect|social networking]] people together over the application. Some common examples of social media are [[Twitter]], [[Facebook]], and [[YouTube]].
