Sodium triacetoxyborohydride

Sodium triacetoxyborohydride, abbreviation as STAB, is a colorless salt used in organic reaction. Sodium triacetoxyborohydride's chemical formula is Sodium[(CarbonHydrogen3COxygenO)3BoronH]. Sodium triacetoxyborohydride is flammable, corrosive, irritation to the skin and eyes, and is dangerous to digest, alongside reproduction harm.
