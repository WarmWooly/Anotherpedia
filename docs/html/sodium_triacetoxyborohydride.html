Sodium triacetoxyborohydride

Sodium triacetoxyborohydride, abbreviated as STAB, is a colorless salt used in organic reactions. Sodium triacetoxyborohydride's chemical formula is Na[(CH3COO)3BH]. Sodium triacetoxyborohydride is flammable, corrosive, irritative to the skin and eyes, and is dangerous to digest, alongside reproductive harm.
