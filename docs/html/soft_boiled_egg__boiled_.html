Soft Boiled Egg (Boiled)

The soft boiled egg is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. A soft boiled egg can be obtained by boiling an egg in a boiler. It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. A soft boiled egg can be boiled into a hard boiled egg in a boiler, taking 5 seconds at 2,000 heat and awarding 0.1 exp. Soft boiled eggs can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
