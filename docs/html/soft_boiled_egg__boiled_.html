Soft Boiled Egg (Boiled)

<>The soft boiled egg is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). A soft boiled egg can be obtained by boiling an egg (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 5 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). A soft boiled egg can be boiled into a hard boiled egg (Boiled) in a boiler (Boiled), taking 5 seconds at 2,000 heat and awarding 0.1 experience (Minecraft). Soft boiled eggs can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
