Sorter art ({{iMindustry}})

<>{{bSorter art}} is the [[act|action]] of using [[sorters|sorter (Mindustry)]] to [[draw|drawing]] on the [[minimap|minimap (mindustry)]]. When a sorter is placed, it's selected [[item|item (Mindustry)]] is displayed as a solid color on the minimap, instead of the color of the [[team|team (mindustry)]] who placed it. Sorter art is most prevalent on [[servers|server (mindustry)]], where they can be seen by anyone. Sorter art can also be used to [[grief|griefing]], such as displaying distasteful imagery on the minimap, alongside using copious amounts of [[Copper|Copper (Mindustry)]] and [[Lead|Lead (Mindustry)]].<><>
