Sorter art (Mindustry)

Sorter art is the action of using sorter (Mindustry) to drawing on the minimap (mindustry). When a sorter is placed, it's selected item (Mindustry) is displayed as a solid color on the minimap, instead of the color of the team (mindustry) who placed it. Sorter art is most prevalent on server (mindustry), where they can be seen by anyone. Sorter art can also be used to griefing, such as displaying distasteful imagery on the minimap, alongside using copious amounts of Copper (Mindustry) and Lead (Mindustry).
