Soul Over AI

Soul Over AI was an anti-AI tool website that tracking and categorization various musician who utilized generative artificial intelligence to make or edit AI music generation, originally created by the GitHub user xoundbyte in October 2025 at <>. It would later be closed in mid-February 2026 by xoundbyte due to the increased difficulty of determining the line between AI and human-made content and the project's growing project scale.
