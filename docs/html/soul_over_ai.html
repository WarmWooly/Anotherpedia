Soul Over AI

Soul Over AI was an anti-AI website that tracked and categorized various musicians who utilized AI to make or edit music, originally created by the GitHub user xoundbyte in October 2025 at <>. It would later be closed in mid-February 2026 by xoundbyte due to the increased difficulty of determining the line between AI and human-made content and the project's growing scale. The website Zoundhub would be made by xoundbyte the following March, which does not explicitly make any claims about the authenticity of an artist's works. The link for Soul Over AI redirects to Zoundhub <>.
