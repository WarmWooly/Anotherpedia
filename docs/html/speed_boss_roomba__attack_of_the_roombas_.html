Speed Boss Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

<Kills|30 killsinfo>>The Speed Boss Roomba is a boss enemy in Attack of the Roombas, being faster than their Normal counterpart. The Speed Boss Roomba will only appear once, and upon defeating it, will grant the player an increased camera view and movement speed.
