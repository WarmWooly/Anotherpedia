Sphere

For the venue, see Sphere (venue).
A sphere is a 3D shape defined by being of equal distance, as set by the radius, away from a single point, known as the center. It can be described as being a projection of a 2D circle. The distance going from one side of the sphere to the other, crossing the center, is known as its diameter. The surface area of a sphere can be calculated by multiplying pi with 4 and the squared radius (SA = 4&mpir2). The volume of a sphere can be calculated by multiplying pi by 4/3 and the cubed radius (V = 4/3&mpir3).

Spheres with a radius of 1 are known as unit spheres.
