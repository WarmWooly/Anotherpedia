SpineNeck

SpineNeck was a massager made by SpineDeck. It was used to reduce tension in the neck by rocking the head back and forth across the aparatus. SpineDeck claimed that it could reduce neck pain, headaches, and bad posture in 10 minutes of use. It is no longer available for purchase <>. The SpineNeck had a rating of 4.8/5 stars.
