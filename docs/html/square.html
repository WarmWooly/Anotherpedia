Square

<><>A {{bsquare}} is a [[2D|second dimension]] [[shape]] with 4 [[points|point (geometry)]] connected with 4 [[lines|line]], where each line is the same [[length]] and each form a [[90° angle|right angle]]. The [[width]] is [[equal]] to its length. The [[perimeter]] is four [[times|multiplication]] the length. The [[area]] is the length multiplied by itself.
