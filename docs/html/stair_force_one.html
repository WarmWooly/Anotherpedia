Stair Force One

<><>{{bStair Force One}}, also known as {{bChair Force One}}, refers to [[Joe Biden's|Joe Biden]] [[difficulty climbing|Joe Biden falling]] up the [[stairs]] onto [[Air Force One]], often depicting Joe Biden on a [[stairlift]] up the stairs. It has since become a [[meme|internet meme]] popular in the [[United States]], mocking the [[president|President of the United States]] for their [[old age]]. It was most likely sparked because of Biden's change to use the shorter stairs to get onto Air Force One alongside the now-iconic [[trip|tripping on stairs]] that Biden had early into his presidency.
