Stakes (Balatro)

Stakes are modifiers to Decks (Balatro) in Balatro used to increase the difficulty of run (Balatro) for the player. Each Stake adds to the previous Stake's effect. Stakes are also required for the full completion of Balatro. To play a Stake on a Deck, all prior Stakes must be completed (to play on the Green Stake, the Red and White Stakes must have been completed first). Each Deck will track Stakes separately from one another. When completing a Deck on a Stake, it will be given a Stake Stickers (Balatro) based on the Stake completed. These Stickers will also be applied to Jokers (Balatro) if they were in the player's Joker slots in the final hand of the run. There are a total of 8 different Stakes.<>
