Standard Packs (Balatro)

Standard Packs are a type of Booster Pack in Balatro that give playing cards. There are three tiers of Standard Packs: Standard Packs, Jumbo Standard Packs, and Mega Standard Packs. There are eight unique designs for the Standard Packs. The potential playing cards that can be drawn may come with Enhancements, Editions, and/or Seals.<>
