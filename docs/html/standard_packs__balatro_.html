Standard Packs (Balatro)

Standard Packs are a type of Booster Pack in Balatro that give playing cards. There are three tiers of Standard Packs: Standard Packs, Jumbo Standard Packs, and Mega Standard Packs. There are eight unique designs for the Standard Packs.
