Standard Packs (Balatro)

{{bStandard Packs}} are a type of Booster Packs (Balatro) in {{iBalatro}} that give playing cards (Balatro). There are three tiers of Standard Packs: Standard Packs, Jumbo Standard Packs, and Mega Standard Packs. There are eight unique designs for the Standard Packs.
