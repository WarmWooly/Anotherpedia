States of matter

<><>There are currently 5 accepted {{bstates of matter}} (from least [[energetic|Energy]] to most): [[Bose-Einstein condensate]], [[solid]], [[liquid]], [[gas]], and [[plasma]]. These represent the form taken by nearly all [[matter]] in the [[universe]], which is useful for predicting how they [[interact|Interaction]] with themselves and other [[objects|object]].
