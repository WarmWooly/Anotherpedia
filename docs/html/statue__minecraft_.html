Statue (Minecraft)

For the block, see Copper Golem Statue ({{aiMinecraft).}}
A statue in Minecraft is a build that resembles a person, animal, or character. Statues may be of the player's skin to directly depict the player within the world. The size of statues varies, from tiny models vaguely resembling a figure to massive megabuilds that tower over terrain. Statues can be incorporated as part of a greater build or be the main feature. Statues can also be used as bases, leading to a unique interior structure based around the statue's form.
