Steady Bears (Bits & Bops)

Steady Bears is the eight minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a boy polar bear skating with a girl. The minigame has the boy skating in sync with the girl. The player must hit Button 1 to skate, following a fixed rhythm. Two types of bells can play: two quick steps and two off-beat steps, with the player matching the pattern. During the level, the camera zooms in and out, with additional skaters joining in midway through the level.

If the player is on time, pink strikes emit from the skates. If they are slightly off, yellow strikes will appear. Being really off will cause the boy to fall forward (early) or backward (late). If the input is completely off, the boy will fall face-forward. If there is no input, the boy will fall onto his behind.
