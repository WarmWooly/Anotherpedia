Steam Community

The Steam Community is a collection of Internet forums based on games hosted on Steam. Each game-specific forum is called a Community Hub. It is split up into different tabs, including discussion threads, screenshots, artwork, broadcasts, videos, news (posted by the game owner/manager), guides, and reviews. It also has an "All" tab that shows the most popular posts and recent news. Content within the Community Hub is generally geared towards the game it is posted to, with user-made guides, accomplishments, memes, and fan art prominent.
