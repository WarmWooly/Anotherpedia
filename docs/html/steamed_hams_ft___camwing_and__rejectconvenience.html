Steamed Hams ft. @camwing and @rejectconvenience

"Steamed Hams ft. @camwing and @rejectconvenience" is a YouTube video made by Ceject Ronvenience (the second channel to Reject Convenience) and camwing on November 29, 2025, where camwing and Reject Convenience are animation in a custom recreation of Steamed Hams. camwing takes place of Gary Chalmers (The Simpsons) and Reject Convenience of Seymour Skinner. Unlike the normal segment, the video does feature a aurora borealis, which Reject Convenience allows camwing to see. The video is 2 minute and 27 second in length. It was originally at the end of Reject Convenience's "How to do anything (without AI)".
