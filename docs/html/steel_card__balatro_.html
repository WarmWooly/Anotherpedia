Steel Card (Balatro)

A Steel Card is an Enhancement for playing cards in Balatro. When a Steel Card is held in the hand during scoring, it gives X1.5 Mult. This effect stacks for every Steel Card in the hand. The Tarot Card The Chariot can be used to turn 1 card into a Steel Card. The Joker Steel Joker gains X0.2 Mult for every Steel Card in the full deck.
