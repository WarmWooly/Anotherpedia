Steel Plate (Ad Astra mod)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Ad Astra used in many crafting recipes required for spaceflight. The following recipes in a compressor are used to make steel plates:
Steel Block --> 9 Steel Plates (800 ticks; 40 seconds)
Steel Ingot --> Steel Plate (100 ticks; 5 seconds) Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
Airlock - 7 steel plates and 2 steel blocks (place the steel blocks in the bottom-left and top-right corners and fill the remaining grid with plates).
