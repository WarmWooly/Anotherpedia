Steel Plate (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked)

<>The {{bsteel plate}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iAdvanced Rocketry - Reworked}} used in crafting rocket parts, circuit boards, and machines. In a rolling machine (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked), a steel ingot (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked) and 100 millibucket of water (Minecraft) can be rolled into a steel plate. The process requires 6000 redstone flux (Minecraft modding) over 15 seconds (20 RF/tick (Minecraft)). In a small plate presser (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked), a steel block (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked) can be pressed into 4 steel plates.
