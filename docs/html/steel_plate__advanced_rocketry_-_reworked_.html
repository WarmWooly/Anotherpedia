Steel Plate (Advanced Rocketry - Reworked)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Advanced Rocketry - Reworked used in crafting rocket parts, circuit boards, and machines. In a rolling machine, a steel ingot and 100 mB of water can be rolled into a steel plate. The process requires 6000 RF over 15 seconds (20 RF/t). In a small plate presser, a steel block can be pressed into 4 steel plates. Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
Advanced Motor - 3 steel plates, 2 gold coils, and 2 steel rods (place a rod in the center, a rod to its left, the coils above and below, and the plates in the right column).
