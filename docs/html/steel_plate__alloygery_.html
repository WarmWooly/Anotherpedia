resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Alloygery used in crafting steel armor plating. Steel plates can be smithed from a steel ingot in a smithing anvil with a steel hammer or better. Steel plates are used in the following smithing recipes with a steel hammer or better:
2 Steel Plates --> Steel Boots Plating
4 Steel Plates --> Steel Chestplate Plating
3 Steel Plates --> Steel Legging Plating
2 Steel Plates --> Steel Helmet Plating
