Steel Plate (Alloygery)

<>||Stats||Rarity (Minecraft)|Common||Stack size (Minecraft)|64||Fire-resistant item (Minecraft)|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Alloygery used in crafting steel armor plating. Steel plates can be smithed from a steel ingot (Alloygery) in a smithing anvil (Alloygery) with a steel hammer (Alloygery) or better. Steel plates are used in the following smithing anvil (Alloygery) recipes with a steel hammer (Alloygery) or better:
2 Steel Plates --> Steel Boots Plating (Alloygery)
4 Steel Plates --> Steel Chestplate Plating (Alloygery)
3 Steel Plates --> Steel Legging Plating (Alloygery)
2 Steel Plates --> Steel Helmet Plating (Alloygery)
