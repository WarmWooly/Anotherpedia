Steel Plate (GregTechCEu Modern)

<>The {{bsteel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod GregTechCEu Modern used in many early-game LV recipes. The steel plate can be crafted using 2 steel/magentic steel ingots and a hammer (place the hammer in the top-middle slot and the ingots below). A magnetic steel plate can be smelted in a furnace to turn it into a steel plate. In a bender with the programmed circuit set to 1, a steel/magnetic steel ingot can be bent into a steel plate. The process requires 1,344 EU over 2.8 seconds (24 EU/t) on LV.<> In a cutter, a block of steel/magnetic steel can be cut into 9 steel plates with the following fluids:&sp31 mB of Distilled Water - 20,160 EU over 33.6 secs (30 EU/t) on LV.&sp10 mB of Lubricant - 13,440 EU over 22.4 secs (30 EU/t) on LV.&sp42 mB of Water - 26,880 EU over 44.8 secs (30 EU/t) on LV. In an extruder with an extruder mold (plate), a steel/magnetic steel ingot can be extruded into a steel plate. The process requires 3,136 EU over 2.8 seconds (56 EU/t) on MV. In a fluid solidifier with a casting mold (plate), 144 mB of liquid steel can be solidified into a steel plate. The process requires 280 EU over 2 secs (7 EU/t) on ULV. In a forge hammer, 3 steel/magnetic steel ingots can be forged into 2 steel plates. The process requires 896 EU over 2.8 secs (16 EU/t) on LV. In a macerator, a steel plate will be ground into steel dust. The process requires 448 EU over 2.8 secs (8 EU/t) on LV. In an arc furnace, a steel plate with 56 mB of oxygen gas will be scrapped into a steel ingot. The process requires 1,680 EU over 2.8 secs (30 EU/t) on LV. In a polarizer, a steel plate will be magnetized into a magnetic steel plate. The process requires 896 EU over 2.8 secs (16 EU/t) on LV. In an extractor, a steel plate will be melted into 144 mB of liquid steel. The process requires 6,720 EU over 2.8 secs (120 EU/t) on MV.
