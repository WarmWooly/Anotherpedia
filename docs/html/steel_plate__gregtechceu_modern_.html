Steel Plate (GregTechCEu Modern)

<>The {{bsteel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) GregTechCEu Modern used in many early-game low voltage (GregTechCEu Modern) recipes. The steel plate can be crafting (Minecraft) using 2 steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern)/magnetic steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern) and a hammers (GregTechCEu Modern) (place the hammer in the top-middle slot and the ingots below). A magnetic steel plate (GregTechCEu Modern) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) to turn it into a steel plate. In a bender (GregTechCEu Modern) with the programmed circuit set to 1, a steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern)/magnetic steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern) can be bent into a steel plate. The process requires 1,344 Energy unit (GregTechCEu Modern) over 2.8 seconds (24 EU/tick (Minecraft)) on low voltage (GregTechCEu Modern). In a cutter (GregTechCEu Modern), a block of steel (GregTechCEu Modern)/block of magnetic steel (GregTechCEu Modern) can be cut into 9 steel plates with the following fluid (GregTechCEu Modern):
31 millibucket of Distilled Water (GregTechCEu Modern) - 20,160 EU over 33.6 secs (30 EU/t) on LV.
10 mB of Lubricant (GregTechCEu Modern) - 13,440 EU over 22.4 secs (30 EU/t) on LV.
42 mB of Water (Minecraft) - 26,880 EU over 44.8 secs (30 EU/t) on LV. In an extruder (GregTechCEu Modern) with an extruder mold (plate) (GregTechCEu Modern), a steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern)/magnetic steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern) can be extruded into a steel plate. The process requires 3,136 EU over 2.8 seconds (56 EU/t) on medium voltage (GregTechCEu Modern). In a fluid solidifier (GregTechCEu Modern) with a casting mold (plate) (GregTechCEu Modern), 144 mB of liquid steel (GregTechCEu Modern) can be solidified into a steel plate. The process requires 280 EU over 2 secs (7 EU/t) on ultra low voltage (GregTechCEu Modern). In a forge hammer (GregTechCEu Modern), 3 steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern)/magnetic steel ingot (GregTechCEu Modern) can be forged into 2 steel plates.
