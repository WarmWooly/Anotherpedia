Steel Plate (Immersive Engineering)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Immersive Engineering used in producing various steel-based crafting components, the metal press molds, and steel armor. A steel plate can be crafted with a steel ingot and an engineer's hammer (shapeless). Placing a steel ingot onto a metal press with a metal press mold: plate will produce a steel plate. Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
Extended Magazine - 2 steel plates, 4 copper wires, and 1 iron mechanical component (place the component in the bottom-left corner, the plates in the center and top-right corner, and the wires on the sides).
Focused Nozzle - 2 steel plates, 2 fluid pipes, and 1 iron mechanical component (place the component in the bottom right corner, the plates above and to the side of it, and the pipes in the center and top-left slots).
Heavy Plated Shield - 6 steel plates, 2 treated wood planks, and 1 shield (place the shield in the center, the planks above and below it, and the plates in the left and right columns).
Large Tank - 4 steel plates, 1 fluid pipe, and 2 red dye (place the pipe in the top-left corner, the dyes in the center and bottom-right corner, and the plates on the sides).
Razor Wire x3 - 1 steel plate, 3 steel wires, and 2 treated wood fences (place the plate in the center, the wires to the left, right, and below of it, and the fences in the bottom corners).
Reinforced Blast Brick - 1 steel plate and 1 blast brick (shapeless).
Revolver Drum - 4 steel plates and 1 steel rod (place the rod in the center and the plates to the sides).
Rock Softening Acid - 3 steel plates, 2 buckets of redstone acid, 2 fluid pipes, and 1 steel mechanical component (place the component in the bottom-right corner, the pipes to the left, the plates on the left, right, and top sides, and the buckets in the center and top-left slots).
Rockcutter Blade - 4 steel plates and 4 diamonds (place the diamonds in the corners and the plates on the edges).
Sawblade - 4 steel plates and 4 steel ingots (place the ingots in the corners and the plates on the edges).
Steel Boots - 4 steel plates (place two sheets in the bottom corners and two above those).
Steel Chestplate - 8 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the top middle slot).
Steel Helmet - 5 steel plates (fill the middle and bottom rows besides the bottom middle slot).
Steel Leggings - 7 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the center middle and bottom middle slots).
Steel Mechanical Component - 4 steel plates and 1 copper ingot (place the copper in the center and the plates in the corners).
Steel Sheetmetal x4 - 4 steel plates (place the plates on the edges in a circle).
Steel Wire - 1 steel plate and 1 engineer's wire cutters (shapeless). In an engineer's workbench, the following items can be produced using steel plates:
Banner Pattern Plating - 1 steel plate, 1 paper, and 1 engineer's blueprint Banner Patterns
Metal Press Mold: Bullet Casing/Gear/Packing 2x2/Packing 3x3/Plate/Rod/Unpacking/Wire - 3 steel plates, 1 engineer's wire cutters, and 1 engineer's blueprint Metal Press Molds
Steel Mechanical Component - 2 steel plates, 1 copper ingot, and 1 engineer's blueprint Crafting Components In an Arc Furnace, a steel plate can be recycled into a steel ingot.
