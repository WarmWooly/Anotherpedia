Steel Plate (Immersive Engineering)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Immersive Engineering used in producing various steel ingot (immersive engineering)-based crafting components, the metal press molds (Immerseive Engineering), and steel armor (immersive engineering). A steel plate can be crafting (Minecraft) with a steel ingot (Immersive Engineering) and an engineer's hammer (Immersive Engineering) (shapeless). Placing a steel ingot (Immersive Engineering) onto a metal press (Immersive Engineering) with a metal press mold: plate (Immersive Engineering) will produce a steel plate. Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
Extended Magazine (Immersive Engineering) - 2 steel plates, 4 copper wire (Immersive Engineering), and 1 iron mechanical component (Immersive Engineering) (place the component in the bottom-left corner, the plates in the center and top-right corner, and the wires on the sides).

Reinforced Blast Brick (Immersive Engineering) - 1 steel plate and 1 blast brick (Immersive Engineering) (shapeless).
