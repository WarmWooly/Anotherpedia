Steel Plate (Immersive Engineering)

<>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Immersive Engineering used in producing various steel-based crafting components, the metal press molds, and steel armor. A steel plate can be crafted with a steel ingot and an engineer's hammer (shapeless). Placing a steel ingot onto a metal press with a metal press mold: plate will produce a steel plate.
