Steel Plate (IndustrialCraft 2)

<>The {{bsteel plate}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iIndustrialCraft 2}} used in crafting advanced parts and machines. In a block cutting machine (IndustrialCraft 2), a steel block (IndustrialCraft 2) can be cut into 9 steel plates. In a metal former (IndustrialCraft 2) set to "Rolling", a steel ingot (IndustrialCraft 2) can be rolled into a steel plate. In a compressor (IndustrialCraft 2), 9 steel plates can be compressed into a dense steel plate (IndustrialCraft 2). In a metal former (IndustrialCraft 2) set to "Rolling", a steel plate can be rolled into 2 steel item casing (IndustrialCraft 2).
