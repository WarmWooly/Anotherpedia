Steel Plate (IndustrialCraft 2)

<>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod IndustrialCraft 2 used in crafting advanced parts and machines. In a block cutting machine, a steel block can be cut into 9 steel plates. In a metal former set to "Rolling", a steel ingot can be rolled into a steel plate. Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:&spAdvanced Machine Casing - 4 steel plates, 2 advanced alloy, 2 carbon plates, and 1 basic machine casing (place the casing in the center, the carbon plates above and below, the alloy to the left and right, and the steel plates in the corners).&spBlock Cutting Blade (Steel) - 8 steel plates and 1 iron ingot (place the ingot in the center and surround it with plates).&spIridium Storage Box - 4 steel plates and 4 iridium reinforced plates (place the steel plates on the sides and the iridium reinforced plates in the corners).&spJetpack Attachment Plate - 1 steel plate, 2 advanced alloy, 2 carbon plates, and 4 iridium shards (place the steel plate in the center, the alloy above and below, the carbon plates to the left and right, and the shards in the corners).&spLarge Steel Pipe - 6 steel plates (fill the left and right columns with plates).&spMedium Steel Pipe x2 - 6 steel plates (fill the top and bottom rows with plates).&spSteel Rotor Blade - 6 steel plates and 3 steel ingots (fill the middle column with ingots and the left and right columns with plates).&spSteel Storage Box - 4 steel plates and 4 steel item casings (place the item casings on the sides and the plates in the corners).&spSteel Tank - 4 steel plates and 4 universal fluid cells (place the cells on the sides and the plates in the corners).&spSteel Turbine Blade - 8 steel plates and 1 steel ingot (place the ingot in the center and surround it with plates). In a compressor, 9 steel plates can be compressed into a dense steel plate. In a metal former set to "Rolling", a steel plate can be rolled into 2 steel item casings.
