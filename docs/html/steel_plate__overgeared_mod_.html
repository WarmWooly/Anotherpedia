Steel Plate (Overgeared mod)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Overgeared used in crafting steel armor and the cauldron. The steel plate can be smithed by hitting (using) a smithing hammer on a steel ingot placed in a smithing anvil 4 times. Steel plates can't be smithed on a stone smithing anvil. Steel plates are used in the following smithing recipes:
Cauldron - 7 steel plates (place in a U shape; requires 5 hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Boots - 4 steel plates (place two sheets in the bottom corners and two above those; requires 3 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Chestplate - 8 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the top middle slot; requires 5 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Helmet - 5 steel plates (fill the middle and bottom rows besides the bottom middle slot; requires 3 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Leggings - 7 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the center middle and bottom middle slots; requires 4 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
