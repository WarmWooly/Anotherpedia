Steel Plate (Overgeared mod)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Overgeared (Minecraft mod) used in crafting steel armor (Overgeared mod) and the cauldron (Minecraft). The steel plate can be smithed by hitting (using) a smithing hammer (Overgeared mod) on a steel ingot (Overgeared mod) placed in a smithing anvil (Overgeared mod) 4 times. Steel plates can't be smithed on a stone smithing anvil (Overgeared mod). Steel plates are used in the following smithing recipes:
Cauldron (Minecraft) - 7 steel plates (place in a U shape; requires 5 hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Boots (Overgeared mod) - 4 steel plates (place two sheets in the bottom corners and two above those; requires 3 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Chestplate (Overgeared mod) - 8 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the top middle slot; requires 5 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Helmet (Overgeared mod) - 5 steel plates (fill the middle and bottom rows besides the bottom middle slot; requires 3 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
Steel Leggings (Overgeared mod) - 7 steel plates (fill the entire grid besides the center middle and bottom middle slots; requires 4 timed hits on a smithing anvil).
