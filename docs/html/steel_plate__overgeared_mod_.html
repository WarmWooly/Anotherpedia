Steel Plate (Overgeared mod)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Overgeared (Minecraft mod) used in crafting steel armor (Overgeared mod) and the cauldron (Minecraft). The steel plate can be smithed by hitting (using) a smithing hammer (Overgeared mod) on a steel ingot (Overgeared mod) placed in a smithing anvil (Overgeared mod) 4 times. Steel plates can't be smithed on a stone smithing anvil (Overgeared mod).
