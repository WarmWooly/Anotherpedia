Steel Plate (Railcraft Reborn)

<>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Railcraft Reborn used in crafting steel-based machines and decorative train blocks. The steel plate can crafted with 4 steel ingots (make a 2x2 square; makes 4). Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:&spDumping Track Kit x4 - 1 steel plate, 1 track parts, 1 planks, and 1 redstone dust (shapeless).&spElectric Locomotive - 1 steel plate, 2 charge motors, 1 nickel-zinc/nickel-iron battery, 2 steel gears, 1 minecart, and 1 redstone lamp (place the battery in the center, the plate above it, the motors to the left and right, the minecart below, the gears in the bottom corners, and the lamp in the top-left corner).&spFeed Station - 1 steel plate, 4 golden carrots, and 4 planks (place the plate in the center, the carrons on the sides, and the planks in the corners).&spFrame x10 - 3 steel plates and 5 rebar (fill the top row with plates, then fill the middle and bottom rows besides the center middle slot with rebar).&spHigh Pressure Steam Boiler Tank x2 - 2 steel plates and 1 invar plate (place the invar plate in the center and the steel plates above and below).&spSteam Oven x4 - 8 steel plates and 1 furnace (place the furnace in the center and surround it with plates).&spSteam Turbine Housing - 4 steel plates, 4 blocks of steel, and 1 charge motor (place the motor in the center, the plates on the sides, and the blocks in the corners).&spWhite Steel Tank Gauge x8 - 4 steel plates and 5 glass panes (place the glass in the center and corners and the plates on the sides).&spWhite Steel Tank Valve x8 - 4 steel plates, 4 iron bars, and 1 lever (place the lever in the center, the plates on the sides, and the bars in the corners).&spWhite Steel Tank Wall x8 - 4 steel plates (make a 2x2 square). Steel plates are used in the following manual/powered rolling machine recipes:&spSteel Electrode - 3 steel plates (fill the middle column).&spTurbine Blade - 3 steel plates (place plates in the center, the bottom-left corner, and the top-right corner).
