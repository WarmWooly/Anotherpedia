Steel Plate (Railcraft Reborn)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Railcraft Reborn used in crafting steel-based machines and decorative train block (Minecraft). The steel plate can crafting (Minecraft) with 4 steel ingot (Railcraft Reborn) (make a 2x2 square; makes 4). Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
