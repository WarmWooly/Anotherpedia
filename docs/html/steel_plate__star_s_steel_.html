Steel Plate (Star's Steel)

<>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Star's Steel used in crafting steel-based decorative blocks. The steel plate can crafted with 3 steel nuggets (fill the middle row; makes 2). Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:&spFramed Block of Iron x3 - 8 steel plates and 1 block of iron (place the iron in the center and surround it with plates).&spPlated Block of Steel x8 - 9 steel plates (fill the grid).&spSteel Girder x4 - 5 steel plates (place plates in the center and the corners).
