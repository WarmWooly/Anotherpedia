Steel Plate (Tech Reborn)

<>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Tech Reborn used in crafting advanced machines and industrial storage. In a compressor, a steel ingot or steel block can be compressed into 1 and 9 steel plates respectively. The process requires 3000 E over 15 seconds (10 E/t).
