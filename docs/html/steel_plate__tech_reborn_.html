Steel Plate (Tech Reborn)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Tech Reborn used in crafting advanced machines and industrial storage. In a compressor (Tech Reborn), a steel ingot (Tech Reborn) or steel block (Tech Reborn) can be compressed into 1 and 9 steel plates respectively. The process requires 3000 E (Tech Reborn) over 15 seconds (10 E/tick (Minecraft)). Steel plates are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Advanced Machine Casing (Tech Reborn) x1/x4 - 6 steel plates, 2 advanced circuit (Tech Reborn), and 1 standard machine casing (Tech Reborn)/advanced machine frame (Tech Reborn) (place the casing/frame in the center, the circuits to the left and right, and the plates in the top and bottom rows).
Advanced Unit Upgrader (Tech Reborn) - 3 steel plates, 1 advanced storage unit (Tech Reborn), 1 digital display (Tech Reborn), 2 industrial circuit (Tech Reborn), and 1 industrial machine frame (Tech Reborn) (place the frame in the center, the unit to the left, the display below, the circuits in the bottom corners, and the plates in the top row).
Industrial Storage Unit (Tech Reborn) - 3 steel plates, 2 advanced storage unit (Tech Reborn), 1 digital display (Tech Reborn), 2 industrial circuit (Tech Reborn), and 1 industrial machine frame (Tech Reborn) (place the frame in the center, the units to the left and right, the display below, the circuits in the bottom corners, and the plates in the top row).
Industrial Tank Unit (Tech Reborn) - 3 steel plates, 2 advanced tank unit (Tech Reborn), 1 digital display (Tech Reborn), 2 industrial circuit (Tech Reborn), and 1 industrial machine frame (Tech Reborn) (place the frame in the center, the tanks to the left and right, the display below, the circuits in the bottom corners, and the plates in the top row).
Vacuum Freezer (Tech Reborn) - 4 steel plates, 2 advanced circuit (Tech Reborn), 2 extractor (Tech Reborn), and 1 reinforced glass (Tech Reborn) (place the glass in the center, the extractors above and below, the circuits to the left and right, and the plates in the corners).
