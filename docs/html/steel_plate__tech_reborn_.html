Steel Plate (Tech Reborn)

<>The steel plate is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Tech Reborn used in crafting advanced machines and industrial storage. In a compressor (Tech Reborn), a steel ingot (Tech Reborn) or steel block (Tech Reborn) can be compressed into 1 and 9 steel plates respectively. The process requires 3000 E (Tech Reborn) over 15 seconds (10 E/tick (Minecraft)).
