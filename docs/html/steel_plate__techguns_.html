resistance|Noinfo>>The steel plate is an item in the Minecraft mod Techguns used in crafting advanced machines and industrial storage. In a metal press, 2 steel ingots can be pressed into 2 steel plates. Steel plates are used in the following crafting recipes:
Armor Upgrade: Projectile Protection I - 4 steel plates, 4 steam armor plating, and 1 gold ingot (place the ingot in the center, the armor plating on the sides, and the steel plates in the corners).
Armor Upgrade: Protection I - 4 steel plates, 4 iron plates, and 1 gold ingot (place the ingot in the center, the iron plates on the sides, and the steel plates in the corners).
AUG - 1 steel plate, 1 assault rifle magazine, 1 hardened barrel, 1 plastic gun stock, 1 plastic sheet, 1 steel receiver, and 2 glass panes (place the receiver in the center, the barrel to the left, the stock to the right, the plate above, then going clockwise from the top-left corner: pane, pane, magazine, and sheet).
Chainsaw Blades (Obsidian) - 2 steel plates and 3 mechanical parts (hardened) (place the plates in the top and bottom slots, and the parts in the right corners and left side slot).
Charging Station - 4 steel plates, 1 circuit board, 2 coils, and 2 gold wires (place the board in the center, the wires above and below, the coils to the left and right, and the plates in the corners).
Combat Knife - 1 steel plate and 1 plastic sheet (place the sheet in the center and the plate in the top-right corner).
Crowbar - 3 steel plates and 1 steel nugget (place a plate in the center, plates in the bottom-left and top-right corners, and the nugget on the top side).
Fabricator Housing x4 - 4 steel plates, 2 cybernetic parts, 1 electric engine, 1 elite circuit board, and 1 mechanical parts (carbon) (place the engine in the center, the mechanical parts above, the board below, the cybernetic parts to the left and right, and the plates in the corners).
Grenade Launcher - 1 steel plate, 1 hardened barrel, 1 plastic gun stock, and 1 steel receiver (place the receiver in the center, the barrel to the left, the stock to the right, and the plate below).
Pistol - 1 steel plate, 1 hardened barrel, 1 mechanical parts (hardened), 1 pistol magazine, and 2 plastic sheets (place the parts in the center, the plate above, the barrel in the top-left corner, the sheets on the right side and top-right corner, and the magazine in the bottom-right corner).
Reaction Chamber Heatray Focus - 4 steel plates, 2 elite circuit boards, 1 gold wire, and 2 redstone lamps (place the boards in the top corners, the wire on the top side, and fill the remaining left and right columns with plates and the center column with lamps).
Reaction Chamber Housing x9 - 4 steel plates, 1 chemical laboratory, 2 cybernetic parts, 1 elite circuit board, and 1 mechanical parts (carbon) (place the laboratory in the center, the mechanical parts above, the board below, the cybernetic parts to the left and right, and the plates in the corners).
Small Ore Drill (Steel) - 2 steel plates and 5 steel ingots (place a plate in the center, a plate above, and the ingots in all slots besides the bottom corners).
Turret Armor (Steel) - 5 steel plates (place a plate in the center, a plate below, and the remaining plates in the top row). A steel plate can be smelted in a furnace to get a steel ingot. Steel plates are used in the following metal press recipes:
Steam Armor Plating - 1 steel plate and 1 bronze plate
