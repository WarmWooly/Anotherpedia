Stellated octahedron

A stellated octahedron is a 3D shape consisting of an octahedron that has been stellation (as seen by the 8 triangular prism-shaped points) on all side. It resembles a pair of intersecting triangular prisms with one rotated 180 degree (angle) on a single axis. A stellated octahedron has 8 triangle face (geometry), 12 edge (geometry), and 8 vertex (geometry).
