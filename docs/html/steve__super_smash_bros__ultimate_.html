Steve (Super Smash Bros. Ultimate)

Steve (alt. Alex, Zombie, Enderman) is a downloadable content fighter (Super Smash Bros. Ultimate) in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate as a part of the Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Fighters Pass Vol. 2 (or a standalone purchase) from Minecraft as Fighter number (Super Smash Bros.). Steve's most notable ability is the mining and crafting mechanic, allowing Steve to harvest material from the stage, place destructible blocks, repair and upgrade his tools, and give him access to more moves based on collected materials. He tier list of fighters in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate 1st as S+ tier and is widely considered the most powerful fighter in the roster.
