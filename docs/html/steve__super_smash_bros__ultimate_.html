Steve (Super Smash Bros. Ultimate)

Steve (alt. Alex, Zombie, Enderman) is a DLC figher in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate as a part of the Fighters Pass Vol. 2 (or a standalone purchase) from Minecraft as Fighter #77. Steve's most notable ability is the mining and crafting mechanic, allowing Steve to harvest material from the stage, place destructible blocks, repair and upgrade his tools, and give him access to more moves based on collected materials. He ranks 1st as S+ tier and is widely considered the most powerful fighter in the roster.
