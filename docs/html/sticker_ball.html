Sticker ball

A sticker ball or ball of stickers is a ball comprised of layers of sticker layered on top of each other. Unlike a rubber band ball, a sticker ball is not used for storing stickers. The Guinness World Records holder for the largest ball of stickers is a 308.25 pound (unit) ball by Sticky Brand on November 11, 2020.<>
