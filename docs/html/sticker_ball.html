Sticker ball

<><>A {{bsticker ball}} or {{bball of stickers}} is a [[ball]] comprised of layers of [[stickers|sticker]] layered on top of each other. Unlike a [[rubber band ball]], a sticker ball is not used for storing stickers. The [[{{iGuinness World Records}}]] holder for the [[largest ball of stickers]] is a 308.25 [[lb|pound (unit)]] ball by [[Sticky Brand]] on November 11, 2020.<>
