Stone Flushwork Body (Knapped)

<>The stone flushwork Body is a stone flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The stone flushwork Body can be cut from any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The stone flushwork Body can be cut into any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
