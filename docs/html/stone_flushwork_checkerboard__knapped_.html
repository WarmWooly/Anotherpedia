Stone Flushwork Checkerboard (Knapped)

The stone flushwork Checkerboard is a stone flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The stone flushwork Checkerboard can be cut from any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The stone flushwork Checkerboard can be cut into any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
