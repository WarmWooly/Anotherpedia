Stone Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<>The {{bstone flushwork Panel}} is a stone flushwork (Knapped) block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iKnapped (Minecraft mod)}}. The stone flushwork Panel can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 stone (Minecraft) and 2 knapped flint brick (Knapped) (shapeless). The stone flushwork Panel can be cut from any other stone flushwork (Knapped) (besides Stone Flushwork Thing (Knapped)) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft). The stone flushwork Panel can be cut into any other stone flushwork (Knapped) (besides Stone Flushwork Thing (Knapped)) in a Stonecutter (Minecraft).
