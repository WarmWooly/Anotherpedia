Stone Flushwork Panel (Knapped)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The stone flushwork Panel is a stone flushwork block in the Minecraft mod Knapped. The stone flushwork Panel can be crafted with 1 stone and 2 knapped flint bricks (item) (shapeless). The stone flushwork Panel can be cut from any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter. The stone flushwork Panel can be cut into any other stone flushwork (besides Thing) in a Stonecutter.
