Stonecutter (Minecraft)

The stonecutter is a block in Minecraft used to cut stone or metal blocks into different shapes or variations. Stonecutters naturally generate in the mason house of a village. The stonecutter can be crafted with 1 iron ingot and 3 stoneJE/3 stone blocksBE (place the iron in the center, and fill the row below with stone).
