Strap (Better Than Wolves)

<resistance|No||Buoyancy|Floatsinfo>>The strap is an item in the Minecraft mod Better Than Wolves used in crafting various leather-based industrial items. Straps can be crafted with 1 cut tanned leather and 1 iron/diamond shears (shapeless; makes 4). Straps are used in the following crafting recipes:
Belt - 4 straps (place in a circle).
Breeding Harness - 4 straps and 5 tanned leather/cut tanned leather (place the leather in a + shape, then place the straps in the corners).
Diamond Armor Plate - 2 straps, 1 diamond ingot, and 1 padding (place the padding in the middle, ingot on top, and the straps to the left and right of the ingot).
Ender Spectacles - 1 strap and 2 oculars of ender (place the strap in the middle and the oculars to the left and right).
Haft - 1 strap, 1 glue, and 1 wood moulding (place the moulding on the bottom, glue in the middle, and strap on top).
Steel Armor Plate - 2 straps, 1 padding, and 1 soulforged steel ingot (place the padding in the middle, steel on top, and the straps to the left and right of the steel). In a stoked cauldron, 8 straps can be processed into 1 glue.
