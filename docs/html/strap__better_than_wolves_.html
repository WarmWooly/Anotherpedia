Strap (Better Than Wolves)

<>The strap is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft modding (Minecraft) Better Than Wolves used in crafting (Minecraft) various leather-based industrial items. Straps can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 cut tanned leather (Better Than Wolves) and 1 iron shears (Better Than Wolves)/diamond shears (Better Than Wolves) (shapeless; makes 4). Straps are used in the following crafting recipes:
Belt (Better Than Wolves) - 4 straps (place in a circle).
Breeding Harness (Better Than Wolves) - 4 straps and 5 tanned leather (Better Than Wolves)/cut tanned leather (Better Than Wolves) (place the leather in a + shape, then place the straps in the corners).
Diamond Armor Plate (Better Than Wolves) - 2 straps, 1 diamond ingot (Better Than Wolves), and 1 padding (Better Than Wolves) (place the padding in the middle, ingot on top, and the straps to the left and right of the ingot).
Ender Spectacles (Better Than Wolves) - 1 strap and 2 ocular of ender (Better Than Wolves) (place the strap in the middle and the oculars to the left and right).
Haft (Better Than Wolves) - 1 strap, 1 glue (Better Than Wolves), and 1 wood moulding (Better Than Wolves) (place the moulding on the bottom, glue in the middle, and strap on top).
Steel Armor Plate (Better Than Wolves) - 2 straps, 1 padding (Better Than Wolves), and 1 soulforged steel ingot (Better Than Wolves) (place the padding in the middle, steel on top, and the straps to the left and right of the steel). In a hibachi (Better Than Wolves) cauldron (Better Than Wolves), 8 straps can be processed into 1 glue (Better Than Wolves).
