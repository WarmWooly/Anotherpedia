Stratagem

<><>A {{bstratagem}} is a [[plan]] used to [[complete]] a [[goal]]. Stratagem is often used in the context of getting the [[upper hand]] on an [[opponent]], especially in [[underhanded]] dealings. Stratagem is [[synonymous|synonym]] with terms such as [[trick]], [[feint]], [[artifice]], [[ploy]], and [[ruse]].
