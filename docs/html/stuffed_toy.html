Stuffed toy

A stuffed toy (also called stuffed animals, plushes, or plushies) are a toy with internal stuffing contained within a textile covering. Stuffed toys often resemble animal (hence the common name), but they may also resemble human, character, fictional creature, or object (such as food or vehicle). Stuffed toys are used by both children and adult for play, sleeping, and display. They can be industrial manufacturing or homemade, making them widespread among household.
