Stuffed toy

<><>A {{bstuffed toy}} (also called {{bstuffed animals}}, {{bplushes}}, or {{bplushies}}) are a [[toy]] with internal [[stuffing]] contained within a [[textile]] covering. Stuffed toys often resemble [[animals|animal]] (hence the [[common name]]), but they may also resemble [[humans|human]], [[characters|character]], [[fictional creatures|fictional creature]], or [[objects|object]] (such as [[food]] or [[vehicles|vehicle]]). Stuffed toys are used by both [[children]] and [[adults|adult]] for [[playing|play]], [[sleeping]], and [[displaying|display]]. They can be [[industrially manufactured|industrial manufacturing]] or [[homemade]], making them [[widespread]] among [[households|household]].
