Stuffed toy collection

A stuffed toy collection (also called a stuffed animal collection or a plush collection) is a collection of stuffed toy, usually as a personal collection by a single person. While some stuffed toy collections may be made with a deliberate theme, including a single animal, character, or franchise, most tend to be a reflection of a person's interest, which generally changes with time.
