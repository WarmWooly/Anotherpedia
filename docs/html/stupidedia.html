Stupidedia

Stupidedia, also known as Stupedia and Stupipedia, is a German satirical online encyclopedia, parodying Wikipedia, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of stupid and encyclopedia. Stupidedia was created in 2004 and has since ceased the ability to create accounts in January 2018. Stupidedia has nearly 24 thousand articles <>.
