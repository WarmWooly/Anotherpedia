Stupidedia

{{bStupidedia}}, also known as {{bStupedia}} and {{bStupipedia}}, is a German satirical online encyclopedia, parody Wikipedia, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of {{istupid}} and {{iencyclopedia}}. Stupidedia was created in 2004 and has since ceased the ability to create accounts in January 2018. Stupidedia has nearly 24 thousand articles <>.
