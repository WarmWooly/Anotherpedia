Sugar

<><>{{bSugar}} is typically a [[sweet]] [[powder]] used in [[food]] for flavor. Sugar can come in many types, such as [[white sugar]], [[brown sugar]], or [[cane sugar]]. [[Plants|plant]] generate sugar through [[photosynthesis]], which they use as energy and to create substances such as [[cellulose]] and [[starch]].
