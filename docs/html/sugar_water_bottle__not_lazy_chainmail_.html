Sugar Water Bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The sugar water bottle is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used for glazing fruits. In a brewing stand (Minecraft), water bottle (Minecraft) can be brewed with a sugar cane (Minecraft) to produce sugar water bottles. splash water bottle (Minecraft) and lingering water bottle will also be converted into sugar water bottles. Sugar water bottles are used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Glazed Apple (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 apple (Minecraft) (shapeless; a g;ass bottle (Minecraft) is returned).
Glazed Glow Berries (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 glow berries (Minecraft) (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).
Glazed Sweet Berries (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 sweet berries (Minecraft) (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).
Sweet Bread (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 bread (Minecraft) (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned). In a brewing stand (Minecraft), sugar water bottles can be brewed with a fermented spider eye (Minecraft) to produce vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail).
