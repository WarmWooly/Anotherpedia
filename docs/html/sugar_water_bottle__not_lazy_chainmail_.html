Sugar Water Bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The sugar water bottle is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for glazing fruits. In a brewing stand, water bottles can be brewed with a sugar cane to produce sugar water bottles. Splash and lingering water bottles will also be converted into sugar water bottles. Sugar water bottles are used in the following crafting recipes:
Glazed Apple - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 apple (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).
Glazed Glow Berries - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 glow berries (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).
Glazed Sweet Berries - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 sweet berries (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).
Sweet Bread - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 bread (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned). In a brewing stand, sugar water bottles can be brewed with a fermented spider eye to produce vinegar bottles.
