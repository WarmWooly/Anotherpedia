Sugar Water Bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The sugar water bottle is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for glazing fruits. In a brewing stand, water bottles can be brewed with a sugar cane to produce sugar water bottles. Splash and lingering water bottles will also be converted into sugar water bottles. Sugar water bottles are used in the following crafting recipes:&spGlazed Apple - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 apple (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spGlazed Glow Berries - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 glow berries (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spGlazed Sweet Berries - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 sweet berries (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spSweet Bread - 1 sugar water bottle and 1 bread (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned). In a brewing stand, sugar water bottles can be brewed with a fermented spider eye to produce vinegar bottles.
