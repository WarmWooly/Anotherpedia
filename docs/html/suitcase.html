Suitcase

<><>A {{bsuitcase}} is a [[baggage]] used for holding [[personal belongings|personal belonging]] while [[travelling|travel]], including [[clothing]], [[personal care products]], and [[electronics|personal device]]. Suitcases may have [[wheels|wheel]] and [[handles|handle]] to aid in moving the case around without carrying the entirety of it. Suitcases are prevalent at [[airports|airport]] for [[commercial air travel|commercial aviation]] and [[hotels|hotel]] during [[trips|trip]] and [[vacations|vacation]]. Many [[materials|material]], including [[metal]], [[plastic]], and [[textiles|textile]], can be used for suitcases. While the shape tends to be rectangular, the size of the suitcases varies depending on the carrier's use case.
