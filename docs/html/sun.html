Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System, holding all the planets and other objects in the system, including Earth. Nuclear fusion inside the Sun sustain the light and heat emitted. The radius of the sun is 432,690 miles (to compare, Earth's radius is 3,958.8 miles) and is on average 1 AU away from Earth. The Sun has been around for over 4.6 billion years.
