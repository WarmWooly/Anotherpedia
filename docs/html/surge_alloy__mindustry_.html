Surge Alloy (Mindustry)

Surge Alloy is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir that can be produced in a Surge Smelter using Copper, Lead, Titanium, and Silicon. For Erekir, Surge Alloy can be produced in a heated Surge Crucible using Slag and Silicon. The description of Surge Alloy is the following:Used in advanced weaponry and reactive defense structures.It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 75%. It is the only item with Charge, creating sparks that damage nearby units and buildings on destruction. It is used in late-game buildings, ammo, and units.
