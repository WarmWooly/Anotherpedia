Surge Alloy (Mindustry)

{{bSurge Alloy}} is an item (Mindustry) in {{iMindustry}} on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) that can be produced in a Surge Smelter (Mindustry) using Copper (Mindustry), Lead (Mindustry), titanium (Mindustry), and Silicon (Mindustry). For Erekir, Surge Alloy can be produced in a heat (Mindustry) Surge Crucible (Mindustry) using Slag (Mindustry) and Silicon. The description of Surge Alloy is the following:It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 75%. It is the only item with Charge, creating sparks that damage nearby units and buildings on destruction. It is used in late-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry).
