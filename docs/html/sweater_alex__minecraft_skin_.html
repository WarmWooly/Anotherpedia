Sweater Alex (Minecraft skin)

Sweater Alex, also known as Warm Wooly, is a skin from the Minecraft pack Holiday Skin Pack 2015 of Alex wearing a green sweater with a white "snowflake" pattern. Sweater Alex uses skinny arms. Sweater Alex is named "Warm Wooly" on Minecraft: Pocket Edition.
