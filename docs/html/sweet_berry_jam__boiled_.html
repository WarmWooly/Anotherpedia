Sweet Berry Jam (Boiled)

<>Sweet berry jam is a simple food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Sweet berry jam can be obtained by boiling sweet berries (Minecraft) in a boiler (Boiled). It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 experience (Minecraft). Sweet berry jam is used in the following crafting (Minecraft) recipes:
Sweet Berry Jam-Filled Bread (Boiled) - 1 sweet berry jam and 1 bread (Minecraft) (shapeless). Sweet berry jam can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation.
