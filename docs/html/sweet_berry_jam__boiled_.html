Sweet Berry Jam (Boiled)

Sweet berry jam is a simple food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Sweet berry jam can be obtained by boiling sweet berries in a boiler. It takes 10 seconds at 2,000 heat to boil, awarding 0.2 exp. Sweet berry jam is used in the following crafting recipes:
Sweet Berry Jam-Filled Bread - 1 sweet berry jam and 1 bread (shapeless). Sweet berry jam can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation.
