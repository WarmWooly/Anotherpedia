Sweet Tooth (Bits & Blocks)

Sweet Tooth is an item in the Minecraft mod Bits & Blocks based on the minigame of the same name. It can be displayed on a minigame stand. Sweet Tooth can be crafted with 1 minigame case and 1 cake (shapeless). Sweet Tooth is used in the following crafting recipes:
Jungle Mixtape - 1 Sweet Tooth, 1 mixtape case, 1 Flipper Snapper, 1 Rock, Paper, Showdown!, and 1 Pantry Parade (shapeless).
Tooth Necklace - 1 Sweet Tooth and 1 string (shapeless).
