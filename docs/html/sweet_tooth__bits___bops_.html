Sweet Tooth (Bits & Bops)

Sweet Tooth is the second minigame within Bits & Bops, where the player is a pink crocodile snapping up food thrown by the keeper. The minigame has the crocodile bite down when the thrown food reaches its mouth. The player must hit Button 1 in time to catch the food. Either a piece of cake or two cupcakes will be thrown, requiring one or two presses respectively. Throughout the level, candy corn-beaked swamp hens swim across, collecing naturally growing marshmallows.

If the player is on time, a thick outline will appear along the crocodile's snout. If they are slightly off, only a thin line will appear. Being really off will cause a yellow snap to appear, upwards if early and downwards if late. If there is no input or the timing is off, the food will fall past the crocodile.
