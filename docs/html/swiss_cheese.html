Swiss cheese

For cheeses from Switzerland, see Swiss cheeses.
Swiss cheese is a popular medium-hard cheese cheese that is light-yellow in color, well-known for the holes, or "eyes (cheese)," inside the cheese. Swiss cheese originates from Emmental, Switzerland, but Swiss cheese is a general term that does not specify that the cheese must be from Switzerland. Swiss cheese is now cheese production in the United States, Finland, Ireland, and Estonia alongside Switzerland. "Swiss cheese," "Swiss-style cheese," and "Emmental cheese" may be used interchangeably in some contexts.
