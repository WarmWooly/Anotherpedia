Synthetic food additive

A synthetic food additive, also known as an artificial food additive, is a food additive which improve the appearance, taste, texture, nutrition, shelf life, or other attribute of the food. Synthetic food additives are non-natural and are often used for most commercial food. Some uses of synthetic food additives are food coloring, flavor enhancer, artificial sweetener, and food preservatives.
