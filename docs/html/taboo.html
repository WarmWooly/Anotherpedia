Taboo

For the board game, see aiTaboo (game).
A taboo is the social restriction that makes certain actions, words, or topics forbidden to be done. Social norms and religion are major components to deciding what is taboo.

Different cultures see different things as taboo. The treatment of the dead, types of food, gender roles, and profanity are all often taboos. Taboos are used to regulate people in society by preventing unwanted acts via these social restrictions.
