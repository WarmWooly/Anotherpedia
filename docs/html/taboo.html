Taboo

For the board game, see {{aiTaboo (game).}}&spA taboo is the social restriction that makes certain Act, Word, or Topic forbidden to be done. Social norms and religion are major components to deciding what is taboo.&pDifferent Culture see different Thing as taboo. The treatment of the Death, types of food, gender roles, and profanity are all often taboos. Taboos are used to regulate people in society by preventing unwanted acts via these social restrictions.
