Tags (Balatro)

Tags are an optional benefit when skipping a Small or Big Blind in Balatro. There are a total of 24 Tags that can be obtained by skipping a Small or Big Blind. Tags can give Booster Packs, create or add an addition to a Edition in the Shop, award money, increase hand size, add Vouchers, and other beneficial effects. Selling the Joker Diet Cola will give a Double Tag and defeating a Boss Blind with the Anaglyph Deck will also give a Double Tag.<>
