Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch

Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch, commonly shortened to Talk Tuah, is a mature podcast hosted by Haliey Welch, who is most notable for the viral meme Hawk Tuah, and produced by Betr Media. Talk Tuah was announced on September 3rd, 2024, from the Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch YouTube channel. The first episode of Talk Tuah, "I SAID HAWK TUAH AND NOW I'M HERE w/ Whitney Cummings", was released on September 10th, 2024. <>, there are 20 episodes of Talk Tuah.

Talk Tuah covers a wide range of topics, many relating to Haliey's experience of sudden fame, what Haliey and guests have going on in their lives, personal opinions (especially on crude or controversial topics), relationships, and funny stories. Reactions have been mixed on Talk Tuah, with some arguing that Haliey should not be as popular as she is because of Hawk Tuah, others making fun of the idea of the Hawk Tuah meme being stretched further and further into a personal brand for Haliey, and people who are interested in how Haliey has dealt with fame and situations she's been in.
