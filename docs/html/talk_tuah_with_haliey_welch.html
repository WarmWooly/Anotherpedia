Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch

{{iTalk Tuah with Haliey Welch}}, commonly shortened to {{iTalk Tuah}}, is a mature podcast podcast host by Haliey Welch, who is most notable for the internet virality internet meme Hawk Tuah, and produced by Betr Media. Talk Tuah was announced on September 3rd, 2024, from the Talk Tuah with Haliey Welch YouTube channel. The first episode of Talk Tuah, "I SAID HAWK TUAH AND NOW I'M HERE w/ Whitney Cummings", was released on September 10th, 2024. <>, there are 20 episodes of Talk Tuah.

Talk Tuah covers a wide range of topic, many relating to Haliey's experience of sudden fame, what Haliey and podcast guest have going on in their personal life, personal opinion (especially on crude or controversial topics), interpersonal relationship, and funny personal story. Reactions have been mixed on Talk Tuah, with some arguing that Haliey should not be as popular as she is because of Hawk Tuah, others making fun of the idea of the Hawk Tuah meme being stretched further and further into a personal brand for Haliey, and people who are interested in how Haliey has dealt with fame and situations she's been in.
