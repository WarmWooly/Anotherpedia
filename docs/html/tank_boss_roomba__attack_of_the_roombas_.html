Tank Boss Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

<>The Tank Boss Roomba is a boss enemy in Attack of the Roombas, being tougher and faster than their Slow Roomba (Attack of the Roombas) counterpart. The Tank Boss Roomba will only appear once, and upon defeating it, will grant the player (Attack of the Roombas) a decreased reload time.
