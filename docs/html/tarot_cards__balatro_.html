Tarot Cards (Balatro)

Tarot Cards are a type of Consumable in Balatro. There are a total of 22 Tarot Cards based on tarots from Major Arcana. Tarot Cards often add Enhancements to playing cards, but they can also destroy cards, award money, change the Suit of a card, and other effects.
