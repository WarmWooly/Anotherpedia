Tartrazine

Tartrazine, also known as yellow dye number 5, is a synthetic dye used to color food, drugs, and cosmetics. Tartrazine often is in the form of a yellow powder. Tartrazine's chemical formula is Cs-b16Hs-b9Ns-b4Nas-b3Os-b9Ss-b2. Tartrazine is considered an irritant and a health hazard because of allergies and breathing difficulties.
