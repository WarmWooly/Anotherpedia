Task Force Baguette

Task Force Baguette is a unit of French volunteers fighting for Ukraine in the Russo-Ukranian War. There are also multiple members from the United States, mostly veterans. Task Force Baguette has been active since April 2022. They are often wearing tan camoflauge, helmets, masks, the Ukraine flag as a patch, and their own patch. Task Force Baguette is active on social media, spanning several platforms. The primary languages they use when communicating online is French and English. They always blur the faces of volunteers and other sensitive information when producing any footage or photos.
