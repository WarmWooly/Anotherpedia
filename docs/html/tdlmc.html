TdLmc

TdLmc is a YouTuber, Twitch streamer, and the official MEA God for the Minecraft total conversion mod Better Than Wolves. "TdL" in TdLmc stands for "The Dark Lizard", with "mc" meaning "Minecraft". TdLmc has a variety of accounts. <>, these are the official TdLmc accounts:

TdLmc would often play with mods including those that added other elements from popular franchises, including Minecraft and Pok√©mon, and the hardcore mod Primal Fear. TdLmc would branch out to Sons of the Forest and Minecraft, trying knock-off games, and playing the Minecraft modpack RLCraft. TdLmc would branch away from Ark content and focus in on RLCraft, alongside playing other mods including Cave Dweller, Minecraft Comes Alive, Pollution of the Realms, Physics Mod, and many others, before moving onto DawnCraft and Blood N Bones. TdLmc would also get into making videos for Terraria. On October 20, 2023, TdLmc posted his first YouTube video on Better Than Wolves. Since then, all further uploads have been related to Better Than Wolves, and then the Mobs Enhancement Addon for BTW. Terrance is a character created by TdLmc to represent a fictional editor for TdLmc, often screaming "at him" mid-stream to add effects or remove parts of videos. A fan of TdLmc, Franzy, has taken up the official mantle of roleplaying as Terrance within TdL's Discord server.
