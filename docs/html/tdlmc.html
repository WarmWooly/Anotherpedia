TdLmc

TdLmc is a YouTuber, Twitch streamer, and the official Mobs Enhancement Addon God for the Minecraft total conversion mod Better Than Wolves.<> "TdL" in TdLmc stands for "The Dark Lizard", with "mc" meaning "Minecraft". TdLmc would branch out to Sons of the Forest and Minecraft, Reviewing knock-offs for popular titles, and playing the Minecraft modpack RLCraft. TdLmc would branch away from Ark content and focus in on RLCraft, alongside playing other mod (Minecraft) including Cave Dweller (Minecraft mod), Minecraft Comes Alive, Pollution of the Realms, Physics Mod, and many others, before moving onto DawnCraft and Blood N Bones (Minecraft modpack). TdLmc would also get into making videos for Terraria. On October 20, 2023, TdLmc posted his first YouTube video on Better Than Wolves. Since then, all further uploads have been related to Better Than Wolves, and then the Mobs Enhancement Addon for BTW. Terrance is a character created by TdLmc to represent a fictional video editor for TdLmc, often screaming "at him" mid-stream to add effects or remove parts of videos.
