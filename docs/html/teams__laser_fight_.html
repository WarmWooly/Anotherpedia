Teams ({{iLaser Fight}})

<><>{{bTeams}} is a [[coop|coop (Laser Fight)]] in {{i[[Laser Fight|Laser Fight (Roblox game)]]}} where players join into 2 teams of 8. Players spawning in will spawn in the red and blue spawning areas in the [[map|map (Laser Fight)]]. Players can't join the team with the most people (i.e. red with 3 and blue with 2 will force the player to join blue). Teammates can't be harmed by each other's bullets from [[guns|gun (Laser Fight)]] or from [[streaks|kill streak (Laser Fight)]] (such as [[Turret|Turret (Laser Fight)]] or [[Nuke|Nuke (Laser Fight)]]).
