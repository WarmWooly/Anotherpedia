Telemagnetic Alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Telemagnetic alloy is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting mid-tier teleportation-related items and machines. Telemagnetic alloy can be crafted with 1 iron ingot, 1 redstone dust, 1 lapis lazuli, and 1 ender pearl (place the ender pearl in the middle, redstone to the left, lapis to the right, and iron below). Telemagnetic alloy are used in the following crafting recipes:
Conveyor Warper - 1 telemagnetic alloy, 2 iron composite ingots, and 2 chain conveyors (place the alloy in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Experience Accumulator - 2 telemagnetic alloy, 3 copper chain carpets, 2 copper ornate glass panes, and 1 iron composite chain carpet (place the copper carpets in the bottom row, the panes to the sides, the alloy in the corners, and the iron composite carpet above).
Item Aggregator - 2 telemagnetic alloy, 6 iron composite nuggets, and 1 bright spool block (fill top and bottom rows with nuggets, the left and right with alloy, and the center with the spool block). Telemagnetic alloy are used in the following melding recipes:
Attractive Leather Padding - 1 telemagnetic alloy and 1 leather padding
Shuffle Rune - 1 telemagnetic alloy and 1 pseudorock
