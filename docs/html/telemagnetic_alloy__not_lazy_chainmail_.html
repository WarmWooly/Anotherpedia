Telemagnetic Alloy (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>Telemagnetic alloy is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used for crafting (Minecraft) mid-tier teleportation-related items and machines. Telemagnetic alloy can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 iron ingot (Minecraft), 1 redstone dust (Minecraft), 1 lapis lazuli (Minecraft), and 1 ender pearl (Minecraft) (place the ender pearl in the middle, redstone to the left, lapis to the right, and iron below). Telemagnetic alloy are used in the following crafting recipes:
Conveyor Warper (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 telemagnetic alloy, 2 iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 2 chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the alloy in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Item Aggregator (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 telemagnetic alloy, 6 iron composite nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail), and 1 bright spool block (Not Lazy Chainmail) (fill top and bottom rows with nuggets, the left and right with alloy, and the center with the spool block). Telemagnetic alloy are used in the following Melder (Not Lazy Chainmail) recipes:
Attractive Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 telemagnetic alloy and 1 leather padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)
Shuffle Rune (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 telemagnetic alloy and 1 pseudorock (Not Lazy Chainmail)
