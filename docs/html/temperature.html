Temperature

<><>{{bTemperature}} is the [[quantity]] of [[heat]] in an [[object]] or [[location]]. Temperature is often [[measured|measurement]] using a [[thermometer]]. [[Units|unit]] to represent temperature include [[Fahrenheit]], [[Celsius]], and [[Kelvin]]. Temperature can be described as a way to see how fast [[atoms|atom]] or [[molecules|molecule]] are vibrating.
