Tendon

<><>A {{btendon}} is a dense [[collagen]] [[connective tissue]] that connects [[muscles|muscle]] and [[bones|bone]], allowing control of the [[body]]. It [[expands|expansion]] and [[contracts|contraction]] based on the muscle to move the bones and also withstands [[tension]] that becomes present. Tendons are similar to [[ligaments|ligament]] (which connect bones to bones). In the [[adult body]], there are over 4000 tendons.
