Tetration

{{bTetration}}, also known as {{biterated exponentiation}}, is the act in Mathematics of iterating on an exponent, similar to how an exponent is iterated multiplication. To show tetration normally in math, you put a superscript before the Term (mathematics), unlike with an exponent, where you put the superscript after the term.
