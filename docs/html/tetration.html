Tetration

<><>{{bTetration}}, also known as {{biterated exponentiation}}, is the act in [[math|Mathematics]] of iterating on an [[exponent]], similar to how an exponent is iterated [[multiplication]]. To show tetration normally in math, you put a [[superscript]] before the [[term|Term (mathematics)]], unlike with an exponent, where you put the superscript after the term.
