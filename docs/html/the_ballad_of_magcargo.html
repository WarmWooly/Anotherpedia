The Ballad of Magcargo

"The Ballad of Magcargo" is a folk ballad by Brian Hull uploaded to his YouTube channel Brian Hull Plus on March 6, 2024. The song is about a video where Brian finds his favorite Pokémon using the Favorite Pokémon Picker, with Magcargo going much further than expected. Come, my friends, and I'll tell you the tale
Of a great and mighty fire snail
On a quest to find his favorite mons,
Brian looked through every Pokémon
He looked and looked, and so it went on
As he went through every Pokémon
He picked the ones who caught his eye
'Til he saw this little guy

It was the fire lava snail, Magcargo, Magcargo
He thought Brian would never bail, Magcargo, Magcargo

Brain bravely continued his quest,
Picking mons he saw above the rest
In the highest of ranks did Magcargo stay
'Til Brian's mind started to sway
Carracosta, Great Tusk, and Flygon too
Saw Magcargo's heart begin to turn blue
Brian left Magcargo behind in the dust
Hopefully fire snail tears do not rust

He left the fire lava snail, Magcargo, Magcargo
No one'd ever thought he'd get so far, Magcargo, Magcargo

The snail watched onward as he wept
Thinking this competition he could have swept
He fought so long and he fought so hard
Only to be beat by Charizard
