The Bells' Arietta

{{iThe Bells' Arietta}} is a late-2025 The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild-inspired puzzle-platformer fan game created by Flogelz, with development starting in 2018. The game is centered around a small, player-controlled Guardian (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild called the Guardian Buddy (The Bells' Arietta) in a shrine (The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild)-style labyrinth.
