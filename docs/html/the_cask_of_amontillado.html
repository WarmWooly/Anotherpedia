The Cask of Amontillado

The Cask of Amontillado is a story written by the writer Edgar Allen Poe, which was published in November of 1846. The story is about a man named Montresor who seeks revenge against Fortunado because Fortunado insulted him. Montresor traps Fortunado behind a brick wall in a crypt by the end of the story.
