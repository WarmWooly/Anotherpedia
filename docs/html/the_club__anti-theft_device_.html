The Club (anti-theft device)

The Club is a car anti-theft device that locks to the steering wheel to prevent theft. The Club comes with a pair of keys used to unlock the device from the wheel. The Club has two hooks used to stay on the wheel, and several grooves The Club will snap and lock to. The Club is most often used on cars made by Kia. They had even gave away The Club to Kia owners to prevent the trend to steal Kias.
