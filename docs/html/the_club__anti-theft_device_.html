The Club (anti-theft device)

<><>{{bThe Club}} is a [[car]] [[anti-theft device]] that locks to the [[steering wheel]] to prevent [[theft]]. The Club comes with a pair of [[keys|Key]] used to unlock the device from the wheel. The Club has two hooks used to stay on the wheel, and several grooves The Club will snap and lock to. The Club is most often used on cars made by [[Kia]]. They had even [[gave away|Kia Giving The Club to Kia Owners]] The Club to Kia owners to prevent the trend to [[steal Kias|Kia Challenge]].
