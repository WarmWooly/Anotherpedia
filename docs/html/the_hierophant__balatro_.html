The Hierophant (Balatro)

The Hierophant (V) is a Tarot Card in Balatro based on The Hierophant Major Arcana tarot card. When used, it applies the Bonus Enhancement to up to two selected playing cards. Other Enhancements are replaced on the applied cards. The Hierophant is not available during the Fragile challenge.
