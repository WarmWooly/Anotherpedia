The Hierophant (Balatro)

<>The Hierophant (V) is a Tarot Card (Balatro) in Balatro based on The Hierophant (tarot card) Major Arcana tarot card. When used, it applies the Bonus Card (Balatro) Enhancements (Balatro) to up to two selected playing cards (Balatro). Other Enhancements are replaced on the applied cards. The Hierophant is not available during the Fragile (Balatro) challenges (Balatro).
