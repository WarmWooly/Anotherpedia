The Most Epic Stuff!

The Most Epic Stuff!, also known as html-final-project-1, was a website that was developed by epicroblocgamer on Replit with the URL <>.

This webpage was an opinion by epic, showing what things that epic thinks are the most epic. This list included 4 cars, 3 shoes, 4 NBA players, and 3 breeds of dogs. I can attest that these things are pretty epic.

Unfortunately, the tabs HOME, Shoes, NBA Players, and Dogs only led back to the main page, with the tab "Vehicles" in a slightly darker color going to a broken link. Main page: Replit's removal of legacy hosting
On January 3rd, Replit changed how hosting worked on its platform, removing the support for legacy hosting. After this change, the website was replaced with a message stating the "Replit Legacy Hosting has migrated to Deployments". Since then, the site can no longer be reached.
