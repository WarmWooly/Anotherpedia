<><>{{bThe Most Epic Stuff!}}, also known as {{bhtml-final-project-1}}, was a [[website]] that was developed by [[epicroblocgamer|Epicroblocgamer (Replit)]] on [[Replit]] with the [[URL]] <>.&pThis webpage was an [[opinion]] by epic, showing what [[things|Thing]] that epic thinks are the most [[epic]]. I can attest that these things are pretty epic.&pUnfortunately, the tabs HOME, Shoes, NBA Players, and Dogs only led back to the main page, with the tab "Vehicles" in a slightly darker color going to a broken link.<>{{tMain page: [[Replit's removal of legacy hosting]]}}&spOn January 3rd, Replit changed how hosting worked on its platform, removing the support for legacy hosting.
