The Nonchalant Elevator Collab

"The Nonchalant Elevator Collab (60+ Animators)" is a YouTube video collaboration organized by GarrettTheCarrot and premiering (YouTube) on June 20, 2026. It features 64 other indie animation having their character participate in the nonchalant elevator challenge, with each entry rated out of 10. The idea is to enter the elevator right before it closes by stopping it in some fancy way, with multiple participants being shown to fail the challenge in the video. Characters that made it are shown inside the elevator at the end, while the rest are on the emergency exit stairs outside the building. The video is 12 minute and 42 second in length. The participants of the video are listed in the order they appear, with their corresponding in-video rating.<>
