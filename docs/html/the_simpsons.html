The Simpsons

{{bThe Simpsons}} is an animated sitcom that was created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company that promotes satire of the American life depicted in show in the 1980s, mainly focusing around the Simpson family (Homer Simpson (The Simpsons), Marge Simpson (The Simpsons), Bart Simpson (The Simpsons), Lisa Simpson (The Simpsons), and Maggie Simpson (The Simpsons)) and other inhabitants of the fiction town of Springfield (The Simpsons). With prodcuer James L. Brooks, Groening created The Simpsons shorts for The Tracy Ullman Show on April 19, 1987, which then became a full show on December 17, 1989. Since then, it has released over 750 episode <> and is currently on List of The Simpsons seasons The Simpsons (season 35). There has also been a movie, The Simpsons Movie, which was produced in 2007. Each episode runs for 21-24 minute, with many starting with the iconic Couch gag (The Simpsons). The Simpsons are currently made by Gracie Films and 20th Television.
