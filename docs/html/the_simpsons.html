The Simpsons

{{bThe Simpsons}} is an animated sitcom that was created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company that promotes satire of the American life depicted in shows in the 1980s, mainly focusing around the Simpson family (Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie) and other inhabitants of the fictional town of Springfield. With prodcuer James L. Brooks, Groening created shorts for The Tracy Ullman Show on April 19, 1987, which then became a full show on December 17, 1989. Since then, it has released over 750 episodes <> and is currently on season 35. There has also been a movie, The Simpsons Movie, which was produced in 2007. Each episode runs for 21-24 minutes, with many starting with the iconic couch gag. The Simpsons are currently made by Gracie Films and 20th Television.
