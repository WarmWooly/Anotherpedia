Since then, it has released over 750 [[episodes|episode]] <> and is currently on [[season|List of The Simpsons seasons]] [[35|The Simpsons (season 35)]]. There has also been a [[movie]], {{i[[The Simpsons Movie]]}}, which was produced in 2007. Each episode runs for 21-24 [[minutes|minute]], with many starting with the [[iconic]] [[couch gag|Couch gag (The Simpsons)]]. {{iThe Simpsons}} are currently made by [[Gracie Films]] and [[20th Television]].
