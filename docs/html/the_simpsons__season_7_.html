The Simpsons (season 7)

See more: List of {{aiThe Simpsons seasons}}&spThe seventh season of The Simpsons aired it's first episode September 17, 1995, and ran for 25 episodes, which ended on May 19, 1996. The majority of the episodes were produced by Bill Oakley and Josh Weinstein (21/25 episodes), with David Mirkin producing the other four. It is often considered the best season, with an IMDb score of 8.4, while being in the middle of The Simpsons' "Golden Age". It came off from season 6's "Who Shot Mr. Burns? - Part I", with the premiering episode garnering 22.6 million viewers.
