The Slug Site

The Slug Site is a website related to Opisthobranchia (namely sea slug), hosted on TierraNet with Michael D. Miller as the webmaster. It can be accessed through <> or the mirror site <>, and is listed on Applied Math and Science Education Repository in 2010. The site is a mix of information regarding sea slugs, news on such, and a blog for various photograph and video related to such. There is also a "Slug of the Week" that features a new sea slug with an accompanying blurb and a relevant photograph.
