The Souls of Black Folk

{{iThe Souls of Black Folk: Essays and Sketches}} is a book written by W.E.B. Du Bois in 1903 containing several essays regarding race, especially in regards to African Americans in the United States inside American society. A notable term "double consciousness" is used when Du Bois describes the split between being American and being a Negro in society.
