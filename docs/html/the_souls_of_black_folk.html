The Souls of Black Folk

{{iThe Souls of Black Folk: Essays and Sketches}} is a book writing by W.E.B. Du Bois in 1903 containing several essay regarding race, especially in regards to African American in the United States inside 1900s American society. A notable term "double consciousness" is used when Du Bois describes the split between being Americans and being a Negro in society.
