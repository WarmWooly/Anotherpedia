The Tucker Carlson Encounter

{{iThe Tucker Carlson Encounter}} is an interview series on the Tucker Carlson Network where the host Tucker Carlson interviews different popular figures or politicians, such as Alex Jones, Ken Paxton and Kid Rock.

The interviews take place in a wooden room with a large table, with Carlson and the guest on either side. There are multiple windows, a bookshelf, kerosene lanterns, a rug, other decorations, and a river visible in the background. The episodes vary in length, with some under a half hour and some over 90 minutes.
