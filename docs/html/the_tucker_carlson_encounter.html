The Tucker Carlson Encounter

{{iThe Tucker Carlson Encounter}} is an interview series on the Tucker Carlson Network where the show host Tucker Carlson interview different popular public figure or politician, such as Alex Jones, Ken Paxton and Kid Rock.

The interviews take place in a wood room with a large table, with Carlson and the guest on either side. There are multiple window, a bookshelf, kerosene lantern, a rug, other decoration, and a river visible in the background. The episode vary in length, with some under a half hour and some over 90 minutes.
