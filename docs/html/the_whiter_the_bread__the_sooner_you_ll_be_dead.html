The whiter the bread, the sooner you'll be dead

"The whiter the bread, the sooner you'll be dead" is the 37th rule in the book Food Rules: An Eater's Manual by Michael Pollan. The saying is used to convey in a quick and witty manner that enriched flour in white bread is as unhealthy as sugar. Some common variation change sooner with quicker or faster, and some change you'll be with you're. It precedes Favor the kinds of oils and grains that have traditionally been stone-ground and comes after Don't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk.
