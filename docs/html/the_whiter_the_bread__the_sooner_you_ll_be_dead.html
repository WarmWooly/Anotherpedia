The whiter the bread, the sooner you'll be dead

<><>"{{bThe whiter the bread, the sooner you'll be dead}}" is the 37th rule in the [[book]] {{i[[Food Rules: An Eater's Manual]]}} by [[Michael Pollan]]. The saying is used to convey in a quick and [[witty]] manner that [[enriched flour]] in [[white bread]] is as [[unhealthy]] as [[sugar]]. Some common [[variations|variation]] change {{isooner}} with {{iquicker}} or {{ifaster}}, and some change {{iyou'll be}} with {{iyou're}}. It precedes {{i[[Favor the kinds of oils and grains that have traditionally been stone-ground]]}} and comes after {{i[[Don't eat breakfast cereals that change the color of the milk]]}}.
