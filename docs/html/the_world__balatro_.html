The World ({{iBalatro}})

<><>{{bThe World (XXI)}} is a [[Tarot Card|Tarot Card (Balatro)]] in [[{{iBalatro}}]] based on [[The World|The World (tarot card)]] [[Major Arcana]] [[tarot]] card. When used, it converts up to three selected [[playing cards|playing cards (Balatro)]] into the &spadeSuit Spade [[Suit|Suit (Balatro)]], even if the selected card is already a &spadeSuit Spade. The World does not affect the [[Rank|Rank (Balatro)]] or any [[Enhancements|Enhancements (Balatro)]] made on the card. The [[Roman numeral]] XXI on the card equates to [[21]]. The World has three other variations for the other Suits: [[The Star|The Star (Balatro)]] (&diamondSuit Diamonds), [[The Moon|The Moon (Balatro)]] (&clubSuit Clubs), and [[The Sun|The Sun (Balatro)]] (&heartSuit Hearts).
