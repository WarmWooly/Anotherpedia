Thorium (Mindustry)

Thorium is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Laser Drill (Mindustry) or higher. It can also be harvested from a Disassembler (Mindustry). The description of Thorium is the following:Used in durable structures and as nuclear fuel.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 20%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 100%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used in late-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), power production (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry). It is also used to produce the item Phase Fabric (Mindustry).
