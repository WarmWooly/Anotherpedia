Thorium (Mindustry)

Thorium is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Laser Drill or higher. It can also be harvested from a Disassembler. The description of Thorium is the following:Used in durable structures and as nuclear fuel.It has an Explosiveness of 20%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 100%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in late-game buildings, ammo, power production, and units. It is also used to produce the item Phase Fabric.
