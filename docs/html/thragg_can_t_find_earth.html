Thragg can't find Earth

Thragg can't find Earth is a internet meme about the Invincible (TV series)}} character Thragg theoretically mixing up Earth and Urath, playing off an in-series encounter where Allen the Alien made the same mistake. The meme has a crude drawings in meme culture Thragg holding up a book open to pages showing Urath to the left and Earth to the right. The most common variation of the meme has Allen holding the book instead of Thragg.
