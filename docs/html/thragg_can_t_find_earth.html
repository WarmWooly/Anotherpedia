Thragg can't find Earth

Thragg can't find Earth is a meme about the Invincible character Thragg theoretically mixing up Earth and Urath, playing off an in-series encounter where Allen made the same mistake. The meme has a crudely drawn Thragg holding up a book open to pages showing Urath to the left and Earth to the right. The most common variation of the meme has Allen holding the book instead of Thragg.
