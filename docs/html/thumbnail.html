Thumbnail

A thumbnail is a reduced-size image to represent an image, photo, or video. Thumbnails are prominent on operating system, search engine, and website which images and/or video are content-sharing platform, which are designed to make it easier to find digital content, to save bandwidth (computing) when on websites, and to fit images into a smaller area. Its etymology originates from the size of some image being very small, like a nail (anatomy) on a thumb.
