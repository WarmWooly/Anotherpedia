Thumbnail

A thumbnail is a reduced-size image to represent an image, photo, or video. Thumbnails are prominent on operating systems, search engines, and websites which images and/or video are shared, which are designed to make it easier to find digital content, to save bandwidth when on websites, and to fit images into a smaller area. Its etymology originates from the size of some image being very small, like a nail on a thumb.
