Thunderdome

There is only one [[rule]] in the Thunderdome (as stated by [[Dr. Dealgood|Dr. Dealgood (Mad Max)]]):<>[[Weapons|weapon]] of creative variety are littered around the dome, with combatants attached to [[bungee cables|bungee cable]] for enhanced [[mobility]] and to get higher in the dome to get increasingly better weapons. The Thunderdome's main purpose is to keep [[peace]] within Bartertown by giving a "civil" way to dispute.
