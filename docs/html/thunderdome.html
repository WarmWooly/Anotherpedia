Thunderdome

The Thunderdome refers to the metal cage in Mad Mad Beyond Thunderdome in Bartertown (Mad Max) used as entertainment for the population, where two combatants fight to the death in the cage. There is only one rule in the Thunderdome (as stated by Dr. Dealgood (Mad Max)):Two men enter, one man leaves.weapon of creative variety are littered around the dome, with combatants attached to bungee cable for enhanced mobility and to get higher in the dome to get increasingly better weapons. The Thunderdome's main purpose is to keep peace within Bartertown by giving a "civil" way to dispute.
