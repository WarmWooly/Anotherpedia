Titanium (Mindustry)

Titanium is an item (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Pneumatic Drill (Mindustry) or higher. It can also be harvested from a Separator (Mindustry) and a Disassembler (Mindustry). The description of Titanium is the following:Used in liquid transportation structures, drills and factories.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a radioactivity (Mindustry) of 0%, and a charge (Mindustry) of 0%. It is used in mid-game building (Mindustry), ammunition (Mindustry), and unit (Mindustry). It is also used to produce liquid (Mindustry) and items such as Cryofluid (Mindustry), Plastanium (Mindustry), and Surge Alloy (Mindustry).
