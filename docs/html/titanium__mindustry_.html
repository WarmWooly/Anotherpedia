Titanium (Mindustry)

Titanium is an item in Mindustry on Serpulo and that can generate in ores, which can be mined with the Pneumatic Drill or higher. It can also be harvested from a Separator and a Disassembler. The description of Titanium is the following:Used in liquid transportation structures, drills and factories.It has an Explosiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Radioactivity of 0%, and a Charge of 0%. It is used in mid-game buildings, ammo, and units. It is also used to produce liquids and items such as Cryofluid, Plastanium, and Surge Alloy.
