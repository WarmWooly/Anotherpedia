Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream

{{iTomodachi Life: Living the Dream}} is the third entry in the Tomodachi Life (series), released by Nintendo on April 16, 2026, for the Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch 2. It follows Tomodachi Collection (2009) and Tomodachi Life (2013), where Mii live together and interact on an island. It is a social simulation game following player-created Miis randomness in video games interacting, creating friendship and marriage, and having childbirth.
