Toughened Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Toughened leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the leather padding. Toughened leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +1 Armor Toughness. Toughened leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 1 leather padding and 1 diamond. Expert-level leatherworkers have a 50% chance to trade 1 leather padding and 6 emeralds for 1 toughened leather padding. In a leathersmithing table, toughened leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each toughened leather padding adds +1 Armor Toughness, with a full set of armor with 12 toughened leather paddings giving +12 Armor Toughness. Shields will only add Armor Toughness when held in the main hand or offhand. Toughened leather paddings are used in the following melding recipes:
Draconic Leather Padding - 1 toughened leather padding, 1 inflated leather padding, and 1 dragon head
