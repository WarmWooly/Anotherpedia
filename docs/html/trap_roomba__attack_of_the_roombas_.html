Trap Roomba (Attack of the Roombas)

The Trap Roomba is an enemy in Attack of the Roombas that is generated from the Trapper Roomba or the Final Boss Roomba. Every 8.33 seconds, a Trap Roomba will be generated. While they may be immobile, their small size makes them quite a difficult obstacle to remove by the player. Trap Roombas do not award a kill on destruction.
