Trigonometry

Trigonometry, also known as trig, is a study under mathematics that uses functions relating to angles and how they can be applied to calculations. The most commonly used functions are sine, cosine, tangent, cotangent, secant, and cosecant. Degrees and radians are used to measure angles in trigonometry. In the United States, it is taught in high school.
