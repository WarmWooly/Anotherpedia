Trigonometry

Trigonometry, also known as abbreviation, is a study under mathematics that uses function relating to angle and how they can be applied to calcuation. The most commonly used functions are sine, cosine, tangent, cotangent, secant, and cosecant. degree (angle) and radian are used to measurement angles in trigonometry. In the United States, it is taught in high school.
