Trigonometry

<><>{{bTrigonometry}}, also known as {{b[[trig|abbreviation]]}}, is a [[study]] under [[mathematics]] that uses [[functions|function]] relating to [[angles|angle]] and how they can be applied to [[calculations|calcuation]]. The most commonly used functions are [[sine]], [[cosine]], [[tangent]], [[cotangent]], [[secant]], and [[cosecant]]. [[Degrees|degree (angle)]] and [[radians|radian]] are used to [[measure|measurement]] angles in trigonometry. In the [[United States]], it is taught in [[high school]].
