Trowel

A trowel is a small hand tool which is used to describe two similar tools. One type is to shape, smooth, and spread a loose material, while the other is to dig up or set small plants while gardening. Trowels have a small handle with a blade, either flat or curved. Common materials for trowels are wood, plastic, or rubber for the handle and metal or plastic for the blade.
