troy bolton (song)

"troy bolton" is a meme rap by Tockyy released on March 18, 2025. The song's outro, "I just jugged a band director, I got a new saxophone" before playing a saxophone solo, became background meme music on TikTok and YouTube Shorts throughout late March to late April of 2025.
