Trump Of The Day

<><>{{bTrump Of The Day}} is a [[news aggregator]] [[website]] for [[news]] related to the [[United States president|president of the United States]] [[Donald Trump]] created by [[Divij Pawar]]. It features a [[calendar]] to sort through [[news articles]] scraped between January 20, 2025 ([[Trump's second inauguration|Second inauguration of Donald Trump]]) and the present day <>. The titles, [[news site]], date, a [[summary|article summary]], and a relevant image (if possible) of the articles are listed out, with an [[AI|Artificial intelligence]] system to [[rank|result ranking]] articles while attempting to avoid [[redundancy]]. All articles collected feature Trump in some capacity, either as the focal point of the article or as a major contributor to the discussed [[topic]].<><>
