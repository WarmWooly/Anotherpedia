Trump Of The Day

{{bTrump Of The Day}} is a news aggregator website for news related to the president of the United States Donald Trump created by Divij Pawar. It features a calendar to sort through news articles scraped between January 20, 2025 (Second inauguration of Donald Trump) and the present day <>. The titles, news site, date, a article summary, and a relevant image (if possible) of the articles are listed out, with an Artificial intelligence system to result ranking articles while attempting to avoid redundancy. All articles collected feature Trump in some capacity, either as the focal point of the article or as a major contributor to the discussed topic.
