Tucker Carlson Network

The Tucker Carlson Network, abbreviation as TCN, is a Conservative subscription streaming service that was launched on December 4, 2023. <>, the cost is $9 a month, or a discount $72 a year, to subscription to the TCN. The face of the TCN is Tucker Carlson, with other prominent figures Donald Trump, Dana White, and Javier Milei also displayed on their <>. There are several category of content, including The Tucker Carlson Encounter, Tucker Carlson Uncensored, The Tucker Carlson Interview, Tucker Carlson Shorts, Ask Tucker, After The Tucker Carlson Interview, Tucker Carlson Sworn Enemy Tour, and Tucker Carlson Films.&pThe TCN claim that their main purpose is to fight propaganda by telling the truth on matters that the mainstream media is afraid of or attempting to cover-up. By subscribing to TCN, they say that you become a part of "Team Tucker" to join the fight against mainstream media.
