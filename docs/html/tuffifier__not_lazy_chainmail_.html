Tuffifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The tuffifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce tuff. Tuffifers can be crafted using 1 broken cobblefier and 1 dioritifier (shapeless). Tuffifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create tuff. Tuffifers take 4x as long to generate one tuff compared to one cobblestone. Collecting this goes towards the progression of the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
