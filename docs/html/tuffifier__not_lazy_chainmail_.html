Tuffifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{btuffifier}} is an item (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator (Not Lazy Chainmail) to produce tuff (Minecraft). Tuffifers can be crafting (Minecraft) using 1 broken cobblefier (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 dioritifier (Not Lazy Chainmail) (shapeless). Tuffifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create tuff (Minecraft). Tuffifers take 4x as long to generate one tuff compared to one cobblestone (Minecraft). When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector (Not Lazy Chainmail) alongside the 51 other cobblefiers (Not Lazy Chainmail), the player will be awarded the advancement (Minecraft) Cobblestone is Everything (Not Lazy Chainmail).
