Tuffifier (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The tuffifier is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used as a modifier for the Cobblestone Generator to produce tuff. Tuffifers can be crafted using 1 broken cobblefier and 1 dioritifier (shapeless). Tuffifiers, when put in a Cobblestone Generator, will create tuff. Tuffifers take 4x as long to generate one tuff compared to one cobblestone. When placed in an Expanded Barrel Collector alongside the 51 other cobblefiers, the player will be awarded the advancement Cobblestone is Everything.
