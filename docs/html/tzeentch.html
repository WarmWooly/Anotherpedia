Tzeentch

Tzeentch is one of the main four Chaos Gods in Warhammer, specifically Warhammer: The Old World. Tzeentch is known for trickery, cunning, and most importantly, change through Chaos. His followers, once mutated, turn into bird-like deamons, with greater deamons of Tzeentch also following this design. Lesser deamons of the Tzeentch tend to be magical creatures with a variety of magical attacks and spells. The winds of magic are Tzeentch's to command.
