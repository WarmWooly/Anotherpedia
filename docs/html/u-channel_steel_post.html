U-channel steel post

A U-channel steel post is a post made of steel to hold up an object. They are especially popular for holding up wire fencing and street signs, as they can be easily driven into the ground, and for street signs, tend to bend or break on impact. A U-channel steel post has a perforated area, with two bends from the center (making the 'U' shape) and an additional two bends at the ends parallel with the perforated area. These posts can be painted (often a forest green, black, or white) or can be left as a silver color.
