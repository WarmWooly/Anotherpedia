U-channel steel post

A U-channel steel post is a post made of steel to hold up an object. They are especially popular for holding up wire fence and street sign, as they can be easily driven into the ground, and for street signs, tend to bend or break on traffic collision. A U-channel steel post has a perforated area, with two bends from the center (making the 'U' shape) and an additional two bends at the ends parallel with the perforated area. These posts can be steel painting (often a forest green, black, or white) or can be left as a silver (color).
