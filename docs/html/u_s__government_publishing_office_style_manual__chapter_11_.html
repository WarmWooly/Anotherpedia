U.S. Government Publishing Office Style Manual (Chapter 11)

See more: List of {{aiU.S. Government Publishing Office Style Manual}} chapters}}&spChapter 11 is a chapter in the U.S. Government Publishing Office Style Manual, starting on page 269 of the 2016 publication. Chapter 11 is titled "Italic", discussing the use and guidelines for italics. The chapter is 4 pages long and has 5 subsections. Chapter 11 refers to also check Chapter 9 "Abbreviations and Letter Symbols” and Chapter 16 “Datelines, Addresses, and Signatures”.
