Umbreon

Umbreon is a mammalian Dark type Pokémon from the second generation and one of the eight Eeveelutions. Umbreon has similar proportions to a dog or cat, with a coat of black fur, pairs of front and hind legs, and a neck extending to its head. It has a paintbrush-shaped tail and similar ears, each with a single yellow band around the middle. A yellow ring is on the outside of each of Umbreon's legs, with an additional ring on its forehead. Umbreon has large, red eyes with oval pupils and a small snout.

Umbreon's official size is 3 ft and 3 in., while weighing 59.5 lbs. Shiny Umbreons have blue markings with yellow eyes instead of yellow and red.

Umbreon is a portmanteau of umbra and the eon, which is shared across all Eeveelutions. Umbreon's body rings can glow, which occurs on full moons, when excited, or when pouncing. Its dark coat and nocturnal nature allow Umbreon to hide in the darkness for prey. It can secrete a poisonous sweat when angered, which it launches at attackers. An Umbreon has a 87.5/12.5 chance to be male or female. Umbreon is the 197th Pokémon in the Pokédex and has the Pokédex color black.

Umbreon evolves from Eevee with a high enough friendship during the night. Within Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness, the player must have a Moon Shard instead.
