Uncyclopedia

For the big brain version of Uncyclopedia, see Wikipedia.
Uncyclopedia is a satirical online encyclopedia, parodying Wikipedia, which can be found at <>. Its name is a combination of the prefix un- and encyclopedia. Its logo is a parody of Wikipedia's, being a hollowed potato instead of a hollowed sphere. Uncyclopedia was created in 2005 by Jonathan Huang, where users, known as Uncyclopedians, are able to create and edit articles.
