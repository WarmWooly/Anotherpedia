Undertale, But every 10 Minutes a new Hud Gets added

"Undertale, But every 10 Minutes a new Hud Gets added" is a YouTube video made by MelonGod on December 8, 2025, where they play Undertale while images of HUDS (video games) from other video game cover the computer screen. The challenge was inspired by DougDoug video "Mario Speedrun, but every 5 minutes the HUD gets worse" where DougDoug plays Super Mario Odyssey. The Undertale modding used was made by Shinix for the video. Like DougDoug, MelonGod also collaborated with his Twitch chat to create theoretical titles for the game based on all the HUDs added. The video is 28 minute and 47 second in length.
