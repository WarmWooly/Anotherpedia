United States

The United States of America (also known as the United States, USA, US, or America) is a country in the middle of North America. <>, it has a population of over 330 million people. It has 50 states, a federal district, and other territories and islands. The national language of the US is English. The current president of the US is Joe Biden as elected in the 2020 United States election.
