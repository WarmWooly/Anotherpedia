Unrecord

Unrecord is a realistic first-person shooter in development where the protagonist is a police officer with a body camera to record gameplay. It is being developed by DRAMA and made in Unreal Engine 5 with one of the main selling points being the photorealism that is available in Unreal Engine 5. <>, it does not have a set release date, being estimated to come out at the earliest 2025.

The game describes itself as to having a rich story and decisive moral dilemmas, where the player would have to make difficult choices. They also claim that Unrecord with have a "unique" shooting system and little room for error, forcing the player to think tactically when approaching situations.
