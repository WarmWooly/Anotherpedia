Unrecord

{{iUnrecord}} is a realistic game first-person shooter in game development where the protagonist is a police officer with a body camera to recording gameplay. It is being developed by DRAMA (game studio) and made in Unreal Engine 5 with one of the main selling point being the photorealism that is available in Unreal Engine 5. <>, it does not have a set release date, being estimated to come out at the earliest 2025.

The video game describes itself as to having a rich video game storyline and decisive moral dilemma, where the player would have to make difficult choices. They also claim that Unrecord with have a "unique" shooting system and little room for error, forcing the player to think tactical when approaching situation.
