Upsetting (mint)

Upsetting or rimming is a step while coining (mint) coin, specifically turning a coin blank into a planchet to be stamped. Through upsetting a coin, it raises the rim to protect the design of the coin and to harden the coin's edge. Upsetting is performed by an upsetting mill within a mint (facility) or a dedicated planchet factory.
