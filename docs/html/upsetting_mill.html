Upsetting mill

An upsetting mill, also called a rimming mill or upsetting machine, is a machine that is used to upset coin blanks into planchets through raising the blanks' rim. Upsetting mills are utilized in the minting process and may be done within a mint or a dedicated factory. The machine itself has blanks pushed through a track with the space reducing, forcing the blanks' rims upwards as they are squeezed through.
