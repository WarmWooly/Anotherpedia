Uranus (Balatro)

{{bUranus}} is a Planet Cards (Balatro) in {{iBalatro}} based on the planet Uranus. When used, it levels up the poker hands (Balatro), adding 20 chips (Balatro) and 1 multiplier (Balatro) when a Two Pair is played. Uranus is available at the start of the game.
