Uranus ({{iBalatro}})

<><>{{bUranus}} is a [[Planet Card|Planet Cards (Balatro)]] in {{i[[Balatro]]}} based on the [[planet]] [[Uranus]]. When used, it levels up the [[Two Pair|poker hands (Balatro)]], adding 20 [[chips|chips (Balatro)]] and 1 [[mult|multiplier (Balatro)]] when a Two Pair is played. Uranus is available at the start of the game.
