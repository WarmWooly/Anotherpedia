Urns per minute

Urns per minute (UPM) was a rate used by MAGOOdog and his live stream chat while he was running his Skyrim, But I Can Only Loot URNS challenge, starting on April 19, 2026, and ending on May 1. It was used to find the rate that MAGOOdog was collecting urns in Skyrim, measured by dividing the urns collected by the number of minute that had passed since the start of the challenge. For example, collecting 25 urns in 50 minutes would give a rate of 25/50 = 0.5 UPM. During his streams, an inside joke was formed around the UPM as if it were a stock or cryptocurrency, where a higher UPM represented a higher value, with MAGOOdog calling on his chat to "buy in" while it was low.
