Vault (Laser Fight)

A vault is an automatic movement (Laser Fight) in Laser Fight (Roblox game) triggered by a player jump (Laser Fight) near a wall with a height of 5 studs or less. Vaulting increases the horizontal and vertical speed of the player and awards 2 experience (Laser Fight). Main page: Combo ({{aiLaser Fight)}} When a vault is done back-to-back, they will be combined together. After 10 vaults, a new vault chain will be started.
