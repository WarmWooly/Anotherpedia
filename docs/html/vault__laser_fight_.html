Vault (Laser Fight)

A vault is an automatic movement action in Laser Fight triggered by a player jumping near a wall with a height of 5 studs or less. Vaulting increases the horizontal and vertical speed of the player and awards 2 exp. Main page: Combo ({{aiLaser Fight)}} When a vault is done back-to-back, they will be combined together. After 10 vaults, a new vault chain will be started.
