Vegetable Medley (Boiled)

<>Vegetable medley is a food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Boiled (Minecraft mod). Vegetable medley can be crafting (Minecraft) with 1 boiled carrot (Boiled), 1 boiled potato (Boiled), 1 boiled brown mushroom (Boiled)/boiled red mushroom (Boiled), and 1 bowl (Minecraft) (shapeless). Vegetable medley can be eaten as food (Minecraft) to restore hunger and saturation. A bowl (Minecraft) is returned to the player after eating for reuse.
