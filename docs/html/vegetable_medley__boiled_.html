Vegetable Medley (Boiled)

Vegetable medley is a food in the Minecraft mod Boiled. Vegetable medley can be crafted with 1 boiled carrot, 1 boiled potato, 1 boiled brown/red mushroom, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Vegetable medley can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. A bowl is returned to the player after eating for reuse.
