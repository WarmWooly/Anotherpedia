Veggie Salad (Not Lazy Chainmail)

Veggie salad is a mixed food in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail. Veggie salad can be crafted with 1 pickled beetroot, 1 pickled carrot, 1 pickled potato, and 1 bowl (shapeless). Veggie salad can be eaten as food to restore hunger and saturation. The bowl is returned after eating. Upon eating, the player will be awarded the advancement Variety is the Pickle of Life.
