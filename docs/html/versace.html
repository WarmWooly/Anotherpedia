Versace

{{bGianni Versace S.r.l.}}, often shortened to {{bVersace}} ({{iVer-SAH-chey}}), is a luxury fashion company based in Italy. Versace was founded by Gianni Versace in 1978. They mainly offer bold colors and rich blacks and whites, alongside silver, gold, and other bejeweled additions.
