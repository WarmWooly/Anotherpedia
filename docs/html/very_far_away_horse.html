Very Far Away Horse

For the video game based on the meme, see {{aiFar Away Horse.}}
{{iVery Far Away Horse}} is a meme originally from the obvious plant's Museum of Toys pop-up exhibition. The image itself was taken by Vsauce and posted to Twitter on March 3, 2019, alongside others taken at the exhibition. The game would run on the GameCube rated Teen by the ESRB, with the game having an Official Nintendo Seal and being the "Game of the Year edition". The original display was to poke fun at such a mundane game, with the idea that it got Game of the Year and that it would be rated Teen adding to the absurdity. The piece itself was made by the cartoonist pixelatedboat for the exhibition. A popular variation of the image has a Snapchat caption labeled "should i buy this?" over the game.
