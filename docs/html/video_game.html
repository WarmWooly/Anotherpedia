Video game

A video game (or simply game) is a form of game played on an electronic or digital device through a screen, such as a television or computer monitor. Video games are a source of entertainment, simulation, competition, and/or education, depending on the type of game. Video games are defined by their genre and platform (i.e. console games, computer games, arcade games). There are many ways video games are made, from the international industry and many smaller indie developers, due to the relative accessibility of video game creation.
