Video game

A video game (or simply game) is a form of game played on an electronic or digital device through a digital screen, such as a television or computer monitor. Video games are a source of entertainment, simulation, competition, and/or education, depending on the type of game. Video games are defined by their video game genre and video game platform (i.e. console game, computer game, arcade video game). There are many ways video games are made, from the video game industry and many smaller indie video game, due to the relative accessibility of video game creation.
