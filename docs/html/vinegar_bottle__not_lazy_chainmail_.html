Vinegar Bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The vinegar bottle is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used for pickling foods, oxidizing copper blocks, and boosting the chain launcher. In a brewing stand, sugar water bottles can be brewed with a fermented spider eye to produce vinegar bottles. Vinegar bottles are used in the following crafting recipes:&spPickled Beetroot x3 - 1 vinegar bottle and 3 beetroots (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spPickled Carrot x3 - 1 vinegar bottle and 3 carrots (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spPickled Egg x3 - 1 vinegar bottle and 3 eggs (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned).&spPickled Potato x3 - 1 vinegar bottle and 3 potatoes (shapeless; a glass bottle is returned). When using a vinegar bottle on a copper block,<> the block will go to its next oxidation stage (none --> exposed --> weathered --> oxidized) and a glass bottle will be returned. Doing so will award the advancement Oxide On. Waxed copper blocks can't be oxidized using vinegar. If the player is sneaking while using a vinegar bottle on a fully oxidized block, oxide dust will be produced. Main page: Chain Launcher ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail)}}&spWhen a vinegar bottle is held in the offhand, the launch power of chain launcher will increase by 214.3%, "boosting" the launch and dropping an empty glass bottle. Boosting while airborne (with the Ascension enchantment) will award the advancement Flight in All but Name. When launching a mob with vinegar in the offhand, the mob launch will be boosted and the player will be awarded the advancement We're Blasting Off Again!
