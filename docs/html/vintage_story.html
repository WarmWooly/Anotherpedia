{{iVintage Story}}

<><>{{b{{iVintage Story}}}} is a 2016 [[sandbox|sandbox game]] [[survival|survival game]] [[video game]] developed by [[Anego Studios]]. The game was a standalone expansion on their {{i[[Minecraft]]}} [[mod|Minecraft modding]] {{i[[Vintagecraft]]}}, with inspiration from {{i[[TerraFirmaCraft]]}}. The game's main focus is the [[survival mechanics|survival (Vintage Story)]] and the [[lore|Vintage Story lore]] behind the events of the game, including the [[monsters|Supernatural creatures (Vintage Story)]] and [[temporal storms|temporal storm (Vintage Story)]] that plauge the world.<> Various interactions, such as [[knapping|knapping (Vintage Story)]] stones, [[forming clay|clay forming (Vintage Story)]], and [[smithing|smithing (Vintage Story)]] metals uses a [[voxel]] system to add, remove, and shape elements on a granular scale while producing goods for use.
