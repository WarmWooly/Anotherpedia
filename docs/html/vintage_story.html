Vintage Story

{{iVintage Story}} is a 2016 sandbox game survival game video game developed by Anego Studios. The game was a standalone expansion on their Minecraft Minecraft modding Vintagecraft, with inspiration from TerraFirmaCraft. The game's main focus is the survival (Vintage Story) and the Vintage Story lore behind the events of the game, including the Supernatural creatures (Vintage Story) and temporal storm (Vintage Story) that plauge the world. Various interactions, such as knapping (Vintage Story) stones, clay forming (Vintage Story), and smithing (Vintage Story) metals uses a voxel system to add, remove, and shape elements on a granular scale while producing goods for use.
