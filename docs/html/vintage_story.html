Vintage Story

Vintage Story is a 2016 sandbox survival video game developed by Anego Studios. The game was a standalone expansion on their Minecraft mod Vintagecraft, with inspiration from TerraFirmaCraft. The game's main focus is the survival mechanics and the lore behind the events of the game, including the monsters and temporal storms that plauge the world. Various interactions, such as knapping stones, forming clay, and smithing metals uses a voxel system to add, remove, and shape elements on a granular scale while producing goods for use.
