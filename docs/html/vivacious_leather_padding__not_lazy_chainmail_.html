Vivacious Leather Padding (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Vivacious leather padding is an item in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail as an upgrade to the healthy leather padding. Vivacious leather padding is used to upgrade chainmail armor or shields in a leathersmithing table, giving +6 Max Health (3 hearts) and +0.02 Gravity. Vivacious leather padding can be melded in a Melder using 2 healthy leather paddings and 1 block of rose gold chains. In a leathersmithing table, vivacious leather padding can be applied to chainmail armor or shields. Each vivacious leather padding adds +6 Max Health and +0.02 Gravity, with a full set of armor with 12 vivacious leather paddings giving +72 Max Health (36 hearts) and +0.24 Gravity. Shields will only add Max Health and Gravity when held in the main hand or offhand.
