Voidpet

For the species, see Voidpet (fictional species), and for the franchise, see {{aiVoidpet (franchise).}}
Voidpet is a mobile gaming company founded in 2022 by Linda Shad and Ben Awad, with backing by Andreessen Horowitz. The company is known for its Voidpet (franchise) with its mobile application Voidpet Garden (2023) and Voidpet Dungeon (2024).
