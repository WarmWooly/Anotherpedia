Voidpet

For the species, see Voidpet (fictional species), and for the franchise, see {{aiVoidpet (franchise).}}
Voidpet is a mobile gaming company founded in 2022 by Linda Shad and Ben Awad, with backing by Andreessen Horowitz. The company is known for its franchise of the same name with its apps Voidpet Garden (2023) and Voidpet Dungeon (2024).
