Voidpet (fictional species)

Voidpets are a fiction species making up the Voidpet (franchise) that exist within the Void (Voidpet). Voidpets are based on emotion, feeling, and personality traits. While all start as worm-like creatures, they can evolution (Voidpet) into more complex forms. Some of these forms look similar to animal (such as Wonder (Voidpet) as a butterfly or Down Bad (Voidpet) as a dog), while others are more abstract. Voidpets also gain additional eye for each evolution, with a max of five. Their default coloration is black, white, and a muted purple, but they can rarely appear as a Vivid (Voidpet) with a unique coloration, with 96 different Vivids.
