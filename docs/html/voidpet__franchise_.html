Voidpet (franchise)

{{iVoidpet}} is a 2022 creature collector franchise created by the Voidpet consisting of creatures called Voidpet (fictional species) based on emotion. The franchise currently consists of two mobile game, Voidpet Garden (2023) and Voidpet Dungeon (2024), the book Hands of Greed, and various animation on their social media. They had also sold stuffed animal based on Voidpets and had a browser game Voidpet Explore.
