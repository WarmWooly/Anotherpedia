Voidpet (franchise)

{{iVoidpet}} is a 2022 creature-collecting franchise created by the company of the same name consisting of creatures called Voidpets based on emotions. The franchise currently consists of two mobile games, Voidpet Garden (2023) and Voidpet Dungeon (2024), the book Hands of Greed, and various animations on their social media accounts. They had also sold stuffed animals based on Voidpets and had a browser game Voidpet Explore.
