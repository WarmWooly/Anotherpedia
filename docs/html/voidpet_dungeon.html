Voidpet Dungeon

{{iVoidpet Dungeon: Collector RPG}} is a mobile video game created by Voidpet in 2024 within the Voidpet (franchise). Within the app, the player collects Voidpet (fictional species) through earning eggs (Voidpet Dungeon) by completing quests (Voidpet Dungeon). The player gains experience (Voidpet Dungeon) to level up their Voidpets to increase their Stats (Voidpet Dungeon) for combat (Voidpet Garden). With special items (Voidpet Dungeon), the Voidpets can evolution (Voidpet) to higher states. Players can give their Voidpets gear (Voidpet Dungeon) or new colorations (called Vivid (Voidpet)) to increase their stats. The game is separated by different level (Voidpet Dungeon) with new sets of enemies (Voidpet Dungeon) (sometimes other Voidpets or bosses (Voidpet Dungeon)).

Players assemble a team of Voidpets to combat enemies, with the difficulty increasing as the player beats levels. Voidpets have unique Skills (Voidpet Dungeon) based on their emotion and level that have differing effects, including dealing damage, healing allies, and applying effects (Voidpet Dungeon). Voidpets also have elemental types (Voidpet) and general classes (Voidpet Dungeon) that influence their role in the party. An auto battler system is included, where a bot takes control of the player's party to defeat enemies.
