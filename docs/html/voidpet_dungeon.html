Voidpet Dungeon

{{iVoidpet Dungeon: Collector RPG}} is a mobile video game created by Voidpet in 2024 within the Voidpet franchise. Within the app, the player collects Voidpets through earning eggs by completing quests. The player gains experience to level up their Voidpets to increase their stats for battling. With special items, the Voidpets can evolve to higher states. Players can give their Voidpets gear or new colorations (called Vivids) to increase their stats. The game is separated by different levels with new sets of enemies (sometimes other Voidpets or bosses).

Players assemble a team of Voidpets to combat enemies, with the difficulty increasing as the player beats levels. Voidpets have unique skills based on their emotion and level that have differing effects, including dealing damage, healing allies, and applying status effects. Voidpets also have elemental types and general classes that influence their role in the party. An auto battler system is included, where a bot takes control of the player's party to defeat enemies.
