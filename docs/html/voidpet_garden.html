Voidpet Garden

{{iVoidpet Garden: Mental Health}} is a mobile mental health app created by Voidpet in 2023 within the Voidpet franchise. Within the app, the user collects Voidpets through interacting with them, expressing their emotions or mood, and using items. Voidpets gain experience with interaction and battling to gain higher levels and to evolve to higher states. The app keeps track of how the user responds to various evaluations in a journal and a calendar.

Users can interact with their Voidpets, including cuddling (swiping across the screen), feeding them, giving them cosmetics, creating memes, and texting them. They can also grow various plants to unlock self-care activities and attract more Voidpets.

As the user levels up their pets, they can be used in a simple combat system against pre-defined opponents. Different moves can be unlocked or bought for Voidpets to make different attack combinations, taking it to a similar turn-based system to Pokémon.
