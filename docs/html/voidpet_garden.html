Voidpet Garden

{{iVoidpet Garden: Mental Health}} is a mobile application mental health app created by Voidpet in 2023 within the Voidpet (franchise). Within the app, the user collects Voidpet (fictional species) through interacting with them, expressing their emotion or mood, and using items (Voidpet Garden). Voidpets gain experience with interaction and combat (Voidpet Garden) to gain higher levels and to evolution (Voidpet) to higher states. The app keeps track of how the user responds to various evaluation in a journal and a calendar.

Users can interact with their Voidpets, including cuddle (Voidpet Garden) (swiping across the screen), feeding them, giving them cosmetics (Voidpet Garden), creating meme (Voidpet Garden), and text (Voidpet Garden) them. They can also grow various plants (Voidpet Garden) to unlock self-care activities and attract more Voidpets.

As the user levels up their pets, they can be used in a simple combat system against pre-defined opponents. Different moves (Voidpet Garden) can be unlocked or bought for Voidpets to make different attack combinations, taking it to a similar turn-based combat to Pok√©mon.
