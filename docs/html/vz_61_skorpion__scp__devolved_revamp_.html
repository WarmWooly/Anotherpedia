Vz.61 Skorpion (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

The Vz.61 Skorpion is a secondary gun in SCP: Devolved Revamp given to members of the Chaos Insurgency at level 4. While it deals the least damage per bullet out of any gun, its extreme firerate and decent ammo capacity make it a solid secondary.
