Wall launch (Laser Fight)

For the gun, see Wall Launcher (aiLaser Fight).
A wall launch is a movement action in Laser Fight triggered by a player jumping while sprinting out of a wall hang. If the player is not sprinting while doing so, a wall climb will be performed instead. By doing a wall launch, the player gains a lot of vertical velocity to be flung above the wall they were hanging on, which can then be used to get to other raised locations. A wall launch gives the player 2 exp.
