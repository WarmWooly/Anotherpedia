Warm_Wooly

Warm_Wooly is the creator of Anotherpedia, the Minecraft mods Not Lazy Chainmail, Lazy Chainmail, Mulch, Knapped, Create: Knapped and Bread Reborn: Redefined, and the Roblox game Laser Fight. Warm_Wooly often writes pages on Anotherpedia, with over 1500 pages written <>. His name "Warm_Wooly" originates from the Minecraft skin Warm Wooly from the Holiday Skin Pack 2015. He is also Bow Legend on Hypixel.
