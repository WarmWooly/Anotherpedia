Warm_Wooly

Alexander Shapiro, known online as Warm_Wooly, is a student attending the University of Massachusetts Lowell for Data Science and is the creator of Anotherpedia. He was born on March 14, 2006, making him <> years old. His username comes from the Minecraft skin Warm Wooly from the Holiday Skin Pack 2015. Warm_Wooly has also created the Minecraft mods Not Lazy Chainmail, Lazy Chainmail, Mulch, Knapped, Create: Knapped, Bread Reborn: Redefined, and Boiled, and the Roblox game Laser Fight.
