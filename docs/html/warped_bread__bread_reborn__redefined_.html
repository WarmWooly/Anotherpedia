Warped Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Warped bread is food in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. It can be eaten twice as fast as most other foods. Warped flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce warped bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving < 30% chance).(text=u-d0.1note>> experience. Warped bread can be eaten as food. As it has effects, warped bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. Warped bread only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
