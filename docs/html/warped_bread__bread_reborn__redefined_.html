Warped Bread (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<>Warped bread is food (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Bread Reborn: Redefined. It can be eaten twice as fast as most other foods. warped flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined) can be smelting (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft) or a smoker (Minecraft) to produce warped bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience (Minecraft). Warped bread can be used in a breadmaking station (Bread Reborn: Redefined) to make a bread platter (Bread Reborn: Redefined), adding its stats to the platter. Warped bread can be eaten as food (Minecraft). As it has effects, warped bread can be eaten even when the player's hunger is completely full. Warped bread only takes half as long to eat compared to most foods at 0.8 seconds.
