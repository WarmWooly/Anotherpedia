Warped Flour (Bread Reborn: Redefined)

<resistance|Noinfo>>Warped flour is an item in the Minecraft mod Bread Reborn: Redefined. Warped flour can be baked into warped bread. Warped flour can be obtained by milling warped fungus on a millstone. Warped flour can be smelted in a furnace or a smoker to produce warped bread, taking 10 or 5 seconds respectively and giving experience.
