Water (Mindustry)

Water is a liquid in Mindustry on Serpulo and Erekir and can be obtained through various means. On Serpulo, Water can be collected through pools of water tiles on the surface using a Mechanical Pump or better or through a Water Extractor. On Erekir, Water can be collected through Turbine and Vent Condensers and as a byproduct from a Pyrolysis Generator. The description of Water is the following:Used for cooling machines and waste processing.It has an Exposiveness of 0%, a Flammability of 0%, a Heat Capacity of 40%, a Viscosity of 50%, and a Temperature of 50%. On Serpulo, Water can be used to create Spore Pods, Graphite, Oil, and Cryofluid, to generate power in a Steam Generator, can be used as a liquid ammo, and can be used to boost the fire rate of turrets. On Erekir, Water can be used to create Ozone and Hydrogen, to generate power in a Neoplasia Reactor, to run Impact Drills, and to boost the fire rate of turrets. When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit the Wet effect.
