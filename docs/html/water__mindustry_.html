Water (Mindustry)

Water is a liquid (Mindustry) in Mindustry on Serpulo (Mindustry) and Erekir (Mindustry) and can be obtained through various means. On Serpulo, Water can be collected through pools of water tiles on the surface using a Mechanical Pump (Mindustry) or better or through a Water Extractor (Mindustry). On Erekir, Water can be collected through Turbine Condenser (Mindustry) and Vent Condenser (Mindustry) and as a byproduct from a Pyrolysis Generator (Mindustry). The description of Water is the following:Used for cooling machines and waste processing.It has an explosiveness (Mindustry) of 0%, a flammability (Mindustry) of 0%, a heat capacity (Mindustry) of 40%, a viscosity (Mindustry) of 50%, and a temperature (Mindustry) of 50%. On Serpulo, Water can be used to create Spore Pod (Mindustry), Graphite (Mindustry), Oil (Mindustry), and Cryofluid (Mindustry), to generate power (Mindustry) in a Steam Generator (Mindustry), can be used as a liquid ammo (Mindustry), and can be used to boost the fire rate of turret (Mindustry). On Erekir, Water can be used to create Ozone (Mindustry) and Hydrogen (Mindustry), to generate power in a Neoplasia Reactor (Mindustry), to run Impact Drill (Mindustry), and to boost the fire rate of turrets. When touched, either from entering a pool or being shot, it will give the unit (Mindustry) the Wet (Mindustry) effect.
