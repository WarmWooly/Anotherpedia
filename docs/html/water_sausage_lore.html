Water sausage lore

Water sausage lore refers to an inside joke within MelonGod's community surrounding an interaction within Undertale. In Toriel's house, a potted plant can be interacted with, for which the player will not know the name of it. Upon interacting with a bookcase, they learn its real name, allowing the player to interact with the plant again to realize that it's a water sausage. MelonGod would refer to the interaction as "water sausage lore", and frequently triggers it whenever playing Undertale. Because of the repetition of the action and the custom nature of the mods that are made for MelonGod, modders may occasionally omit water sausage lore to goad a reaction out of MelonGod or turn the plant into an enemy.
