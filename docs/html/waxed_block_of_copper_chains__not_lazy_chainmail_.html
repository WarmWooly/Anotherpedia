Waxed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bwaxed block of copper chains}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The waxed block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 waxed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking a block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed block of copper chains. This will award the advancement (Minecraft) Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Waxed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spWaxed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 waxed block of copper chains (shapeless).&spWaxed Copper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 waxed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side). Right-clicking a waxed block of copper chains with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). {{gal}}
