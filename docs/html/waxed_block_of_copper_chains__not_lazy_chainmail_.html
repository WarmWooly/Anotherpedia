Waxed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The waxed block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The waxed block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 waxed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking a block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spWaxed Copper Chain x4 - 1 waxed block of copper chains (shapeless).&spWaxed Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 waxed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side).
