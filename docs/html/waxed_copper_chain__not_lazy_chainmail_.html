Waxed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The waxed copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. There are multiple recipes to obtain waxed copper chains:&sp1 waxed block of copper chains --> 4 waxed copper chains&sp1 copper chain + 1 honeycomb (shapeless) --> 1 waxed copper chain Right-clicking a copper chain with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed copper chain. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On.
