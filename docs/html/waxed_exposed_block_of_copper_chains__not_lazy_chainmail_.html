Waxed Exposed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The waxed exposed block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The waxed exposed block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 waxed exposed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed exposed block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed exposed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Chain Launcher - 4 waxed exposed blocks of copper chains, 1 (bright) spool block, 1 (waxed) exposed copper chain, 1 piston, and 1 lever (place the piston in the center, the chain to the right, the spool block to the left, the lever in the bottom left corner, and fill the top, bottom, and right corners with blocks of chains).
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain x4 - 1 waxed exposed block of copper chains (shapeless).
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 waxed exposed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side). Right-clicking a waxed exposed block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into an exposed block of copper chains. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
