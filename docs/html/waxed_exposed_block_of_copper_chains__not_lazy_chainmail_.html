Waxed Exposed Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The waxed exposed block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The waxed exposed block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 waxed exposed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an exposed block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed exposed block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed exposed blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain x4 - 1 waxed exposed block of copper chains (shapeless).
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 waxed exposed blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side). Right-clicking a waxed exposed block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into an exposed block of copper chains. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
