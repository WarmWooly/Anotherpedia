Waxed Exposed Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The waxed exposed copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. There are multiple recipes to obtain waxed exposed copper chains:
1 waxed exposed block of copper chains --> 4 waxed exposed copper chains
1 exposed copper chain + 1 honeycomb (shapeless) --> 1 waxed exposed copper chain Right-clicking an exposed copper chain with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed exposed copper chain. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed exposed copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
Acacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Bamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Birch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Cherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped cherry logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Crimson Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped crimson stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Dark Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Jungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Mangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Oak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Spruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).
Warped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed exposed copper chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).
Waxed Exposed Block of Copper Chains - 4 waxed exposed copper chains (place in a 2x2 square).
Waxed Exposed Copper Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 waxed exposed copper chains (fill the middle and bottom rows). Waxed exposed copper chains can be crushed into 8 copper chain links. Right-clicking a waxed exposed copper chain with an axe will turn the block into an exposed copper chain. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
