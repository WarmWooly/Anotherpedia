Waxed Oxidized Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The waxed oxidized block of copper chains is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The waxed oxidized block of copper chains can be crafted with 4 waxed oxidized copper chains (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed oxidized block of copper chains. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed oxidized blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spWaxed Oxidized Copper Chain x4 - 1 waxed oxidized block of copper chains (shapeless).&spWaxed Oxidized Copper Chain Carpet x3 - 2 waxed oxidized blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side). Right-clicking a waxed oxidized block of copper chains with an axe will turn the block into an oxidized block of copper chains. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
