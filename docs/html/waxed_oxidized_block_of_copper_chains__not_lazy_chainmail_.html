Waxed Oxidized Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bwaxed oxidized block of copper chains}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The waxed oxidized block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 waxed oxidized copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed oxidized block of copper chains. This will award the advancement (Minecraft) Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Waxed oxidized blocks of copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spWaxed Oxidized Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 waxed oxidized block of copper chains (shapeless).&spWaxed Oxidized Copper Chain Carpet (Not Lazy Chainmail) x3 - 2 waxed oxidized blocks of copper chains (place side-by-side). Right-clicking a waxed oxidized block of copper chains with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into an oxidized block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement).
