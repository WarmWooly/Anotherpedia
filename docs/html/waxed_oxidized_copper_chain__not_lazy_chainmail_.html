Waxed Oxidized Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The waxed oxidized copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. There are multiple recipes to obtain waxed oxidized copper chains:&sp1 waxed oxidized block of copper chains --> 4 waxed oxidized copper chains&sp1 oxidized copper chain + 1 honeycomb (shapeless) --> 1 waxed oxidized copper chain Right-clicking an oxidized copper chain with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed oxidized copper chain. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Waxed oxidized copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:&spAcacia Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped acacia logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spBamboo Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 block of stripped bamboo (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spBirch Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped birch logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spCherry Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped dark oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spJungle Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped jungle logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spMangrove Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped mangrove logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spOak Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped oak logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spSpruce Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped spruce logs (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with logs).&spWarped Hanging Sign x6 - 2 waxed oxidized copper chains and 6 stripped warped stems (place the chains in the top left and right corners, fill the bottom two rows with stems).&spWaxed Oxidized Block of Copper Chains - 4 waxed oxidized copper chains (place in a 2x2 square).&spWaxed Oxidized Copper Chain Link Fence x16 - 6 waxed oxidized copper chains (fill the middle and bottom rows). Waxed oxidized copper chains can be crushed into 8 copper chain links. Right-clicking a waxed oxidized copper chain with an axe will turn the block into an oxidized copper chain. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
