Waxed Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bwaxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used to move mob (Minecraft) and item (Minecraft) around and deal damage to mobs. There are multiple crafting (Minecraft) to obtain waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors:&sp3 waxed weathered copper chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 6 copper nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the conveyors at the bottom row of the grid, fill the rest with copper nuggets) --> 3 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors&sp1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless) --> 1 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. This will award the advancement (Minecraft) Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). When a mob (Minecraft) or item (Minecraft) is on the conveyor, it will move in the direction facing. As it is spiked, mobs will also Bleeding (Not Lazy Chainmail) while riding the conveyor. If powered by redstone (Minecraft), the conveyor will stop moving mobs/items. If ridden by a player (Minecraft), they will be granted the advancement What a Mouthful! (Not Lazy Chainmail) Waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors are used in the following recipes:&spConveyor Warper (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors, 1 ender pearl (Minecraft), and 2 iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the ender pearl in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).&spSort Dropper (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor, 1 dropper (Minecraft), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the dropper in the middle, with the engine below and the conveyor above). Right-clicking a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement).
