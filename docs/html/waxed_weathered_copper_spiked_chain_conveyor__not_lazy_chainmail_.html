Waxed Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Speed|0.5||Bleeding|I for 1 secondinfo>>The {{bwaxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to move mobs and items around and deal damage to mobs. There are multiple recipes to obtain waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors:
3 waxed weathered copper chain conveyors and 6 copper nuggets (place the conveyors at the bottom row of the grid, fill the rest with copper nuggets) --> 3 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors
1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor and 1 honeycomb (shapeless) --> 1 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. When a mob or item is on the conveyor, it will move in the direction facing. As it is spiked, mobs will also Bleed while riding the conveyor. If powered by redstone, the conveyor will stop moving mobs/items. If ridden by a player, they will be granted the advancement What a Mouthful! Waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors are used in the following recipes:
Conveyor Warper - 2 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyors, 1 ender pearl, and 2 iron composite ingots (place the ender pearl in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Sort Dropper x4 - 1 waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor, 1 dropper, and 1 crude engine (place the dropper in the middle, with the engine below and the conveyor above). Right-clicking a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with an axe will turn the block into a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. This will award the advancement Wax Off.
