We Are Charlie Kirk

"We Are Charlie Kirk" is a song released by Spalexma to commemorate Charlie Kirk after his assassination on September 16, 2025 for their album Charlie Kirk Forever Alive. There is a high probability that the song was generated using AI. While originally paired with AI videos of celebrities (especially those within right-wing politics), it spread across social media platforms such as TikTok and YouTube Shorts due to the humorous nature of the phrase "We are Charlie Kirk".
