We have food at home

For the meme based on "we have food at home", see we have food at home (meme).
"We have food at home" is a common response from a parent to a child when they ask to eat out, often with the child asking about a fast food restaurant such as McDonald's. The phrase is among a shared experience for people who grew up in suburban area with fast food locations nearby. "We have food at home" solves the problem of a hungry child while also avoiding paying the extra cost of fast food compared to eating food already bought at their home.
