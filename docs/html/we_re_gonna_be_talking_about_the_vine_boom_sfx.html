WE'RE GONNA BE TALKING ABOUT THE VINE BOOM SFX

"WE'RE GONNA BE TALKING ABOUT THE VINE BOOM SFX" is a YouTube video made by st66zy on July 4th, 2021, playing off an iconic scene in Beavis and Butt-Head Season 2 (Beavis and Butt-Head) episode 14 "No Laughing (Part 2) (Beavis and Butt-Head)". It consists of Coach Buzzcut (Beavis and Butt-Head) getting in Butt-Head (Beavis and Butt-Head) face as Butt-Head tries not to laugh, replacing reproductive organ with the Vine boom Sound effect. There are over 40 times the Vine boom SFX is played throughout the video. Coach Buzzcut: We're gonna be talking about the Vine Boom SFX.
