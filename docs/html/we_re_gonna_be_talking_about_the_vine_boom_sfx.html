WE'RE GONNA BE TALKING ABOUT THE VINE BOOM SFX

"WE'RE GONNA BE TALKING ABOUT THE VINE BOOM SFX" is a YouTube video made by st66zy on July 4th, 2021, playing off an iconic scene in Beavis and Butt-Head season 2 episode 14 "No Laughing (Part 2)". It consists of Coach Buzzcut getting in Butt-Head's face as Butt-Head tries not to laugh, replacing reproductive organs with the Vine boom SFX. There are over 40 times the Vine boom SFX is played throughout the video. Coach Buzzcut: We're gonna be talking about the Vine Boom SFX.
[Butt-Head and Beavis suppress a laugh as Coach Buzzcut leans towards Butt-Head.]
Coach Buzzcut: We'll be talking about the Vine Boom SFX x2. Do you think that's funny, Butt-Head? Do you find it amusing that we'll be talking about the Vine Boom SFX x2?
[Butt-Head puts his hand over his mouth to try and stop laughing, for Coach Buzzcut to pull his hand away.]
Coach Buzzcut: Yes, we're also gonna be talking about Vine Boom SFX x3. Vine Boom SFX x4. Vine Boom SFX x2. Vine Boom SFX x4. Vine Boom SFX ~x24.
