Weather vane

A weather vane or wind vane is a meteorological device shows the wind's direction. Weather vanes are comprised of vanes on a rotatable axis which point in the direction of the wind, typically showing the cardinal directions. Weather vanes may also be present on wind turbines to keep them pointed parallel to the wind. Weather vanes are now often used as a decoration on the top of a house.
