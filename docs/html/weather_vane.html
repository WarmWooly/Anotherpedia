Weather vane

A {{bweather vane}} or {{bwind vane}} is a meteorological device shows the wind wind direction. Weather vanes are comprised of vane on a rotatable axis which point in the direction of the wind, typically showing the cardinal directions. Weather vanes may also be present on wind turbine to keep them pointed parallel to the wind. Weather vanes are now often used as a decoration on the top of a house.
