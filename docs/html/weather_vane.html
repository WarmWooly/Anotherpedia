Weather vane

<><>A {{bweather vane}} or {{bwind vane}} is a [[meteorological device]] shows the [[wind's|wind]] [[direction|wind direction]]. Weather vanes are comprised of [[vanes|vane]] on a [[rotatable]] [[axis]] which point in the direction of the wind, typically showing the [[cardinal directions]]. Weather vanes may also be present on [[wind turbines|wind turbine]] to keep them pointed [[parallel]] to the wind. Weather vanes are now often used as a [[decoration]] on the top of a [[house]].
