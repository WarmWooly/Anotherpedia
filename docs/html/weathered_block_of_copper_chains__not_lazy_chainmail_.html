Weathered Block of Copper Chains (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The weathered block of copper chains is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The weathered block of copper chains can be crafting (Minecraft) with 4 weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place in a 2x2 square). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become a weathered block of copper chains. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking an oxidized block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed weathered block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a weathered block of copper chains. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). Right-clicking a weathered block of copper chains with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed weathered block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a weathered block of copper chains will become an oxidized block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail). Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking a weathered block of copper chains with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into an exposed block of copper chains (Not Lazy Chainmail).
