Weathered Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|Noinfo>>The weathered copper chain is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting and as decoration. The weathered copper chain can be crafted with a weathered block of copper chains (makes 4). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, an exposed copper chain will become a weathered copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking an oxidized copper chain or a waxed weathered copper chain with an axe will turn the block into a weathered copper chain. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. Weathered copper chains are used in the following crafting recipes:
