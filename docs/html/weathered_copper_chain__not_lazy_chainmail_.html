Weathered Copper Chain (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The weathered copper chain is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Not Lazy Chainmail used in crafting (Minecraft) and as decoration. The weathered copper chain can be crafting (Minecraft) with a weathered block of copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) (makes 4). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), an exposed copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become a weathered copper chain. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking an oxidized copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed weathered copper chain (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a weathered copper chain. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement).
