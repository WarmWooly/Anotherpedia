Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<>The {{bweathered copper spiked chain conveyor}} is a block (Minecraft) in the {{iMinecraft}} mod (Minecraft) {{iNot Lazy Chainmail}} used to move mob (Minecraft) and item (Minecraft) around and deal damage to mobs. The weathered copper spiked chain conveyor can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 weathered copper chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) and 6 copper nugget (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the conveyors at the bottom row of the grid, fill the rest with copper nuggets; makes 3). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), an exposed copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) will become a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement (Minecraft) Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking an oxidized copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) or a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off (Minecraft advancement). When a mob (Minecraft) or item (Minecraft) is on the conveyor, it will move in the direction facing. As it is spiked, mobs will also Bleeding (Not Lazy Chainmail) while riding the conveyor. If powered by redstone (Minecraft), the conveyor will stop moving mobs/items. Weathered copper spiked chain conveyors are used in the following recipes:&spConveyor Warper (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 2 weathered copper spiked chain conveyors, 1 ender pearl (Minecraft), and 2 iron composite ingot (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the ender pearl in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).&spSort Dropper (Not Lazy Chainmail) x4 - 1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor, 1 dropper (Minecraft), and 1 crude engine (Not Lazy Chainmail) (place the dropper in the middle, with the engine below and the conveyor above).&spWaxed Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail) - 1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor and 1 honeycomb (Minecraft) (shapeless) Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with a honeycomb (Minecraft) will turn the block into a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail). This will award the advancements Wax On (Minecraft advancement) and Chain On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Either through random tick (Minecraft) or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle (Not Lazy Chainmail), a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor will become an oxidized copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail). Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On (Not Lazy Chainmail). Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with an axe (Minecraft) will turn the block into an exposed copper spiked chain conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail).
