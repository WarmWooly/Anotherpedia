Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor (Not Lazy Chainmail)

<resistance|No||Speed|0.5||Bleeding|I for 1 secondinfo>>The weathered copper spiked chain conveyor is a block in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail used to move mobs and items around and deal damage to mobs. The weathered copper spiked chain conveyor can be crafted with 3 weathered copper chain conveyors and 6 copper nuggets (place the conveyors at the bottom row of the grid, fill the rest with copper nuggets; makes 3). Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, an exposed copper spiked chain conveyor will become a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking an oxidized copper spiked chain conveyor or a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with an axe will turn the block into a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. Scraping the waxed block will award the advancement Wax Off. When a mob or item is on the conveyor, it will move in the direction facing. As it is spiked, mobs will also Bleed while riding the conveyor. If powered by redstone, the conveyor will stop moving mobs/items. Weathered copper spiked chain conveyors are used in the following recipes:
Conveyor Warper - 2 weathered copper spiked chain conveyors, 1 ender pearl, and 2 iron composite ingots (place the ender pearl in the middle, then the iron composite above and below, and the conveyors to the left and right).
Sort Dropper x4 - 1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor, 1 dropper, and 1 crude engine (place the dropper in the middle, with the engine below and the conveyor above).
Waxed Weathered Copper Spiked Chain Conveyor - 1 weathered copper spiked chain conveyor and 1 honeycomb (shapeless) Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with a honeycomb will turn the block into a waxed weathered copper spiked chain conveyor. This will award the advancements Wax On and Chain On. Either through random ticks or by right-clicking with a vinegar bottle, a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor will become an oxidized copper spiked chain conveyor. Using a vinegar bottle to oxidize will award the advancement Oxide On. Right-clicking a weathered copper spiked chain conveyor with an axe will turn the block into an exposed copper spiked chain conveyor.
