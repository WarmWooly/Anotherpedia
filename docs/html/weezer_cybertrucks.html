Weezer Cybertrucks

The {{iWeezer Cybertrucks}} are Cybertrucks with a custom wrap of the band Weezer's album Weezer (also known as the Blue Album). The original image of the first Weezer Cybertruck has been passed around social media, especially Reddit, Twitter, and TikTok.<><> The first post related to the Weezer Cybertruck was on Reddit's r/CyberStuck in mid-2024, with r/weezer also having this post passed around. Following this, a second Weezer Cybertruck was found, having the image on the hood of the Cybertruck instead of along the side.<> There are currently only two images of Weezer Cybertrucks <>.
