Weighted stuffing beads

For the glass material used for a similar purpose, see micro glass beads.&spWeighted stuffing beads or poly pellets are plastic pellet used for adding weight to stuffed animal or weighted blanket. Weighted stuffing beads are especially useful for adjusting the center of gravity on stuffed animals to prevent them from tipping force. The beads are generally stored within a small bag or container within the weighted object to prevent the beads from spilling out in the case of a tear.
