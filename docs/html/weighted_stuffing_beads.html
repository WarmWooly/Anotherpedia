Weighted stuffing beads

<><>{{tFor the [[glass]] material used for a similar purpose, see [[micro glass beads]].}}&sp{{bWeighted stuffing beads}} or {{bpoly pellets}} are [[plastic]] [[pellets|pellet]] used for adding weight to [[stuffed animals|stuffed animal]] or [[blankets|weighted blanket]]. Weighted stuffing beads are especially useful for adjusting the [[center of gravity]] on stuffed animals to prevent them from [[tipping over|tipping force]]. The beads are generally stored within a small bag or container within the weighted object to prevent the beads from spilling out in the case of a [[tear]].
