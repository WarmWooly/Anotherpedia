Welcome To The Void

For other terms relating to "welcome to the void", see welcome to the void (disambiguation).
"Welcome To The Void" is a 1969 psychedelic rock song produced by Morgen (band) as the first song of the music album Morgen (album). The song references folklore and fairy tale, including Peter Pan (play and novel), "Jack Be Nimble", "There Was an Old Woman Who Lived in a Shoe", and "Beauty and the Beast".
