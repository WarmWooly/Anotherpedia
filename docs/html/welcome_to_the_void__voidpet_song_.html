Welcome to the Void (Voidpet song)

"Welcome to the Void" is a song by Linda Shad and music composing by Ryan Martin Potts. It was made for the Voidpet (franchise) and is described as the "Voidpet Anime" on their website. The accompanied music video was produced by Vann Oba and Kassandra Chapa, which was released on January 6, 2025, on YouTube. Welcome to the Void
