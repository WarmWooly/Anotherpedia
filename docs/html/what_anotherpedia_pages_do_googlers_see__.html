What Anotherpedia pages do Googlers see!?

"What Anotherpedia pages do Googlers see!?" is a YouTube video made by Warm_Wooly on November 29, 2025, where he reads the top 50 Google Search based on the search position of Anotherpedia while guessing which page (Anotherpedia) it is linked to. It was made to commemoration getting 1,000 search impression on Google in one day. The video is 3 minute and 37 second in length.
