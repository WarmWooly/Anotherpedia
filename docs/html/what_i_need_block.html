What I Need block

For the window guard brand, see Winblock.
What I Need block, abbreviation to WIN block, is a school block in many American school middle school and high school which gives time to student to get help with homework and classwork from their teacher. It can also be used for other school activity depending on the school. The block is typcially between 30-45 minute long. The goal of the WIN block is to give schedule flexibility of the student's school schedule.
