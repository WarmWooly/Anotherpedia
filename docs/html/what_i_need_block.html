What I Need block

For the window guard brand, see Winblock.
What I Need block, abbreviated to WIN block, is a block in many American middle and high schools which gives time to students to get help with homework and classwork from their teacher. It can also be used for other activities depending on the school. The block is typcially between 30-45 minutes long. The goal of the WIN block is to give flexibility of the student's schedule.
