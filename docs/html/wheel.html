Wheel

A wheel is a generally circle object connected to an axle that can rotation. Wheels are commonplace among machine, vehicle, and other device with rotating component, especially for transportation. tire or similar coverings can be placed on wheels to cushion the interface between the wheel and the surface it is rotating off of. gear, a type of wheel, utilizes rotation to transport torque or rotational energy between other gears.
