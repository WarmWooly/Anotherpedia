Wikiciv

Wikiciv is an encyclopedic survival guide for rebuilding civilization from scratch, which can be found at <>. Its name comes from wiki and civilization. Wikiciv's main selling point is the how-to information present within Wikiciv to have meaningful steps to survive, compared to Wikipedia's general knowledge approach to articles. Wikiciv runs on MediaWiki.
