Wikipede (video)

<><>"{{bWikipede}}" is a [[YouTube]] [[video]] made by [[Rohan Mehra|RohanMediaUK]] on July 5, 2012, with a simple [[animation]] of a [[worm]]/[[centipede]] comprised of the [[logo|Wikipedia logo]] of [[Wikipedia]]. The video is not related to the proposed [[mascot for Wikipedia|Mascots for Wikipedia]], [[Wikipede]]. The video is 10 [[seconds|second]] in length.<>&pThe text "Wikipede" is centered towards the top of the video. A centipede comprised of 16 Wikipedia logos with drawn-on eyes, antennae, and a smile moves along the video in a [[wave]]. Each step of the animation removes the back segment, adds a front segment, and moves the face to the front segment.

This is a pre-rendered snapshot for search engines.
