Winding Jig (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Winding Jig ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
<>The winding jig is a block (Minecraft) in the Minecraft mod (Minecraft) Lazy Chainmail used to turn iron wire (Lazy Chainmail) and iron spool (Lazy Chainmail) into iron coil (Lazy Chainmail). The winding jig can be crafting (Minecraft) with 3 wooden slab (Minecraft), 4 stick (Minecraft), and 1 iron ingot (Minecraft) (fill the bottom row with slabs, the left and right columns with sticks, and the top slot with the iron). Winding jigs require an industrial belt to run, with a chainmail industrial belt (Lazy Chainmail) decreasing the time it takes to wind compared to one made of kelp industrial belt (Lazy Chainmail) or leather industrial belt (Lazy Chainmail). An iron wire (Lazy Chainmail) or iron spool (Lazy Chainmail) can be wound by right-clicking a winding jig with an industrial belt while holding the wire/spool. When the wire or spool is fully wound, it will drop into iron coil (Lazy Chainamil). The wire will create one coil, while a spool will create 10 coils and drop an empty spool (Lazy Chainmail) to be reused. Each wind will decrease a player's hunger (Minecraft), and a player can't wind when at 1 or fewer shanks. In a furnace (Minecraft), blast furnace (Minecraft), or smoker (Minecraft), 1 winding jig can fuel for 40 second (4 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (Minecraft) (64 winding jigs, 2560 seconds, 256 items). gal
