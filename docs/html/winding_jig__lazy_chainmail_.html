Winding Jig (Lazy Chainmail)

Not to be confused with Winding Jig ({{aiNot Lazy Chainmail).}}
The winding jig is a block in the Minecraft mod Lazy Chainmail used to turn iron wires and spools into coils. The winding jig can be crafted with 3 wooden slabs, 4 sticks, and 1 iron ingot (fill the bottom row with slabs, the left and right columns with sticks, and the top slot with the iron). Winding jigs require an industrial belt to run, with a chainmail industrial belt decreasing the time it takes to wind compared to one made of kelp or leather. An iron wire or spool can be wound by right-clicking a winding jig with an industrial belt while holding the wire/spool. When the wire or spool is fully wound, it will drop into coils. The wire will create one coil, while a spool will create 10 coils and drop an empty spool to be reused. Each wind will decrease a player's hunger, and a player can't wind when at 1 or fewer shanks. In a furnace, blast furnace, or smoker, 1 winding jig can fuel for 40 seconds (4 items), with a maximum of 1 stack (64 winding jigs, 2560 seconds, 256 items). gal
