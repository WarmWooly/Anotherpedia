Winding Jig Ruins (Not Lazy Chainmail)

The winding jig ruins are a small structure in the Minecraft mod Not Lazy Chainmail that can spawn in Birch Forest/Dark Forest/Flower Forest/Forest/Old Growth Birch Forest/Old Growth Spruce Taiga/Plains/Sunflower Plains biomes in the Overworld with various items related to Not Lazy Chainmail in a chest and a winding jig. Winding jig ruins have a minimum distance of 13 chunks apart, with the average set to 20 chunks. Winding jig ruins are 6 blocks long and 5 blocks wide, while being 4 blocks tall.
