Wish list

Not to be confused with a bucket list.
A wish list or wishlist is a list or collection of desire goods or service, for a person to obtain, generally associated with holiday or birthday. Wish lists may be given to friend, family, or others who would be potentially able to fulfill the request made on the list. Wish lists come in many forms, including writing on paper, a conveyed message, or as a built-in feature on a online shopping.
