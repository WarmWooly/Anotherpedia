Woggle

A woggle, also known as a neckerchief slide, is an accessory for a neckerchief worn by Boy Scouts and Girl Scouts. It is an open tube which is made out of rope, leather, wood, plastic, or metal. It is used for fastening the neckerchief into place and can be a symbol for a troop or scouting.
