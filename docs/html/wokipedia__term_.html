Wokipedia (term)

{{bWokipedia}}, also written as {{bWokepedia}}, is a term that combines {{iwoke}} and {{iWikipedia}} to describe the political bias of Wikipedia towards liberalism ideals, especially when used as an insult of Wikipedia. The most notable usage of the term is from Elon Musk, utilizing it as an insult against Wikipedia for being "woke".
