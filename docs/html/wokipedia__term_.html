Wokipedia (term)

<><>img>>{{bWokipedia}}, also written as {{bWokepedia}}, is a [[term]] that combines {{i[[woke]]}} and {{i[[Wikipedia]]}} to describe the [[political bias of Wikipedia]] towards [[liberal|liberalism]] ideals, especially when used as an [[insult]] of Wikipedia. The most notable usage of the term is from [[Elon Musk]], utilizing it as an insult against Wikipedia for being "woke".
