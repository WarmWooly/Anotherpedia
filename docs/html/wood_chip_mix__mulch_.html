Wood chip mix can be crafted (Minecraft) with 6 of any wood chips (Mulch) (shapeless). Wood chip mix can be used as fuel (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft), smelting 4.5 items per mix. Placing wood chip mix into a composter (Minecraft) has a 100% chance of raising the compost level by 1. It takes 7 wood chip mixes to fill a composter, with every stack (Minecraft) averaging 9.14 bonemeal (Minecraft) composted. Throwing wood chip mix into a wood chipper (Mulch) will turn it into wood pellets (Mulch). Using wood chip mix on a cauldron (Minecraft) containing water (Minecraft) will turn it into wood pulp (Mulch). sneaking (Minecraft) while doing so will convert the entire held stack into wood pulp.
