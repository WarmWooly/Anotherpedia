Wood Chip Mix (Mulch)

<>Wood chip mix is an item in the Minecraft mod Mulch used to create wood pellets and wood pulp. Wood chip mix can be crafted with 6 of any wood chips (shapeless). Wood chip mix can be crafted with a water bucket to make wood pulp (shapeless; an empty bucket will remain in the crafting grid). Wood chip mix can be used as fuel in a furnace, smelting 4.5 items per mix. Placing wood chip mix into a composter has a 100% chance of raising the compost level by 1. It takes 7 wood chip mixes to fill a composter, with every stack averaging 9.14 bonemeal composted. Throwing wood chip mix into a wood chipper will turn it into wood pellets. Using wood chip mix on a cauldron containing water will turn it into wood pulp. Sneaking while doing so will convert the entire held stack into wood pulp.
