Wood Pellets (Mulch)

<>Wood pellets is an item in the Minecraft mod Mulch used as fuel, lasting twice as long as coal. When wood chip mix is thrown into a wood chipper, it becomes wood pellets. Wood pellets can be used as fuel in a furnace, smelting 16 items per item. Placing wood pellets into a composter has a 50% chance of raising the compost level by 1. It takes 14 wood pellets on average to fill a composter, with every stack averaging 18.29 bonemeal composted. Throwing wood pellets into a wood chipper will turn it into 6 ambiguous wood chips.
