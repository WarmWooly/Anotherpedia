Wood Pellets (Mulch)

<>Wood pellets is an item (Minecraft) in the Minecraft modding (Minecraft) Mulch (Minecraft mod) used as fuel (Minecraft), lasting twice as long as coal (Minecraft). When wood chip mix (Mulch) is thrown into a wood chipper (Mulch), it becomes wood pellets. Wood pellets can be used as fuel (Minecraft) in a furnace (Minecraft), smelting 16 items per item. Placing wood pellets into a composter (Minecraft) has a 50% chance of raising the compost level by 1. It takes 14 wood pellets on average to fill a composter, with every stack (Minecraft) averaging 18.29 bonemeal (Minecraft) composted. Throwing wood pellets into a wood chipper (Mulch) will turn it into 6 wood chips (Mulch).
