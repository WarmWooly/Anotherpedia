Wood Pulp (Mulch)

<>Wood pulp is an item in the Minecraft mod Mulch used to craft paper. Wood pulp can be crafted from 1 wood chip mix and 1 water bucket (shapeless; an empty bucket will remain in the crafting grid). Using wood chip mix on a cauldron containing water will turn it into wood pulp. Sneaking while doing so will convert the entire held stack into wood pulp. Wood pulp can be crafted into 2 paper (shapeless). Wood pulp can be used as fuel in a furnace, smelting 0.5 items per item. Placing wood pulp into a composter has a 100% chance of raising the compost level by 1. It takes 7 wood pulp to fill a composter, with every stack averaging 9.14 bonemeal composted. Throwing wood pulp into a wood chipper will turn it into 6 ambiguous wood chips.
