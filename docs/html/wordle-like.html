Wordle-like

A {{iWordle-like}} or {{iWordle spinoff}} is a subgenre of puzzle video game based on the mechanics of the browser game Wordle. While there aren't many specific restrictions on what constitutes a "Wordle-like", most games in the category are based on guessing some term from limited information, which is expanded through experimentation and trial-and-error. They also tend to be browser games and have a randomized daily game to play. Many Wordle-likes also use a similar pattern of revealing information like Wordle, where completely incorrect information is white/gray, partially correct information is yellow, and correct information is green.
