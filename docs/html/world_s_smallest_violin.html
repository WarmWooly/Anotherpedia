World's smallest violin

For the song, see World's Smallest Violin (song).&spThe expression "world's smallest violin" is used to demonstrate a lack of empathy to someone else's problems. It is typically used to show that someone's problem is insignificant and does not deserve pity. It is usually gestured as the rubbing of the index finger and the thumb, as if you were playing a violin.
