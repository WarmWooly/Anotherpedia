World's smallest violin

<>{{tFor the [[song]], see [[World's Smallest Violin (song)]].}}&sp<>The [[expression]] "{{bworld's smallest violin}}" is used to demonstrate a lack of [[empathy]] to someone else's [[problems|Problem]]. It is typically used to show that someone's problem is [[insignificant]] and does not deserve [[pity]]. It is usually [[gestured|Gesture]] as the rubbing of the [[index finger]] and the [[thumb]], as if you were playing a [[violin]].
