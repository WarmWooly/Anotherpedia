Worth it or Woke

{{bWorth it or Woke}} is a Conservative (American politics) media rating website that rates movie and tv show on the plot, cinematography, actor performance, story direction, and if the media is woke or based (term). Worth it or Woke also provides information on the movie cast and movie production crew of the media, a summary, a content review, and a breakdown of "woke" aspects of the media. Latest releases, exclusive reviews, and full woke breakdowns are paywall by a subscription service to Worth it or Woke.
