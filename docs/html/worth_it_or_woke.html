Worth it or Woke

Worth it or Woke is a Conservative media rating website that rates movies and tv shows on the plot, cinematography, performance, direction, and if the media is woke or based. Worth it or Woke also provides information on the cast and crew of the media, a summary, a review, and a breakdown of "woke" aspects of the media. Latest releases, exclusive reviews, and full woke breakdowns are paywalled by a subscription service to Worth it or Woke.
