<><>A {{bwrapper}} is a thin [[sheet]] made of [[plastic]], [[paper]], or [[foil]] used to protect [[consumer goods]]. The most often wrapped object are [[foods|food]], such as to keep in [[heat]] of [[precooked]] [[meals|meal]] at a [[grocery store]] or [[stadium]] or to keep [[hard candy]] from sticking to the inside of [[pockets|pocket]].
