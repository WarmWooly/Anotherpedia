You are dead (email)

{{iYou are dead}} is an email sent by cody rudland to Jeffery Epstein's Yahoo! Mail on August 13, 2019, 6:48 AM. The email was released alongside the Epstein files. It was sent three days after Epstein's death, and reads, "Lol good riddance". The email address behind cody rudland is redacted, and there hasn't been a credible source on who it could be. It is mainly notable for being at the top of Jmail and the humorous message sent by rudland to Epstein post-mortem.
