Zombie (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

<><>A zombie is a hostile Non-player character created by SCP-049 (SCP: Devolved Revamp) in SCP: Devolved Revamp. A zombie is created whenever a player (Roblox) is killed by SCP-049, spawning on top of their body. They remain idle until a player gets close. The zombie will chase and attack nearby players, dealing damage on contact.
