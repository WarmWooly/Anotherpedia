Zombie (SCP: Devolved Revamp)

A zombie is a hostile NPC created by SCP-049 in SCP: Devolved Revamp. A zombie is created whenever a player is killed by SCP-049, spawning on top of their body. They remain idle until a player gets within range. The zombie will chase and attack followed players, dealing damage on contact.
