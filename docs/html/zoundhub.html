Zoundhub

Zoundhub is a website that data aggregation on various musician, created by the GitHub user xoundbyte in March 2026 at <>. It immediately follows xoundbyte's Soul Over AI, made in October 2025 and closed in February 2026. It primarily uses Spotify, Deezer, and SubmitHub for relevant data on artists. While it distances itself from making explicit claims like the former Soul Over AI, it does keep track of whether an artist is likely to have used generative artificial intelligence through Deezer and SubmitHub.
